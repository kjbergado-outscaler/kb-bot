How to check which carrier is responsible for delivering the order?

  1. Open an order in Fluent Commerce

  2. Go to Fulfillments section, then click in "Fulfilment location". On the red circle you'll be able to to see the carrier assigned to the order. On the left you're able to see the the tracking label (that can be used to track the order).


​

If the tracking number/carrier info is not available, it's because a carrier hasn't been assigned to the order yet.  


What differenciate the carriers? 

Some of them are one-man handling and others are two-men handling carriers. 

  • ​

    1MH:

    when there's just 1 person responsible for delivering the order (e.g: accessories, box beds and mattresses <40kg). There's no scheduling service for delivery of 1MH items.

    • Troubleshooting issues with 1MH carriers here.

    ​

  • ​

    2MH:  

    when there's 2 delivery people (e.g: most beds and all the mattresses >40kg). There's scheduling service for delivery of 2MH items, therefore the carriers call the customers/send email and align a delivery date. When it's 2MH, the customer has direct contact with the carrier and can request address alterations or a cancellation by themselfs.

     

    That's what it means in the matrix bellow"Yes - self service (call or email)".

     

    • Troubleshooting issues with 2MH carriers here.

    ​


What to do if there’s no tracking updates?  
If there's no updated for more than 3 working days, please inform customers that they can reach out to the carriers by using the contact information available here.  If they come back saying that they didn't had any reply, please open a ticket to Backoffice under "Carrier issues" and Backoffice agents will contact the carrier.

How does the Reject at the Door process work? 

Process is described here with details. Some highlights: once the customer rejects the order, they need to inform us. In about 48h, the carrier will change the status of the order in OMS to “return_sender_complete” *. Once this is done, the agent needs to open a JIRA for a Refund.  It’s important to inform the customer from the beginning that this whole process may take around 20 business days to be completed as it’s not automatic.  

* If the status is not updated to "return_sender_complete" in the next 48h after the refusal date, please open a Jira to Backoffice (if Central Europe) or contact the carrier directly (other markets).<
