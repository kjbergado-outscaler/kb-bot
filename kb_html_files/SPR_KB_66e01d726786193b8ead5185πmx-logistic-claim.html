What are Logistic Claims?


Logistics claims are customer complaints related to inconveniences experienced during the delivery of the products. Claims may result in cancellation, return, replacement, or even monetary compensation.

Validation Criteria


After identifying the Reason for the Complaint, we must validate the proof of the claim.

At the same time as ensuring that the right team takes action on the complaints.


Proof of Claims


For damage (either to Emma's product or the customer's property), is require a picture of the actual damage and proof of shipment ("Track Order" Screenshot)


If the claim is related to lost product

We must verify the proof of delivery and subsequently, notify to the Warehouse about the customer's claim and request that it prepare an investigation report with the corresponding courier.


Once we have a response on the investigation, (if it is recovered and delivered to customer or lost) we proceed to evaluate if the customer wishes to cancel order or we proceed to send a supplement.
