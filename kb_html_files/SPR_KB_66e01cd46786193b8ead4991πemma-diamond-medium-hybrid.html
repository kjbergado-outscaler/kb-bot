Diamond Degree Foam


Diamond Degree helps your body to cool down by itself, but there won´t be any risk of overcooling.


Benefits

  • Temperature regulating

  • Facilitator of deep sleep phase

  • Mattress keeps its shape during its whole life since heat doesn´t affect properties on the mattress after it dissipates.

  • Low rolling resistance

    • What does it mean?
      If the rolling resistance is high, the body needs more strength to toss and turn, and then the person will wake up more during the night.

    • How does it work?
      The bottom layer is supportive, it helps the body to move effortlessly, which means there is not a lot of strength required to turn over while sleeping.

How does it work?

  • During the 1st hour of sleep: your body temperature reduces by 0.5°

  • The heat of your body is absorbed by the mattress. On a normal mattress (w/o Diamond Degree), the mattress warms up. With Emma Diamond Degree, the following happens:

    • Step 1 – The PCM coating on the downside of the top layer absorbs the heat

    • Step 2 – Graphite and diamond particles release the heat away from the mattress to the edges of the mattress, where the extra heat is dissipated.

  • After 1 hour, you will be in the deep sleep phase and your body temperature has dropped by 1.5°C.

  • On a normal mattress, the heat loaded previously will return to you, making the foam under your body warmer. This will disturb your deep sleep phase and could awaken you. On the other hand, thanks to the release of the heat done by the Diamond Degree foam, the foam under your body has the temperature of your body. This will support your body to go into a deep sleep and will lengthen your deep sleep phase.

    SLEEP FACT: The longer the deep sleep phase, the more recovery a person feels. This improves his/her immune system and cognitive abilities.


3 Zone Pocket Spring


The 12cm pocket springs, with an average of 250 springs/m2, is encased by supportive HRX cold foam


Benefits

  • Providing 3 zones of dynamic adjustment to the body, especially after changes in sleeping position, optimal air transfer to drive away excess heat and moisture

  • A spring mattress feels bouncier than a foam mattress. Each spring works independently. If two people share the same bed, the mattress will support each of them individually, giving them appropriate support.

  • The hip zone is harder than the rest of the mattress. Thanks to this, the customer does not sink too much into the mattress (=guarantee good spinal alignment)

  • Barrel shape which allows the mattress to adapt to the shape of the person + a motorized slatted frame (Vs square springs)

  • The ventilation of a spring mattress is better than that of a foam mattress. A spring mattress stays cooler than a foam mattress (important in summer).
