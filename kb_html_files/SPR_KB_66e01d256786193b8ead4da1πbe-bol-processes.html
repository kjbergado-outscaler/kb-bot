Open questions:

Q: It has been told to us, that when Bol asks for a discount, we have to wait until the 100 days are passed. because when we do this discount already, and the CST decides to not keep the product and returns it, bol gives the entire amount back as refund, not looking at the already given discount, so they would have a full refund from bol.com + the discount from our side. how can we handle on this?


Answer: Until solution is found with bol.com we can offer the customer a voucher for a future purchase instead.

Q: Some CST ask for TAX invoice. We can not make it, as the prices between bol and Emma differ. As the CST is buying through bol, it should make sense that also bol creates this TAX invoice, no?


Answer: A PDF Document has been made which shows how we can make TAX Invoices for orders

Q: CST ordered 160X200 mattress and paid the price for a mattress with size 180X200. Because when we check all the prices on bol, we see that there is a different price. So the CST paid too much, we are telling the bol agent that it's on their side, but they say that they don't calculate the prices for the products and we (Emma) can adjust them, so we have to take care of it?


Answer: Yes, Bol will not do anything on their side for this case, we can provide them the difference in price.

Q: we´re always supposed to be matching the prices of bol.com


Answer: the prices should be the same as the prices on the Emma website

Q: Cancel: bol gives an estimated delivery time, when this has passed, bol cancels order and refunds CST, while we are still going to deliver. So, we need to make a new invoice to still receive money so what do we do?


Answer: Currently, the order processing on Magento is linked to the Bol account. So, Orders will automatically be updated on the Bol account. We discussed this with our account manager and unfortunately it was not possible to increase the delivery time. As a short-term solution, we are sending out an email that the order will most likely be delayed, so the customer is at least informed.

Q: Return labels: if customer asks for a return, they don't receive a return label. We have to make it in Magento and then also put in bol portal the return so the customer receives a refund


Answer: Customers now get an automatic email concerning returning accessories and the link where they can make a return label, identical to the process we have for D2C.
