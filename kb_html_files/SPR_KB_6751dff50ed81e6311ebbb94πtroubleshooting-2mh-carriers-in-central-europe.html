Introduction:

Remember: 2MH service is when there's 2 people doing the delivery (e.g: most beds and all the mattresses >40kg). 

 

If  Markets: 

  • Austria (AT) 

  • Switzerland (CH) 

  • Germany (DE) 

  • Belgium (BE) 

  • The Netherlands (NL) 

And Carriers are: 

  • Hermes: DE, AT, CH 

  • Dynalogic: NL, BE 

  • PostNL Home: NL, BE 

 

If the answer to both is yes, you will find the instructions in this article. If not, please revisit “The Last Mile (delivery carrier) Matrix”  article.

Main queries

Is it possible to cancel an order (after dispatched)? 

The carrier calls the customers/sends email to align a delivery date. At this moment, the customer can request a cancellation by himself and inform Emma. Once he has done so, the carrier updates the status of the order to “return to sender”, and agent  can request a Cancellation and Refund.  
 

How to Edit/change order (add or remove items) 

It’s not possible to do it if it’s already with the carrier. The customer option is to accept the order and make another purchase (if it’s the case, offer help placing the order – most of the deliveries in CE are free).  In case the customer asks for a cancellation, please follow the Cancellation process  (depending of the stage it may include a Refuse upon Delivery).  
 

Is it possible to schedule a specific delivery date? 

It's possible as there’s a scheduling service for delivery of 2MH items. Carrier calls the customer or sends an email to align a delivery date.  

  • Important: if carrier email scheduling system is not working, let them know that they’re able to contact the carrier quicker by themselves by using the contact’s available at carrier’s email / portal. They can also find the carrier contact information on the Order Status Page. If, for some reason, none of this works, please open a Jira to Backoffice so they can contact the carriers.  

Address change / correction 

  • If there's no delivery label (tracking number), it's never possible to request the carrier to change the address. If that's the case, kindly ask the customer to wait for the tracking number to arrive (status in OMS = “In transit”) .  

  • Once / If there’s a tracking number available: carrier will contact the customer to schedule a delivery date via email or cellphone. They can share the correct address with the carrier directly using the same contact channels. 

 

Is it possible to request a re-delivery to the carrier? 

Customer is eligible for the 3 delivery attempts. As carriers call / send an email to contact them to schedule a delivery date via email or cellphone, they can request a re-delivery directly using the same contact channels. 

 
2MH Carriers provide assembly services? Is it possible to request an assembly service after the delivery? 
Yes – they provide if the customer opted in for the assembly service before placing the order or if it’s already included. It’s not possible to request assembly if the order has already arrived at the customer’s house (and it wasn't included in the purchase). The only exception Post NL Home – they don’t offer assembly services. 

 

What to do if a customer ordered a product with assembly included and the carrier didn't assemble? 

The correct process is to contact the carrier in order to schedule a new assembly. If that happens, we don't offer compensation. But, if the customer contacts us explaining that the carrier did not assemble the product, but he did, please offer a maximum value of 25 euros in compensation (not eligible for Post NL Home). 
 

What to do if CS paid for Express Delivery (ED) but carrier didn't fulfill it? 
This option is currently not available for Central EU.
