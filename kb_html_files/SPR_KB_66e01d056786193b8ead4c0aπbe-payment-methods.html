How does it work?

Bancontact works like a traditional debit or credit card. Customer has to scan a QR code with their phone and enter their card details. Then the customer can pay right from his bank account.

​

No link available as the customer will receive the invoice directly by email.

​

​

What is it?

Alma is a payment method that offers the possibility of paying in 3x without paying interest.

​

Conditions

Minimum purchase limit of 100€

​

How to use?

Customer will be redirected to Alma's server to pay the order's amount.

​

​

​

How does it work?

​

Pay Now allows you to make a fast and easy payment of your online purchases using your online banking details. At the checkout, shoppers are redirected to the online banking environment where payment details and beneficiary are pre-filled. The customer completes the bank transfer and uses the authentication procedure enforced by the bank. The purchase amount is instantly taken away from the account and settled to the merchant within one working day.

​

Useful links

  • ​​Click here for more information

  • Link to access payment portal from customers side:https://app.klarna.com/login​

​

​

How does it work?

​

Klarna is a ‘Buy Now, Pay Later’ service that allows our customer to pay for products later in the month at no extra cost. It means, in effect, that Klarna buys the items for the customer. The customer then pays Klarna back at a later date.

​

Useful links

  • ​Proforma Invoice Creation - Klarna​

  • ​Link to access payment portal from customers side:https://app.klarna.com/login​

​

​

How does it work?

Put simply, a credit card is a type of loan: the bank or credit card issuer extends the customer a line of credit — money that they otherwise would not have. In exchange, the customer pays them back by the monthly payment due date.

​

Customers can pay conveniently and securely by credit card. Their credit card details are encrypted and transmitted using the secure SSL method. We accept Visa and MasterCard.

​

Useful links

  • Link to access payment portal from customers side: https://www.mastercard.com/global/en.html​

​

​

How does it work?

PayPal works as an intermediary between the customer and a bank. A user adds a bank account or credit/debit card to the PayPal system and whenever they are making an online payment, they can select which account to debit. All transactions are processed through PayPal, instead of the user's bank.

​

If the customer chooses this payment method, they will automatically be redirected to Paypal.

​

​Important note: The express method will fetch directly customer information from PayPal and provide it to Emma.

 

This means that this might lead to incorrect address, email or other personal information issues. 

In this scenario provide support and update necessary information.

What is it?

This option allows the customer to use their Google or Apple wallet directly to proceed with the payment.

​

How to use it?

The wallet is an app for smartphones that has a digital version of a credit card and facilitates payments. Select it and further instructions will appear.
