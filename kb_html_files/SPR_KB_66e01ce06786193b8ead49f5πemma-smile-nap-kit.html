Summary of the product


The Emma Smile Nap Kit includes an ergonomic memory foam pillow and an extremely soft blanket that comes in a practical pouch with a zipper and also with a transparent transport bag.


Pictures



USPs


Comfort wherever you are
With our Emma Smile nap kit you will never worry about being uncomfortable again while napping. Whether it’s your kids in the back of your car, in the train on the plane or if it’s just too cold in your office - With the Emma Smile nap kit you got handy accessories for big and small.
Supportive pillow and cozy blanket

  • Emma Smile pillow: Through the memory foam’s thermoelastic foam the Emma smile pillow provides optimal support for your head and neck.

  • Cozy blanket: With our cozy blanket you will always have a soft and warm partner with you.

2 options for transportation modes

The Emma Smile Nap Kit includes two products and two convenient ways to carry them:

  • Soft bag with zipper: You can transport our cozy blanket in the branded zip-able bag, complimentary to the color of your pillow;

  • Transparent PVC: Additionally, you have the option to transport both items in our transparent PVC bag - which can also turn into your sturdy new bag for the library.


Comes in two colors You can choose the pillow in orange and blue.
