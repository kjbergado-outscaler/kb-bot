IMPORTANT: DO NOT edit an Anonymous profile, don't insert any customer information or order information. You must follow the steps indicated in this article.

What are Anonymous cases?

An Anonymous case is when the customer reaches out as an unidentified contact. This means that their name, phone number, email will not appear on our system once the case is created.

And what is the impact of those cases?

​

Technically, all the Anonymous cases get register in the same Anonymous profile, no matter the market or the language. This means the same profile can have information from different customers and orders numbers, and those conflict information will make difficult for everyone to really understand the request.

​

That's why is PROHIBITED TO EDIT anything on an anonymous profile when receiving an anonymous incoming contact. Agents must instead follow the steps indicated in this article.

​

How to handle Anonymous cases?

After indentify and confirm that the contact is Anonymous, please follow the steps:

Step 1: Inform the customer that the incoming case is showing as Anonymus is our system and confirm if the customer allows you to save the information on the system and identify himself/herself. Please explain that this will create a profile on the system that register all the interactions they have with us and facilitates when looking for previous cases.

If customer says NO: you cannot save their information on the profile.

Step 2: If customer allows, it is MANDATORY that you create a new case/outbound email in order to be able to register the customer information in a new case, and not in the one that was open during the incoming contact.

Note: Check here the article "How to create new case/email outbound".

Step 3: You have to send the outbound email when still in the call/live-chat with the customer. Because the outbound email will create a new case, and automatically a new profile, in which you can add the customer information correctly.

After that, on the "case collaboration" column, you need to leave a note explaining the case was originally from an Anonymous incoming contact and put the original case number just for reference. But everything else from that customer, will be handled form the new profile created.
