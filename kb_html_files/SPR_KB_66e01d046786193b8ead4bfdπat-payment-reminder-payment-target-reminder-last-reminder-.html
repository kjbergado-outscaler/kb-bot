Payment reminders can be sent by both Emma and Unzer (formerly Payolution/Paylater). Reminders are sent exclusively by Unzer.

Emma

  • Emma sends payment reminders only for the "advance payment method" .

  • Emma only sends a payment reminder.

  • Emma sends the payment reminder 11 days after the order.

  • An unpaid or underpaid order will be automatically canceled 11 days after the payment reminder or 22 days after the order was placed. The customer will be informed via cancellation confirmation.

Procedure Emma

  • Example: 01.01. Ordered

  • 11 days later (01/11): payment reminder

  • 11 days later (01/22): Cancellation

Even in the case of a cancellation due to a lack of payment, Emma does not report any data to the Schufa or anywhere else. We just cancel the order.

Payolution / unzer

  • Unzer sends the payment reminder 14 days after the invoice has been issued/received, provided there was no error.

  • More precisely: Unzer sends the first payment reminder 14 days after Emma has set the capture at Unzer. This is done with the invoicing at Emma. The invoice is created with the dispatch.

  • A new payment term of 7 days is set in the payment reminder.

  • There are no further payment reminders, but warnings.

Process Payolution / unzer

  • Payment reminder on the day of the payment target (e.g. 01.01.)

  • 7 days later (08.01.): first reminder

  • 7 days later (01/15): final reminder

  • 7 days later (01/22): Transfer to debt collection. A letter from the collection agency follows (addressed to billing add
