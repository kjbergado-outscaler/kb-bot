We can cancel order before it is sent to Submitted to warehouse/shipped.

When cancelling the order click on Cancel in Magento please select “Full refund” for purchases on Amazon. Please advise customer it will take 3-10 working days to receive their refund to original payment method

If customer wish to cancel their order once it has been shipped but has not been delivered:

  • Contact relevant delivery company to advise to return the order to us.
  • In email heading include Amazon, tracking number, Magento order number and request details (Send order back to sender).
  • In email’s body include customer’s details full name address, phone number, email address and product and quantity.
  • Inform customer to refuse delivery if attempted.
  • Once the order has been sent back to us (make note of it and remember to check status of order after 2-3 days) please start return process on Magento. Click on Return products select “Full refund” and “Product back at warehouse”. This will start refund process.
  • Please advise customer it will take 3-10 working days to receive their refund to original payment method.
