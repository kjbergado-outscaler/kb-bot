Overview



As you know, we are migrating systems, and due to that we may find challenges in our new platform.


If your team identified an issue, and you need to report this to Emma Order Management product development team, this is the right article for you.


This portal will be dedicated to any technical issues you are experiencing with Fluent Commerce. All the issues raised here will go directly to Emma tech team, so please be mindful when creating a ticket.

    • Orders as example
    • Screenshots, screen recording to show the issue
