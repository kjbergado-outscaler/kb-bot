Summary of the product


THe Emma AirGrid Microfiber is a pillow with the AirGrid technology. Essentially is the product that combines all the characteristics: adjustable, the cover is cooling due to breathability, and also has improved ergonomics, giving better support compared to standard microfiber pillows.


USPs

    • Summary of Product: the cover is cooling and helps to control the temperature as it has heat dissipation features. 

    • USPs:  

    • Adjustable Height: Plush/Fluffy pillow with an extra layer for custom support: The soft microfiber filling in this pillow molds to the user's head for better neck support and quickly bounces back, so he doesn’t have to wake up and adjust it. The user can also personalize it - at home, just add or remove the extra fluffy layer to adjust the height to fit his neck, sleep position, and comfort. Plus, by changing the number of inner layers, the user can control how firm or soft the pillow is. More layers make it firmer, while fewer layers make it softer, so he can choose what feels best for him. 

    • Thermoregulation: Thermoregulatory grid keeps optimal temperature and is gentle on skin: This pillow has a special, comfortable soft-touch cooling layer with hundreds of air pockets that keeps the user body temperature just right, so he stays comfortable all night. He won’t need to wake up to flip the pillow to find the cool side. 

    • Pressure Relief and Total Relaxation: Honeycomb design for total relaxation and pressure relief: Thanks to the honeycomb mesh layer, this pillow absorbs pressure and gives to the user the head, neck, and shoulders support they need, spreading weight evenly for extra comfort. 

    • Features: 

    • Improved Ergonomic 

    • Adjustable Height: 2 inner layers 

    • Advance Temperature Regulation: Air Grid Technology 

  • Not OKEO-TEX certified
