General Information


Riverty (also known as Afterpay) is a payment service provider (PSP) that we use in our online stores in Belgium (BE) and Netherlands (NL). Riverty is a 3rd party provider who collects payment from customers and eventually remits the payment to Emma, in accordance with the agreed terms and conditions.


Transaction ID/Payment Reference


You can identify the payment method of an order by checking the Payment Information in Magento order screen.
