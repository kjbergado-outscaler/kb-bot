Step-by-step guide


1. Open customer order and click on "invoices" tab



2. Open the invoice you want to edit by clicking on the respective row



3. Edit the billing address by clicking on "Edit"



4. Insert the VAT number provided by the customer and then click on "Save Order Address"



5. Issue the invoice by clicking on "Print"



6. Confirm that the correct VAT number was added to the invoice and send it to the customer





Alternative solution guide


It might occur that the "Edit" button does not appear as shown above in the step 3. (see example in the picture below).


In what follows, it'll be explained how can you circumvent situations like that:


1. In the order view click on the customer's name



2. Click on the "Addresses" tab on the left-hand side of the page


3. Insert the VAT number provided by the customer and click on "Save Customer"


4. Then print a new invoice, verify that the right VAT number is included and send it to the customer
