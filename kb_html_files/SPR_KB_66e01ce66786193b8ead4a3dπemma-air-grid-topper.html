Summary of the Product

Take sleep to a new level: temperature control, ergonomic design, total relaxation!


Our most breathable and cooling top mattress. It has exceptional pressure relief for optimal comfort, adapts to your sleeping position and absorbs movement, and has easy maintenance with machine washable cover.

Pictures



USPs (Unique Selling Points)


  • Advance Temperature Regulation: Enjoy sweat-free sleep thanks to airflow improved to the maximum: 

  • The topper effectively dissipates heat, ensuring a consistent, comfortable temperature throughout the night. 

  • The TPE material's cool touch from the start promotes quicker relaxation and helps you fall asleep faster 

  • Responsiveness/Improved Ergonomics: A sleep experience that is smooth and uninterrupted: 

  • ​As the TPE layer is highly responsive, it immediately conforms to your body's contours, making it an ideal topper for everyone 

  • Unlike foam, which has a delayed rebound after compression, the TPE layer immediately conforms to your movements, allowing you to toss and turn during the night without any disruption 

  • Total Pressure Relief and Relaxation: Step up to the most comfort and relaxation for your shoulders, spine, and hips​: 

  • The Air Grid Technology provides exceptional pressure relief and support 

  • The Air Grid Technology offers support to broader areas like the back while cradling pressure points throughout your body 

  • Innovative Technology: Zero Gravity Lying down feeling: 

  • The innovative AirGrid® technology, provides a distinctive Zero Gravity sensation that dynamically adapts to various sleeping positions, promoting a straight back, independent sleep, enhanced breathability, and effective pressure relief – all in a single product designed for all comfort. 

  • Features: 

  • Improved Ergonomic 

  • Total Pressure Relief and Relaxation 

  • Advance Temperature Regulation: Air Grid Technology 

  • Washable Cover 

  • Not OKEO-TEX certified
