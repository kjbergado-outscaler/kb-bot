We can receive 4 different kind of tickets.

  • Customer contact us directly through our website.

We need to ask customer for their order number or search order in Magento using their name/address.

  • Customer contact us through Amazon.

The order number is usually displayed in Properties in Market Place Order Number

  • Amazon can contact us on behalf of customer

We reply to Amazon saying

“Hi Amazon”… and after answering enquiry

  • Amazon send us return request from customer. We can reply directly on that message to customer and either ask for reason/offer comfort layer or process return for customer.
