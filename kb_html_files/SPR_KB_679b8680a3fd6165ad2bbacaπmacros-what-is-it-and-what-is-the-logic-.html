What is a Macro in Sprinklr?

A Macro in Sprinklr is an automated workflow that allows users to perform repetitive tasks quickly and efficiently. Macros help streamline processes by executing predefined actions or rules based on specific conditions. They are primarily used in customer service management to ensure consistency and reduce manual effort when handling similar types of inquiries or requests.

​

Purpose of Macros

  • Automate repetitive actions.

  • Improve response time and agent efficiency.

  • Maintain consistency in customer interactions.

  • Reduce human error.

  • Enable faster resolution of customer issues.

​

Logic Behind Macros

This is similar to the "Create a Rule" function in Freshdesk, but in this case, you need to manually execute the macro. You must click to apply the macro for the rule to take effect.

Macros in Sprinklr work on a trigger-action/rule basis. When a predefined condition (trigger) or a certain rule is met, the system executes a series of actions automatically.
