Retailer and Marketplaces Contacts


This article will provide you with guidelines on how to redirect a contact coming from a Retailer and/or from a customer that purchased in Store or through one of our Marketplaces. 



Customer Service DO NOT assist retailers and/or retailer's customers directly



Influencers Contact


Whenever an Influencer that works with us contacts the support, please redirect the email to collaborazioni@emma-materasso.it

Store's Customers


Phone/ Email/ Chat Contact

If a customer that purchased in Store contacts us via phone/email/chat, we should thank them for the contact and inform them that any inquiry should be redirected to the Store where they made the purchase.


No action should be taken on CS side.


Retailer's Contacts

Phone/ Email/ Chat Contact


If a Retailer contact us, we should redirect them to their dedicated channel:


Jysk: If the contact is coming from Jysk, we should redirect them to write an email to assistenza-jysk@emma-materasso.it


All Retailer: If the contact is coming from any other retailer, we should redirect them to write an email to assistenza-retail@emma-materasso.it

Marketplaces Customers

Current marketplaces for Italy


Active:

Amazon

Leroy Merlin

Maison du Monde

Mano Mano


Inactive:

Metro

Ebay

GiordanoShop


Phone/ Chat Contact

If a customer that purchased through one of our Marketplaces contacts us via phone/chat, we should inform them that someone from the dedicated team will reach out to them as soon as possible.


We then create a New Ticket and we assign it as "Open" to the Group "Marketplaces".


Email Contact

If a customer that purchased through one of our Marketplaces contacts us via email, we should change the Group to "Marketplaces" and make sure that the status is "Open". 



If the contact is for an order placed with Leroy Merlin and/or Maison du Monde, please redirect the customer to contact them directly. DO NOT SEND THESE CONTACTS TO MARKETPLACE
