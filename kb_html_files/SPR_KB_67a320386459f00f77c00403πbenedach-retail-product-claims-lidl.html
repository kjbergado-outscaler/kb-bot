Overview

Product claims are complaints of the customers related to the quality of production of our products.

Per our terms and conditions, when a customer buys a product with us, we are responsible for replacing any product that presents a production/quality defect.

You can find the claim pictures requirements and examples here and how to validate the claim here.

We are not authorized to process product claims related to the mattress cover being cut during the unboxing process using the Emma Cutter, or defects resulting from sleeping on the incorrect side of the mattress.

Dealing with Lidl

Please follow these steps when Lidl contacts us regarding a product claim and you deal directly with the retailer.

Analyze the Lidl E-mail and Request

  • Review the email from Lidl carefully.

  • Determine if the order was placed in 2023. If it was, the order details will be in Magento.

Locate the Order

  • Search for the Order: Use the external order number (490XXXXXX) provided in the Lidl email to locate the order in Magento/FluentCommerce.

  • Verify Order Details: Confirm that the order matches the product reported by the customer.

  • Check Customer Information: Ensure that the customer’s name and address are correct.

Contact Lidl

Request pictures and any other necessary documentation to proceed with the warranty process, if it wasn’t already sent.

If the pictures are already attached, you may proceed with the analysis/supplement creation if needed, or creating a return with refund (credit memo).

Dealing with the End Customer

Please follow these steps when Lidl contacts us regarding a product claim and requests that we handle the issue directly with the end customer.

Analyze the Lidl E-mail and Request

  • Review the email from Lidl carefully.

  • Check if the order was placed in 2023. If it was, the order details will be in Magento.

Locate the Order

  • Search for the Order: Use the external order number (490XXXXXX) provided in the Lidl email to locate the order in Magento/FluentCommerce.

  • Verify Order Details: Confirm that the order matches the product reported by the customer.

  • Check Customer Information: Ensure that the customer’s name and address are correct.

Create a new case in Sprinklr

Go to Sprinklr and create a new case.

  • Enter the customer’s email, online shop number, and BANF (customer reference number).

Fill in Customer Details

  • Provide the customer’s full name and email address → Next.

Set the Email Subject

  • The subject line must follow the template:
    “Ihre Lidl Bestellung [ONLINE SHOP NUMBER] / [LIDL ORDER NUMBER]”.

  • Create the Case: Click "Next" and then “Create” to generate the case.

Assign the ticket to Yourself

  • Assign the Case: Once the case is created, click on the "Makro" option and select “Assign sub-case to Me” to assign the ticket to yourself.

Contact the End Customer

  • Send the Lidl Mail Template:

    • Use the “Lidl Mail” template to request pictures and any other necessary documentation to proceed with the warranty process.

    • If the order is older, also request the customer’s updated address.

  • Close the Sprinklr case: Once the email is sent, close the Sprinklr ticket.
