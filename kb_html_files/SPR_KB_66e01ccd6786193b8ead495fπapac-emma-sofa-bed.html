Summary of the Product


The Emma Sofa Bed is perfect for those who want the best of both worlds – a stylish and comfortable sofa by day, and a cozy bed by night. This versatile piece of furniture features a soft, easy-to-clean fabric and a simple design that makes set-up super easy. Whether entertaining guests or just relaxing at home, the Emma Sofa Bed is sure to provide with the ultimate sleeping experience.



Pictures



USPs (Unique Selling Points)



Comfort Both Ways
Relax upright, sideways, or on your back! Our two-sided cushion design makes sitting and sleeping equally as comfortable. With two extra waist pillows, the Emma Sofa Bed gives maximum comfort and support so you can unwind on your nights off.
Easy Set-up
Spend less time setting up and more time settling down. The sofa bed takes less than a minute to assemble, and just seconds to transform from a comfy sofa to a cozy bed. No tools are needed!
Convenient Storage
Spare bedding, holiday décor, tablemats—store them all in your sofa bed! Each armrest comes with a built-in compartment for an easily accessible storage solution to help keep your room tidy and your belongings handy.
Certified Sustainable
Lounge is worry-free as the Emma Sofa Bed is designed from the ground up to be sustainable. It’s lined with fabric made from recycled plastic that also has OEKO-TEX Standard 100 Class II certification, CertiPUR-US certified foam and FSC-certified wood.
