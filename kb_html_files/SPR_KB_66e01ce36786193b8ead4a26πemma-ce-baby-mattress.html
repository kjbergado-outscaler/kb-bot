Information Updates [09/08/22]



Missing puller


The cover missing puller is NOT classified as a quality issue, this is done on purpose to ensure baby safety. Emma removed the puller to make it impossible for the child to open the cover.


Please, provide the customers with this explanation in case of missing puller claims.

IMPORTANT: Warning Information



Please, notice:

Do not sleep with your newborn baby in one bed. Eg the Emma One or Emma Original. Adult mattresses are not hard enough. There could be a risk of suffocation. To reduce that risk - buy an Emma baby mattress.


Please, never mention zero risk, always mention reduced risk.
