Overview

Each market/country has its own portfolio, and each store displays a different set of products.

Emma stores portfolio has a mix of e-commerce products (available on the Emma website) and offline exclusive products. This means that NOT ALL online products will be available for sale or for testing in the Emma stores.

Emma Stores Portfolio
