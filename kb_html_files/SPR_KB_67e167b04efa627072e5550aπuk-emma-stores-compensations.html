Monetary Compensation Requests

Context & Purpose

Compensation is provided to customers when the company is unable to fulfill their requests due to stock unavailability or service issues. The preferred compensation method is offering lower-cost alternatives (e.g., pillows) instead of cash refunds.

This applies to:

  • When a requested item is out of stock.

  • When a service failure occurs, such as a delayed or incorrect order.

  • When a manager approves compensation.

Instructions

  • Assess the customer’s request.

    • Calculate the eligible compensation percentage based on the Compensation Matrix .

    • Try to deescalate the situation and explore non-monetary resolutions first.

  • Suggest an alternative (e.g., pillows instead of refunds).

  • If the customer agrees:

    • Process the order for the compensation item in Magento (Supplement).

    • Inform the customer about the estimated shipping timeline.

If the customer insists on a different form of compensation, escalate to the Offline CE mailbox for approval.
