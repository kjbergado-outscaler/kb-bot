Platform Usage & Overview

  • The Choose platform is used exclusively for Claims Management.

  • This platform is not customer-facing; direct communication with end customers must not occur on Choose, they should occur via Sprinklr.

  • If a claim request is clear and complete, agents may proceed to process it directly within the platform.

​

​

  • If additional details are required to proceed, agents must use the Choose internal chatbot to request clarification or further information from the appropriate service team.
