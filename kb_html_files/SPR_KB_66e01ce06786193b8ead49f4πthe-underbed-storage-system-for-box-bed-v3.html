USPs

Extra space to add on your bedroom

Our Emma underbed storage is here to help you put away all your stuff, so you can relax and focus on the important things. The integrated stoppers give you the flexibility whether you show the front of the drawers or hide them completely under your bed, just to your personal preferences!

Premium fabrics that match perfectly with your Emma Original Bed!

The Emma underbed storage was perfectly designed to match the Emma Original bed. Its OEKO-TEX certified fabrics give the premium style and the perfect storage size will make you feel comfy in your home.

Quick assembly and easy-rolling system

The underbed storage can be assembled in 5 -10 minutes in four simple steps and comes with special wheels, so you can slide it in & out easily whenever needed. Our wheels material guarantees smooth and easy rolling so your floor stays scratch-free. Good on wooden or on the carpet floor.



Product Specification


Components

The Underbed Storage System will perfectly match the Emma Box Bed V3 as it has been designed from the same fabrics & colours and it is extremely easy to assemble.

One package will contain 2 drawers, each weighing 17 kg.

Packaging will be the same as the one of BBv3.


Product Measurements

We are offering a ‘one-size fits all‘ product that is suitable for all bed sizes: 81 x 61 x 17 cm.

(Packaging Dimensions 82*60*10)

  • Drawer height at highest point: 19 cm

  • Sideboard & backboard height: 13 cm

  • Frontboard height: 15 cm


Materials

  • All sides are covered in boxbed fabric, which is 100% polyester.

  • Non-woven fabric on the inside.

  • Bottom of the drawers is held by wooden lamellas.


Fabrics

  • D440-15: Dark Grey

  • D440- 52: Light Grey

  • D440- 57: Emma Blue


Assembly and disassembly

The drawers are extremely easy to assemble and can be done by the customer in approximately 10 minutes.

In order to assemble it, the customer will need to have:

  1. 2 x drawer bottom board

  2. 2 x drawer front board

  3. 4 x drawer sideboards

  4. 2 x drawer backboard

  5. 4 x accessory metal plates

  6. 4 x small screws for plate assembly

  7. 2 x L-shaped inbus key


The box will also contain 1 assembly leaflet, which will also be available on the website.

Packaging & Shipping


The underbed storage system will be shipped in one box, which will contain 2 drawers and will weigh around 17 kg, with only one man-handling required for all markets.


  • Boxes should have size, SKU #, and Barcode printed on the box.

  • The estimated delivery time: Looked up under “order”. The order will be sent to Emma’s production team once full payment is received and the order will be delivered within the prescribed delivery time. Depending on the payment method this can take a couple of days.
