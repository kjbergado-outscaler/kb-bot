The identical B2B procedure applies to each of our partner stores. However, we have a few exceptions for Business customers, Leclerc, Ubaldi, and Emma Stores.

Exceptions

Business Customers

  • The customer sends the CS a request for a quote.

  • The quote will be prepared according to the reductions approved by the sales person and sent to the customer who needs to sign and date it upon return.

  • Upon receipt of the customer return, the order will be manually created on Magento and placed in "pending" status

  • Once the consumer has transferred the money, the accounting team verifies the payment and updates the order status with “payment received”.

Leclerc

  • Orders are organized by operation and managed by Philippe Chouraqui, who is the point of contact with the Leclerc centers.

  • Once orders have been confirmed, the Operations team manually prepares the delivery labels and transfers them to the carriers.

  • The carrier then handles deliveries directly from the warehouse, without having to create an order.

  • Orders are integrated into MAGENTO either by delivery or by total quantity, and are processed manually.

Note: The purpose of this integration is to manually generate the corresponding invoices and credit notes in the event of a return and refund request.

Ubaldi

  • From September 2024, Ubaldi will need to prepay orders before they are created in our system (Fluent Commerce).

  • The store will send a request for a proforma invoice to distributeurs@emma-matelas.fr, with a copy to the sales representative.

  • Customer service will prepare the document and return it to the store.

  • The accounting department confirms receipt of the amount corresponding to the proforma invoice.

  • he customer service creates the order and manually prepare a delivery note which it will send simultaneously to Ubaldi and the DBS delivery agency.

  • Return requests and claims processing will always go through the Ubaldi headquarters, which will send us the request for intervention via distributeurs@emma-matelas.fr.

Emma Stores

  • Request Reception

  • Verification of the Availability of Requested Products and sharing this information with the store.

  • Order Setup by dividing it according to the available stock status.

  • Ongoing Order Tracking and intervening in case of any blockages (escalating the issue to OPS).

  • Sending an Email to the Store with a detailed follow-up on ongoing deliveries.

  • Tracking Order Statuses.
