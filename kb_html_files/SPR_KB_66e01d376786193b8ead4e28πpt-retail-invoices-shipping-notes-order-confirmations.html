Invoices & Shipping Notes/order confirmations

Magento

Sometimes, barcodes don't update, and the Invoices and Shipping Notes are not created in Magento. In these cases, we can create them manually.

If you have any cases like this, please escalate to OPS.


Fluent Commerce

In Fluent Commerce it's not possible at all, if you have an order that has been delivered and the fullfillment has not updated to complete and there is no invoice, open a jira to AR please.
