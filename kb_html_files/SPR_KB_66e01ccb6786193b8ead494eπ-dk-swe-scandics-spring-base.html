Summary of the product


This bed base is a particular type of bed sold in Scandinavian countries. It consists of a boxspring bed base and a topper on top without a mattress, and the customer can choose which type of topper they want (Flip or Diamond).


On the websites, this product has the following names:
DK: Emma-boxmadras
SWE: Emma Flip Ramsäng (with Flippable Topper of 6cm) OR Emma Diamond Ramsäng (with Diamond Topper of 9 cm).

Unic Selling Points (USPs)

  • Temperature regulating: On a normal topper or mattress, the heat loaded previously will return to you, making the foam under your body warmer. Thanks to the heat released by the diamond degree foam, the foam under your body has the temperature of your body. This will help you in a better restful sleep.

  • Comfort: Superior comfort and optimal body support. The combination of the springs in the bottom plus our topper gives the body the ideal level of responsiveness and comfort.

  • Convenience: The spring base is not as bulky as a BoxSpring Bed. Ideal for smaller spaces or a guest room/summer house.


Product Information


Product Communication


The SpringBase bed consists of a boxspring bed base (a wooden structure with a one or more springs layers) and a topper (that can be the Flippable or the Diamond). There is no mattress with this kind of bed.


The main difference in comparison to the "normal" BoxSpring Beds is that it has no mattress, is more common in single sizes but can be double as well.



Product Specifications


Components


Every bed consists in:

  • 1 box with 6 feet
  • 1 base
  • 1 topper (either Flip or Diamond)

Materials


Base:

  • 100% polyester fiber on the sides of the boxspring - 60g/m2
  • Fabrics: Black spunbond - Polypropylene (PP)
  • Chipboard with wooden slats
  • Polyester fiber (100% polyester)
  • Springs: 12cm height Bonnell springs, 2.2mm steel wire
  • 2 cm of foam T-3050 (density of 30kg/m³) on the top


Feet: Solid wood


Topper: Check the article for Flip Topper or for Diamond Degree Topper.

Spare part Process


No process is set for this yet, since the product is in test launch phase. The only separated pieces are the feet, and 5% extra was ordered and will be available in DSV for replacement. Any other quality issue requires the replacement of either the bed or the topper.
