Emma Foams


  • Foams are the product of the reaction of Polyols and Toluoldiisocyanat (TDI).

  • The Emma foams are not heated (cold foam).

  • The Emma foams are stabilized and colored by additives.

  • The Emma foams rolled with high pressure, so all Pores can open.

  • The Emma foams differ in density, resilience, consistency, open pores, and compression hardness.



What is density?


  • Density means KG of material per cubic meter.

  • The higher the density, the more stable the mattress.

  • The higher the density, the heavier the mattress becomes.

  • The higher the density, the lower is the breathability of the mattress is.

  • Density is not the decisive factor of the hardness of a mattress but there is an influence.



What is compression hardness?


Compression hardness refers to the structure created within a foam by the action of pressure inside a foam.

At a low compression hardness, the foam structure is neatly arranged on top of each other, the pressure exerted from above is distributed linearly in the mattress and the foam offers little resistance. The result - the mattress is very soft.


Although, If you increase the compression hardness, you change the structure of the foam. In a simplified representation, you can imagine the structure as brickwork with staggered stones. The pressure exerted from above is distributed downwards in a pyramid shape and the foam acquires a significantly higher restoring force. The foam is very firm.



Core Properties



Properties of Cold foam - HRX


  • Ideal support material for mattresses.

  • Stable.

  • Open-pored.

  • High restoring force.

  • Low-pressure relief.

  • Adaptation through various cutting techniques.



Properties of viscoelastic foam/ memory foam/ adaptive comfort foam


  • Most adaptable foam.

  • Visco offers the highest-pressure relief.

  • Adapts pressure immediately and ensures a quiet lie.

  • Extremely compact due to high density.

  • Uneven lying behavior depends on the season.

  • Visco has nothing to do with viscose - a textile fiber but describes the material behavior.



Properties of Gelfoam / Airgocell

  • More pressure relieving than cold foam, less pressure relieving than Visco.

  • More resilient than Visco - but more elastic than cold foam.

  • Best ventilated foam.

  • Stable and durable.


Properties of Diamond Degree


  • Graphite particles are added to the surface of the mattress and topper. Underneath the foam is a graphite layer that dissipates the heat.

  • Demonstrably more heat was conductive!

  • It helps the body to reach the lowered sleeping temperature of 35.5 degrees faster.

  • Relieves pressure like Visco.

  • Conducts heat away in advance.

  • Ideally ventilated.

  • Prolongs restful deep sleep phases by up to 12%.
