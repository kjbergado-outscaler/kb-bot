Instructions

Search for the Order

  • Log into FluentCommerce.

  • Use the external order number to search for the order.

Verify the External Order Number

  • Confirm that the external order number matches the one you copied from FluentCommerce.

  • Check the order status: If it's Booked or Waiting for Stock, it indicates the order is still in progress and we are waiting for the replenishment.

Identifying the Carrier

  • The courier is flagged in Fluent Commerce. It might be either GLS or Mobel.

    • GLS is used primarily for mattress and accessories deliveries.

    • Mobel handles orders with beds.

Tracking the Order in the Carrier's Platform

Access Fulfillment Information

  • Navigate to the “Fulfillments” section of the order details.

  • Click on the “ID” number.

​

Copy the Tracking Label

  • Copy the tracking label associated with the order.

​

​

You might see more than one tracking number if there are multiple items in the order.

​GLS Tracking

  • Go to the GLS Tracking Website .

  • Click Buscar Envíos;

​

​

  • Paste the tracking number in the field Referencia and click Buscar.

​

  • You’ll be able to see the tracking status and download the POD by selecting the option Ir.

  • If the tracking is updated, download the Proof of Delivery (POD) and send it to the customer.

If you see inconsistences/delays, please forward an email to atc.schenker@gls-spain.com (with es.sm.mad.emmab2balcala@dbschenker.com in cc) to request more information about the delivery.

Mobel Tracking

  • Go to the Mobel tracking portal.

  • Go to Service Consult;

  • Enter the tracking number found in Fluent Commerce.

  • Monitor the order status for updates.

​

​

​
