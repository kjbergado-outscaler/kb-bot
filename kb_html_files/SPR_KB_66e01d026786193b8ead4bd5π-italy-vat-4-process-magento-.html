Tax Reduction - Law 104


In this article you will have the steps needed to place an order with reduced VAT (4%) according to the Italian Law 104. 


Customer will have to place the order normally, and we will then refund the difference after the documentation has been checked.



Documentation Request


We need the customer to provide all necessary documentation after the order is placed. 


We will only be able to refund the difference if the documentation is compliant with the law requirements.


What documentation we need?


  • ID Card (front and back) "Carta d'identità o Passaporto"
  • Healthcare Card (front and back) "Tessera Sanitaria"
  • Medical Prescription (Made by family doctor/ Specialized Doctor that attests the correlation between the product wanted and the disability)
  • Proof of disability from INPS or ASL certifying the presence of functional disability of a permanent nature



The ONLY products available to purchase with reduced VAT 4% are EMMA ORIGINAL AND MEMORY FOAM PILLOW.


N.B. If the customer adds to the order other products other than the ones listed above, they will be charged with VAT 22% on the additional items.


Once the customer provides the documents, make sure they are compliant and then send a request to your Supervisor in order to proceed with the next steps.


Invoice correction (Supervisors Only)


Once we confirmed that the documentation is valid and the customer is eligible to purchase with discounted VAT(4%), we should proceed with the invoice correction and refund request.


Please fill in the Invoice template you can find attached to this article and send it in the JIRA to the refund Team.


Please use the following template in the JIRA:


Subject: Partial Refund VAT 4%


Body:


Hi Team,


Attached the Invoice template, can you please issue the correct invoice and refund customer of the difference of € xxxx?


Please cancel the previous invoice.


Thank you


Once the new invoice will be generated, it will be sent via email; you will then have to forward it to the customer.
