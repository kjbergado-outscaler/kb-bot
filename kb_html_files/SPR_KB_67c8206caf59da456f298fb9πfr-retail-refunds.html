The end-customer refund process takes place in two stages:

  1. Emma reimburses the Retailer: Reimbursement is made via a Credit Note, which will be deducted from the payments made by the retailer every 30, 60 or 90 days. These payments, which represent the sum of all invoices after deduction of the Credit Notes, are received via a payment statement (POP);

  2. The retailer refunds the customer internally.

Magento orders

  • Credit Notes (CM) are created and sent manually.

Fluent Commerce – COS

  • Credit notes for returns are created and sent automatically once the return is in “complete” status.
