Order Cancellation Process

Cancellations should be avoided when possible. The first approach is to deflect cancellations by offering alternatives or compensations. If the customer insists, the order must be canceled promptly to maintain a positive customer experience.

Before you proceed, inform the customer that the order cannot be reinstated once canceled.

When to Apply

  • When a customer requests an order cancellation due to delays or unavailability.

  • When an order is still within the cancellation timeframe (see section below).

Instructions

Attempting to Deflect Cancellations

  1. Determine the reason for the cancellation.

  2. Offer alternatives (e.g., a similar item in stock, compensation like pillows).

    Please note: while alternative stock might sometimes be available in-store, we cannot ship orders from stores.

  3. If the customer accepts, process the alternative solution in Magento.

Proceeding with Cancellation in Magento

If the customer insists on the cancellation and the status is Waiting for Stock, Packing or Prepared for Export, you only need to cancel the order in Magento:

  • Locate the order in Magento.

  • Click on the Cancel Order button.

​

​

  • Select the cancellation reason and the Refund £XXX,XX or cancel payment option.

  • Click Cancel entire order.

Cancelling Orders in the Carrier Platform

  • If the order is already at the Order Submitted to Warehouse stage onwards, you’ll also need to cancel the order in the carrier platform:

    • Open the courier platform (e.g., Rhenus, DPD).

    • Locate the order using the tracking number.

    • Click on Cancel Order and enter the required information.

    • Save the cancellation request.

Processing the Refund

  1. Submit a JIRA ticket for refund processing.

  2. Include the order details and reason for cancellation.

  3. Copy the JIRA ticket number into Magento notes for reference.

  4. Monitor the status of the refund request.

  5. Inform the customer that:

    • The order has been canceled.

    • The refund has been requested and will be processed within 7-14 business days.

Make sure to provide complete information in the JIRA ticket and process the necessary requests in Magento (e.g. Product Return) to avoid any delay in the processing of the refund.
