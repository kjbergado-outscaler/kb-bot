Feira dos Sofás

Feira dos Sofás is a strategic retailer with various stores all over Portugal, that buy most of their products in Bulk - both for their Central Warehouse or for the Stores:

  • Stock Orders for the Central Warehouse will arrive in CS for order creation. These need to be escalated to Backoffice for manual creation.

  • Stock Orders for the Storeswill arrive in CS for order creation. These need to be escalated to Backoffice for manual creation.

​

In order for these to be created manually by Backoffice, minimum quantities need to be asked in the PO:

- At least 16 Pillows

- At least 6 Mattresses

​

Otherwise, the order should be created in the Retailer Portal.

​

Note: As of November 15th, 2024, there is no minimum quantity required for mattress requests. All requests should be created manually.

Find Stock Orders

Stock orders can be found in Magento -> Sales -> Orders

We need the invoice from the retailer with the date of the purchase

  • Bill Name: Put *Gfeira

  • Select minimum quantity as “30”;

  • Click in “Search” and “Select Visible”

  • Click in “Actions”, choose “Export Orders” and Submit

​

Best Practices

​

  • When requesting assistance about an order, always attach the invoice in the e-mail. All customer's data are there, and this expedites the resolution.

  • To find the order number, you must open this spreadsheet: B2B Pallets Iberia.xlsx and look for the SKU in the Stock palette, looking from the older order to the newest.

    • Context regarding Gfeira returns:

      Gfeira only places orders in full pallet format. Therefore, when a product is returned, they are unable to identify which pallet order the SKU originally belonged to.

      Procedure to follow:

      When the retailer’s invoice related to the return is received, the process is as follows:

    • Go to the pallet orders file.

    • Locate the oldest order that includes the returned SKU.
