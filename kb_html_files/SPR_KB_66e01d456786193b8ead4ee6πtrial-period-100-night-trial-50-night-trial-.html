The Trial Period


The Risk-free Trial Period is an exclusive benefit offered to our Emma customers.

Depending on the country, you may find policies that cover different products: 200-nighs, 100-nights or 50/30-nights for accessories.




The specific trial policy can be checked on your country's related website according to each product category. Always refer and apply the trial policy that is being indicated on the website.


The benefit starts on the day the item arrives at the customer's shipping address.



For bundles: in case the bundle items are delivered in different parts and arrives to the customer on different dates, it is still considered a bundle, and if the market considers the highest trial period, the time of the trial will start counting from the last item delivered.




Some exceptions can be made in extenuating circumstances. For example, a delivery is marked as compiled but the customer has not received it yet. However, these are rare cases.



Important information


This is a condition that applies simply to the decision of the customer to return the mattresses. It is the right of the customer to decide to keep or not the product, in accordance with their experience with the product.


The reasons may be:

  • Wrong size ordered
  • Ordered Twice
  • Too firm
  • Too Soft
  • Too Warm
  • Medical/ allergy issues
  • Wrong quantity
  • Not required anymore


Whenever we face any of those return requests, we can always try to improve the customer experience with us by approaching each topic with the right argumentation, such as time adaptation, an example. You can find more information about our Return deflection procedure in the Return deflection: Making Impact over returns.



If the product has presented a defect, or the return is requested due to any delay or logistic issue, the return request should be handled by the Customer Service team as a Claim, not as a return under the trial period.



Returns


If the Customer service team was unable to deflect the return, and the product does not attends the expectations, we will collect the product at the delivery address at no cost and the full amount will be refunded with no hidden fees.


  • A courier will contact the customer between two weeks after the request, and schedule an appointment for the pickup.
  • The customer needs to send us proof of pickup, and we can trigger the refund via our Order Management system which will happen in 30 business days at the most, depending on the payment method.
  • Accessories should be sent by the customer through the post office. They should create the label via our Self Portal or request it from our Customer Service team.

Refunds


  • Emma will only be able to refund up to the full amount that was charged, under the 100-night-trial period. This means that if we've previously processed a rebate or discount for the customer, that amount is already added to their refund.
  • Rare instances of refunds done outside of the original payment method happen, but these are usually prompted by the Refunds team. For example, refunding customers for sales tax.
