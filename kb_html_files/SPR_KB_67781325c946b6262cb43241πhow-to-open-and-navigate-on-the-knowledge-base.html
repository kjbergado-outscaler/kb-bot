Knowledge Base Overview

You’re already familiar with your home page on Sprinklr, the Agent Care Console. On the left side menu, you can find the button directly to open the Knowledge Base.

That’s the KB Home Page. There, you’ll be able to see the search bar, filters, categories and articles. The home page may not show all the "Categories" folders at once, so please make sure to click in "Show All".

Search Bar and Filters

At the Home Page, you have the option to overview all articles published, as indicated on the image above. And right at the top of the page, you’ll find the search bar that can find any article in the entire Knowledge Base. . There, you can look up key-words, articles or folder names.

Using “Sprinklr” as an example, the system provides all the articles and folders with this key-word. Related to search of processes, we recommend to type between 2 to 3 words to make the search more accurate.

Example: Prefer to type "make a return" or " how to return" instead of just using just "return".

In addition to that, we recommend to use the Filters option to narrow down your search. The filters are pretty straight-forward and easy to navigate. On the top right, you have your standard configuration filters, being “Sort By”, “Manage Columns” and “Hide Filters”.

Then, you have your search filters. There, you can either filter by Category, Status or Language, or create your custom “Quick Filter”.

  • Category: Those are the main Folders organized per topics, each of them have sub-categories. This filter makes it easier for you to filter directly by sub-category, if you prefer.

  • Status: Always and only search and read the articles with the status "Approved".

  • Language: We have 60 language options in our “Language” filter. However, the majority of the articles are (and should) be in English, since this is the Emmie's universal language. We have very few exceptions, but always look for information with the English option.

How to open an article?

Always open articles in Third Pane button, directly from the article list view below in the screenshot.

​

In this option, the article will open on a smaller pane, and you’ll be able to navigate through the KB simultaneously.

​

DO NOT open the article from the button "View Article". Unfortunately that option don't show the right visualization of the article in terms of medias and technical layouts.

How to open an article while handling a case?

At this part you can check how to search for KB articles while having a case open and handling the customer contact.

At the Case page, you have the customer interaction place on the left and at the middle you can find the widget called "Smart Assist". And that's from there that you will start to follow the steps indicated bellow:

Step 1: Click on the button "Show All" at the Smart Assist Widget:

​

Step 2: ​A new window side will open and there you need to click where says "Knowledge Base":

​

Step 3: On the top you have the search bar, where you should insert the key-words related to the topic you want to find information about. Type what you need and click "Enter".

Then, you will see a list and the number of articles available that are related to that topic. You can scroll up and down to check for the one more appropriate for your need (indicated in yellow at the image below).

​
