Introduction


In this article, you will learn how to guide the customer to check the status of their order on Emma's website. And also, understand what information can be found there.



What is the "Order Status" page?


This page is to help our customers in being able to track the situation of their Emma request, directly via the website:





Our customers can track their Order request, as soon as it goes to the status "Booked" and onwards.


IMPORTANT: For the moment, Supplement Orders are not visible on the order status page.


If you need to refresh about all the Order Status, please read the article OMS Overview: definitions and logics.



How to check the Order Status?



-> Customers have to go on the link mentioned above, and then insert their Order Number and the Zip Code to where the product will be delivered.






-> After clicking on "Search", they will be redirected to the status page and will be able to check their request status.

What is displayed on the Order Status page?


Once the customer opens the page, the following topics are what they can found there:

1) Authentication for Displaying Personal Information: The customer has to enter the Zip code on top of the order number to reveal the personal details (name, address):




2) Multiple shipments of one order in the form of tabs:


3) Information about the status and planned delivery date (Note: The grey boxes are links that redirect to the carrier portal):



4) Delivery or reroute options for recipients to customize delivery preferences through a link to the carrier's website:


5) Some carriers provide a Live Tracking Map, that if available, will also appear on the page for the customer.



6) After the Delivery, customers can give a vote about the delivery:

How do the track links work for Magento and for Fluent?

Even if a country is already 100% migrated to FluentCommerce there are still some orders to be delivered that are still placed in Magento. For those, we must provide the Magento OSP link from the table above.
