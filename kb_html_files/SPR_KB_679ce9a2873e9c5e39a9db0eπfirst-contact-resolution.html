Introduction

In our effort to enhance the customer experience, we aim to ensure that our communications with customers are as effective as possible.

Topics that are First Contact Resolution (FCR) oriented should be addressed and resolved as completely as possible to minimize the need for customers to engage with customer support beyond what is necessary.

Note: Only applicable for the markets: UK; PT; ES

Bellow you can find a list of Topics and sub-topics that are designated as First Contact Resolution oriented.
