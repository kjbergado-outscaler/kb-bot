What is the new process?

Old process: All Agents leaving Internal Notes on the section "Case Collaboration".

New process:

For D2C cases (Support, Sleep Experts, Online Reputation, HLE): Internal Notes must be added to the section "Profile Collaboration" for all cases that we have a customer profile. If the profile is from carriers, partners, returnal portal, etc, we keep using "Case Collaboration" to register the internal notes.

For B2B (Wholesale) and O&O (Stores): No changes. Agents leaving Internal Notes on the section "Case Collaboration".

How to leave an Internal Note?

IMANDATORY:

Check in which scenarios it is mandatory to leave Notes on Sprinklr: "System Notes" .

Leaving Notes on "Profile Collaboration":

Step 1: Prepare your template that MUST be use when leaving notes on this scenario:

  • CN: _______________ (Sprinklr Case number)

  • ON: ______________ (Order Number)

  • ENQ: _____________ (Enquiry)

  • ACT: ______________ (Actions taken)

  • JIRA: ______________ (JIRA ticket number) [if applicable]

Step 2: When you have a case open, click on the person icon that indicates the "Customer 360" button on the right side menu:

Step 3: On that page, in the middle column, you will find a section called "Profile Collaboration".

​

​

​

​

Step 4: Use the bar that indicates "Add a profile note" to type and add your Internal Notes and just click to post it.

Leaving Notes on "Case Collaboration":

The "Case Collaboration" feature appears in the middle column when you have a case open and reflects all internal notes related to that case.

​

You can either type directly from the "Case Collaboration" on the space named "Add a case note", or you can make the note from the conversation space with the customer, on the bottom right, by clicking on "Add note".

The notes will appear consolidated all under the "Case collaboration" box.
