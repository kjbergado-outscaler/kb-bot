Introduction


In this article, you can find instructions about the new process for spare parts ONLY for the Bed BBv5. At this moment, only applicable for BE, NL, DE, AT, CH and FR markets.

This new procedure is in a test phase until at least the middle of July. The Quality team in Emma wants to understand the complexity of it and any point of improvement before applying for more products and countries.



ATTENTION: For the BBv5 do not follow the process at the article Spare Parts SOP. Make sure to follow the instructions you can find below.



What are Spare Parts?


First, it is necessary to have a clear definition of what is considered a spare part:

  • Parts of a bed that can shipped to customers in order to replace a defective, missing, or damaged part;

  • Parts that are usually a faster solution for the customer than resending the whole box;

  • Products/items that CAN NOT be sold separately;

  • Universal and/or small parts of a product are considered spare parts (example: bag of screws, connectors and legs);

  • Bulky and color/size-sensitive parts ARE NOT considered spare parts (example: boards).



Spare Parts Process for BBv5

When to use the spare part process?


When the customer contacts us to inform about a logistic claim or a product claim related to the BBv5. It can be that a part was not received or it was missing or has a defect that compromises the assembly of the bed.


This means that we need to create a supplement order as a replacement for that part. And for this, you need to request the SKU for the exact part.


IMPORTANT: If we don't have the individual part available to be replaced, you will need to open a supplement for the entire box.

How to do the spare parts process?


Step 1: Identify the missing/damaged part


First, you need to be sure about what is the part that the customer is talking about. Please guide the customer to use the Booklet to identify the part (by the letter that represents it) and you can also find the booklet below in this article.

For this, it is needed to use the Booklet of the BBv5 to support the identification, both from the customer and for you.

Step 2: Check if the spare part is registered and part of the list


Step 3: If YES, check the SKU of the spare part. If NOT, check the box where the part is in.


Step 4: Create a Supplement order for the spare part OR for the box.
