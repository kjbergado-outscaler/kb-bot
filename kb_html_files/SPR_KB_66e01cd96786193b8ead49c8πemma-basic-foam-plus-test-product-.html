Introduction


In this article, you can find more detailed information about the Emma Basic Foam Plus, a new pillow that integrates the updated German pillow range. This pillow is an upgraded version of the also new Emma Basic Foam due to the cooling treatment technology: a slim blue patch that goes on top of the foam to prevent people from sweating at night.



Pictures






USPs


Memory foam support for spinal alignment
Construction guarantees optimal support for the spine. Recommended for side and back sleepers. Memory foam pillows are firmer but contour to the shape of your body, and even adjust as you shift or move during sleep.
Temperature regulation

The smart cooling treatment of the foam part helps your body to cool down by itself, but there won ́t be any risk of overcooling. With a cooler sleeping climate, sleepers will be able to achieve a deep sleep phase faster and enjoy a longer deep sleep phase and to wake up more refreshed.

Flippable

By flipping the Emma pillow, you can choose the side that suits you best. There are two sides for customers to choose:

  • Cooling side (blue) -> it stays cool in hot conditions to provide a cooler sleeping climate.
  • Isolation side (white) -> it acts as an insulator in cold temperatures.
Breathability cover

Every night your body loses fluid. That's why the cover of the Emma cushion is particularly breathable. So, your body can release excess energy in the form of heat, and you keep a pleasantly dry sleep climate. And another benefit is to don’t wake up sweating.

Allergic-friendly and machine-washable pillow cover

People with allergies can use this pillow freely. Users can wash the pillow cover to stay hygiene.
