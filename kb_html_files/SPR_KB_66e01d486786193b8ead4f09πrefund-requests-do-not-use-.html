Refund Status


After returning their product, or an agreement of a discount or compensation, customers may reach us to get to know the status of their refund.


When dealing with refunds, we should always have in mind we have two different phases of it:


  • Refund by our Emma refunds team
  • Balance visibility (Bank / PSP deadlines)


Let's deep dive into it:




Refund by Emma


every time you trigger a return or a compensation/discount request via the Order Management System (Magento or FluentCommerce), the order will be on a pending refund list to our Emma-related team.


If the refund request follows all the required conditions, the refund will be issued at the following expected times:



API:
Non-API:


API means Application Programming Interface

API = automatic

Non-API = manual


Balance visibility (Bank / PSP Deadlines)


After a refund is placed by our team, Banks or payment service providers (PSP) will have different deadlines to have the amount available reflected in their account.


If the customer reaches us after we already completed the refund, they should reach out directly to their bank or payment provider company to get further information and more specific deadlines.


You can find a list of the expected balance deadline depending on the payment method (Payment provider).




Common delays reason


If the payment has not been placed by the Refunds team, it may go back to the CS agent who have escalated it due to wrong information sent. Review below the most common reasons for delay:


  • Missing IBAN, faulty or incomplete
    Orders that need to be refunded via bank transfer should always have the complete/right IBAN
    Iban should always be from the Order's owner.


  • Duplicate request for Refund
    » same requests were submitted twice due to incorrect escalation: in this case, the agent should correct information within the same original escalated ticket.
    » the same refund request was sent twice by mistake (impacting team as both of the requests should be verified and only one validated).


  • Incorrect/ Lack reason for refund
    The customer was not entitled to a refund or the reason is not correct and the team needs to follow up reason with the related team.


  • Incorrect amount of refund requested
    Wrong percentage or wrong currency amount


  • When requesting for VAT refund: absence of VAT Exemption Certificate
