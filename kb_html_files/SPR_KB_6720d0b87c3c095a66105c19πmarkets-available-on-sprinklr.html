Introduction:

In this article, you can find information about which Markets are already available on Sprinklr.

Why is it important to check the Markets available on Sprinklr?

If the market is available on Sprinklr, it means we have CS agents from that market handling the queues and cases on Sprinklr and a case can be transferred to them directly via Sprinklr.

If the market is NOT available on Sprinklr. In that case, it means you need to follow the instructions in the table below or - depending on the country - just simply indicate the customer to open the Emma website in their country and reach out to the support team directly from the forms indicated there. DO NOT TRANSFER THE CASE, even if you can see the market code inside Sprinklr.

Markets currently available on Sprinklr

IMPORTANT:

Any market that is not on the list it means they are NOT on Sprinklr so you cannot transfer the case. Please explain the customer to reach out to support via the contact information from their country/market website.
