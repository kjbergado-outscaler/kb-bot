USPs (Unique Selling Points)


Foams can be swapped → Customizable to sleep preferences


The Customer is able to swap the foams - or take out one of the foam layers according to their preferred laying feeling

  • For side sleepers
    • Side sleepers have the greatest distance between the head and the shoulder -> recommendation -> All 3 layers
  • For back sleepers
    • It is recommended to use between 2 or 3 layers, depending on individual lying preference and also on the hardness of the mattress.
  • For stomach/abdominal/belly sleepers
    • It is recommended to use only 1 layer, depending also on the hardness of the mattress.


Reversible/ flippable


By turning the Emma Diamond pillow, you can choose the side that suits you best and solve specific problems in the shoulder and neck area. There are three layers and each with a different laying feeling:


  • Temperature regulating side (grey) -> helps your body to cool down by itself, but there won ́t be any risk of overcooling.
  • Supporting side (white) -> it offers an ideal support function for your head and neck.
  • Comfort side (blue) -> it offers a light lying feeling and supports your head while at the same time sinking in ideally.


Temperature regulation/Deep sleep optimization


Because of our cooling open cell visco foam with graphite and carbon infusion, the pillow fits every season. It stays cool in hot conditions and acts as an insulator in cold temperatures.


Breathability – Cover & foams


Every night your body loses fluid. That's why the cover of the Emma Diamond Pillow is particularly breathable. So your body can release excess energy in the form of heat, and you keep a pleasantly dry sleep climate.


Top and bottom foams are breathable
