We stopped trading with them on 1st of March 2020. Therefore, enquiries we deal with are past orders related mostly to exchange and return of faulty mattresses.

DFS and Carpetright need to send us either PO or ACK order number so we can locate order in Magento.

We search “PO order number” in external # in Magento. Example of PO number: 4611418932

We search “ACK order number” in Order # in Magento. Example of ACK number: 600907258

Often, we cannot find PO number in our system. In this case we have to ask for ACK order number (explain to them it is 9-digit number starting with number 6), and we can also try to search customer’s name.

If DFS send you order number in this format: M1D1AJTJW try to switch 1,2nd letter with 3,4th letter.

The order number will be then D1M1AJTJW and often this helps to find it in our system. Remember to tick “Archive” when searching.

Sometimes when we cannot find number we also can ask if there was any transfer chain:

Return:

If customer change their mind and would like to return mattress (within 100/200 nights period) ask if the mattress is packed or unpacked and reason for return.

Unpack mattress return:

Check what shipping address information we have in Magento and if the delivery was sent to DFS or Carpetright store update it with customer details (where the collection of mattress will take place). Also check that customer has not moved since purchase of mattress. In Magento click on Return Products and create return and select “NO REFUND”. Don’t click on “Product back at warehouse”

Send DFS and Carpetright standard macro for Returns- Out of box mattress-return process started and tell them to forward this information to customer.

DFS and Carperight will send refund the customer once the mattress has been collected. If customer contacted us directly we will need to ask customer for local store DFS/Carpetright email address and once mattress has been collected send them email with customer’s details (PO order number, name, address, product) and ask them to refund customer.

Packed mattress return:

There should not be many requests as we don’t trade anymore however if we situation arise please follow return label procedure and select “NO REFUND” when processing return in Magento.

Exchange of faulty mattress:

Ask DFS and Carpetright to send us photo showing fault use macro Ebay Faulty mattress Ask for photo. and customer’s details including address, phone number and email address Once you have received the pictures and address please create Product Claim in Magento and select “Supplement order” (if customer wish to receive another mattress) or “Return” (if customer would like to get refund and are within 100/200 nights trial). Check what shipping address information we have in Magento and if the delivery was sent to DFS or Carpetright store update it with customer details. This needs to be done in both cases when creating “Return” or “Supplement order”. In case of “Return” customer’s details needs to be there for accurate collection pick up.

Create “Supplement order” for customers who wish to exchange mattress Change store email address to customer’s address so customer can receive tracking delivery updates. Do not add OMR (Old mattress removal) service to supplement order.

Enter information in “Exchange and Charity Collection” excel file. Send macro reply to DFS/Carpertright and tell them to pass information to the customer Exchange-Confirming Exchange after photos (Scotland).

Click on “Return” for customer who wish to receive refund and are still within (100/200 nights trial). Select “NO REFUND” option. Send macro reply to DFS/Carpertright and tell them to pass information to the customer: Returns- Out of box mattress-return process started
