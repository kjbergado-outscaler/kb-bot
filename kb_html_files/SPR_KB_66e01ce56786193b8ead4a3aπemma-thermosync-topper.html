Core 


Thermosync Foam


Thermosync helps your body to cool down by itself, but there won´t be any risk of overcooling.


Benefits:

  • Temperature regulating

  • Facilitator of deep sleep phase

  • Mattress keeps its shape during its whole life since heat doesn´t affect properties on the mattress after it dissipates.

  • How does the Thermosync technology work?

1. During the 1st hour of sleep: your body temperature reduces by 0.5°
2. The heat of your body is absorbed by the mattress. On a normal mattress (w/o Thermosync), the mattress warms up. With Emma Diamond Degree, the following happens:

  • Step 1 – PCM absorbs the heat

  • Step 2 – Graphite and diamond particles release the heat away from the mattress to the edges of the mattress, where the extra heat is dissipated.

3. After 1 hour, you will be in the deep sleep phase and your body temperature has dropped by 1.5°.
4. On a normal topper, the heat loaded previously will return to you, making the foam under your body warmer. This will disturb your deep sleep phase and could awaken you. On the other hand, thanks to the release of the heat done by the thermosync foam, the foam under your body has the temperature of your body. This will support your body to go into deep sleep and will lengthen your deep sleep phase.
