Introduction

Currently one of the challenges you as an agent might face, is how to explain the customer what and how to take the proper product pictures for a valid Product Claim.

​

Here you can find all the templates to send to the customer on how to take properly the pictures for their specific product claim topic.

To make it simple we have it available in several languages, therefore the breakdown will be per language per topic.

​

Note: We have also added example of the label pictures, these can't be shared with the customer but will help you explain to the customer how they llok like

​

Please note: It's mandatory to use this templates when requesting pictures to the customers

  • Instruct the customer to fill out and return the completed form. Explain that this form replaces the product label photo in the claims process.

If the customer refuses to sign or return the legal form:

  • Clearly explain that the form is required to process their claim internally.

  • Emphasize that this is a standard requirement when the product label is missing.

If the customer continues to refuse after your explanation:

  • Accept the claim without the product label photo or the signed legal form.

​

Only proceed in this way if:

  • The product label is genuinely unavailable, and

  • The customer explicitly refuses to sign the form after being informed of its necessity.

Record in the claim notes:

  • That the product label was unavailable.

  • That the legal form was offered and refused.

  • That the claim is being processed without the label photo or signed form due to customer insistence.

Continue with the standard claims process, ensuring all other required information is collected and documented.
