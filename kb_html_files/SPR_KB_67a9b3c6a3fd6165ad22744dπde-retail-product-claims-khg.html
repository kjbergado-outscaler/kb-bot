Overview

Product claims are complaints of the customers related to the quality of production of our products.

Per our terms and conditions, when a customer buys a product with us, we are responsible for replacing any product that presents a production/quality defect.

You can find the claim pictures requirements and examples here and how to validate the claim here.

We are not authorized to process product claims related to the mattress cover being cut during the unboxing process using the Emma Cutter, or defects resulting from sleeping on the incorrect side of the mattress.

Analyze the Request

  • Review the email from the retailer carefully.

  • Determine if the order was placed in 2023. If it was, the order details will be in Magento.

Locate the Order

  • Search for the Order: Use the external order number to locate the order in Magento/FluentCommerce.

  • Verify Order Details: Confirm that the order matches the product reported by the customer.

  • Check Customer Information: Ensure that the customer’s name and address are correct.

Contact KHG

Request pictures and any other necessary documentation to proceed with the warranty process, if it wasn’t already sent.

If the pictures are already attached, you may proceed with the analysis/supplement creation if needed, or creating a return with refund (credit memo).
