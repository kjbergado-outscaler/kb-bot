Point-elastic Airgocell


The top layer adapts to your body and provides good ventilation due to its open pores.

Benefits

  • Temperature regulation = Breathability

  • Moisture (sweat) will be absorbed and then will evaporate, so the customer doesn't wake up sweating

  • Mattress keeps its shape during its whole life since moisture doesn't affect properties on the mattress after it dissipates.

  • Low rolling resistance

    • What does it mean? If the rolling resistance is high, the body needs more strength to toss and turn and then the person will wake up more during the night. It has much higher responsiveness than memory foam, which has a slower response. Because of this, using this foam on the top layer will provide bounciness on a mattress.

    • How does it work? The bottom layer is supportive, it helps the body to move effortlessly, which means there is not a lot of strength required to turn over while sleeping.


3 Zone Pocket Spring


The 12cm pocket springs, with an average of 250 springs/m2, is encased by supportive HRX cold foam


Benefits

  • Providing 3 zones of dynamic adjustment to the body, especially after changes in sleeping position, optimal air transfer to drive away excess heat and moisture

  • A spring mattress feels bouncier than a foam mattress. Each spring works independently. If two people share the same bed, the mattress will support each of them individually, giving them appropriate support.

  • The hip zone is harder than the rest of the mattress. Thanks to this, the customer does not sink too much into the mattress (=guarantee good spinal alignment)

  • Barrel shape which allows the mattress to adapt to the shape of the person + a motorized slatted frame (Vs square springs)

  • The ventilation of a spring mattress is better than that of a foam mattress. A spring mattress stays cooler than a foam mattress (important in summer).



Supportive and Comfort HRX (Cold) Foam


Adapts to your body and promotes optimal pressure load, both for low and high weight loads. This foam distributes the pressure optimally. This technology allows you to sleep comfortably in any position.

Benefits

  • This foam supports you in the right places, where it matters.

  • 3 Zone cut-outs

  • Very long durability. No sagging (Liegekuhle), even after a long time.

  • Sparwelle (material saving cutting): helps support

  • the firmness of the mattress. Perfectly designed height and distance of this wave to create the perfect firmness/ softness of the mattress.

  • It is high point elastic. The supportive bottom layer helps the body to move effortlessly, which means there´s not a lot of strength required to turn over while sleeping.

  • With over 100 supporting points, the Emma Diamond Medium (Hybrid) contours your body perfectly alleviating pressure points, as well as distributing your weight evenly.

  • Ergonomically designed for all body shapes (see illustration).

  • The combination of the individual layers and foams of Emma Diamond Medium (Hybrid) ensures particularly effective adaptation to the different body and sleep types. The mattress is suitable for all HEIA body shapes, both in the spine and lateral position as well as in the prone position.

Cover

Emma Select/DUN Smart Select Collection - Helix/Fusion Helix

This high-quality, fully removable cover allows the foam to dissipate heat as effectively as possible. Its aerial structure allows the mattress to breathe during the day while not blocking its thermoregulatory effect.

Characteristics

  • Top fabric white knitted fabric, 99% Polyester 1% Elastane

    • Knitted white fabric going 9 cm over the sides.

    • Completely detachable top to bottom light gray zipper between border and top panel fabric.

  • Border fabric light grey knitted fabric, 100% Polyester

    • Light grey fabric with waterfall design going 5cm into the bottom to create a mirror. On one side a brand label is sewed in.

    • Grey embroidery of Emma / DUN branding

    • Handles 100% polyester

    • Vertical handles, 8cm wide

    • Piping

    • Branded puller

  • Bottom fabric 87% Polyester, 13% Polypropylene

    • Anti-slip/ white woven fabric going 12 cm to the sides to create a mirror.

    • On one side a brand label is sewed in.

Benefits
