We can cancel order before it is sent to API/shipped.

When cancelling the order click on Cancel in Magento please select “No refund”. Please advise customer it will take 3-10 working days to receive their refund to original payment method

If customer wish to cancel their order once it has been shipped but has not been delivered:

Renewed/Refurbished:

  • click on Return products on Magento select “No refund” click on “Product back at warehouse”.
  • Visit Keen and Able delivery portal and place order on “HOLD” If it is not possible to change status on “HOLD” (once delivery date is confirmed) email Iwetta to cancel the delivery.
  • Advise customer it will take 3-10 working days to receive their refund to original payment method. Put the wowcher code, customer email and reason for cancelation “canceled before dispatch” and send to wowcher. (look up in section: Refund)


Essential: Please contact relevant delivery company to advise to return the order to us (UPS please contact warehouse where order originated eg Stacey or sale carpenter).

  • In email heading include tracking number, magento order number and request details (Send order back to sender). In email include customer’s details full name address, phone number, email address and product and quantity.
  • Inform customer to refuse delivery if attempted.
  • Once the order has been sent back to us (make note of it and remember to check status of order after 2-3 days) please start return process on Magento. Click on Return products select “No refund” and “Product back at warehouse”. This will start refund process.
  • Please advise customer it will take 3-10 working days to receive their refund to original payment method.
