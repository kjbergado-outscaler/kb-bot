Logisimple is the carrier that works for JJ Louro in the case of deliveries and collections of sommiers.

If product is fulfilled by Lusocolchão/JJLouro (Sommier):

  1. Create child ticket** to Lusocolchão, at emma@jjlouro.com;

  2. Receive label / pick-up information from Lusocolchão and forward to customer;

  3. Insert tracking reference in the OMS;

  4. Check return status on planned pickup date / after 1 week on Logisimple tracking page;
