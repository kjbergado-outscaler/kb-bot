Introduction:

Remember: 1MH service means 1 delivery person, covering accessories, box beds and mattresses <40kg. 
 
If  Markets: 

  • Austria (AT) 

  • Switzerland (CH) 

  • Germany (DE) 

  • Belgium (BE) 

  • The Netherlands (NL) 

And carriers are:

  • GLS: DE, AT, CH, NL, BE 

  • PNL Parcel: NL, BE

  • DPD (PS & Dataform): DE, AT, CH, NL, BE, PL 

 

If the answer to both is yes, you will find the instructions in this article. If not, please revisit “The Last Mile (delivery carrier) Matrix” article.  

 

Main queries

Is it possible to cancel an order (after dispatched)?

It’s not possible to cancel the order when it's already with the 1MH carrier, so customers need to refuse the item upon delivery. Process here.  

How to Edit/change order (add or remove items) 

It’s not possible to do it if it’s already with the carrier. The customer’s option is to accept the order and make another purchase (if it’s the case, offer help placing the order – most of the deliveries in CE are free). In case the customer asks for a cancellation, please follow the Cancellation process (depending of the stage it may include a Refuse upon Delivery).

​

Is it possible to schedule a specific delivery date? 

Not possible. If the customer is not at home to receive the product, then the carrier will inform via email that it wasn’t possible to deliver the order. If this happens, they are going to send the order to the nearest parcel shop (information is sent by email or SMS).

Address change / correction 

  • If there's no delivery label (tracking label/number), it's never possible to request the carrier to change the address.  If that's the case, kindly ask the customer to wait for the tracking number to arrive (status in OMS = “In transit”).  

  • Once / If there’s a tracking label/number available, follow this process.

​

Is it possible to request a re-delivery to the carrier? 

For 1MH carriers, no. If customer couldn't be at the address in the delivery date, then the carrier will send the order to the nearest parcel shop. 

​

What to do if customers complain that the carrier didn’t really ring the bell and contact them even if they were at the house waiting for the order? 
Frontoffice agent can open a Logistic Claim as Carrier Misbehaviour.

Does the 1MH carrier provide assembly services? Is it possible to request an assembly service after the delivery? 
No, they don’t. It's not possible.

What to do if CS paid for Express Delivery (ED) but carrier didn't fulfill it? 
This option is currently not available for 1MH carriers in Central EU.
