For Mattresses


Packed


If the Mattress is Packed: If the mattress is still packed, the return can be set via label creation or pick-up appointment.


Label creation means we are sending the label to the customer (manually) and they will post the item in the post office.


Pick up appointment means we are setting up with the carrier to pick the item up, we need a date and the hour (period of 4 hours)

Pick-up conditions: from Monday to Friday, business days


Unpacked


If the mattress is unpacked - the return will be done via pick-up by ONG.


In Mexico, reverse logistics are carried out through NGOs.


It is necessary to indicate to the client that the NGO will pick up their product and ask for the information for its collection

1 or 2 telephones where you can answer the call from the NGO (schedule to answer the call)

Contact email


This information must be register in the file Pedido de Recogidas
