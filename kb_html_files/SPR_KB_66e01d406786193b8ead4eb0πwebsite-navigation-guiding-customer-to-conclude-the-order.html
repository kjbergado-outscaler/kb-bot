In this article, you will see a step-by-step guide about how the customer purchase an Emma product, from the selection of the item until the payment confirmation.


Depending on the country, the Emma website can have different layouts and setups. Those differences are based on user experience and market strategies. Even though, the customer buying process has always followed the same order.





Please check below, how to guide a person in every step:



1. Product Category


Chose the Product Category on the menu and click on it to see the options available.







2. Add to cart


Here the customer can check the price, the materials, and more details of the products. Depending on the product, they will have to choose the size. After this, just have to click on Add to cart.






3. Go to cart


Now, 2 pop-up windows will appear: the central one suggesting other products to purchase, and the small one on the corner with the item added and the option to Go to cart and finish the order.





4. Checkout page


On the cart page, they can see the product selected, the price, and icons with the payment methods. If the customer has discount code and didn't include it yet, in this step we can check if the discount is applied and if not, just click on "Add Promo Code". To go forward, they have to click on Checkout.





4.1. Email Address

Inside the Checkout page, they can only go forward after filling up the topic before. The 1st step is to put an Email Address:






4.2. Billing Information

On the Billing Information all the spaces with a star (*) on it are mandatory to be filled. They can also choose to create an account or not, but it’s not mandatory to have one. Before clicking on “Save and continue”, they can tick if the delivery will be for the same address as the billing one. If it’s, they just have to click on the box; if not, they will be able to add another address on the next step.




4.3. Shipping Information

This will be already filled up if the billing and shipping address are the same and the customer just has to click on “Save and continue”. If it’s not, they have to include the new details of the Shipping Information and then continue.





4.4. Shipping Method

Then, on the Shipping method customers can choose their preferred delivery option based on what is available in each country.




4.5. Payments Methods

In the area of Payment methods, every country has different options. The example below refers to Portugal. In this step, depending on the option selected new fields will appear or the customer may be redirected to another page.


Please check the market-related article about payment methods here.



4.6. Confirming Payment

Finally, on the Review order the customer will be able to double-check the products selected and all the information filled in the previous steps. They will have to mandatorily accept Emma’s Terms and Conditions before finalizing the request by clicking on Pay securely here.





5. Confirmation Email


After the website part is done, the customer will receive the Confirmation Email with all the details of the request, including the Order Number.
