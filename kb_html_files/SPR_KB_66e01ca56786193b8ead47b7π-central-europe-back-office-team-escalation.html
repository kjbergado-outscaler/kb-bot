If you have a back office team available in your country, this means you have dedicated agents to support you on Order Management investigations, late cancellations, and possible payment issues, among other topics.


Ensure you follow Escalation: Jira Portal guidelines before escalating a case to the backoffice team.




Carrier Issue


As you know, depending on your market you may work with different carrier partners.


This escalation is to follow up any specific request with a carrier: creation or change of labels, carriers, 2MH (2 Man Handling) premium delivery request, etc.


In cases where delivery did not go as planned and customer would like a follow up.




Return Request

When a pickup or a drop-off label needs to be created manually, something did not go as planned with the return, you need the status of the return to be checked, a ticket will need to to be created in this Central Europe portal. This returns request is divided into the following subcategories:

  • Pick-up Request
  • Drop-off Label Request
  • Return Cancellation
  • Return Adjustment (e.g. Cst Contact/ Address Change)
  • Pick-up Rescheduling
  • Return Status Check - Customer received the item then requested a return and customer is now asking about the status of the return (Post-Delivery)
  • Return Status Check - Customer refused to receive the item then returned it and customer is now asking about the status of the return (Pre-Delivery)


Payment Issue - NO - O2C


Request to be escalated when the payment method of the order is Klara, Split or Lusopay:


Issues related to payment not captured, order status pending, or unidentified ones.


Any other related Payment / Refund request should be escalated directly to the Refunds team as per Escalation article

Barcode Stuck


If the barcode presents an error in your Order Management System. If any status is out of the happy path and the order is stuck, you should escalate it to the back office team for further investigation. (e.g. "API error").


In case of an obvious address mistake, please correct the address in Magento and do not open an escalation: house number missing or in the wrong position; please make sure there is a space between the street name and house number.


If the status is “packing” or any earlier status, simply correct the address in Magento. The order will then automatically be processed further and no escalation is required.

Please open a JIRA escalation in the below cases:


  • All Barcode statuses before "Customer Shipped".
  • There is no obvious "address mistake".
  • Status is stuck for more than 4 working days, i.e. if it is 1st of May we escalate on the 5th.


Special Case:

  • Status is "Waiting for stock" even if we are still within promised delivery date, Back Office will attempt to get an update on the stock either when it will be replinished or there is no expected replinishment date, so the customer can decide how to proceed with the order.

Make sure that the promised delivery date will be within the following 2 weeks.


Cancellation Request (Late Cancellation)


If the order has already been transmitted to the warehouse and the button is enabled, but the status of the barcode has not moved to "Shipped" yet, we can try to stop the delivery by communicating with our partners.



Incomplete delivery - NO



If there was an incomplete delivery and the order status seems as complete. The 1st level agent should create a logistic claim in Magento for incomplete delivery and create a supplement for the missing product.


WIMO Update


Order is out of expected delivery time, no barcode stuck and there is no updates/ information available related to "Where is My order": you can escalate it for further investigation.


Please note that: in case of out of expected delivery time and barcode stuck priority will always be for the Barcode Stuck.


Reason for this: when a barcode is stuck, updating the promised delivery date will have no affect on the progress of the case since the main issue here is the barcode itself not the delivery date.


If the issue was raised as WIMO, and the barcode was stuck by time, Back Office will change the topic to barcode stuck.


If the issue was raised as barcode stuck, and the promised delivery date passed while the case is being investigated, the case will remain barcode stuck.


In case the barcode stuck issue got resolved, and the promised delivery date was not updated automatically, we reach out to Team Leaders.




Priority Requests


Priority Request means the order you are escalating to the back office team should be prioritized and delivered with urgency by our logistic team.


Be aware out of stock scenarios will still pend on product/ SKU availability, but this means we will prioritize this order shipment when we have stock available.


We only prioritize:

1. Specific situations Flagged by the CE Central Europe team (e.g. specific SKU orders, or specific incidents communicated in advance).

2. Possible customer escalated scenario (dissatisfied & angry - and Expected delivery time has already passed.
