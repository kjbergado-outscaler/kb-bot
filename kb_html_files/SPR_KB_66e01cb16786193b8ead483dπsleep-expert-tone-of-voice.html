Now that you are a Sleep Expert, it is important that you voice out the right tone and help customers to perceive us on the right way.

The Sleep Expert Tone of Voice is a supplement to the existing Emma Tone of Voice. The Sleep Expert Tone of Voice refers primarily to mindset and responsibility.

As a Sleep Expert, I am the expert in all aspects of pre-sales and return deflection. As an expert, I am aware of my responsibility towards the client.

1) The customer entrusts me with 1/3 of his life. I am aware of this and I implement it with every customer and try to justify it.

2) The customer is the centre of everything I do. That is why I always look at sleep systems holistically and offer the customer complete solutions.

3) Customers contact us because they have questions. We also have questions. Open questions, closed questions, alternative questions. We listen to the client - we don't lecture them.

4) I am the expert, so I appear confident but not arogant. The customer calls us and expects to be helped. We don't have to convince him that we can do it.
