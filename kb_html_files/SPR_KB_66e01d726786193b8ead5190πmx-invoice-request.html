Introduction


Once the purchase has been made, customers can request the issuance of their invoice, please follow the following process:



Process to request invoice

  1. Reply to the customer with the template: Pedido de Factura (Solicitud de datos)
  2. Place the ticket in the status “Waiting on customer”
  3. Once the customer replies with the date required in template, reply to the customer with template: Solicitar esperar por la factura
  4. After replying to the customer, complete the following information indicated in the templante (Pedido de Factura), place the ticket on 2nd lvl and assign it to the significant person taking care of 2nd lvl task.
  5. In the case, that customer ask for the status of the invoice, please response with template: Pedido de Factura (status).
  6. 2nd lvl will send the invoice directly to the customer once is ready.
  7. Once you get the invoice, please the the template: Envío de factura
