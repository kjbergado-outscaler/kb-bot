Scope

This SOP outlines the process for managing order tracking, delivery status, cancellations, and obtaining proof of delivery (POD) using the DPD Carrier Portal.

Instructions

Accessing the DPD Portal

  1. Log in to the DPD Portal:

    • Portal URL: DPD Portal

    • Username: MARTRATZEN2

    • Password: Kindly request to your Supervisor.

  2. Navigate to the Delivery Search:

    • Enter the tracking number obtained from Magento.

Order Tracking and Status Check

Search for an Order:

  • Paste the tracking number into the Delivery Search field.

  • Review the Order

View Delivery History:

  • Click on the image in Delivery History to see a detailed timeline.

  • The most recent update will be at the top.

Managing Delivery Actions

Cancel Delivery (Return to Sender):

  • If a delivery needs to be canceled, click Return to Sender.

Obtain Proof of Delivery (POD):

  • Click on the image under Delivery History to view the POD.

Locating Consignment Numbers in Magento

Find the Consignment Number:

  • Magento displays the DPD consignment number, which is used for searching orders in the DPD portal.

Search by Consignment Number:

  • (Input the consignment number in the DPD delivery search to retrieve order details).

Common Issues & Solutions

Issue

Solution

Tracking not found

Double-check the consignment number in Magento.

Parcel status is 'Awaiting Goods'

Verify if the warehouse has dispatched the parcel to DPD.

Cancel delivery request

Use the Return to Sender option in the DPD Portal.

Proof of Delivery not available

Check if the parcel status is Delivered, then click on the image for POD.
