Summary of the product


It can also be called as “Emma 25 Flip” due to the name coming from the height of 25 cm. This product is active in Germany, where it has a test-winning seal.


The product will be marketed both as an option for the dedicated premium segment as well as an upgrade option for customers primarily interested in the baseline option (this upgrade being called “upsell”). In the German market, the product is positioned as an affordable premium.

Mattress cover


Breathable and Climate Regulating Mattress Cover 


The Emma cover consists of a light fiber that immediately releases moisture and ensures a good sleeping climate. At the same time, the cover is stretchable and can adapt well to the body.


Characteristics 


1) Top fabric:

  • 100% polyester
  • Knitted white fabric going 5 cm over the sides.
  • Completely detachable top to bottom light gray zipper between border and top panel fabric.


2) Border fabric:

  • 100% polyester
  • Dark grey fabric going 1cm into the bottom fabric
  • On one side a brand label is sewed in.


3) Bottom fabric:

  • 100% polyester
  • Knitted white fabric, the same as on the top side


4) Handles:

  • 100% Polyester
  • 4x Vertical Handles


Benefits

  • Breathable cover;
  • Elasticity. It fits perfectly to the mattress core;
  • Completely removable and washable at 40°C;
  • Cover is more sustainable compared to our previous covers. Making use of 100% polyester for the border and top fabric, which makes it easier to recycle.



Brand and wash & care label included
