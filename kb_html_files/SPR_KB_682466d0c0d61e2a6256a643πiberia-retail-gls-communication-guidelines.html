I need more details of my order

Once you have located the order, you can click on the IR button to view the status of the order in more detail:

In these details you will see all communications from the GLS system, about your order and there may even be a map showing where it is currently located, or once delivered you can view the POD.

Here you will find messages such as:

Incomplete address, Missing phone, Not answering phone, Missing packages, re-channeling, Incorrect CP, ABSENT, Facilitated solution by addressee, and others. Always pay attention to messages with the most recent date and time.

In case you see that your customer's data is missing, you can give the correct information to GLS so that they can complete the data in their system and deliver it correctly.

1. In case of missing items, you must consult with GLS to inform you if they have them or not located in their system, in case of not having them located, SCHENKER will act giving the relevant instructions or solutions, which may be a reshipment with the same tracking or ask for a supplement while confirming the missing item. DO NOT SEND SUPPLEMENTS WITHOUT PRIOR CONFIRMATION FROM SCHENKER AND GLS.

2. Estado GRABADO o con LECTURA UNICAMENTE EN ORIGEN 280

GRABADO means that GLS has not read the label with its scanners, for several reasons.

LECTURA UNICAMENTE EN ORIGEN 280, means that it was read in the origin Hub but the bundle has had no further updates.

What happened? It could have lost the label and been sent back to the warehouse as it had no information. SCHENKER then proceeds to report a reshipment with the same tracking or a supplement for the complete order.

3. RECANALIZADO: change of address or postcode, these changes must be made at least 24 hours in advance. The addressee must be the same.

4. POD: they are available for download on the extranet, in case you do not see it you can request it by email.

5. DETERIORADO: GLS provides photos of the damaged packages, it must be managed to know if it is delivered or returned to the warehouse, once this management is done, the corresponding supplement can be made. NOT FORWARDED FROM THE WAREHOUSE WITH THE SAME TRACKING.

Cases that do not meet these requirements will not be dealt with in order to avoid unnecessary mail threads.

Collection Requests

TITULAR: By default the next working day is displayed, it can be changed.
Pick-ups in the morning must be made more than 24 hours in advance, i.e. you have to leave a day in between.

Pick-ups are from Monday to Friday, no weekends or holidays.

HORARIO: minimum margin of 4 hours, between 8:00 and 19:00,

Schedule a date and time with the customer, put 9:00- 19:00 if no time is specified.

COLLECTION DATA:

Important to put mobile or email, so that GLS can contact the customer, GLS sends SMS or EMAIL, do not call before collecting.

Obs. NO RECOGER SI NO TIENE (this is an agreement with GLS to avoid collecting open products) if you want you can put other remarks. GLS MUST NOT COLLECT OPENED PRODUCTS OR PRODUCTS FOR DESTRUCTION.

Contenido: be simple enough to describe what is to be collected, avoid references such as EMAOR090190AAB, etc.

Bultos: specify total number of packages to be collected
