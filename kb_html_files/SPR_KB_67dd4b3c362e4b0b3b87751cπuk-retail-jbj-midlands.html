Context

JBJ orders are managed via truck shipments. We do not have visibility into individual orders but handle faulty issues.

Instructions

Receiving Product Claims

  • Customer complaints are received via email (Sprinklr).

  • The claim must include:

    • Customer’s name

    • Mattress type and size

    • Purchase Order (PO) number (used for truck shipment tracking)

    • Customer’s reference number

    • Delivery date (critical for verifying the 200-night trial period)

  • Ensure that all necessary details are included before proceeding.

Analyzing the Photos

  1. Request photographs from the customer showing the mattress issue.

  2. Verify that the provided images clearly depict the described fault.

    • If the images do not meet criteria, request additional photos.

  3. If the photos are valid, proceed with return/replacement approval.

You can find the claim pictures requirements and examples here and how to validate the claim here.

We are not authorized to process product claims related to the mattress cover being cut during the unboxing process using the Emma Cutter, or defects resulting from sleeping on the incorrect side of the mattress.

Collection Requests

  • Simon from JBJ contacts us via email to arrange the collection of the mattresses they have on their Warehouse. He should provide:

    • PO numbers of faulty/returned mattresses.

    • Quantity of mattresses to be collected.

  • Proceed with collection once JBJ sends confirmation.
