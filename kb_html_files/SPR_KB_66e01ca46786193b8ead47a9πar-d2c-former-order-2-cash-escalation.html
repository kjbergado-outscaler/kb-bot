Below how the Portal currently looks like and all the main topics available, continue reading for further details of each on of the topics.



Breakdown of Ticket topics:

  • Billing

    • Invoice Request: Fullfilment status is complete but the customer didn't received the Invoice.
    • Incorrect Invoice: The customer Received an Invoice but the details are incorrect and adjustment is required.

  • Refunds

    • Refund Request: Non-Magento orders only, Refund requests for discounts, compensation and partial refunds. Cancellation with Refund requests. Refund topics below:
      • Refund Initiation
      • Refund Follow-up
      • Failed Refund
      • VAT Refund
      • Lawyer Fees
      • Return and Refund
      • Cancel and Refund
    • Proof of Refund: Customer needs proof of refund for credit memo with completed status.

  • Global Payments

    • Fluent Order activation (Order status not updated 2 days after payment).
    • Shopify Order Activation.
    • Payment Concerns/Issues: Customer paid to Emma instead of a 3rd party such as Klarna or Unzer incorrectly.

  • Order Management

    • Access Request to Fluent (Supervisors only): Request for new or additional access to Fluent Commerce.
    • Cancel Without Refund: in case refund is needed the cancellation should be done under Refund Request.
    • Edit Order Request: Modifications such as Customer Name, Address and Email address
    • Bulk Orders Creation Request: Mainly for retail operations.
    • Manual Supplement Creation.
    • Order Stuck Concerns (Before Transition to Warehouse): Applicable only to the following order status, Booked Without Fulfilments, Created, Pending and Pending_Fraud_Check.

Priority or Reason for Escalation


We have a new field named "Reason for Escalation"




Usual customer Requests should fit under the P2 requests, these are known as the business as usual types of requests.


For P1 Requests proof of urgency is required


P1 – Suspicious Customer Transaction / Fraud – Customer is claiming fraud situation, proof needed such as screenshot of the interaction and claim of the customer.


P1 – Issue escalated to Emma top-level management – Make sure to provide the High Level Escalation Ticket.


P1 - Issue is affecting Emma's Reputation through Social Media – Provide a screenshot of the social media page with the customer’s comments affecting Emma reputation.


P1 - Issue is being escalated with legal threats – Provide proof showing the customer is threatening to take legal actions, mainly if related with the 14-day compliance matter.



Best Practices


  • Not all Order stucks need should be escalated to O2C jira portal, the O2C team is responsible for the order stucks related with payment issues, the moment the payment went through and the order was handed over to the warehouse if you face fulfillment orders stucks you need to open a ticket to Order Fulfillment Concerns here.
    Example:


  • When escalating a cancelation request mkae sure to open the correct ticket considering if its a request with or without Refund.


  • Make sure to complete all relevant fields to prevent tickets to be incorrectly closed.


  • Priority Levels are to be complied, make sure to provide proof when selecting level P1 of Urgency.
