1.1 Refunds for Compensation & Discount follow-up requests

  • Currently, whenever you need a refund of a specific amount for compensation, you are yet not able to perform this on Fluent Commerce.

  • For partial refunds (compensation / discount follow-up or composable products cancellation refunds: you should proceed with the manual request via our Order-2-Cash Portalto our Emma Refunds team.

​

1.2 Credit Memo

  • When a cancellation is done in Fluent commerce, automatically a credit memo is created in the system.

  • Each credit memo has a unique reference number.

  • For a return WITH REFUND, the credit memo is created only after the status of the return is updated to Complete.

Friend Referral Process (How does it work)

  1. Upon finishing the purchase process, the customer will be informed about the referral program and the benefits for both parties.

  2. The customer in this case will be considered the "Advocate" and will create a personalized code for their referral code.

​

How can the Friend use the code?

  1. By Link sharing, the Advocate can send their friends the referral link.

  2. By using the Advocate personalized code.

Friend referral is a loyalty campaign we have available to all of our customers.

"Hey, do you know Emma? I have a mattress and I have been sleeping much better after I bought it.
Have you consider buying a new one? I have a referral code that I can share with you for a discount!"

​

The program tends to motivate customers to refer Emma products to their friends and by doing so, both benefit from it. On one side, the customer who is referring wins a Bonus, on the other side, the "friend" buying a new product has a discount.

1.1 Refunds for Compensation & Discount follow-up requests

  • Currently, whenever you need a refund of a specific amount for compensation, you are yet not able to perform this on Fluent Commerce.

  • For partial refunds (compensation / discount follow-up or composable products cancellation refunds: you should proceed with the manual request via our Order-2-Cash Portalto our Emma Refunds team.

​

According to Portuguese law (DL n.º 24/2014, dated 14 February - Article 12 - image below), Emma needs to make the refund within 14 days of the customer notifying us of his intention to withdraw from the purchase contract.

​

These 14 days are NOT working days, so weekends and bank holidays will be counted as well.

​

Once a customer has completed the return of their product, refund will be processed by Refunds Team in Magento within 3-5 business days; however, there are instances where a refund is still pending after 5 business days, thus, having refund follow-up tickets from the customer.

​

If the refund status of the item returned is already tagged as "Refunded", this means that the refund was already processed and the amount should be received by the customer within 14 business days (except when using a credit card which takes 2-3 weeks).

​

If refund is not received within 14 days after processed in Magento, escalate the ticket to Refunds Emma. One of the possible reason why the refund is not yet processed is because the invoice is missing for the returned item. If the invoice is not available in Magento, manual creation of invoice is needed. Follow this article on the steps to create an invoice in Magento - Manual Invoice Creation - Magento.

1.3 ARD2C JIRA Portal

When opening a Jira ticket for a Refund request at the AR D2C Portal, one key field that needs to be properly inserted is the refund amount.

Every time a refund is applicable and the agent needs to request it through the ARD2C Jira (manual refund), they must send the following macro to the customer:

​

Hi [Customer's first name],

​

Thank you for your patience.

​

We have now initiated your refund request which is currently being processed by our internal team. If the account details and personal information we have on file are complete and correct, the refund will be processed accordingly. In this case, you can expect to receive it within 14 working days.

​

However, if there is any missing or incomplete personal information in our system, our Refunds team will contact you via email, and ask you to complete a form providing all the necessary details.

​

Once again, we do appreciate your patience and understanding throughout this process. If you have any questions in the meantime, please feel free to contact us.

​

Kind regards,

[Agent's first name]

  • Numerical Value only regardless of currency : Sample 220.23 / 1,600.21 / .10 for Centavos 

  • Use "," to indicate Hundreds, Thousands value  

  • Use "." to indicate Centavos  

  • No words, no percentage in Amount Fields  

​

​

ARD2C monitors Refunds processing and for failed refunds, they will either:

  1. Reprocess it ;

    or

  2. Send the Outbound Bank Details Form, requesting customer’s IBAN to process the refund manually.

    ​

ARD2C will then leave a comment in FluentCommerce with the Refund’s details:

  • Date;

  • Amount refunded and IBAN used for it. (initial 4 and last 4 digits of the IBAN, due to GDPR).
