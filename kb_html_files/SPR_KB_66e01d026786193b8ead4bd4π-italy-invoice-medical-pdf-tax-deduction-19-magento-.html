When is it needed?


Whenever a customer purchase one of the Medical device products on our website. At the moment, these products are: EMMA ORIGINAL AND MEMORY FOAM PILLOW.



How do we provide it?


When the order is delivered, the Medical PDF will be automatically available on Magento.



By clicking on the Medical PDF button, you will be able to download a copy and send it to the customer via email.


Together with the Medical PDF, you will also have to send the Invoice. This should arrive automatically to the customer; in case it didn't, you can download it from Magento by going to Order View > Invoices.



Click on the Invoice # and then on the blue Print button. You can then send the downloaded invoice to the customer, together with the Medical PDF.
