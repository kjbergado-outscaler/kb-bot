Order Management

Order creation

  • Shopify is used as a tool to make the sales at the shop floor.

  • We have an automatic connection that uploads orders from Shopify in Magento twice a week (either Monday or Tuesday & Thursday or Friday). Only orders marked as paid and with all the customer information will be uploaded in Magento. ​

  • The order is transmitted to the warehouse (if we have stock).

  • Warehouse prepares the order.

  • Carrier picks up the order.

  • Carrier contacts customer for delivery.

  • Order delivered to end customer.

Status Description

 

Magento Barcode Status

Description

Waiting for Stock

Barcode is still in Emma System, but specific item is out of stock. Waiting inventory to fulfill the order.

Packing

Barcode is still in Emma hands, not yet submitted to the warehouse. Shipping address or dispatch date can still be changed in Magento.

Prepared for Export

The barcode information is ready to be sent to its assigned warehouse

Order Submitted to warehouse

The barcode has been packed and the warehouse has been booked. No changes can be made to order in Magento.

Order Received by the warehouse

The barcode has now been received by the warehouse and its system

Waiting for delivery date

Barcode's delivery date has been set to a later time by customer and the system is waiting for this date to process the order to the warehouse

Parcel data submitted/Ready for shipping/Ready for dispatch/Customer shipped

Barcode is ready in the warehouse to dispatch; data has been submitted to the carrier

Arrived at sorting center/out for delivery/etc.

Barcode is at the carrier's hand

Delivered

Barcode has been marked as completed and delivered to the customer

Deleted

When a return is completed, the barcode is deleted. Status referred.

Offline Sales Portfolio

Each market has its own portfolio, and each store displays a different set of products. This means that all stores (per market) have the same products available for purchase, but customers cannot try all those products on the shopfloor (dependent on the size of each store). ​

Emma Stores portfolio is a mix of e-commerce products (available on the Emma website) and offline exclusive products.

​

​

​

The sales team updates the portfolio on Airtable. There, you are able to verify the products you can sell in the stores.
