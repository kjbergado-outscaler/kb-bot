USPs

Sustainable

FSC wood certificate & Produced in EU ( France)

Increases durability

Thanks to proper ventilation and the extra support it provides, the slatted frame helps extend the lifetime of your mattress. It will keep your mattress in place and stop it from sagging so you can rest well for years to come.

Optimal support

Thanks to the 3 comfort zones, the Emma Sommier optimizes the support of an Emma mattress. It improves the proper alignment of your spine throughout the night.

Air circulation

The Emma Sommier optimizes air circulation and completely removes moisture. You enjoy an ideal freshness in your sleeping space throughout the year.

Easy and quick to assemble

The Emma Sommier is delivered as a kit. Very simple to assemble, the assembly can be done alone, in 20 minutes. The dimensions of the package are 162 x 26 x 12 cm, which makes it easily transportable even in difficult access.

Packaging & Shipping


The Emma Sommier will be shipped in one box, which will have the dimensions of 162 x 26 x 12 cm, only one man-handling required for all markets.

  • Boxes should have size, SKU #, and Barcode printed on the box.

  • Packages are shipped by DPD Predict. Once the package has been sent to the receiving agency, the carrier will contact you directly to schedule the delivery appointment. After the package is available and handed over to the carrier, the customers will receive an SMS or e-mail with the delivery tracking.
