Welcome to Emma's learning program!



For the next 5 days, you will be able to find below all the needed training content to prepare you to be a great Emmie!




Write down all your questions, review the articles whenever you need them, have fun during the learning journey, and be ready to make a real impact when handling our customers' interactions!


To make it simple our Modules are sub-divided as follows:




  • Learning Content: Consists of the theoretical knowledge that you will need to know, ready is mandatory!
  • Live Training Session: Presentation with your trainer to dynamically discuss some of the key subjects of the current module.
  • Knowledge Test: Short Knowledge quiz will allow you to identify your knowledge's strongest points and those that need further improvement. (Note: Tests are mandatory however the result if negative will not prevent you from proceeding with the training.)
  • Feedback Survey: We are always looking to improve the content we have available, therefore your feedback is Key, considering that we will be requesting it at the end of the module.


Always make sure you follow your trainer's indications when changing modules.
