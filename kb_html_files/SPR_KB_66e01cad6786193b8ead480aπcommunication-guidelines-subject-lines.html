Introduction & Context


In email communication, the format and appearance of the email is equally important as its content, as it will immediately convey a sense of professionalism and inspire trust, when everything is polished and aligned.


As such, the subject line will also play a big role in the perception our customers will have of our Customer Service team. It is for this reason, and in order to promote standardization that we have created the below guidelines on how to create a proper subject line for email communication with Emma customers.
