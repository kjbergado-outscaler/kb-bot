FAQs



Q. What is the difference between the Halo Memory Foam and the normal Memory foam?

The Halo-Memory foam is a more open-cell type of foam, in which exists more space between the cells, resulting in high breathability.



Q. In the image, the springs have different colors. What is the difference between them?

The dark color is to indicate the body zones, such as the shoulders and the hips. But this is just for visual information. The actual mattress doesn't have different colors.


Q. Since there is no foam around the springs, there is any protection if a customer removes the cover to wash, for example?

Yes, the entire mattress is protected by a mattress sock, that's is a thin fabric layer (you can check on the image above, with the inside layer of the mattress.



Q. How are the laying behavior and the bouncing aspect of the Hybrid V2?


The V2 is more bouncing than the current version of the Hybrid, however, the customer will not feel it or feel the springs. The laying is a medium-soft, so even though the springs are harder than a foam, the pocket springs of the V2 are higher, and the highest it is, making the mattress more adaptable.
