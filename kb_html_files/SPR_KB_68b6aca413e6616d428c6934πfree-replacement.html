Article Use Case(s)

​

​WHAT IS A FREE REPLACEMENT?

  • A Free Replacement is when a customer’s original item is swapped for another product at no extra cost for them. It's done via a tool available in Fluent Commerce.

  • It can only be used in the early post-purchase stage (before the delivery, while fulfillment is pending/waiting for stock).

  • Typical triggers: original product is phased out, out of stock (OOS) with no UDD or UDD >14 days after PDD, or customer requests a change in color/characteristics.

  • The customer’s invoice is updated with the new item, but they pay nothing extra.

​

​Conditions

  • ​Tool can only used on a pre-delivery scenario, until fulfillment status is "Pending" or "Waiting for Stock"

  • Items needs to be a standalone - not composite (beds, sofa beds). If an order has both, only the standalone items can be replaced.

  • Agents should not replace an item more than once. Escalations or special cases should be reviewed by Emma internals.

  • Refurbished products should not be replaced.

  • ​Tool should be used after or during a delivery process

  • Agent's shouldn't place a supplement more than one time. If there's an error, it should be reported for Emma internals

Global Process and Workflow

​Global Process and Workflow / Market specifics: Is your market present? Yes: Click your Market tab, ignore Global Process. No: refer to the Global Process and Workflow.

The tool can be used in two different scenarios:

  1. Reactive: customer contacts Emma with an issue

  2. Proactive: mass/ad-hoc communication offering a replacement is sent to customers by Emma internals (orders with phased-out items).

    ​

​Customer contacts us with an issue. After analyzing, agent asses that it's one of the following 3 scenarios:

1. Order is blocked as some or an item is Out of Stock (OOS) and there is either no UDD or the UDD is more than 14 business days later than the Promised Delivery Date (PDD);

2. Order is blocked as an Item is phased out OR

3. Customer changed their mind about the product color (wrong color, the same RRP cost)

​

Key Differences from Reactive Flow

  • SKU is predefined by Performance Manager.

  • Only one upgrade option is offered.

  • No stock/replacement file checks needed.

  • No follow-up if no reply in 5 days.

​

Process Completion

  • Replacement is processed in the system, and the customer is informed of the outcome and the PDD. Agent leaves a NOTE in Fluent Commerce and Sprinklr explaining which item was replaced and when.

  • If no suitable replacement is available, or the customer declines, alternative resolutions (cancellation, waiting, or keeping the original item) are offered.
