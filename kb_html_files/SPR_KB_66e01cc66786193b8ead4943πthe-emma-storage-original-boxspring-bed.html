The Emma Boxspring Bed = feels like a holiday every day


Our designers had one goal: that you wake up as refreshed and relaxed as if you were on holiday - every day.

With the Emma Boxspring Bed, the luxurious comfort of hotels comes right into your bedroom. The padded headboard invites you to sink right into it, and our designed mattress with 7-zone pocket springs as well as a base of Bonell springs guarantees  outstanding ergonomics.

The extra height of the base makes it easy to get into bed and…and well…also easy to stay in it! ;)



Great Ergonomics
Our designers are known to create award-winning products with great ergonomics! Bonell springs in the base coupled with 7-zone pocket springs in the mattress are the perfect combination for dynamic adaptation, all night long. Adding to that our HRX foam and Helix Cut technology for the great support and pressure relief.
Ultimate quality &
timeless design
Our Boxspring Bed is created with thoughtfully selected materials. It convinces with a sturdy birch oak wooden base and feet, and the most durable furniture fabrics – scrub and light resistant. All OEKOTex100 certified. The elegant overhead of the headboard and yet timeless design create a supreme look in your bedroom.
Luxurious comfort
The Emma Boxspring Bed convinces with extra thick padding on the headboard and luxurious upholstering. This invites you to sink right into it. Further, the combination of springs and the open cell technology in our foams ensure great breathability and a great sleeping climate. And our Emma topper is the cherry on top to add the extra portion of comfort

Product specifications


The box spring bed consists of two-bed bases (right and left) that would have upholstered fabric on the sides and an anti-slip fabric on top. Under it will have two storage boxes (right and left).


Base Specifications

Storage Base part (lower)

Chipboard upholstered base with storage space with a butterfly opening construction

Lifting mechanism - 750N

Fabric - 100% PES, Sawana (from Davis, OEKO-TEX certified)

Legs - Plastic steps - 2.5 cm (12 pcs)

Height without legs - 21 cm

Height with legs - 23,5 cm


Bonnell Base part (upper)

Core - Bonnell spring unit, wire diameter 2,0 mm, 4 turns, height 7 cm

Frame - pinewood base frame

Felt - Hard felt 600 gsm x 2 layers

Layer - PU foam - 2 cm, Density 25 kg/m3, Hardness 4,2 kPa

Border - PU foam - 7 cm, Density 25 kg/m3, Hardness 4,2 kPa

Fabric - 100% PES, Sawana (from Davis, OEKO-TEX certified)

Top fabric - Anti-slip

Bottom fabric - Black non-woven fabric

Height - 13 cm

Total height of base - 36,5 cm


Headboard

Thickness - 10 cm

Height - 115 cm

Width - 5 cm overhang on each side

Core - Pine wood frame

Fabric - 100% PES, Sawana (from Davis, OEKO-TEX certified)

Wadding - xx gsm

Back - black nonwoven fabric

Design - plain and tufted

Tufted has PU foam 1,5 cm, 25 kg/m3


Mattress cover

The mattress cover will have anti-slip on the bottom side, woven fabric on the sides matching the upholstery, and a removable washable knitted fabric on top with a 360 zipper. The care label will also be attached to it.


Fabric - Knitted 100% PES, wadding 200 gr/m2

Border fabric- woven 100% PES

Bottom fabric - Anti-slip, 100% PES

Spare part process


In case of a quality issue, customer service team will contact Emma Quality Team to send the spare parts directly to our customer.


What’s considered as spare parts:


  1. Plastic feet
  2. Headboard sliders
  3. Middle connector


Each component is packaged in a separate plastic pouch with a label on it specifying the name
