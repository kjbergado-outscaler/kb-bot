Summary of product


The Emma Sofa Bed comes to the market to offer the perfect combination of sleeping and sitting comfort in one. This product delivers a real mattress system in a sofa that provides greater guest accommodation, also considering space purposes for the daily routine. It is a 3-seater sofa bed that can be converted into two single 80x200cm beds or a single 160x200cm bed. It comes in two different fabrics and in two different colors.

USPs (Unic Selling Points)


Comfort
Real mattress system, optimized seating, and sleeping comfort thanks to the two sides of the Emma mattresses. Also includes 5 pillows included to create a soft cocoon.
Modern design
Compact size to fit small spaces and have multiple configurations possible: possibility of attaching the bed bases together (makes a double bed 160x200cm) or the possibility of separating the bed frames (makes 2 single beds 80x200cm OR 1 single bed + 1 sofa).
Anti-stain fabrics
Good quality with strong fabrics and that are easy to clean.
Easy opening system
Quick and easy to assemble: 10 seconds to switch from sitting to sleeping position.



Product information


Product specifications


Mattress: The Sofa Bed includes 2 Emma Mattress (80x200cm).


  • Number of mattresses per sofa bed: 2

  • Type of mattress: 2-sided, flippable mattress

  • Core height: 15 cm (each layer height is 7.5cm)

  • Height with cover: 18cm

  • Weight: 5 kg per mattress

  • Covers (included in the sofa box): Top mattress cover: double-sided with sitting and sleeping side // Bottom mattress cover: double-sided with both sleeping sides.

  • Firmness: Around 6.5 on the soft side (ideal for sleeping position) // 7.5 on the hard side (ideal for sitting position).

  • Weight support: The specific information about the support that the Sofa Bed can take is still to be established. Regarding only the mattress, each one can support between 80-100kg.









Sofa Bed:


  • Sitting surface: 3 seater sofa - 80x200cm

  • Single size bed surface: 160x200cm (or 2* 80x200cm)

  • Number of bed bases per sofa bed: 2

  • Material: metal frame with spring birch slats

  • Siting/sleeping height: 55cm

  • Overall height: 90cm

  • Overall width: 222cm

  • Depth: 92cm

  • Weight: 60kg

  • Pillows: 3 big pillows and 2 small ones, all made of 100% polyester fiber.

  • Slatted frame: Metal and wood. Contains 14 slats on the sofa part and 13 slats on the drawer bed.

  • Covers: The pillows and the back-rest comes with covers that are removable and can be washed. Only the arm-rest cannot be removable.

FAQ's


Q. Is the Sofa Bed fabric pet resistance?


The fabric options that we have for the sofa Bed have Oeko-Tex certification and they are anti-stain and with high abrasion resistance, tested and approved to resist intensive circumstances.



Q. Does the Sofa have removable covers? Can we choose another color?

Our product was launched with the objective to be more than just a regular sofa bed, we want to guarantee the comfort of our customers in both versions (sofa and sleep positions) and that’s why we have on it an Emma quality level mattress, instead of a just sofa sitting that is the standard option for this kind of product. So, that is our initial focus and that’s why we only launch in 2 colors (grey and beige), but it’s really good to have the feedback that is interesting having more colors.



Q. Why don’t you have other sizes available?


Emma developed this product with the goal of optimizing comfort, no matter the version the customer is using (sitting or sleeping position). For this, the Sofa Bed is composed of Emma quality level mattresses on the size of 80cmx200cm. And the dimension of the entire sofa was designed to perfectly accommodate this mattress.



Q. Can I handle the assembly of it myself alone?


Yes, definitely. We develop the product in a way that is simple and possible to assemble by a person alone, without extra help. The idea is to assemble it like a “Lego”, just connecting the parts one in the other, and it’s not even necessary to use any tools for it. On the Emma website, we have available a video of just one person assembling it alone.



Q. If I’m using a mattress protector or a topper on the mattress the under the bed, it will fill inside when I close it to put it back on the sofa version?

Our goal is to have the modern sofa design combined with the comfort of all Emma mattress products. So the Sofa Bed is developed to have a compact size to fit small spaces and allow multiple configurations. A thin mattress protector or sheets will still fit, but not a topper. Nevertheless, the under-bed was designed specifically to accommodate only the Emma mattresses which is the size of 80cmX200cm and 18cm in height.
