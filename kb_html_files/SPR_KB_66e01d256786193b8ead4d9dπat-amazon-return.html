EMMA


Return mattresses packed


We offer two options for the packed mattresses:


Collection by GLS

  1. The customer should give us a suitable pick-up date between MON-FRI (ATTENTION! We need at least 2 working days in advance to be able to book it).
  2. The customer should contact you again after collection so that we can apply for a refund, since the refund is not carried out automatically.
  3. The shipment number of the return is stored in Magento and we can check the collection at www.glspakete.de/sendungs Tracking.
  4. The refund will be made in 5-7 business days.


Independent shippment by GLS/DPD

  1. We record the return in Magento.
  2. The customer does not get the label automatically - we have to forward it to the 2nd level so that they can create the label.
  3. Once the label has been created, a message will be sent via email to the agent who processed the ticket.
  4. The agent must send the label to the customer as an attachment.
  5. The customer should report as soon as his Emma has been handed over to the parcel shop - we will then check the return at www.glspakete.de/sendungs Tracking and collect the return.
  6. The refund will be made in 5-7 business days.

We always prefer the GLS label, although in exceptional cases a DPD label can also be requested if the customer wishes so.
