Summary of Product


This pillow is the Version 2 of the AirGrid Foam Pillow - Purple. The old version is still being sold in some countries, but eventually, it will be entirely replaced by the new Blue version.


Difference from the previous version: In terms of benefits and qualities there are no differences. The principal changes are in the color and the shape of the pillow, that now has the same format as the remaining foam pillow portfolio.

USPs


  • Advanced Temperature Regulation:

    • Fits every season -> temperature regulation. It stays cool in hot conditions and acts as an insulator in cold temperatures to improve getting a restful sleep phase and having better restorative sleep.

  • Adjustable Height:

    • There are three removable layers of foam inside, you could adjust the height and firmness of the pillow by taking it out or put it back in, as well as opening each of them to adjust the level of firmness.

    • To optimize and keep correct cervical alignment as well as, a comfortable support for the head and neck:

      • Side Sleepers: It is recommended to use the 3 layers

      • Back Sleepers: It is recommended to use the 2 layers

      • Stomach/Belly Sleepers: It is recommended to use only 1 layer, depending on individual lying preference.
