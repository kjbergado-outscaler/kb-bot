1. Reasons to contact a carrier

Customer Service can contact carriers for the following reasons

  • Edit the details of a shipment (customer address or telephone number)

  • Shipment blocked at the carrier hub or warehouse for more the 3 business days

  • Additional details on a shipment that has been partially delivery

  • Logistic claim

​

2. Identify the correct carrier

To make sure you're contacting the correct partner please make sure to:

  • Look for the order on Fluent

  • Click on the fulfillment tab

  • Under carrier you'll find the name of the partner, while Tracking Label is the tracking reference
