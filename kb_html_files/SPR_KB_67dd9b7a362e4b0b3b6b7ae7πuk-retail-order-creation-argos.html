Order Processing

  • Orders are automatically imported into Fluent Commerce from the Argos Portal.

  • Navigate to the "Orders" tab to view new and processing orders.

  • Check order status and expected delivery dates.

  • Tracking numbers are updated automatically unless manual intervention is required.

  • If an order has multiple items, confirm if tracking updates are accurate; if not, update manually.

  • Monitor stock levels and identify backorders to update estimated delivery timelines.

Manual Order Processing

Updating Tracking Information

This procedure applies to all orders that require manual tracking updates due to system limitations, such as multiple-item orders or incomplete tracking status updates.

Make sure to check and do this procedure daily to avoid delayed or missing payments from Argos due to incomplete delivery records.

Identifying Orders for Manual Tracking Update

  • Review the tracking file and system-generated reports daily.

  • Identify orders where tracking has not been updated automatically.

  • Prioritize orders that are past the promised delivery date or have incomplete tracking data.

  • Go to the "Orders" tab and filter orders with missing tracking details.

  • Click on the specific order requiring an update.

Checking Tracking Status

  • Access the DB Schenker Portal;

  • Select Order → View Outbound Order;

  • Search for the customer through the BU Key;

  • There, you’ll be able to identify the customer’s order status and tracking information.

​

​

​

Updating Tracking Information (Argos Portal)

  • Fluent Commerce automatically updates tracking for single orders.

  • Orders with multiple items may require manual tracking updates:

    • Export order data.

    • Update tracking information manually if needed.

    • Import updated data back into the system.

​

​

  • Open the order;

  • Select "Action" and input:

    • Carrier’s name (e.g., DPD, UPS).

    • Tracking number.

    • Dispatch date.

  • Roll the page down and click "Save Changes" to update the portal.

  • Check the tracking update in the portal to confirm the changes reflect correctly.

  • Review tracking updates daily to ensure order status accuracy.

Handling Orders with Out of Stock or Delayed Replenishments

  • If an order is out of stock or the replenishment is delayed, update the expected delivery date based on the latest stock replenishment timeline.

  • Notify the key account manager and support teams of the delay.

  • Move the order to Back Orders with a new agreed delivery date.

  • Inform Argos to manage expectations and provide updated delivery timelines.

  • Monitor stock levels and make sure to update the tracking status once inventory is replenished.

Back Orders

Updating the order status to Back Order ensures that customers are informed about stock shortages and expected fulfillment timelines. These updates help manage expectations and reduce customer escalations.

  1. Open the order.

  2. Select Actions → Back Order.

  3. Choose Reason → Out of Stock.

  4. Enter the estimated delivery date based on stock availability.

  5. Ensure the delivery method remains Direct to Customer.

  6. Click Submit.

  • Criteria for setting the date:

    • Check the replenishment date from the Stock Report.

    • Make sure to add a 7-day buffer from the expected replenishment date.

Handling Errors in Zenbridge

  • Access Zenbridge to monitor EDI order errors.

  • Review error logs and identify orders that failed to upload.

  • If a retry option is available, click "Retry Upload."

  • If an error is due to incorrect customer information, manually input and upload the order.

Searching for Errors

  • Navigate the “Transactions” tab → Filter by Partner and select Argos;

  • Look for errors in the EDI Status column;

  • Click in the file with error.
