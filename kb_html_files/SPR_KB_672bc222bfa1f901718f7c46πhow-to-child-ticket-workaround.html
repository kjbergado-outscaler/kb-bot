Introduction

In this article, you can find the temporary solution for when you need to connect one case with the other, what we normally refer to as "child-ticket". This feature is not yet deployed on Sprinklr, so for the moment we need to handle it as explained below.

When to do this?

This is for when you have the customer's case open and need to send an email to a third party (carriers, charities, etc.), but the communication needs to be related to the customer's case. Or for when APAC agents need to contact the customer after receiving a return request from the Typeform tickets.

In situations like:

- Communication with Carriers;

- Communication with Warehouses;

- Communication with Charities;

- Handling Typeform/ Returns in APAC (check at the end of the article how the cases will look like).

How to proceed?

Step 1: Go to the on the button on the left-side menu and click on Create an Email case:

Step 2: Insert the details to be able to create the Email Case.

Step 3: After that, a case will be automatically created and a profile as well. And you can write and send the Email to the third party from the contact box as normal.

Step 4: Open again the case from here the request originally came from, and leave a note on "Case collaboration" indicating the actions done and the email sent in the other case number.
