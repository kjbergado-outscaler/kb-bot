The First Step


In case you still don't have access to the reports, please request it via this email: ce.lkm@emma-sleep.com

Please be mindful and analyze if a reporting account is really needed.

In case you already have the reporting account, just follow the steps below.



How to retrieve the data?


1 & 2. Go to the Emma Learning Hub backend. You will have a special reports account that will give you access to it:


3. Go to the reports tab


4. Go to Report Favorites

Relevant Reports:

  • Course participants
  • Agent course satisfaction
  • Agent score on the knowledge test
  • Agent Average Learning Time

5 & 6. Once you click on one of those reports, you will have to filter the desired data. First, you will select the course/knowledge test/feedback survey and then, under OU, you should put your contact center code.



Please contact your Emma Market Manager in order to know your contact center code.

From where to retrieve the data?


  • For option "Client" - always select External Learners
  • For option "OU" - Select you contact center code
  • For option "Course Activation" - Select the desired course here:
