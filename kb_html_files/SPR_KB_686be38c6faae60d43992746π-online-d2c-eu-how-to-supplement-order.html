Introduction

In this article, you will check the cases where a Supplement Order can be created and also how to create it in the Fluent Commerce tool.

Supplement Order: General Information

What is a Supplement Order?

Every time an item should be added to an order with no cost (zero value) to the customer, a Supplement Order should be created and added to the original one. This action on the system will be triggered in specific scenarios in which we need to send an additional item to the customer associated with his original request.

When to create a Supplement Order?

In the scenarios that the customer is entitled to have a Replacement, or when it's a case for an Add-on.

​

  • For reasons Replacement, we consider:

    • Logistic claim for wrong product sent (from Emma's side);

    • ​Logistic claim for lost/stolen;

    • Logistic claim for Carrier Misbehaviour (if there's a lack of consistency in the information provided by carriers and the customer isn't the one to blame for the fact that the order was Returned to Sender);

    • Product claim (any).

Examples: Customer ordered a 140x200cm mattress but received a 160x200cm; Or if the product if the customer ordered a bed and didn't receive all the parcels for the assembly; Or if the customer gets a product that has a broken foam or a dimension deviation; Or if the customer states that the carrier didn't appear at their house and product was Returned to Sender (please review on the tracking page if the customer was informed about the delivery attempts. if it was, then no supplement should be done, if it wasn't, then we're ok to supplement).

​

  • For reasons Add-on, we consider: the products are sent to the customer as an added value to the order or original product.

​

Examples: Sending a comfort layer on return deflection cases.

​

Supplement Reasons​

When creating the Supplement, ensure you always select the correct reason:

LC - Carrier Incident

Use this when the issue is clearly attributable to the carrier.
Examples include:

  • The parcel was returned to sender without reaching the customer.

  • There was a failed delivery attempt due to an address error not caused by us.

    The package was delayed beyond the acceptable timeframe and was never delivered.

LC - Damaged Parcel

Select this when the parcel arrives with visible damage
Examples include:

  • Torn or crushed outer packaging.

  • Water-damaged boxes.

  • Signs of mishandling (e.g., punctures, dents, or excessive tape from rewrapping).

  • Visible damage that indicated that the product may have been compromised in transit.

LC - Lost / Stolen / Incomplete

Choose this when the parcel is missing, tampered with, or arrived with missing contents.
Examples include:

  • The customer reports non-receipt, and tracking confirms delivery to the wrong address or an unresolved issue.

  • There is no update in the tracking for over 10 calendar days, and item is still not delivered.

  • A package arrives partially opened or with missing items.

LC - Wrong Item

Select this reason when the customer receives the wrong product due to an error by the carrier, or our warehouse.

Examples include:

  • The item delivered has a different SKU, size, color, or model than what was ordered.

  • Indication that the carrier may have switched or misdelivered the parcel during transit.

​

Product Claim

Select this when the issue is related to product quality or a manufacturing defect.
Examples include:

  • Malfunctioning items out of the box.

  • Faulty or defective components.

  • The product does not match the description or images.

  • Missing items with no damage signs in the outterbox

  • Wrong item received with no evedencice that this was caused by the carrier

    ​

Return Process

Use this for standard return-related supplements.
Examples include:

  • Plastic bags when required by process

  • Comfort layers when included in the deflection actions

Exceptions (Approval Required)

Use this reason when a supplement has already been sent, but a second is required due to a special or complex case.

In such situations, escalate to a Senior Agent for approval.Senior Agents must add a clear Fluent note documenting the approval or rejection, along with the reason.

If the supplement was initially created by a Senior Agent, Supervisor approval is required.

Only in exceptional cases,already escalated to Supervisors,should this be escalated internally.

Examples include:

  • The first supplement was received incorrectly or incomplete or damaged.

  • Supplement is taking too long to arrive.

  • Additional items are needed to fully resolve the issue.

​

When NOT to create a Supplement Order?

We have certain scenarios in which we SHOULD NEVER create a Supplement Order. Those are the following:

  • If you previously created a supplement that arrived in a condition requiring replacement, please return the entire item for a refund

    • If the customer is upset and unwilling to return the full item for a refund, please escalate the case to a Senior Agent to request approval for providing an additional supplement, ensure to use the correct reasons for the supplement reason "Exceptions (approval required)"

      • @Senior Agents: Ensure you leave a comment in Fluent documenting your approval or rejection of the additional supplement.

  • If the customer does not like his SKU or wants to return it during the trial period (exchange the color of the product, for example);

  • If the customer ordered the wrong SKU (due to a fault of his own);

  • If the customer does not like an SKU that is part of a bundle;

  • Phase out SKUs

  • If the customer bought a “limited stock” SKU that is no longer available.

  • If the customer clearly states that there was damage caused by them (e.g., the mattress was stained by a spill, torn during handling, or damaged by a pet), please make a note of it and remmeber in such cases, the customer will not be eligible for a return during their trial period


For those cases above, the correct process is: the original product needs to be returned and the customer refunded; and then the customer can place a new order in the webshop. If SKU being sent is incorrect, please inform your supervisor and our internal team accordingly.

If a phased SKU qualifies for a valid claim and the customer chooses an upgrade instead of a return/refund, we’ll supply the nearest equivalent model. Please note that the standard trial-period return right will not apply to this replacement, and it cannot be returned simply because it does not meet personal expectations. This policy is also detailed in our Terms & Conditions.

​

If a full supplement order is to be sent to the customer, ensure the return is only initiated after the supplement has been successfully delivered (request customer to contact back once suplement is received). This prevents the customer from being without the product while awaiting the replacement.

IMPORTANT NOTE (UK & EI):
Do not send a supplement before confirming pickup. In exceptional cases where this is necessary, please escalate the issue internally before proceeding.


In addition to that, please also NEVER create Supplement Orders to unblock orders (any scenario in the order is not on the normal flow). The correct process is: to escalate via JIRA to:

  • Order Management team: AR D2C Portal (formerly Order-to-Cash) - Raise a request - Jira Service Management for the status covered on the "Order Stuck Concerns" choice, or;

  • Back-Office team (if your market has a back-office team).

  • Any other status, inform customer of the expected lead time or offer cancellation and refund.

  • If item is phased out or there's no stock inform the customer that the item is currently unavailable. The options for the customer are:

    • Wait for when there's stock so we can supplement (you can check for the lead time in the website for a "replenishment date")

    • Return product according to the T&Cs

    • If it's a product claim and customer decides to keep it, we can offer a compensation according to the Compensation Matrix.

​

  • If there's stock: once you gathered the information required, go to the original order of the customer

Supplement Order Number logic

The Supplement order number is based on the original order number. It will always start with the original order number, followed by a dash (-), and finally a new Supplement number. Supplement Order for Magento Orders



Once Fluent Commerce is implemented, we do not ship orders placed in Magento anymore. This means: that in case you receive a customer request that the order was placed in Magento, but now you need to make a Supplement, the supplement CANNOT be opened in Magento. The new order needs to be created in Fluent Commerce, and for it, you must open a JIRA ticket at the Order-2-Cash Portal.



How to handle:


1. Submit the JIRA ticket:
Provide all information in the ticket form and submit it.


2. Leave a note in Magento related order registering the request has been submitted to Fluent Commerce team.


3. Manage customer expectation. Customer will receive a confirmation email as soon as the order is placed by the team.


In case you need visibility of the status of the supplement order, you can always:


i.. Search in fluent commerce (use customer email or any available data you may have)

ii. Reach out your team lead


IMPORTANT:

The Order Management Team will handle the request within 5 business days. Please do not open a new request to the Back-Office team. If the timeframe has passed and you didn't get a reply, please reach out to your supervisor so the issue can be escalated internally.
