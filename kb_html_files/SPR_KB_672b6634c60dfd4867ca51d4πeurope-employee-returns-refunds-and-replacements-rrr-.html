Employee Orders and Return Requests

Employees can also become customers if they choose to order products from the company. Here are some reminders on employee return, refunds, and replacement (RRR) for Europe Markets.

  • Products ordered by employees are applicable for the 100-Nights Trial (or the applicable policy in the market).

  • Products ordered by employees are applicable for the 10-Year Warranty (or the applicable policy in the market).

How to handle tickets involving Employee RRR Requests

Employee RRR Requests are considered high-priority tickets. Employees are instructed to send an email to the CS Team and then inform the Performance Manager of the Order Number. Once the Performance Manager gives the Freshdesk(or Sprinklr) Ticket Number associated with this request to the CS Team, they should immediately prioritize the resolution of this ticket.


Check out the steps in handling Employee RRR requests below:

Note: Warranty and Trial period times are based per market and the same for Employees and Customers.


STEP 1. Is the employee return/refund/replacement request valid?
If YES, proceed to STEP 2.
If NO, inform the employee customer that the request for return/refund/replacement is not valid.
STEP 2. Set-up a collection for the product return. Refer to the process of your market on how return collections are handled.
(note: If applicable, proceed with the supplement in Claim cases. Skip next steps if Refund is not needed.)
​
STEP 3. For refund, Access the AR D2C Portal: Refund Initiation - Order-2-Cash Portal - Jira Service Management (atlassian.net). Make sure that we mention the Internal Order and the need for "Employee Savings Adjustments".
​
STEP 4. Use the following format for the Summary field: "[Market] Employee RRR - Date Requested". Indicate the ticket number (or Case number in Sprinklr) in the Freshdesk Ticket # field.
​
STEP 5. Fill out the other necessary information on the Jira ticket.
​
STEP 6. Submit the Jira ticket by clicking Send. Close the Freshdesk ticket (Or Sprinklr Case) only when the Jira ticket is resolved and closed.
