What is Pagolight


Pagolight serves as one of the installment payment methods tailored for Italy. Within the portal, you can access a comprehensive view of completed orders, including details such as the order amount, the products involved, and the installment count. Furthermore, the platform offers a streamlined refund process, allowing you to initiate total or partial refunds with ease, requiring only a few clicks.



General Information


We will solely provide support for customer inquiries related to cancellations and returns, whether total or partial.


For all other Pagolight-related questions, customers will need to redirect themselves to dedicated support teams, which are organized as follows:


Online support: Through a chat available on every screen of the purchasing process. Currently, the response is not real-time, but requests should be handled within the hour. The chat operates from Monday to Friday, from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.


Post-sale support: If a customer has completed their request and requires assistance regarding their account, installment plan, etc., they can call the number: 0248244307 and/or send an email to: info@pagolight.it.

How to make a refund


Whenever a customer initiates a return for a refund, we just have to follow the process for Fluent. In fact, the refund team, whenever the item is triggered as returned in Fluent, will process the refund accordingly (if the option "with refund" was chosen).
