USP


Why pocket-springs at the bottom?

Pocket springs are individual springs and thus adapt perfectly to the shape of your body. They provide extra point flexibility compared to Bonell springs. The daring silhouette that characterizes the Signature Boxspring bed is thanks to the narrow bottom with pocket springs. We chose pocket springs to offer you luxurious and superior comfort.

Made for you!

We've done a lot of research to bring you the best design: a box spring bed that is sleek, stylish, and modern with a floating headboard and elegant legs. You can design the box spring bed of your dreams thanks to our two types of headboards, two types of legs, and four different colors. It is made to order according to your specifications and your taste!

A luxurious experience

For the Emma Signature Boxspring bed, we chose ultra-soft fabrics that cover the entire bed. It was designed to give you plenty of space under the bed, draw a bold silhouette and provide easy access. Our soft headboards are ideal for resting when you read in bed or for a delicious breakfast in bed. Emma has thought of everything so that comfort doesn't stop when you wake up, but continues as the Signature Boxspring bed becomes part of your everyday life.

Ready for sleep?

In addition, the Emma Signature Boxspring bed is delivered and assembled in your room by our partners. The suppliers also take care of the residual packaging: once the bed has been assembled, all you have to do is lie down in it!



Product Specification


  • The box spring bed consists of two-bed bases (right and left) that would have upholstered fabric on the sides and an anti-slip fabric on top. It is made of a pinewood frame. The design of the bed base is sleek and light. Under suspension is a 9 cm Pocket spring with comfort foam upholstery. The bottommost part has slats.



  • ‘Bloq’ from Textaafoam which is a water repellant. It uses micro chenille yarns for softness and fullness laminated with heavy fleece backing. Washable at also 30 degrees.


- Top knitted fabric: 60% Polyester, 40% Lyocell

- Side woven fabric/border: 85% Polyester, 15% Nylon

- Bottom knitted fabric 100% PES (270gsm)

  • The headboard frames are made of fir wood (frame thickness 2.5 cm), covered with foam on both sides and fabric covering on the outside. The headboard has a height of 100 cm and a thickness of 5 cm. The tufting is either like a pillow or has vertical stitchings.

  • The bed contains 4 visible feet in total and 2 supporting feet on the inside. The two types of feet include angled wooden feet and corner metal feet both of height of 20 cm.

  • The mattress cover will have anti-slip on the bottom side, upholstered fabric on the sides, and a removable washable knit material on top with a 4-sided zipper. The care label will also be attached to it.

Assembly & Disassembly


  1. The assembly of the Boxspring bed is easy and takes only 10 minutes to do it. The website also offers an assembly service at a cost as an add-on.

  2. The assembly instructions are available as an attachment to this article.

  • Align the right and left bed bases in such a way that the sliders are on the same side.

  • The 2 visible feet should be added to the left corners for the left bed base and 2 visible feet should be added to the right corners for the right bed base.

  • Add the support feet to the center of the bed bases.

  • Use the velcro between the two-bed bases to join them together.

  • Slide the headboard into the sliders of the bed bases until the headboard is fully seated inside.

  • Open the 4-sided zipper of the mattress cover and place the Emma hybrid mattress inside the cover, then zip the upper washable fabric to close the cover of the mattress.

  • Place the topper on top of the mattress




Packaging & Shipping


  • The bed will be 2-man handled and optional assembly service will be provided at the customer’s place.

FAQ


Do I have to assemble the bed myself?

  • We want to offer you the most convenient experience there is. That's why we offer you our free assembly service, which assembles the bed in your bedroom in less than 10 minutes.

How do I set up my box spring bed?

  • Align the right and left bed frame so that the sliders are on the same side.

    The 4 wooden feet should be attached to the visible corners of the bed.

    The 2 non-wooden feet should be attached to the non-visible corners of the bed.

    Use the connector between the two bed frames to connect them together.

    Slide the headboard into the bed frame sliders until the headboard touches the floor.

    Unzip the 4-sided mattress cover and place the Emma Helix mattress inside the cover, then zip the top washable fabric to close the mattress cover.

    Place the topper on the mattress - you can choose either a soft or a hard side.

I would like to buy my box spring bed without a headboard, is that possible?

  • For the real box spring bed experience, the headboard, the topper and the mattress are a must for optimal lying comfort. That's why we only offer you the Emma box spring bed with a headboard.
