Introduction


Thuiswinkel.nl is a Dutch e-commerce association that promotes safe and reliable online shopping. The organization sets standards and guidelines for online retailers and provides a trustmark, the Thuiswinkel Waarborg, to companies that meet their criteria.


The Thuiswinkel Waarborg is a quality mark for online shops in the Netherlands. Companies that are awarded the Thuiswinkel Waarborg comply with the association's Code of Conduct, which includes requirements for customer service, privacy protection, and secure payment. The trustmark assures customers that they are dealing with a reliable and trustworthy company, which can help increase consumer confidence and sales for the business.


In summary, Thuiswinkel.nl and the Thuiswinkel Waarborg are important for companies operating in the Dutch e-commerce market as it provides them with a means to demonstrate their commitment to consumer safety and secure online shopping.


How to handle Thuiswinkel requests

Whenever there is a complaint from a customer via Thuiswinkel, klachten@thuiswinkel.org will forward this complaint to ce.complaints@emma-sleep.com. This email will go into the Global Escalations queue, which should be handled with priority by the Online Reputation Team. 


Important! From the moment the complaint is sent to us, we have only 5 working days to reply!



Step-by-Step guide:


  1. Check the details of the complaint in the email;
  2. Handle the complaint as you would with any other customer but keep in mind this customer already went through a lot of trouble reaching out to Thuiswinkel so give the customer priority and pay extra attention to empathy;
  3. Reach out to the customer, preferably by phone and confirm the solution to the complaint by email;
  4. Once you reached out to the customer, confirm this with Thuiswinkel by replying to their email that the customer is contacted and the complaint is dealt with.
  5. Thuiswinkel will confirm they received your reply and that the complaint on their platform will be closed. You can simply close this ticket, no need to reply again.
