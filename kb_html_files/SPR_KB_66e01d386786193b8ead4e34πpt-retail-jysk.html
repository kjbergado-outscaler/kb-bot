Complaints

How to proceed in case a complaint is received with the following table:

  1. Click the link to update the claim.

This step will depend on the claim. For example, if the retailer did not send enough information to proceed, then we select “reject with reason” and write the request for the information we need in the box that will appear.

The information request we will send if the retailer has not provided any details will depend on the information already received. The following template outlines the general approach, but it may vary based on what the retailer already sent. Please check the information sent carefully and adapt your request if/when necessary:

"Hello,

First of all, it is a pleasure to help you. Could you please clarify exactly what you need help with? Are you requesting a refund or submitting a claim for a replacement mattress? Either way, we'll need the following information in order to proceed with your case:

​

  • Order number;

  • Customer's name;

  • Pick up address (in case we need a pick up);

  • Customer's telephone number;

  • Photos of the product's condition (defects and general state of product, 4 photos minimum);

  • SKU photo (is the code of the product that is in a label inside the zipper of the mattress) (Total of 6 photos minimum).

​

Once we have these details, we can move forward with your request. Thank you for your cooperation."

2. We leave a note in the ticket that says:

"Customer JYSK created a claim No. xxx. I requested more information by rejecting the order through the link."

3. We resolve the ticket (it is not possible to send a response).

We should receive another email with the necessary information to proceed with the product claim.
