Cancel an item / all items / bundles



Customer may reach out customer service team for editing or cancelling their items after it has been placed on our online store.

E.g. they order the wrong size of the mattress, or they placed the order twice by mistake.


This article will guide you through the steps of canceling an item in the Fluent Commerce system, which will vary depend on the fulfillment status:



Identify the Fulfillment status




Assigned / Waiting for stock
Cancellation button is enable - follow the cancellation steps below processing the cancellation Cancel item - Step by Step


Submitted to FL and its following statuses up to Ready to dispatch

Cancellation to be submitted to the warehouse. Request should be done via Jira Portal to Emma Order Management team.
Submit Cancelation request via Jira portal and manage customer expectation as per steps: Submit a Cancellation request

Not possible to cancel as the parcel is already in the carrier hands.

Cancel an item - Step by Step


Items can be canceled via Fluent Commerce any status before Submitted to FL (fulfilment location) status.


The cancellation option will be enabled in the case is still possible to proceed with it.


Step by step:


1. Access the order page and click the kebab button.

2. click on the button Cancel order items.




A cancellation window will populate for the next steps:





3. Select item(s) you would like to cancel

4. Provide Reason for cancellation





5. Confirm the cancellation by clicking on Submit





A confirmation window will pop up. The order status will be updated accordingly, together with the Updated Date.









A Credit memo will automatically be created and a refund of the customer will automatically be triggered after the cancellation is confirmed. No manual request is needed. In case of an error, the case will be forwarded to the customer service team by our Emma Refunds team.


Note on Bundle* Cancellation: It is NOT possible to cancel separate itens in the bundle. In case of bundle cancellation, all the products included will be cancelled and refunded.

Bundle* Definition: Group of products sold together.






You can review the Refunds / Credit memo label and logic by accessing How to: Refunds & Credit memo article



CHILE: please be aware, credit memo are not automatic for CHILE Orders, ensure you follow up your original procedure - Cancellation article here.




Late Cancellation & Refuse upon delivery cases


Process for Late Cancellation





In the image above, you can check the status in which the order has been transmitted to the warehouse already. Once that happens, our internal Emma team will try to stop the item's journey by requesting a late cancellation to the warehouse directly.


The process is:


Submit a Jira Ticket with the cancellation request. Once submitted, you must manage customer expectations in at least 5 business days. In case cancellation is confirmed by the warehouse: a cancellation email will be sent via email in the next 48 hours and a refund will be issued automatically (a credit memo will be created with cancellation).



For ALL MARKETS (except Brazil**): Open a JIRA ticket to Back-Office team, as indicated below:


From now on, all Front-Office agents can open a ticket to the Back-Office Team ONLY for requests RELATED TO LATE CANCELLATION.

Process for Refuse upon delivery



In here, the image shows that after "Dispatched" the warehouse is no longer able to proceed with the late cancelation. Then, you must ask the customer to refuse the product upon delivery.


The process is:


  1. Inform the customer that order cannot be canceled in the system and that customer must refuse the delivery;

  2. Ask customer to contact us once the order has been rejected;

  3. Reply to customer and manage expectations about the refund:

    3.1 -> You must check the order in the OMS and if the status is already updated to "return_sender_complete", please proceed to create a JIRA for a Refund in the ARD2C Portal.

    3.2 -> If status is not updated to "return_sender_complete", you need to confirm with the carrier the refusal of the delivery. And manage customer expectations by explaining that first, the carrier confirms in the system that the order was refused, and only after this the refund can be triggered.

  4. If the Fulfillment Status is not updated to ""return_sender_complete" in the next 48h after the refusal date, please contact the carrier.



IMPORTANT: Do NOT create a Return with a Refund.




Access: ARD2C
