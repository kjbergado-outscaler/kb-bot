Who is the Payolution/Unzer?


Payolution, or Unzer, is a payment service provider. Payment service providers take over the processing of the offered payment method. This means that a provider like Emma can offer the payment method without having to take care of it themselves. This is particularly relevant for payment methods such as purchase on invoice and installment, since you may have to push the customer to actually do the payment, if they do not pay in time/ do not pay at all.

How does the purchase on invoice/installment between Emma and Payolution/Unzer work?


In short, the Payolution/Unzer pays us FOR the customer and after that gets the money back from the customer. The customer does not transfer to Emma, but to Payolution/Unzer.


A little more specific:


Step 1:


If an order is placed for purchase on invoice or installment, Payolution/Unzer checks at its discretion whether they would like to agree to the process or not. We don't know exactly what is being checked. However, a credit check will definitely take place. However, this will not be the only criteria after the Payolution/Unzer decides whether to agree or not. Other possible reasons may be that there is already an active invoice purchase on account or installment purchase from another company that works with the Payolution/Unzer or that they simply do not have any data for the credit check and therefore cannot measure their risk.


Step 2:


If the Payolution/Unzer has agreed (it will be checked in a few seconds), the order will be sent and transferred to our and the Payolution/Unzer system.


Step 3:


With installment purchases, the customer receives an installment plan in the mail. He signs this and pays the installments according to the installment plan. The installment purchase is due immediately, regardless of the delivery time, whether the item(s) has already been shipped or delivered.


When purchasing on invoice, payment is only due when we notify Payolution that the item has been shipped. This process is called "capture". It is currently carried out with the creation of the invoice for the item sent. The invoice is therefore created when the item is shipped. Articles that were not sent together will receive their own invoice. Payment of this amount is due 14 days after the invoice is issued.


Any returns, refusals of acceptance or obstacles to delivery are not visible to Payolution/Unzer and can therefore not be taken into account.

Can an installment purchase be converted to an invoice purchase or an invoice purchase to an installment purchase?


Usually yes. The customer must contact Payolution/Unzer.+

Can we change the billing address for purchases on account or hire purchase?


No, we don't do that with any payment method!

Can we do an invoice purchase or installment purchase for the customer?


No, that's not possible! If at all, we only give an option to pay in advance, but we try to avoid that too.
