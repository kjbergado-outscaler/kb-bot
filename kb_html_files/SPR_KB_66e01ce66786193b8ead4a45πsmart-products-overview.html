What is a Smart Product?


The Emma Motion is a new category of smart products that come to give our customers an unprecedented relaxation experience. Emma is introducing a smart mattress to the European market for the first time. We are launching a trio of interactive products with the greatest sleep technology innovations of our time. Those are unique products that can actively support people while lying down.


Our smart mattress uses technology to allow customers to adjust the mattress according to their body type and structure, and their individual sleep position. Unlike a standard mattress that reacts exclusively to the pressure that our body applies on the mattress, so your correct lying position is subject to advice and good habits, and also to the factor of luck. With our products, people have the possibility to control the firmness of the mattress accordingly to their needs and sleep habits. This gives them a new level of individuality and comfort.

Why do we need a Smart Product?


In addition to significantly ergonomics improvements, which help in the prevention of back pain, the Smart Products can also enable improvements on sleep tracking data. The information collected by the products can help identify health problems in advance, discover and register sleep habits, and fastly react to specific sleeping issues.


In particular with the Emma Motion Base is possible to obtain sleep information really close to a medical quality level. For example: during REM sleep, the brain blocks active body movements. This is a protective mechanism to protect the body from injury during the active phase of dreaming. In some diseases, such as Alzheimer's, this protective mechanism is lost. By tracking the sleep phases and the parallel tracking of the body movements, such symptoms can be detected early. This symptom is one of the first indications of Alzheimer's disease. It usually appears 15 years before the main onset of the disease. But even minor infections can be reliably detected through irregularities in the tracking of our smart products.

Products differences


First, let’s see the differences between our smart mattress and then how the base bed complements our Emma Motion products.


Shift X Sense


The Emma Motion Shift allows a person to manually adjust the firmness of the mattress and no electricity is required for this process. By using the Smart Valve, it's possible to push and release the air pressure inside, and with this making the mattress softer. The 3 principal features of this product are: Smart Valve, Shift Core (3 in 1) and Aquagel comfort.



On the other hand, the Emma Motion Sense has an automatic mode to monitor sleep and give people the best support for a deeply restful night. Also, an interactive adjustment of the mattress is done via the Emma Motion App. The sensors of the mattress can detect your sleeping positions and inflate or deflate automatically based on your movements during your sleep. By using the app it's possible to set up the firmness of each zone of the mattress depending if a person sleeps in lateral, supine and abdominal positions. The automated adjustment guarantees a perfect spinal alignment. In this case, our 3 principal features are: Contour Interaction, Deepsleep Optimization and App control.



Base


By using this product, a person can adjust the structure of the bed by remote and app control, in order to help for example with snoring, and muscular issues and keep a track of vital functions. The 3 principal features are: Sleep Tracking, Wellbeing features and App Control. The Base works almost silently and imperceptibly to guarantee the sleep quality.
