What does PLUS mean?


Mattresses Naming


The mattresses with the new cover variant will have Plus added to their names.

For example, Emma Original Hybrid is now called Emma Original Hybrid PLUS.


The New Cover Variant


Without changing the appearance when compared to the previous covers, the new mattress top cover fabric is treated with ‘SweatFree’ technology.


It has the function of immediately absorbing the sweat and evaporating quickly for a cool and comfy snooze time. It allows to keep the body fresh all night long. The technology adapts to all types of sleepers, but especially to hot sleepers who sweat a lot during the night. Read more about it under Product Specification in this article.


Plus Badge



A "Sweat Free Technology" Badage will be marked on the key visual image on the websites to indicate the product was treated with the new technology.

Cover FAQ


Are there any visual differences from the new cover variant?


The two variants look exactly the same in appearance, and the only difference is the added label next to the care label inside of the cover, marking the new cover treated with "Adaptive" technology (shown in the picture below).


This special label works as a trackable mark in case consumers complain that they suspect receiving the same product as the standard variant.



What if customers complain that they can't perceive the benefit?


The technology is designed for all types of sleepers, but it might have some limitations for a certain group. In case consumers complain that they cannot feel the benefit, we can refund the extra money they've paid compared to the standard variant.

For instance, if standard Emma One Foam costs 199 euros, and Emma One Foam Plus costs 205 euros, we can refund the extra 6 euros to avoid the potential return of the product.
