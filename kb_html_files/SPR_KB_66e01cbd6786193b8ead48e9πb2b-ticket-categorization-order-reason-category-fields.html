The ticket system plays a vital role in helping our business.


In our CRM (Customer Relationship Management) tool, Freshdesk, we can track and measure valuable customer service metrics, such as First Response time (how long does our team take to reply to a received ticket), Resolution time ( how long did we take to resolve a ticket ) or even measure our efficiency (how many times we had to reply to the customer to resolve the situation).


But also one of the key data we can generate and track from it is the reason of customers' contact, where we can easily identify needs and work on improvement points due to it.


That's why providing accurate data is essential and a mandatory step when handling customer interaction in the relevant tools' fields (Freshdesk, Freshchat, Aircall & our OMS - Magento, and Fluentcommerce).



Mandatory Fields


Freshdesk


The Order number is always crucial information needed, so we can relate the ticket request from our CRM tool (Freshdesk, Freshchat, Aircall, etc) to the proper order from our Order management tool (Magento or Fluentcommerce).


Category will always be related to the product category of the customer inquiry.


When using our CRM tools, we generate data related to the phase of the order request by separating it into 3: B2B Pre Sales, B2B Pre Delivery, B2B Post Delivery and B2B Post Others.


From that, we should provide related topic and sub topic tagging to specify the reason for the contact of the retailer, such as "Product information", "return request" or "Invoice" as examples.


Review the Freshdesk: Ticket Categorization list article here



Also, we need to provide the outcome of the ticket based on its Status and the related procedure in place:


Open: Default ticket status: action required

Ticket status for reopened tickets


Waiting on Customer: Used for when we're waiting for a customer action/response


Waiting on Third Party: We're waiting for information from a third party (e.g. waiting for WH response)


Resolved by Third Party: (should not be used)


Pending: (should not be used)


Resolved: Interaction is fully resolved. (Immediately triggers a CSAT survey when used)


Closed: (should not be used



Some ticket fields will be automatically filled by our systems, such as group (Country related ticket) and language.

Mandatory fields: Order number, Category, Phase, Topic and Sub topic, & Status
