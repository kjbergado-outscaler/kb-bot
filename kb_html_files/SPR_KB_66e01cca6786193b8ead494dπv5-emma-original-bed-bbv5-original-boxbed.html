Construction Specs

Frame Construction per Supplier

Sleemon -15 mm MDF Core side and footboard

Bed Line -16 mm MDF Core side and footboard

Backboard Construction

40mm box construction (solid wood & wooden composites)

Rolled Slats:

-Flat slats.

-Plywood: 60mm wide and 15mm thick

-40mm as gap between slats, connected with 1.5 cm white woven fabric.

-9 slats per roll


Corner Legs

Sleemon

-Solid rubber wood

-Felt sticker on the bottom, 2mm, min. Ø 3cm.

-Conical: top Ø 60mm, bottom Ø 40mm.

-Screw already assembled on the leg, 20mm high, M8.

-To be assembled onto the corner connector with nut and washer.

-Plastic cap on top of the screw for transport.

-Coated Black all over (including top)

Bed Line

-Solid rubber wood

-Felt sticker on the bottom, 2mm, min. Ø 3cm.

-Conical: top Ø 60mm, bottom Ø 40mm.

-Screw already assembled on the leg, 20mm high, M8.

-To be assembled onto the corner connector with nut and washer.

-Plastic cap on top of the screw for transport.

-Coated Black all over with plastic round cap on top

Metal elements

-1.2mm metal pipe thickness for headboard supportive legs, 40x20mm profile.

-3mm metal plate for corner connectors

-L-shape connectors

-Sideboard metal plate
