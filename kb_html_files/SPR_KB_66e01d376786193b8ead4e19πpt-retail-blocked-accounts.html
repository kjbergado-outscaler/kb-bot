Blocked Accounts

When a customer fails to pay invoices and reaches a situation of concern, Emma may block the retailer's account in Magento until the situation is regularized.

In these cases, retailers will not be able to place orders in the Retailer Portal and the orders must not be accepted in CS. If the retailer questions why orders cannot be placed, please inform them that the account is blocked, and that they should check their outstanding invoices.
