As you could see in our articles, more than selling our products, we want to build relationships and create connections with our customers, and for that Empathy is the must-have skill for our customer service.


Maintaining positive fulfilling relationships means going beyond basic communication and polite thank-yous. And for that, we must feel empowered in our front line, able to address situations at hand and create positive and memorable experiences for each customer that is reaching us. But how?


By putting Empathy into practice.


Turn on your sensitivity radar, and start reading and understanding the customer who is on the other side of the ticket.


Take your chance to create moments that will increase customers' emotional attachment to us. And as a first step you should have in mind is that every ticket you receive has a person on the other side that needs your help, feeling emotions that you may have the power to connect with and make them feel better.



You hold the power to transform the interaction into an opportunity to make an impact.







Understanding Customers


Whenever you touch a case, you need to make it human. Question yourself:

Who is this customer and what is his/her emotional statement?


By identifying the customer you are interacting with, you can be prepared to deal with their behaviors and anticipate their needs, providing a personalized customer experience.

When you look at the person on the other side of the ticket and try to understand the feelings they have, you more than actively & sincerely listen to the person, you are also touching the human side of the conversation and by that, being able to empathize with it.


Example: when someone is uncertain, as they are not sure if the order will arrive in time, or maybe afraid the wrong decision was made by choosing Emma, or even worried if the money was a bad investment and the product will not arrive, question yourself: how can you make this person feels better?What are the feelings that could transform this experience with us?


Maybe making this person feel valuable to us, showing how glad we are for trusting our company. Assure we will deliver our promises and in case there is an issue we are facing, give the confidence we will find a solution as we value their experience with us. That is much more than just updating order status, is giving people an appropriate and engaging emotional experience.

Turn on your sensitivity to read our customers, take the best of the interaction and you will feel empowered.
