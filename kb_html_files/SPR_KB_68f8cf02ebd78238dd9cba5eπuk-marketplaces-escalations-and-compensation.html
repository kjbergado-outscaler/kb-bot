Scope - This SOP applies to ALL UKI Marketplaces

​

Escalated cases requiring additional support must be escalated to offlineCE@emma-sleep.com, and include case number (if applicable, from Sprinklr), external platform order number, Emma order number and a short summary of the support needed. Cases requiring additional support should first be escalated to the team supervisor, who can further escalate to the Emma team as needed.

Cases where the customer is requesting a partial refund due to product defects or additional hardship must be escalated to CE to obtain Sales approval every time. Please note that it is not within our standard policy to offer compensation to marketplace customers. You can use the following template to address this:

​

Subject: Clarification Regarding Refund and Warranty Policy

Dear [Customer's Name],

Thank you for reaching out to us. We appreciate your support and your recent purchase from Emma.

We understand your concerns and always aim to provide the best possible experience for our customers. However, we would like to clarify that, as stated in the product description, our refurbished items are sold with a limited warranty. In line with this policy, we do not offer partial refunds or additional compensation on refurbished goods.

Our limited warranty is designed to ensure that any issues covered within the stated period are resolved appropriately, and we encourage you to review the warranty terms included with your product for full details.

If you have encountered a problem within the warranty period, please let us know, and we’ll be happy to assist you within the scope of our policy.

Thank you again for your understanding.

Warm regards,

[Your Full Name]

Emma Customer Support Team
