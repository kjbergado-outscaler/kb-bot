Introduction

Every time you face a case where the customer has informed us that an injury has happened by an Emma Product, it is MANDATORY that the Legal & Compliance (L&C) team is informed about it. So, in this article, you will find examples of cases that need to be forwarded to them and how to do it.


Cases of injury to be shared with L&C

Below you can check all situations which the L&C team needs to be aware of:

  • Any type of physical injury - (reasons: eg. needles, malfunctioning of the product that ends up in an injury, etc);

  • Any type of rash, allergy, etc caused by the product;

  • Any type of situation where the customer went to the hospital;

  • Any type of situation where the customer is requiring chemical analysis (called "REACH regulation");

  • Situations where a PRODUCT claim escalates into a legal threat;

  • Product Claims where the customer requires restitution or compensation (NOT A REFUND). [not a refund of the product value, but an extra value of money due to the injury - compensation].


Note: If the customer cut her/himself while opening a package this is not at all a reason to request a refund, unless the cut was inflicted using Emma's proprietary cutter.


How and when to inform our Legal & Compliance team

For every case related to a product injury, it is MANDATORY that the agent handling it opens a JIRA ticket to inform the L&C team.

The agent will need to input the type of the claim, the product related to it, the product's SKU, the country where this was purchased and the Sprinklr ticket number.

Please note that the agents will need to handle these cases normally. The escalation to L&C via JIRA is only for visibility, as the L&C team will not handle the cases, but the must be informed about it.

In case a customer directly asks for compensation (outside an issue with mold) or threatens to go to court due to an injury, please have your supervisor escalate the matter to Emma's Customer Excellence team, so that they can review it with L&C and find the best course of action.

What do you need to do after the customer has accepted our resolution proposal and the matter is sorted out

After the matter is sorted out and the customer has agreed to our solution, the agent handling the ticket will need to request for any sensitive data (doctor's notes, pictures of the injuries, medical receipts, etc.) in said ticket to be deleted from Sprinklr.
