Advantages

Sateen 200TC - 100% Cotton
  • Cotton sateen is made from the highest quality yarn and has a certain elasticity and greater wear resistance – looks great even after many washings

  • Contrary to silk, it’s much easier to care at home, and by being made from cotton it’s hypoallergenic.
  • Cotton sateen has a superior durability and can be processed with minimal treatments and chemicals.
  • Great to put straight into the washing machine without any problems
