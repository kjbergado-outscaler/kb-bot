USPs (Unique Selling Points)


Ideal support

The combination of foam and the 12cm pocket spring core provides optimal support for all sleeping types (i.e., back, side, and stomach sleepers) as well as all body types. Especially the pocket springs are providing flexible and immediate alignment to the body after changes in sleeping position. Therefore, the spine is always in the ergonomically correct position for relaxing and healthy sleeping.


Temperature regulation

The top foam layer (Airgocell foam) in combination with the pocket spring core provides great breathability – i.e., creating an optimal air flow to drive excess heat and moisture away from the sleeper. This way, cool sleeping is always ensured.


Recycling

A hybrid mattress is more eco-friendly than a pure foam mattress. The spring core mainly consists of wires; these could be recycled.


Durable

The Emma Dynamic mattress is developed in Germany. We use steel springs for extreme durability and test all of our foams for functionality and durability
