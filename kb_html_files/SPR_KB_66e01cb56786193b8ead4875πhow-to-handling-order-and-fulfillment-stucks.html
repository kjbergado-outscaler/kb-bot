Introduction


In this article, you will learn about different scenarios that can lead to an order being stuck in FluentCommerce, how to identify those cases, and how to proceed or when to escalate depending on the combination of order, fulfillment, and article statuses.

What to do if the Fulfillment Location is "RJT_XXX"?


The RJT_XXX only means that the status of the fulfillment is Pending/Waiting_for_stock. So, please just follow the decision flow chart that you will find below as if the scenario is pending or waiting_for_stock.





"Action_Required" Orders


In certain situations, you may see an order with a red banner disclaimer indicating there is an issue with the order. Those are the ones called "Action_required" Orders. It means the system has recognized the rejection and it is awaiting further action.







How to handle Action Required orders:


Agents can take action or fix an issue ONLY if the rejection reason is:

  1. Address error;

  2. Canceled because there is no stock in the warehouse;

  3. Canceled because the customer requested cancellation.


-> Action Required: Address error


  1. Check and confirm that the rejection is due to an address error;

  2. Click on "Other Actions";

  3. Click on "Address change";

  4. Include the correct address and click to submit. Note: DO NOT leave any field blank on the address-editing window;

  5. A pop-up window will appear asking to "Confirm Refulfillment", please click to confirm it.




-> For UK only


In regards to "address error", before following the step 1 mentioned above, you need to check the rejection reason first, which will be indicated in this file. After checking it, you can act according to the explanation.




-> Action Required: Cancelled due to no stock


Open a JIRA ticket at the Order Fulfillment Concerns portal (OFC).


* For AT, BE, CH, DE, N and PL only: If any other rejection reason (that is not the ones mentioned above), please file a ticket in the OFC portal.



-> Action Required: Cancelled due to customer requested cancellation



Open a JIRA ticket at the ARD2C portal, under the option "Order Management -> Order Stuck Concerns".






Stuck Fulfillments


The moment a customer contacts us asking "Where is my order?", the first step is to identify the status of their order in our system. After this, if there is a problem going on with the customer purchase and you need to escalate the issue, the team that can support it will differ according to the different status of the order, the fulfillment, and the article.

In this part, you find the explanation of what it means each combination of statutes and when and how to escalate issues with those.



IMPORTANT:


To confirm if the order is stuck, always check the "Activities Tab" in FluentCommerce and look for the last update there. Based on this information, you will follow the steps below and be able to identify if the order is stuck or not.

Others Fulfillments stucks



Missing fulfillments:


  • Open a JIRA ticket at the ARD2C portal (OTC), under the option "Order Management -> Order Stuck Concerns".

  • DO NOT create a Supplement order


Returns stucks:


  • If the fulfillment status is COMPLETE, open a JIRA ticket at the Returns Portal.

  • If the fulfillment status is NOT COMPLETE, open a JIRA ticket at the ARD2C portal (OTC), under the option "Order Management -> Order Stuck Concerns".
