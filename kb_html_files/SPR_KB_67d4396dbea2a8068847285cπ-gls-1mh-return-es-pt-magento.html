Returns for packed mattresses in Spain and Portugal are handled by GLS. As opposed to accessories, which are managed via the Return Portal, returns for packed mattresses need to be created manually on the GLS Portal.

Note: GLS does not automatically update the status of the return in our Order Management system. This means that you (the agent) must check the status on the GLS portal to see if the item has been picked up. After it has been picked up, you need to manually complete the return in the OMS, in order to start the refund process.

How are Status Updates handled for GLS 1MH returns in Spain and Portugal?

  • Status updates for Magento are not automated.

  • You (the agent) must monitor the return status on the GLS tracking page on the planned pickup date.

  • Follow up after one week to ensure the return is processed.

  • Update the return status in the Order Management System (OMS).

GLS does not automatically update the status of the return in our Order Management system. This means that you (the agent) must check the status on the GLS portal to see if the item has been picked up. After it has been picked up, you need to manually complete the return in the OMS, in order to start the refund process.

Important information about setting customer expectations

  • GLS sends an SMS or EMAIL, they do not call before picking up.

  • GLS makes 2 pick up attempts. If the customer is not at home at least 1 of the attempts, the return will be cancelled.

What to do when there is an incident with the return

  • Pickups do not change from “GRABADO” (Eng. 'Recorded') status when they have an incident, always confirm with GLS before recording again and contact the customer to come up with a solution.

  • Material a bierto , not picked up

  • No mercancia: arrive to pick up and are told there is nothing to pick up or do not know what is to be picked up

  • Absent, a second pick up is reattempted.

  • No realizada, the pickup must be re-recorded.

  • Select short datelines to have more accuracy. 1-2 days

How to track the return on the GLS status page

To know the orders that are in each of the statuses, click on the magnifying glass on the left of the page. The orders corresponding to that status will appear on the back of the page, with the name of the customer and the city where it is being picked up. The 'X' is the possibility to cancel the pickup.

Most frequent statuses:

  • Solicitada: the collection has been recorded

  • Recibida: GLS has received the order in their system

  • Asignada: an office has been assigned to perform the collection

  • Recepcionada en agencia, when the request is made to deliver in agency instead of searching at home and has already been received

  • Realizada con éxito: pickup completed pickup completed

  • Realizada con incidencia: you went to pickup and did not pickup. Ask GLS what happened to take the corresponding actionhappened to take the corresponding action.

  • No realizada: the collection was not carried out.
