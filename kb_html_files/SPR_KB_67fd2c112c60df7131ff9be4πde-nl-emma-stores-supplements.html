Process Replacement Order

A Replacement/Supplement scenario might happen in Product Claims and Logistic Claims cases. It can also happen if the customer’s product is no longer sold, and we need to offer a similar product in case of defect.

​

IMPORTANT: We have a limit of 3 supplements per order. ​

If, for some reason, the agents need to create a 4th supplement for the same order, they should escalate to their supervisors and the supervisor should reach out to the Order Management POC for review of the supplement need – Maigerlis Correia.

Validate Product Issues

  • Request photos from the customer.

  • Ensure photos meet requirements.

  • If valid, proceed with replacement.

You can find the claim pictures requirements and examples here and how to validate the claim here.

Process Replacement Order

  • Once confirmed, create the replacement order as a Supplement in the system.

  • Create a Supplement Order in Magento with the correct details and the total price.

​

​

Removing Items

  • If there are any unwanted items in the supplement, select the item and click Remove. Then click Update Items to remove it.

Adding New Products

  • Search for Products: Click on Add Products and search for the product you need by SKU or name.

  • Select Product: Choose the appropriate product and select the quantity needed.

  • Add to Order: Once selected, click Add Product to Order to include it in the supplement order.

  • Verify Prices: Make sure all products are listed with a price of 0.

    ​

Beds

  • For supplements for beds, if the supplment is created pre-deliver (e.g. a SKU change and not a replacement for a damaged component), please create a supplement for the full product.

​

​

​

Shipping Details

  • Confirm Shipping Address: Ensure the shipping address is correct. This information should never be changed unless there is a necessary adjustment (e.g., changing the recipient’s name/delivery address).

​

​

You should never change the billing address information.

​

  • Shipping Method: Select the Free Shipping option at the top. This is the default and should always be applied.

​

Important note for supplement orders where additional services are required:

​

These services need to also be booked for the suppement order.

Dynalogic:

Select any additional services that are required to be executed at the customer’s location.

  • For assembly services, choose BEDINS (if the supplement order is for a complete BSB bed, or a replacement part where assembly needs to be carried out).

  • For just moving the boxes in the house to the right location (without assembly services) choose KAMERK

  • If it concerns a Motion Sense Matrass, choose the EMMAMS service.

External ID (Optional)

  • External ID: In most cases, leave the External ID unchanged. However, the customer might provide you this information.
