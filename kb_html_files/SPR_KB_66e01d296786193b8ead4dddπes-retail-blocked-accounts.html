Blocked Accounts

Retailers, unlike the common customer, has credit with the company so their orders can be processed without the need to pay them at the moment. Depending on the retailer, they may have 1, 3 or 6 months to pay for their orders placed.

When a customer fails to pay invoices and reaches a situation of concern, Emma may block the retailer's account in Magento until the situation is regularized.

​

In these cases, retailers will not be able to place orders in the Retailer Portal and the orders must not be accepted in CS. If the retailer questions why orders cannot be placed, please inform them that the account is blocked, and that they should check their outstanding invoices.

Retailers with financial problems and cannot regularize the situation now. We have made special agreements with them, so if any of these retailers ask to place orders, we have to:

  • Ask Backoffice to create the order and leave it in "pending";

  • Inform the retailer that the order will be put in progress once they send the proof of payment

  • Do the POP procedure and pass the order to "Payment Received".


Open the Retail Backoffice Portal and choose Order Creation -> Single Order Creation -> One-time Retailers

​

Please give the following information to the Backoffice team:

  • Account ID

  • External Number

  • Delivery Address

  • SKUs

  • Leave the order in "Pending" Status

​

After creating the request, paste the Jira number as an internal comment in the Freshdesk Ticket.

​

Once the Jira is completed, confirm that the order is correct and inform the retailer that the order will be put in progress once they send the proof of payment.

​

Once they send the POP, do the POP procedure and pass the order to "Payment Received". At this time, inform the retailer about the Expected Delivery Time and send the Order Confirmation document.If any of the products appear as Out Of Stock, please inform that these SKUs may arrive later due to lack of stock.
