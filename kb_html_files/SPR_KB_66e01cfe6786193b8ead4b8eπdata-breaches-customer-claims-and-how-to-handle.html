Customer claims regarding data breaches

Sometimes customers and other individuals may reach out to Emma to report data breaches or security incidents, for example:

  • Customer claims he had unauthorized access to customer data or confidential information;

  • Claims of data leaks or accidental exposure of sensitive information;

  • A customer claims the accidental exposure of his/hers personal data to unauthorized parties;

  • Customer claims inadequate security measures leading to data breaches.

    ​

How to handle a customer reported data breach

-> If you receive a ticket related to a data breach or data security incidents, immediately escalate the issue to Emma's Legal & Compliance team: privacy@emma-sleep.com

Make sure to forward the thread including the report/claim and please provide us with any information received along with a clear and detailed description of the situation (including the relevant ticket number and/or document, what happened, when it happened, who was involved and affected, the relevant email thread, what actions were taken, when was the data breach detected and reported, etc.)

You should inform the reporter or the customer that we have received their message and that you have escalated it to the relevant department.

Dear [Customer's first name],

​

Thank you for your email. We have forwarded your inquiry to the relevant department, and they will respond to you in due course.

​

IMPORTANT NOTES:

  • Besides the above canned response, do not respond or confirm anything about the issue until the Data Protection Team provides you with guidelines and an appropriate response.

  • Please refrain from sending/forwarding blank emails that are not relevant to the Legal & Compliance team.
