Retailers contacts


This article will explain you how to handle contacts coming from Retailers for Italy.



Deliveries and Returns

Deliveries

Retailers in Italy have a portal they use to place orders. CS is not responsible for placing orders on behalf of the retailer, unless is a supplement for an existing order. 


Retailer orders can appear as 1 order per customer, or a list of products to be delivered to the retailer address.

You can identify if a retailer order is going directly to the customer or not by looking at Billing and Shipping address. In the example below, for instance, you can see a retailer order that is going directly to the customer.




Moreover, you can identify retailer's orders by looking at the "Type".



Returns

Retailer might contact us to ask for help in performing returns for their customers. The return process is exactly the same we follow for CS, with one small difference: 


Always ask the retailer if the return needs to be booked from the retailer address or the customer address.


N.B. Independently if the order is individual (only the products from one customer are in there) or a bulk order (retailer ordered different products for different customers to his shop), you need to perform the return of the requested product(s) and issue a Credit Memo.

WE DO NOT REFUND ORDERS OF RETAILERS ON MAGENTO

Return to Refund

As mentioned above, we do not perform refunds for retailer orders. The refund is made via a Credit Memo, that will later deduce this amount on the payments made every 30 / 60 or 90 days by the retailer. We receive these payments, that are the sum of all invoices, deducting all the Credit Memos, via POP.


Whenever you are performing a return to refund (customer is returning and is asking to be refunded), you follow the process for returns based on the type of product and the status of such product, and you will choose the option of "No refund".


Right after, you will need to open a JIRA, requesting for the Credit Memo to be issued; remember to specify the order number, the SKU and the amount.


Once the JIRA is solved, you will be able to find the Credit Memo in Magento



Never assist directly a customer that purchased through the retailer. If the customer contact us, we need to redirect them to the retailer directly.
