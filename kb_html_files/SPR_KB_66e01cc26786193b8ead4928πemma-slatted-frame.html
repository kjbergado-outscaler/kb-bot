Product Communication



Our Emma slatted frame consists of 28 solid real wood slats framed by colorful rubber end caps in the official Emma colors - orange and blue. With the ideal combination of mattress and Emma slatted frame, you are in a dreamy bed and thus ensure a restful sleep. Our slatted frame is also a real adaptation artist, which can be adjusted to your individual needs and dimensions using adjustable sliders. And so that you can sleep peacefully, we have made sure that your Emma slatted frame does not start squeaking, even after years of use.

USPs (Unic Selling Points)



Support
The Emma slatted frame supports you and your mattress ideally and offers you the optimal basis for restful sleep. The stable frame is rounded off with 28 slats made of resilient wood and central straps for extra stability. If you like to read in bed, the slatted frame can be easily set up higher thanks to the strap handle on the headboard.
With or without a mattress
The Emma slatted frame fits almost any mattress and any bed of the appropriate size. It feels most comfortable with an Emma mattress, which supports the frame perfectly thanks to its individual zoning.
Customizable
A good slatted frame should offer you the right suspension. That's why the Emma slatted frame comes with progressive suspension and adjustable sliders that you can adjust to your individual measurements. With a few simple steps, you decide what suits you best.
