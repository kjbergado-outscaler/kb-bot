Sleep Consultations

Overview

By offering tailored sleep insights and expert advice, Emma builds deeper trust and connection with customers, transforming browsing experiences into confident purchasing decisions.

This personalized approach not only strengthens brand loyalty but also encourages customers to explore premium sleep solutions, leading to increased sales and higher cart values. The consultative model positions Emma as a trusted sleep partner, differentiating the brand in a competitive market and fostering long-term growth.

​

  • Which stores offer this service?

All our stores offer the sleep consultation service, completely free of charge.

​

How to Book

Sleep consultations can be booked by anyone on Emma’s website, specifically on the Emma stores Landing page. Below you can find the links that will lead you to those pages.

​

PT: Lojas Emma

ES: Tiendas físicas de Emma en España | Emma Colchón

NL: Bezoek onze Emma winkels | Emma Sleep®

DE: Emma Geschäfte

UK: Emma Mattress UK Store Locator | Emma - The Sleep Company

​

After selecting the preferred location for a sleep consultation in the link above, customers will be led to our booking system, and will be able to see all the available slots.

​

​

After selecting and inputing their data, customers will receive an email confirmation with the location, date and time of their appointment.

 

Emma stores do not offer any additional service besides the sleep consultations. All the nights-trial, assembly, etc. services follow the same set up as e-commerce.
