Email Template for Escalations

Escalations occur when a customer is dissatisfied with the provided resolution and requests higher management involvement.

When to Escalate

  • When a customer explicitly requests to speak to a manager.

  • When an issue remains unresolved after multiple attempts.

  • When the customer threatens legal action.

  • When the customer cc's the CEO.

  • When a complaint is posted publicly on social media or review platforms.

Instructions

  1. Gather details of the customer complaint, including previous interactions.

  2. Inform the customer that their case will be reviewed by management.

  3. Forward the case to the Offline CE inbox and to your Supervisor via Outlook, including relevant information.

  4. Wait for approval and response from management.

  5. Once a resolution is approved, communicate it to the customer.

  6. If compensation is offered, process it following the Compensation Process.

  7. If the customer refuses the resolution, escalate further as directed by management.

If a complaint was raised by an Online customer in the Store, the Store Manager must open a HLE Request in Jira.
