Guarantee Request



As per the trial period topic, customers may reach us to clarify questions under their guarantee rights before they purchase our products.


At Emma, we guarantee the quality and durability of our selling products.

We work with several guarantee durations and they vary accordingly to the product type and the country. The most common guarantees offered are: The 10-years guarantee, is related to mattresses, and the 2-years guarantee is related to accessories.


In case the obligation is not fulfilled, for example, the product arrives defective or damaged to the customer, Emma is responsible to replace the product, or the money paid as consideration will be refunded to the customer.



Mostly, a guarantee request will be handled as a post-delivery topic, as customers may reach us after receiving the product and may need support with certain unexpected issues.

Proof of claim: validation criteria


If the customers want to avail of the guarantee, they need to send us photographic proof of the issue so our customer service team can investigate if it qualifies and the claim will be validated under the guarantee conditions.




If Return (Trial Period request): Not a Guarantee claim

If the customer claims to wish to know about a replacement or a refund due to wrong size order (mistake), or comfort preferences (did not adapt to the mattresses), those requests are not valid under a Guarantee request, it should always be handled under the Return (Trial Period Request).

Friend Referral



Friend referral is a loyalty campaign we have available to all of our customers.


The program tent to motivate customers to refer Emma products to their friends and by doing so, both get beneficiated from it. On one side, the customer who is referring wins a Bonus, on the other side, the "friend" buying a new product has a discount.


Customers may reach us to get information on how to participate in the program, recommend new friends, understand expected deadlines, and refund conditions.
