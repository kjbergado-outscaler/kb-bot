Introduction


In this article, you can check how to verify the information about the option inside FluentCommerce that shows Updated Delivery Dates. This new feature will be available globally, for all orders from Emma's website (D2C / non-wholesale).


Updated Delivery Dates


When to check this?


Whenever a customer contacts us asking "Where is my order?" and after checking the order in FluentCommerce you see a red banner or the "Promised Delivery" in the past already.


How it works?


Every time there is a change in the delivery estimation for an order to arrive to our customers, this will be updated and reflected in the system:


  • The Updated Delivery Date is shown in the "Order Item" list at the Order details page;

  • A red notification banner appears on the Order details page;

  • And the message on the banner says "See Updated Delivery Date" and it is hyperlinked to the ‘Order Item List’ section below.



The image below shows in blue the "Promise Delivery date", and next to it, in orange, the "Updated Delivery Date" with the new estimation of the delivery arrival to the customer.





Note for Wholesale orders: this new column will only show up for non-Wholesale Orders as Wholesale already has an entity called Expected Delivery Date.



When the update was done?


It is possible to verify when the field "Updated Delivery Dates" was last refreshed. You can check this to make sure this is a recent update and to assure the customer about it.

  1. Click on the SKU of the item



2. Go to the section "Attributes" and check the information at the option that says "deliveryDateCalculatedAt". The date indicates the last day the delivery was updated in the system.
