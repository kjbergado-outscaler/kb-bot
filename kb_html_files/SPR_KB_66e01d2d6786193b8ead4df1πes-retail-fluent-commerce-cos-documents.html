Confirming your account creation

Cognito expires the temporary password after 7 days.

As soon as your Cognito account is created, you should receive a new email with confirmation:

With this temporary password and your username, you need to go to Cognito URL (depending on the environment):

  • Production URL

There you can confirm your account and set a new password.
 Remember it! You will use this password to access SFTP

Access SFTP

To access SFTP, you need to use Cyberduck, Filezilla or any SFTP client (it should support FTP over SSH):

Download WinSCP: https://winscp.net/eng/index.php;

Use your username and password to login.
