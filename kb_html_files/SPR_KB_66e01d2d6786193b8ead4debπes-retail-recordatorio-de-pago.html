Recordatorio de Pago

Retailers don't pay the orders like a regular customer. They accumulate invoices (that needs to be payed) and credit memos (that needs to be took off of the paying value) and then pay them to Emma.
This payment can be made via SEPA - a direct debit, where no confirmation is needed on our side - or via Banking Transference - where we need to make the connection between the Retailer and Accounts Receivables (the finance team).
When Retailers don't pay their invoices, you will receive an e-mail from the OPS Manager requesting to send the 1st Recordatorio de Pago and, if this one is not answered in a reasonable period of time, the 2nd Recordatorio de Pago.

​

Send Recordatorios de Pago

Once you receive an e-mail from the OPS team about sending Recordatorio de Pago, you should:

​

1. Check in this document the financial contact you should contact.


2. Open a Child Ticket to this e-mail, making sure you delete the information in "Description" (otherwise, this will be sent as an email to the retailer)


​

3. Insert the respective Canned Response (1º Recordatorio de Pago or 2º Recordatorio de Pago)

​

4. Add the value in the document sent by OPS Manager and attach the document to the e-mail.

​

6. Put a "Ticket Snooze" of 14 days.

  • If you receive the documents (Bank Transference Proof and List of Invoices) please follow the POP Procedure and answer OPS ticket with that information;

  • If the cliente don't respond in 14 days, send the "2º Recordatorio de Pago" and answer OPS ticket with that information.
