Summary of the product


The Emma Silkset includes a Sleep Mask and a Pillowcase made from 100% Mulberry Silk. The products are hypoallergenic, super soft and breathable.

USPs


Mulberry silk
Both, the silk eyemask and the silk pillowcase are made out of 100% mulberry silk. Mulberry silk is considered the highest quality silk in the world. It is smoother, stronger and finer than any other type of silk, giving it the ability to insulate cold and heat and absorb up to a third of its weight in water.
Good for your skin and hair
Mulberry silk naturally supports human moisture retention, soothing the skin and hair.
Improve your sleep quality
The smallest light source already increases uncomfortable sleep. With a sleep mask, unwanted light can be easily blocked out and the natural day-night rhythm is maintained.
The perfect gift
The perfect gift for yourself and your loved ones. It comes in a special box with the slogan “You deserve it“ on the top.
Comes in two colors You can choose the pillow in dark gray and golden beige.

Care and Label


Emma Label on the outside of the pillowcase.


Care label: included and placed on the inside of the cover:

  • Handwash only, to max 40ºC;

  • Dry horizontally, avoid direct sunlight;

  • Do not bleach. It could lose color or be damaged during bleaching;

  • Do not iron too hot;

  • Caution: Silk is more sensitive when wet and must not be exposed to strong mechanical stress (rubbing, pulling, kneading).
