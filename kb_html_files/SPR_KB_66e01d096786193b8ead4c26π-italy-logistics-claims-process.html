Logistic Claim

In this article you can find the IT country specific for the Logistic Claim. 

Please check the process for Logistic Claim here.


Important reminder: all the communications regarding deliveries occur between Customer Service team and Shipments (courier.bassano@bracchi.it) via Sprinkrl.

Logistic claims are complaints of the customers related to inconveniences experienced during the delivery of the products that they’ve ordered. Claims can lead to a cancellation, return, replacement, or even monetary compensation (which is a possible refund of a certain amount of the cost paid by the Customer, based on the country-specific Compensation matrix).

Note: this process is valid also to open Logistic claims for returns that are lost.

Note: Make sure to always adde the Formato apertura Pratica to your first contact to Bracchi.

How to open a Claim

1. Create an outbound case (check here the Knowledge base article)and fill it with the Shipment information

2. Add the following details:

- Subject: Richiesta dettagli su Spedizione - FULFILLMENT REF.

- Description: based on the type of Claim, we'll use different information. Please check below.

- Note: attach the file Formato apertura pratica fully filled (for all carriers)

Make sure you'll select the account Supporto IT Emma Materasso as Sender


How to fill the Form

A. SDA / INVIO

Follow these instructions carefully to ensure the proper completion of the case.

1. LDV - Lettera di vettura

Enter the LDV or tracking number corresponding to the shipment.

2. Case Type - Tipo di pratica

Select the type of case to open from the dropdown menu:

  • Danneggiamento: The goods were damaged during transport.

  • Smarrimento: The shipment was not delivered.

  • Ritardo: Delivery occurred beyond the expected timeframe.

  • Altro : Specify another issue.

3. Note

Provide a detailed description of the anomaly or issue identified.

For Example: Il cliente riporta la ricezione parziale del suo ordine, solo il materasso è stato consegnato ma mancano i cuscini. La tracciabilità indica la spedizione completata.

4. Tipo Imballo

Select the type of packaging used from the dropdown menu:

  • Cartone: The goods were packed in a cardboard box.

  • Plastica: Plastic materials were used for packaging.

  • Legno: The shipment was contained in wooden packaging.

  • Altro: Specify the type of packaging if different.

5. Imballo interno

Indicate if internal packaging was used and select its type from the dropdown menu:

  • Polistirolo: The interior was protected with Styrofoam.

  • Pluriball: The inner layer consisted of bubble wrap.

  • Carta: Paper was used for protection.

  • Nessun: No internal packaging was used.

6. Contenuto

Provide a detailed description of the shipment's contents. Include SKUs and quantities of the items affected by damage, loss, or other issues.

7. Tipo di Contenuto

Select the type of content from the dropdown menu:

For Emma products, select Other and specify the type (e.g., mattress, pillow, duvet).

8. Valore Merce

Indicate the economic value of the shipped goods, following the COGS reference table. If multiple products are damaged or lost, include the total value of the affected products.

COGS File value.

9. Valore Assicurazione Merce

Specify the insured value of the goods, if applicable.

10. Mittente

  • Ragione Sociale: Bracchi Srl

  • Località: Oppeano (VE)

  • Indirizzo: Via Dolomiti 7 Ca degli Oppi

11. Destinatario

  • Ragione Sociale:Specify the name of the company or recipient.

  • Località: Provide the destination city.

  • Indirizzo: Enter the recipient's complete address.

​

Important Notes:

​

  • Save the file in the following format: format_apertura_pratica_ tracking number 

  • If the issue is limited to packaging damage and the customer accepts the delivery, reimbursement for the cardboard is not included.

  • If the shipping cost is disputed (e.g., the customer rejects a damaged package), select Other and include in the notes the explanation, such as: Reimbursement request for shipping cost due to damaged package. Attach a photo of the product condition as proof.

  • Whenever possible, attach a photograph showing the condition of damaged goods, including the product label with the courier's AWB clearly visible in the image.

B. GLS

For GLS we just need to attach to the email to Bracchi a screenshot of Fluent showing the following information:

- Customer Name and address

- Items in the orders

- Value of the item lost

Canned response

To contact Bracchi about the Logistic claim topic we would need to add all the important information in the contact.

The canned response are available for you and you can find them in Sprinkrl.

  • IT - Logistic claim - No updates on LDV. (the delivery/return tracking shows no movement for more than 5 days)

  • IT - Logistic claim - Not received by customer (Customer claims not having received the order that is marked as delivered on the tracking)

  • IT - Logistic claim - Missing part of the order (deliver is marked as complete but one or multiple products are missing).

  • IT - Logistic claim - Damaged in transit (the products have been damaged during the delivery - external boxes are damaged as well)

  • IT - Logistic claim - Dispatched senza movimento: on fluent the status is dispatched (it means it has been assigned to a local hub) but LDV shows no movement at all.
