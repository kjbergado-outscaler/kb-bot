Summary of product


In this article, you will find information about the Emma Sofa Bed Version 2, with different materials and compositions to improve our customer experience with this product. The principal differences from the first version are: the new structure is made of wood, and no longer metal; and we are using a different mattress.

It is a 3-seater sofa bed that can be converted into two single 80x200cm beds or a single 160x200cm bed. It comes in two different fabrics (Diosa and Corduroy) and in two different colors (beige and dark).

USPs (Unic Selling Points)


The Sofa Bed V2 USPs can be grouped into 4 main benefits:


1. The sleep comfort Emma is known for, now optimized for small spaces:

  • Optimises space utilization without sacrificing comfort thanks to Emma mattresses.
  • Mattress covers have a sitting and sleeping surface for optimal comfort in each use scenario. To maximize comfort and breathability when sleeping, simply flip the mattresses. Both sides of the mattress cover are washable.


2. Ultimate sofa bed for the ones in need of a versatile piece of furniture:

  • 4 in 1 product that can be used as a 3 seater sofa, corner sofa 2 individual beds, or 1 double bed, due to how it opens from the normal seating position to “sleep mode”.
  • Sleek and modern design that fits every living room and decorative style.


3. Made to last a long time:

  • Wooden construction to maximize sturdiness and durability.
  • Durable easy-clean fabric that can be washed (pillow and mattress covers are removable) to ensure it always looks brand new.
  • Made in Europe.


4. Convenience from purchase to use:

  • Easy assembly process that can be done in under 15 min and includes only 6 screws.
  • An easy opening system that allows going from sitting to sleep positions in under 10 seconds.

FAQ's


Q. What are the differences between this version to the first one? Why Emma made a version 2?

Apart from being visually similar, almost everything is different. Different structure, fabrics, materials and mattress. Main goal was to improve the sturdiness and durability of the structure.
After the launch of version 1, Emma collected feedback from customers to understand the reception of the product and check improvement points. Based on those inputs from our customers, Emma worked on it and made a new improved version of the Sofa Bed.



Q. Is the Sofa Bed fabric pet resistant?


The fabric options that we have for the sofa Bed have Oeko-Tex certification and they are resistant to normal wear and tear.



Q. Is it for daily sleep, or more for occasional sleep?


According to a survey run before the test, guest accommodation was the main reason for people to buy a sofa bed, we could test that (not daily, but “most comfortable guest sleep”). Otherwise, the real Emma mattress could support regular sleep, as well as the 10-second opening system.


Q. Does the Sofa have removable covers? Can we choose another color?

The mattress covers and pillowcases are removable and washable; the structure is non-removable but has easy-clean treatment – washing instructions will be included in the booklet.
Our product was launched with the objective to be more than just a regular sofa bed, we want to guarantee the comfort of our customers in both versions (sofa and sleep positions) and that’s why we have on it an Emma quality level mattress, instead of a just sofa sitting that is the standard option for this kind of product. So, that is our initial focus and that’s why we only launch in 2 colors (grey and beige), but it’s really good to have the feedback that is interesting having more colors.



Q. Why don’t you have other sizes available?


Emma developed this product with the goal of optimizing comfort, no matter the version the customer is using (sitting or sleeping position). For this, the Sofa Bed is composed of Emma quality level mattresses of the size of 80cmx200cm. And the dimension of the entire sofa was designed to perfectly accommodate this mattress.


Q. Can I buy different covers separately?


Despite the mattress covers and pillowcases being removable, we will not ship covers separately to customers.


Q. Can I handle the assembly of it myself alone?


Yes, definitely. The assembly can be done in under 20 minutes. It can be a one-person job, but it will be easier with 2 people due to the length (2m) and heavy parts. Only 6 screws are needed (to attach the back to the base) and the rest can be done simply by connecting parts through the existing interfaces.

Q. How does the opening system work?


Customers can open the sofa bed (and go from sitting to sleeping) in under 10 seconds. Just slide the under-bed drawer out, lift it and the feet will automatically open, raising the second bed to the sofa height.


Q. If I’m using a mattress protector or a topper on the mattress the under the bed, it will fill inside when I close it to put it back on the sofa version?

Our goal is to have the modern sofa design combined with the comfort of all Emma mattress products. So the Sofa Bed is developed to have a compact size to fit small spaces and allow multiple configurations. A thin mattress protector or sheets will still fit, but not a topper. Nevertheless, the under-bed was designed specifically to accommodate only the Emma mattresses which is the size of 80cmX200cm and 15cm in height, and 80cm in depth.
