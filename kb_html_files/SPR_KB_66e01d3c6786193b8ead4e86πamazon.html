Amazon


General Information


Amazon is an Online Marketplace that works in partnership with Emma. We use Amazon to sell our products in several countries.


Customers may have questions while placing their orders on the Amazon website, such as product information or comparison questions, or also may be in a need of support after their product has been received.


Whenever requiring our support, Amazon gives Emma teams 24 hours to reply to their message, to ensure response time and resolution are always under their expected time.


For supporting those inquiries, we may use Amazon Portal or our Freshdesk ticketing system. You will see more details on the Handling inquiries sections in this article.







Order fulfilments


We work in two operational ways with Amazon:


Fulfilled by Amazon (FBA): With this fulfillment option, Amazon is responsible for stores, picking, packing, shipping, and delivering the products to customers. Amazon also handles customer service and returns for those orders.


From a logistic point of view, all products are available in Amazon's warehouse, and they handle the operational need for delivering them to the end customer.



Fulfilled by Merchant (FBM): In this case, the Merchant is Emma, and we list products on Amazon but manage their storage, shipping, fulfillment, and mostly: we are responsible for the customer support for those orders.

Handling Customers Inquiries


Customers may raise questions or inquiries during their journey with Amazon.


  • Pre Sales Questions
    Pre-sales will always be handled by Emma's Customer service teams (whether it is fulfilled by Amazon or Merchant).

  • Cancellation & refund
    Fulfillment By Merchant- Always an order is canceled in the Amazon portal, a ticket should be raised to the refunds team to proceed with the related order refund (cancellation is unfortunately not integrated into our system yet).
    FBA is between Amazon and the customer.

  • Comfort Layer (Add on - supplement order).
    There are certain scenarios where we may offer a comfort layer to the customer to make their product experience better (e.g. if they complain the mattress is too soft for them, we may offer a firm comfort layer). In those scenarios, Amazon will ask our CS team to support creating the supplemental order related to it.

  • Orders with more than 30 days delivered by Amazon
    Once 30 days from Amazon has been completed, orders fulfilled by Amazon that may require any support will be redirected to our Customer service teams.

Internal Communication


The new way to place private notes and update in Freshdesk and Magento cases:

This new formulation should always be used when we open a new case in Freshdesk, follow up another case, or whenever we need to update the case due to new information provided by Tier 2 or the client.

This information must also be reflected in Magento in the Customer inquiries section.



How to place the private notes:


Market: Amazon/eBay/Kaufland.


Issues: Use keywords to summarize the client's problem.


Troubleshooting: Research done by the agent.


Solution: Solution presented, which information was promised to the customer.


Notes: Additional case information, the client has two or more open cases, if you have relevant information about the case place here the ticket number and summary of the previous case.

Example:

  • M: Amazon.

  • I: Return of 100 days.

  • T: I verify that it is within the 100 days trial period, I ask for photos from the customer, and I send a macro of return.

  • S: Return the product, I will wait for the receipt to initiate the full refund.

  • Notes: The customer has another open case #1234567 I verify that they are for the same issue I close the case.


Attention

We must always place these private notes to have the maximum information about the cases we deal with, so we can verify the information more quickly and efficiently. And to be able to offer a better service to our clients.
