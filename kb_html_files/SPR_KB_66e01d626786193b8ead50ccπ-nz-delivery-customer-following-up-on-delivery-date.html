This is the process to follow when a customer reaches out regarding the delivery of their order to ensure that we check all available resources and we provide the most accurate information possible.


Ticket tagging: pre-delivery, shipping status


1. The customer reaches out to us to follow up on the status of their order.
2. Are we able to get the customer's order number?
If YES, proceed to step 3.If NO, ask the customer for their order number.
3. Check the customer's Magento order status. Is it Payment Received?
If YES, proceed to step 4.If NO, escalate to AU/NZ Customer Service chat.
4. Check the order's Magento status and barcode status. Is it on Waiting for Stocks?
If YES, follow Stock Out Process.If NO, proceed to step 5.
5. Is the barcode status Order Submitted to the warehouse?
If YES, proceed to step 9.If NO, proceed to step 6.
6. Is the barcode status Customer Shipped?
If YES, proceed to step 9.If NO, proceed to step 7.
7. Is the barcode status Packing?
If YES, proceed to step 8.
If NO, proceed to step .
8. Check order date and time. Is still within our delivery timelines?
A few things to remember:
  • Our estimated delivery time for Metro (Auckland) is between 2-7 business days and 5-15 working days for regional areas.
  • Order processing cut-off is 7:00 am MNL. Orders placed after this time will be processed the next business day.
  • No order processing on weekends and holidays.
