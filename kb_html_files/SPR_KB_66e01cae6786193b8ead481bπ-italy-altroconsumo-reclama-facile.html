AltroConsumo

AltroConsumo's mission is to protect and promote consumers rights by a set of different activities, mainly: products testing, inquiries, news drafting, enforcement activities and legal actions.

​

Reclama Facile it's the online platform where their associates can leave reviews related to their experiences as consumers. My typing Emma in the search bar we can find all the latest review left by our clients.

When a customer writes on their platform, this triggers a ticket through Sprinkrl, sent by the email address reclami@altroconsumo.it.

Tickets Handling

Customer is identified

When filling in the form on reclama facile, customers are asked to put there details of their purchase, persona contacts and identification data that can help us identify them.

In this cases, the advisor should:

1. Reply to this email with the canned response: AltroConsumo Acknowledgement. We don’t reply to this email providing customers support as this info are public and visible to everyone.

2. They should look for any previous and ongoing communication between customer and Customer Service.

3. Take ownership and act on the case to find the best resolution.

4. Escalate the matter to your manager if extra support is needed to sort the issue for the customers.

Customer cannot be identified

1. Reply to this email with the canned response: AltroConsumo - Customer not identified.


In this scenario, since we don’t have data to protect, we can answer them via the ticket – creating a public answer on the ‘wall’ - asking customers to contact us via email.
