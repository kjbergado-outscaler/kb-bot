USPs

Customizable Comfort

Tailor your sleep experience effortlessly with our height-adjustable inner layer – add or remove the inner layer to find your perfect pillow height. Furthermore, our zipper system lets you fine-tune firmness, from plush softness to supportive firmness, all in one pillow.

Fluffy as a Cloud

Experience sleeping on a cloud with our customizable pillow, as it's 'Fluffy as a Cloud.' Indulge in the fluffiest, cloud-like support that gently cradles your head in any sleep position, while maintaining its shape for lasting comfort night after night.

Easy to Clean

Both the pillow cover, outer layers and the inner layers can be conveniently machine-washed, offering a trouble-free way to ensure your pillow remains fresh and clean, ready for a rejuvenating night's sleep, every time you desire it.

Materials


Cover fabric

100% polyester down proof, with blue piping


Benefits: it attracts sweat and wicks it away from your body.

That means the fabric will not retain the moisture if you sweat during the night, at the contrary it will travel alongside the fibers until they reach the surface where they can evaporate.



Filling

100% White Siliconized Hollow Fiber polyester

Advantages:

  • Hygienic
  • Soft to the touch
  • Can be treated with anti-bacterial chemicals
  • Lightweigh
  • Water-repellent
  • more breathable than foam
  • Long-lasting if cared for properly

Packaging & Shipping


The Emma pillow is delivered in one carton box with white Emma Logo and Emma Sticker. The carton is a mixture of virgin and recycled paper.


The pillow is rollpacked and packed into a plastic bag – there is no logo on the plastic bag.
