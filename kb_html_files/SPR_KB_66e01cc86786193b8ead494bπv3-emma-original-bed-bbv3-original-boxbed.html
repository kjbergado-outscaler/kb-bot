Changes BELGIUM ONLY (July 2022)


Information about changes on the BELGIAN websites:


The V3 Emma Original Bed is now under the “Boxspring” section on the BELGIAN websites.

It is officially not a boxspring bed: it has wooden slatted frames. However, we market this as a cheap boxspring bed because you can buy a spring mattress with the bed. Which means it is a hybrid model.


Our target: People who can't afford a premium version like the Cameleon or Deluxe. 



For BELGIUM: The name of this product changed to Original Boxbed and Le lit Select.

Product Communication


Naming

BBv3

The Emma Bed

The Emma Original Bed

BoxBed V3

Objective

BBv3 is supposed to capture the mid-range market segment – prices will be affordable like our flagship mattresses

Channels

Primary sales channels will be our Emma website and possibly Marketplaces or Retail

Target positioning

BBv3 is designed for mass appeal (18-65 years, both genders, middle to upper income) as well as to speak to the typical Emma customer (specs derived from customer survey with Emma customers, asking what the last bed that they bought looks like)

Markets

The bed is designed to be launched globally, starting in March/April 2021 with our large markets DE, UK, FR, NL and extending to ES, PT, IT, DK & SE

Execution

It will be marketed both as a standalone product (e.g., via Social media ads) and as a cross-sell product (e.g., pop-ups when customers buy a mattress)

Configuration

We will adjust to country-specific bed buying behavior:

  • In some countries (especially DK, SE), customers usually buy a bed system (= bed frame + mattress + optionally a topper)

  • In these countries, we will market the entire bed system; in all other countries, we will market the bed with an optional Emma mattress.

Product Specification


Overview

  • The BBV3, also called ‘The Emma Bed’ is a bed that is made of the following easy-to-assemble components:

  • Bed base consisting of side boards, foot boards, and a sturdy metal structure.

  • Ergonomic and flexible sprung slats

  • Faux wood feet(plastic)

  • An *Emma Design Protected* foldable headboard (if customer has selected this additional component)

Bed quality - tests


Cigarette Test = Passed


UK ONLY: UK required full Fire Retardancy testing passed on headboard, side/footboards fabric, foam and wadding in addition to the fabric slat cover and headboard cover.


  • 100% Vegan - the Emma Boxbed is 100 % vegan and does not contain any animal-based materials, nor is tested on animals.

  • The Emma Boxbed has been tested extensively during development, as well as in its final production stage for durability and quality.  

  • The wooden slats have been tested for bending fracture load, applying up to 500 Newtons force. 

  • The wooden structure has been evaluated for formaldehyde emission for Composite Wood Products, according to international regulations. We have passed.  

  • The fabric used for the upholstery meets the requirements for cover fabrics, according to international furniture regulations. 

  • Cleaning Tips: For small stains, wet a clean cloth with a little bit of soap. For bigger stains, use a special stain remover, such as the one use for sofas, carpets, furniture in general. 


Assembly and disassembly


(Do not share this video - if sharing with customer refer to the country website link)



The assembly of the BBV3 is easy and can be done by the customer. It takes about 35 minutes to assemble the complete bed.

In order to assemble the BBV3, the customer would need to have received:

  1. Bed base part 1 box and Bed base part 2 box

  2. Headboard box (if ordered).


The bed base box 1 contains an assembly booklet and the foil cutter to unpack all the bed elements.


The booklet and manual for assembly is available as part of Bed base part 1 box. The L-shaped wrench is available as part of Bed base box2.


The booklet and manual for assembly will also be available on the website, along with an assembly instruction video.


The BBV3 can be built and dismantled between 10 to 15 times without damaging the structure. The bed can be dismantled in less than 12 minutes.

Packaging & Shipping


The BBV3 is delivered in 3 boxes with each box weighing less than 25 kg therefore, only one-man handling is required for all markets.



  • The weights and dimensions of each part and the overall weights and dimensions of the 3 boxes into which the BBV3 would be packaged are available with your country CS Lead/ operations. Reach out to have more information.

  • The estimated delivery time: Looked up under “order”. The order will be sent to Emma’s production team once full payment is received and the order will be delivered within the prescribed delivery time. Depending on the payment method this can take a couple of days.

Return process


  • After a return is initiated, one of the collection companies will work with the customer and contact them via telephone within 2-3 working days to organize a suitable date for collection.

  • The Emma bed should be dismantled and placed in the boxes it came and ready at the time of scheduled pick-up by the customer.

  • Depending on the time of the year, the returned products will be donated to different charities.
