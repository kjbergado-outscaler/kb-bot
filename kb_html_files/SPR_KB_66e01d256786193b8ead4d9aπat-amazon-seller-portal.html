Messages in the seller portal

  • All the customers messages can also be found in Freshdesk /keyword: amazon_dach/
  • Once a message has been replied to in Freshdesk, it is no longer visible or open in the seller portal
  • If a message is only available in the seller portal and there is no Freshdesk ticket - you can also answer it there (write a note in Magento "Replied via Amazon SP")
  • We find messages in Freshdesk with the Amazon order number or with the billing address of the customer
  • If the answer goes out twice (in Freshdesk and also in the seller portal), the customer only gets the answer once
  • In the case of two-liners (e.g. when the customer thanks you), you can exceptionally click on "No answer required".
  • We have to answer the customer requests within 24 hours (SLA – Service Level Agreement)
  • The SLA is not adhered to on weekends - the tickets should therefore be processed by Monday around 12 noon

A TO Z claim

Customers who are dissatisfied can report an AZ case to AMAZON. First the customer has to contact the seller (i.e. Emma) and after two days he can report an AZ case.


We distinguish three cases:


- Awaiting response: needs to be processed within 5 days


- Under investigation: no answer required, but we are checking the cases


- Objection is possible: no answer required either, but we still process the case completely


Editing:

  1. Customer must be contacted via AZ case, not Freshdesk (Field: Contact the customer)
  2. AMAZON must also be informed that we have processed the case (Field: Reply to Amazon)
