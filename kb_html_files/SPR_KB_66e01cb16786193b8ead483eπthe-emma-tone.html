And how do we communicate with customers in our posts? Have you noticed some techniques?



We are not robots!


The key to feeling authentic is to talk human to human. We speak to our customers as you were speaking with a friend.

We would focus on things they care about hearing such as how transformational can be a great night of sleep.




We are familiar!

We introduce our names and offer help. We call people by their name, we acknowledge our customer situation, and personalize our written communication. We don't use overly complicated or inside jargon, we adapt ourselves to our customers: young or older, we can fit anywhere.



We are friendly

We are human and warm. We have a positive approach, always. We are optimistic and always happy to search and find a solution. We appreciate people coming to us and sharing their feedback. We thank people for trusting and choosing us.



We are Conversational

Our written communication has the same tone when we speak up. Our sentences are short and direct. Our tone is a normal conversation, informal but professional.




We are excited about a customer checking out our products, showing joy over a purchase decision, and stating anticipation of the soon-to-come good night of sleep on the new Emma mattress. We are consistent across the world, and we use our voices to spread our DNA.
