The customer's IBAN is one of the most important data we receive from the customer. For this reason, we handle this data with particular care.


In special cases we need e.g. in the event of a refund, this information so that the issue can be resolved.


Under no circumstances should the IBAN be sent to Freshdesk via email!

In order to protect our customers' data, we ask customers to enter the IBAN in their customer account using the macro: "Privacy - Request IBAN". The Iban is then automatically transferred to Magento. From there, Refunds can use the IBAN for the refund.
