What is the Right to Portability?


The "right to data portability" means that individuals can request to receive their personal data in a commonly used and machine-readable format, and in some cases, to have it transferred to another organization.



Examples:


Example 1:

Sophie has been purchasing sleep products from our online store but wants to explore other options for better deals. She requests her personal data, including her purchase history and product preferences, from our store in a format that she can easily transfer to another sleep products retailer's system to receive personalized offers.


Example 2:

"Can you give me a copy of my data so I can move it to another service?"

This is a request for data portability. They want their personal data in a format they can take with them to another company.


How to handle requests for "Right to Portability"?



Step 1: Forward the request to Legal & Compliance


If you receive the request via Freshdesk, chat, email, call or face-to-face please forward the request right away to:


privacy@emma-sleep.com

The Legal & Compliance team will reply to the DSR.




Step 2: Reply to the Customer using Canned Response


Suggested Response to Reply:


Dear (Customer's first name), 
Thank you for your inquiry regarding personal data. We have forwarded your request to our Data Protection team, and they will reply to you in due course. Thank you, Your Emma Team
