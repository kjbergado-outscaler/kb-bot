Logistic claims are complaints of the customers related to inconveniences experienced during the delivery of the products that they’ve ordered. We have multiple possible scenarios related to Logistic Claims, such as “Lost in Transit”, “Incorrect Shipment”, “Damages” and “Carrier Misbehavior”.

Incorrect Shipment

The following steps outline how to manage an “Incorrect Shipment” claim.

​

Analyze the Claim

Begin by reviewing the details of the claim to understand the issue. For example, if a customer reports receiving a 90x200cm mattress instead of a 160x200cm mattress, proceed with the following steps.

​

Check the Order in FluentCommerce

Verify the details of the order in Fluent Commerce to confirm the product the customer purchased (size, SKU, etc.).

​

Request Product Verification

Ask for Proof of Product Discrepancy

  • Request pictures from the customer showing the product label, including the product name, size, and SKU. This helps verify the error and ensure the correct action is taken.

If the mattress is in its original packaging, the customer doesn't need to open it. They should take pictures of the packaging and labels.

​

Check the Condition of the Product

We must wait for the pictures to proceed with the request. After validating the claim, proceed accordingly.

Create Supplement Order (if applicable)

If the customer wants a new delivery, you should proceed with the pickup request after receiving the pictures. After this step is done, you may proceed with the supplement order creation.

  • Add a Comment to the Order: Document the situation on the order page for visibility.

  • Close your Sprinklr case.

Start Return Process (if applicable)

If the customer opts for the Return/refund, you should proceed with the pickup request after receiving the pictures. After this step is done, you may proceed with the return process “return with refund” → “complete return”.

  • Add a Comment to the Order: Document the situation on the order page for visibility.

  • Close your Sprinklr case.

Carrier Misbehaviour

This procedure applies to any situation involving carrier misbehavior, such as leaving products unattended, refusing delivery, or exhibiting disrespectful behavior.

Receipt of Complaint

  • If a carrier misbehavior claim is received from a retailer, acknowledge receipt of the complaint immediately.

Analyze the Incident

  • Carefully review the details of the complaint to understand the nature of the misbehavior.

    • Identify the specific issue (e.g., unattended products, refusal to deliver, lack of respect).

Respond to the Retailer

  • Apologize for the inconvenience caused by the carrier’s actions and assure the retailer that you will take immediate action to resolve the matter.

Action Plan Based on Issue Type

  • Delivery Issues:

    • Contact the carrier directly via email, requesting that they complete the delivery as soon as possible.

  • Other Types of Misbehavior (e.g., disrespect, inappropriate behavior):

    • Escalate the situation to your Supervisor for further review.

    • The Supervisor will escalate internally to Francisco, who will address the issue with the carrier.

  • Add a Comment to the Order: Document the situation on the order page for visibility.

  • Close your Sprinklr case.
