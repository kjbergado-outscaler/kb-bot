What is the Right to Access?


The "right to access" means that individuals can ask to see what personal data a company has about them and how it's being used. For example, if someone requests a copy of their personal data or if they want to know what information Emma has collected about them, they are exercising their right to access.


In this case, you must forward the data subject request to privacy@emma-sleep.com.



Examples:


John never bought Emma products nor registered to receive Emma’s newsletters but receives marketing communications from Emma. He is curious about the personal data Emma has collected about him over the years. He submits a request to the customer service asking for a copy of all the information Emma has about him, including advertising preferences.


Daniel has been a customer of your online sleep products store for some time. He's curious about the personal data Emma collected about him and how it's being used for marketing purposes. Daniel submits a request to customer service to provide him with a detailed report of his account activity, including his purchase history and any marketing preferences he might have provided.



Other examples of access requests you may receive:


“Can I see what data you have about me?"

  • This is a request to access personal data, which is one of the rights under GDPR. They want to know what information we have about them and how we're using it.

"I'm curious about the information your company has about me. Could you please provide a detailed report of my account activity and personal data?"

  • This request is a general inquiry from a customer who wants to know what information the company has collected about them.

"I suspect that there may be errors in my personal data stored by your company. Can I request access to review and correct any inaccuracies?"

  • This request is from a user who wants to review their personal data for accuracy and potentially correct any errors they find.

"I'm considering using your services but would like more information about how my personal data will be used. Can you provide a summary of the data you collect and how it's processed?"

  • This request is from a prospective customer who wants transparency about the company's data practices before engaging with their services.

"I recently made a purchase from your online store and would like to access my order history and account details for my records."

  • This request is from a customer who wants to access their past purchase history and account information from the company's online store.

"I want to understand how my personal data is being used for targeted advertising by your company. Could you please provide me with a summary of the data collected and how it's used for advertising purposes?"

  • This request is from a user who wants to know how their personal data is used for targeted advertising on the company's platform.


How to handle requests for "Right to Access"?



Step 1: Forward the request to Legal & Compliance


If you receive the request via Freshdesk, chat, email, call or face-to-face please forward the request right away to:


privacy@emma-sleep.com

The Legal & Compliance team will reply to the DSR.




Step 2: Reply to the Customer using Canned Response


Suggested Response to Reply:


Dear (Customer's first name), Thank you for your inquiry regarding personal data. We have forwarded your request to our Data Protection team, and they will reply to you in due course. Thank you, Your Emma Team
