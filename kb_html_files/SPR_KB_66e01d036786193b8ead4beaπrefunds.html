Credit/Debit Card: Customers who placed orders using credit card will be refunded within 3 to 10 working days in Magento

Possible Issues:

  1. Expired credit cards will fail refunds: Refunds will fail in situations if the refund was processed after card expired. In this situation you will observe the following in Magento

Expiry date may show a date older than when refunds was processed as circled in red or show as this

Expiration Date: 00


Resolution

Request for new account details and SORT code from customer, assign ticket to Refunds, add a note which entails new account details of customer title as Failed refunds – Expired ID

PayPal

PayPal refunds are also processed within 3 to 10 working days.

Possible Issues

  1. Pay one – Pay pal technical issues for partially refunded orders.

  2. Incorrect discount calculation (including OMR in the computation).

HOW TO DEAL WITH THE ISSUES?

  • Escalate issues with Alexie or Alice to assist you.
