Introduction

This procedure applies to all customer service representatives responsible for managing product claims and returns for Mattress Next Day. It does not cover order placement and logistics, which are managed separately by Inna and Tiara. For any additional clarifications, stakeholders such as Inna, Tiara, or senior management should be consulted.

Understanding Truck Shipment Orders

Mattress Next Day operates under a truck shipment model. Unlike standard shipments, these orders consist of large quantities of mattresses grouped under a single Purchase Order (PO) number. Because of this, individual order processing differs from direct-to-customer shipments.

Process Overview

Receiving Product Claims

  1. Customer complaints are received via email (Sprinklr).

  2. If cases are missing in Sprinklr, they may be directed to a designated Outlook email.

  3. The claim must include:

    • Customer’s name

    • Mattress type and size

    • Purchase Order (PO) number (used for truck shipment tracking)

    • Customer’s reference number

    • Delivery date (critical for verifying the 200-night trial period)

  4. Ensure that all necessary details are included before proceeding.

Analyzing the Photos

  1. Request photographs from the customer showing the mattress issue.

  2. Verify that the provided images clearly depict the described fault.

    • If the images do not meet criteria, request additional photos.

  3. If the photos are valid, proceed with return/replacement approval.

You can find the claim pictures requirements and examples here and how to validate the claim here.

​

Release Note: We are not authorized to process product claims related to the mattress cover being cut during the unboxing process using the Emma Cutter, or defects resulting from sleeping on the incorrect side of the mattress.

Approving Replacements

  1. If a claim is approved:

    • Advise Mattress Next Day to replace the faulty mattress from their stock.

    • Request a debit note from them for processing credit memos.

  2. Collection Policy: Mattress Next Day does not return faulty mattresses. They are responsible for disposal per agreement with Inna.

Processing Returns & Credit Memo

  1. Locate the corresponding PO number in Fluent Commerce or Magento.

  2. Follow these steps in Fluent Commerce:

    • Search for the BU key (BU-EGEGR) for Mattress Next Day.

    • Locate the specific PO number.

    • Identify the mattress type (e.g., "Emmy Premium Hybrid Mattress King Size").

    • Process a return for the identified item.

Create Return Process in Fluent Commerce

  • Click the three dots in the top-right corner → Select "Create Return with Refund".

Fill Out the Return Form

  • In the return form, you may fill out blank fields with a dash (“-”). Since it’s a retailer order, the form will auto-fill those fields.

    • The fields Company Name, Street, City, Postcode, and Email are automatically filled and you can leave them as they are.

Returned Item Details

  • Quantity of Items: Select the quantity of items to be returned by clicking the “+” icon.

  • Item Condition: Choose the appropriate condition:

    • Packed - Never used

    • Packed - Used/Re-packed

    • Unpacked

  • Return Reason: Select “Product Claim”.

  • Comments: Add the end customer’s name in the “Comments” field.

  • Click Submit on the top right corner.

  • Flag “No pickup disposal by retailer” → Click Submit on the top right corner. 

Finalize and notify Mattress Next Day that the credit memo will be sent to their accounts team automatically upon completion.
