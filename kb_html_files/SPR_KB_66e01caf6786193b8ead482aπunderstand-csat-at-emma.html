What is CSAT?


Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) is a Key Performance Indicator that measures our customers’ satisfaction based on the service we provide to them as CS agents.


CSAT is about identifying gaps between customer expectation and the actual customer experience and doing whatever we can to close them.


High customer satisfaction leads to greater customer retention, higher lifetime value, and a stronger brand reputation.


Overall, CSAT is a valuable tool for understanding customer perceptions, identifying areas for improvement, and ultimately delivering better products and services. By regularly measuring and analysing CSAT scores, a company can make data-driven decisions to enhance customer satisfaction, loyalty, and business success.

Please consider: Customer Satisfaction at Emma is basically based on the resolution of more complex problems!



Why is that?


Don't be afraid! Think from this perspective: If Emma's information is in place and easily accessible, customers are capable to solve their doubts by themselves by checking our websites, FAQ, etc.

So when customers contact Emma's customer service, normally it's regarding more complex problems. For example, order delays.

Improving CSAT: What is your role?


Your role here is to acknowledge the unpleasant situation the customer is communicating and do your BEST to turn it into a positive one!


When dealing with inquiries, you should always remember:



Customers look for a resolution!



Knowing that, ask yourself, am I providing a solution that will solve the customer's problems effectively?

Always aim to gain understanding of the situation, and make the right questions, this will help you to save the customer's time and come to the ideal solution.
