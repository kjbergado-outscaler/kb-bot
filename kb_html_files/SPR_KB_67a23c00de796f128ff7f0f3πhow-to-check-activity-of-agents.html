When an agent status goes to "unavailible"- checking the activity of the agent allows you to understand if this is do to connection issues, or a different issue.

What could be the cause of a "connection issue?"

- headset not in sync

- internet issues

- agent changing it manually

- or a variety of other causes

How to check the activity

1. In the Supervisor Console, open “queue monitoring”

2. Select the agent(s) that you need to check

3. Click the three dots next to their name and select "view activity"

4. A pop up window will appear on the right side of the screen. Change the drop down menu to "Admin"

5. In this screen, look for the information under the indication "User avalibility status" and "Avalibility Status Updation Reason"

From this, you can understand the issue.
