Summary of the product


The Emma AirGrid Foam pillow, also known as Emma Zero Gravity Foam, is one of the new pillows developed with the Air Grid technology. It is adjustable, has a cervical shape and has cooling effect due to the grid that works on the temperature regulation.

USPs

  • Adjustable Height: Contours to you with 3 adjustable layers for personalized support: This pillow’s memory foam shapes to the user head to give him a better neck support and it bounces back, so his won’t need to adjust it during the night. At home, the customer can add or remove layers to change the height and firmness to fit his neck and sleeping style/position/preferences. With more layers the user will make it firmer and more supportive, while with fewer layers he will make it softer and more flexible. 

  • Themoregulation: Keeps optimal temperature with a skin-friendly thermoregulating grid: This pillow has a special, comfortable soft-touch cooling layer with hundreds of air pockets that keeps the user body temperature just right, so he stays comfortable all night. He won’t need to wake up to flip the pillow to find the cool side. 

  • Pressure Relief and Total Relaxation: Provides pressure relief and relaxation with a honeycomb mesh design: Thanks to the honeycomb mesh layer, this pillow absorbs pressure and gives to the user the head, neck, and shoulders support they need, spreading weight evenly for extra comfort. 

  • Features: 

  • Improved Ergonomic 

  • Adjustable Height: 3 inner layers 

  • Advance Temperature Regulation: Air Grid Technology 

  • Not OKEO-TEX certified 


The AirGrid is composed by a gel grid that can have a strong smell once the product is opened. The smell is not toxic and totally harmless. The recommendation is to leave the pillow ventilating for at least 1 day.
