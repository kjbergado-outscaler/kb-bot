What to say if you are contacted by press (journalists and/or PR)?


You’re contacted by a journalist wanting to know more

I’m afraid as you are a journalist and not a customer, I am unable to assist you at the moment. Please contact our Global PR team by emailing media@emma-sleep.com and they will get back to you as soon as they can.

If they’re looking for updates on specific cases…

I’m sorry, but I am unable to share confidential information regarding a customer’s order with you. Please contact media@emma-sleep.com and the Global PR team will get back to you.

If they want you to comment on specific actions we’re taking to rectify the situation

I’m afraid I cannot discuss internal policies and processes with you. I am here to help customers with their orders. Please email your questions to media@emma-sleep.com and the Global PR team will get back to you.



You are contacted by a customer that calls to vent and has no order with Emma


You’re contacted by a customer who just wants to vent

I appreciate your frustrations and I’m sorry for any inconvenience caused. If you would like me to look into any specific details relating to your order, I can do that for you now.

If they don’t have an order…

I appreciate that you’re frustrated, and I’d like to reassure you that we are prioritising finding a solution to the outstanding process issues right now. However, if you do not have an order and do not wish to find out more about our products, I am unable to help you right now. If you would like to call back to discuss a current or future order at any time, please call back and we would be more than happy to help you.

If they have an order but don’t want an update…

I appreciate your frustrations, however as there is nothing specific I can help you with relating to your order right now, all I can do is offer my apologies for any inconvenience caused.
