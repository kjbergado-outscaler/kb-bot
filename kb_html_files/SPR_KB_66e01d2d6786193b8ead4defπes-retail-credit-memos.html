Credit Memo Information

A Credit Memo is a document that "cuts" the total or part of the value of an invoice. It's always associated to an Order.

​

Credit Memos are created whenever we have a cancelation, a return or a discount in the order.

When are Credit Memos needed?

Since the customer in retail market is the retailer itself, the process for the final client's refund is combined by:

  1. Emma refund the retailer: The refund is made via a Credit Memo, that will later deduce this amount on the payments made every 30 / 60 or 90 days by the retailer. We receive these payments, that are the sum of all invoices deducing all the Credit Memos, via POP.

  2. The retailer refund the client: Internally, the retailer refunds the client.

​

If you feel confident to create a Credit Memo, you can create it yourself as per instructions bellow. If you have any doubt, open the Retail Backoffice Portal and choose Orders/Returns Concerns-> Credit Memo Concerns

​

Please give the following information to the Backoffice team:

  • Order Number

  • Amount / SKU to refund

After the creation of the Credit Memo, kindly inform the retailer that the document has been generated. 
Don't send this document unless the retailer asks for it.

How to create Credit Memos?

Magento

Credit Memos can only be created for orders that are invoiced.

If you want to create a credit memo for an order yet not invoiced, you must invoice the order first (but please be aware of items not yet shipped as this impacts the WH transmission!)

​

Credit Memos of Full Order or Single Products

​

1. Go to the order and click "Credit Memo" on top.

​

2. Under "Qty to Refund", put the number of items to be refunded and click "Update Qty’s".

3. Double check the amount in "Refund Totals" and click "Refund Offline".

​

Credit Memos of a Specific Value

​

1. Go to the order and click "Credit Memo" on top.

​

2. Under "Qty to Refund", put 0 and click "Update Qty’s".

3. Indicate the refund amount in "Adjustment Refund" and click "Refund Offline".
