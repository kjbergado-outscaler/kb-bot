AMAZON refund


AMAZON should always refund within 5-7 working days. If the customer reports that no refund has been made, we have to clarify it with Refunds. Therefore do not forward it to Refunds in Freshdesk, but contact the relevant Refunds colleague immediately via Teams. The answer usually comes within 24 hours.

Change of payment method (also for refund)


We refund to AMAZON and AMAZON is responsible for the transfer to the customer. We cannot process/answer the following requests:

  1. Change of payment method
  2. Change of Account for Refund
  3. New credit card number
