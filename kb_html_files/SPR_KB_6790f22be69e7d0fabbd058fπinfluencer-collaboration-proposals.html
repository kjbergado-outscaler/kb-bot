Process step-by-step:

​

  • Greet the customer;

  • Introduce yourself;

  • Get the person’s name (if this wasn’t already provided);

  • Thank them for their interest in Emma;

  • Ask for their email address and explain that this is required for us to be able to assist further;

  • (After getting the email address) explain the process;

  • Thank the customer and bid them farewell in a pleasant way;

  • Create a Jira ticket to the appropriate team.

  • Once you have resolved the conversation, open the Jira escalation channel for Central Europe, select the corresponding market and create a ticket.

You should include all information a brief summary of the request, requester name/ username. email address and a screenshot of the conversation.



When influencers reach out regarding a request from their followers, please refer them to the email address ce.complaints@emma-sleep.com. These come into the group "Global Escalations" and should be handled with priority on a daily base.
