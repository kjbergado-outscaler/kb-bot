Summary of the product


The Emma Zero Gravity mattress has the AirGrid technology, which consists of an instantly adapting and rebounding comfort layer made from a super-stretchy material. This product is developed to give our customers a weightless sleep experience and has 2 performance features: maximum pressure relief and thermoregulation.



Pictures




IMPORTANT: A small amount of the production for the firsts shippments was developed with the GelGrid layer in white colour. The layer is not visible to the customers, but in case of any question about it, please clarify that the technology and the quality is the same, just in a different colour. The marketing of the product will be done only in blue, since the mass production is being developed in this colour.



USPs (Unic Selling Points)



Pressure relief -> Step up to the most comfort and relaxation for your shoulders, spine, and hips.

• Maximum pressure relief thanks to the gel material.


• Unlike foam that takes time to rebound after being pressed down, the gel layer instantly adapts to your every move and cradles pressure points for optimal comfort.

Thermoregulation -> Enjoy sweat-free sleep thanks to airflow improved to the maximum

• Large air channels make it our most breathable mattress.


• The mattress does not trap heat maintaining an ideal temperature all night long.


• Gel material feels cool to the touch which helps you to unwind and fall asleep faster.

Responsiveness-> Individual and ergonomic support for every sleeper

• Since the gel layer is super responsive, it enhances body support.


• This mattress lets you sleep undisrupted by your partner tossing and turning.

FAQ's


Q. What is this mattress compared to the others in terms of bouncing, being point elastic?


Compared to the Diamond Hybrid, for example, is more point elastic.



Q. How it is compared to other Emma's mattresses in terms of firmness?


The Zero Gravity mattress is classified as medium-soft in terms of firmness. Compared to Hybrid, for example, it's considered the same type of feeling, just with the Zero Gravity bouncing back faster.



Q. The springs are the same as the other products or there is any difference?


Yes, the springs are the same.



Q. This Gel Grid layer is made of what material and can the customer feel the airgrid when they lay down?


The AirGrid is made of TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) and it's not possible for the customer to feel it under the foam layer.



Q. Since one benefit is the cooling feature, is there any risk that it gets too cold?


None. The technology is a passive thermoregulator - meaning it cannot become cooler than the environment around it. What it does is get closer to the ambient temperature since it's very good at modulating hot spots (such as you overheating under blankets), so there is little chance of becoming really cold. The product is also very effective at preventing overheating.



Q. Is this mattress recommended for decubitus prophylaxis, in the sense of offering protection for a person who has to lay down in bed for a longer time, not getting decubitus (avoid soreness)?


We wouldn't advise this mattress for those with decubitus given that requires a specific anti-decubitus mattress which is a medical product.
