Overview

While Segmüller does not have an internal portal and there are no specific guidelines for email formats, this document details how to identify the order number, and the steps required for when we receive a penalty balance document. We have a contractual obligation to pay a fee for delivery delays, as outlined in our agreement with Segmüller, and this might result in the necessity to forward this document to the correct team.

Find the Order Number

The customer sends us the e-mails in the following format:

​

​

  • Download the file

  • When you open the file, you’ll be able to find our internal order number as per marked in red below.

​

Locate the Order

  • Search for the Order: Use the order number to locate the order in Magento/FluentCommerce.

  • Verify Order Details: Confirm that the order matches the product reported by the customer.

  • Check Customer Information: Ensure that the customer’s name and address are correct.
