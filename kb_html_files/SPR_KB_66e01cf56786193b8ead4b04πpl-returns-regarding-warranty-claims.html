In Poland, the 10-year warranty period applies to all products.

Step 1: Check the validity of the claim

Emmaâ€™s offer of the 10-year warranty period remains valid. That means we will follow the normal process and must verify if the return was created within 100 days after the last item was delivered to the customer. For the full process, look here.

If the claim is invalid, the process stops here.

Step 2: Check if the product is still usable and propose a compensation

As with any claim, we must first check if the customer is willing to keep the product due to a defect that does not affect the quality - e.g. a color issue. If that is the case, you will first offer monetary compensation (which is a possible refund of a certain amount of the cost paid by the customer. The compensation can be calculated with the Compensation Matrix.

Step 3: The product is no longer usable: Offer a full refund and create a return

Because we no longer operate in the Polish market, we can no longer offer replacements. We will proceed with issuing a refund and organizing a return.
