USPs


Foams can be swapped, taken out

What does it mean?

  • The customer is able to swap the order of the foams, as well as to take one or two out.


Personalized pillow -> adjustable heights for every type of sleep

What does it mean?

  • For side sleepers

    • Side sleepers have the greatest distance between the head and the shoulder -> recommendation -> All 3 layers

  • For back sleepers

    • It is recommended to use between 2 or 3 layers, depending on individual lying preference.

  • For stomach/abdominal/belly sleepers

    • It is recommended to use only 1 layer, depending on individual lying preference.


Temperature regulation (Breathability) - cover

What does it mean?

  • Every night your body loses fluid. That's why the cover of the Emma cushion is particularly breathable. So, your body can release excess energy in the form of heat, and you keep a pleasantly dry sleep climate.

  • How does it work?

    • Open-cell foams provide thermo regulating benefits (top layer and third layer)

  • Benefits

    • Don’t wake up sweating.


Reversible/ flippable

What does it mean?

By turning the Emma pillow, you can choose the side that suits you best. There are three layers and each with a different feeling:

  • Supporting side (white/ Supportive HRX Foam) -> it offers an ideal support function for your head and neck.

  • Comfort side (blue/ Gel Memory Foam) -> it offers a feather-light lying feeling and supports your head while at the same time sinking in ideally.

  • Adaptive side (White/ visco memory foam) -> it distributes optimally the pressure and supports the head and shoulders, relieving pressure efficiently. In a reclining position, it adapts perfectly to your body.
