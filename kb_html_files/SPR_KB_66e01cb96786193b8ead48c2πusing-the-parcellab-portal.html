ParcelLab


Customer Service teams can manage information related to customer queries in the parcelLab Portal.


This article describes the core concepts of the parcel Lab Portal for Customer Service teams. In the parcelLab Portal, you will be able to:


  • Find and access data for tracked parcels.
  • View tracking pages as they are displayed for your customers.
  • Review email notifications that have been sent to your customers.

Trackings

The Trackings module provides an overview of all parcels. There are three local navigation points within the Trackings module. Each of their functions are described below.


Trackings: How to find a tracking


In cases where you need to retrieve additional information for a customer, such as the delivery status of their parcel,

here’s how you can use the Portal’s search functionality.


1. In the search field, fill in any part of the order data associated with the parcel.
The following information can be used to search for a parcel:


  • Tracking number
  • Order number
  • Delivery number
  • Recipient's name
  • Recipient's email address



Among the results that appear, your search query will be highlighted in the tracking record. In the following example, the tracking number was entered into the search field.




2. Click on the listed tracking


The Details tab will open where you can retrieve the full details of the tracking.

On this page, there are three main sections: the full tracking record located on the top of the page, and two sections below it, namely Trace and Notifications.





1. In this section, you will find all the tracking details for the parcel.

You also have the option to click on the following buttons:


  • Tracking Page: This allows you to view the track and trace page configured and designed for your brand as it is seen by your customers
  • Carrier Trace: This allows you to view the carrier’s tracking page.
  • All shipments of order: This allows you to view all parcels in an order, if the order has been split into multiple parcels.
  • All shipments of recipient: This allows you to view a list of all the recipient’s parcels for the last 90 days.
  • Show details: This will display additional information such as the order date and delivery date.



2. In the Trace section, you can view the selected tracking’s Trace status updates, starting with the most recent one.


3. In the Notifications section, you can view the notifications that have been configured in your brand’s look and feel and automatically sent from parcelLab to the recipient.


For each email notification, you can find details on the date and time the email was sent, and if and when it was opened and clicked on. Clicking on an email record will open the email as seen from the customer’s point of view.

Trackings: Resend an email

In cases where a customer may not have received the required information, such as a returns label, here's how you can easily resend an email for the relevant tracking in the Portal.


  1. Search for and select the required tracking. For further information, see how Trackings: How to find a tracking
    The Details tab will open where you can find the email notification to resend.

  2. Click the required email record in the Notifications section.
    The email record will open

  3. Click the Re-send email button at the top of the email record.



A pop-up window will display to confirm the email address and resend the email.


4. Check and if required update the email address in the field.


5. Click Send. A message will display to confirm you want to send the email to the email address entered.


6. Click Yes. A confirmation message will display to notify you whether the email was successfully sent or not. When the message is sent, it will be displayed in the Notifications section of the corresponding tracking and have a Resend- prefix.




Order Confirmation email in ParcelLab



For AU, CH and BE:


The "Order Confirmation" emails for customers from Australia, Switzerland, Belgium are now being sent via ParcelLab and they are visible also for the agents via the ParcelLab portal.


For the customers, this is a new version of the "Order Confirmation" email that has new features that include: promised delivery dates for each item, pre-authenticated links to the Order Status Page, and a display of payment method.


For the agents, it means that via the ParcelLab portal you can also see exactly what and when those emails were sent to customers. Additionally, you can also resend this "Order Confirmation" email via ParcelLab if the customer asks for it.

IMPORTANT:


You may see other emails inside the ParcelLab portal, however, you CANNOT resend those to the customer via the portal, because they are still in test mode. ONLY the "Order Confirmation" can resend it.
