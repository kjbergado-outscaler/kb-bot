Introduction


This article is part of the Out of Stock Replenishment Plan but only for Brazil. The goal here is to give visibility about products Out of Stock - for those cases when the status in the OMS (Magento or Fluent) shows the barcode as "Waiting on Stock" - and replenishment dates.


Here you have access to an Excel file with the Out of Stock Replenishment Plan and to the dates of when the products will be available at the warehouses. Additionally, you will find instructions on how to calculate the appropriate compensation.

Overall step-by-step instructions


  1. Customer reaches out to you with a Where Is My Order request;
  2. You see an item in the order has the status "Waiting on Stock" or with the "Promise Delivery Date" already in the past or to expire in a couple of days;
  3. You check the updated delivery dates in the most recent excel file;
  4. You calculate the appropriate compensation, depending on the severity of the delay using the compensation excel.
  5. You start the refund initiation (Jira portal here) with the subject line "BR OOS" and choose the
    Compensation-Out Of Stock topic;
  6. You send the appropriate canned response to the customer
  7. If the customer comes back and doesn't accept the compensation, you start the cancellation process and answer them appropriately.


Guidelines on how to use the Excel File


Agents must go to the file BR Logistics Control 2024 and on the tab AGENTES - STATUS PEDIDOS they should only make changes on the green cell (Order).




How to check the information?


After pasting the order number as shown in the example below, all relevant information will appear in the sheet.





How to check compensation


  1. You checked the updated delivery date as described above;
  2. Go to the "Compensation Matrix" file to define the appropriate scenario and corresponding compensation;
  3. Add the product (or bundle) value in cell B7 to calculate the compensation amount;
    1. If the customer comes back after already gone through scenario 1, 2 or 3, calculate the compensation amount by filling in the total order value in cell B7 and the previous given compensation in cell B16 to calculate the maximum allowed compensation amount.
  4. Create a JIRA ticket for the Billing and Refunds team to reimburse the customer with the subject line "BR OOS" and choose the Compensation-Out Of Stock topic;
  5. Choose the appropriate canned response, personalize it and send it to the customer.
