Overview


Once a customer has completed the return of their product, refund will be processed by Refunds Team in Magento within 3-5 business days; however, there are instances where a refund is still pending after 5 business days, thus, having refund follow-up tickets from the customer.


This article will help the Customer Service Agents to handle refund follow-up for Product Return.

Checking the refund status in Magento


These are the possible reasons in Magento why customer has not received their refund:

  • Refund status is "Pending" - pending refunds will be refunded by Refunds Team within 3-5 business days
  • Refund status is "Without" - this is not reflected on the pending list of Refunds Team, thus, will not be refunded
  • Refund status is "Error" - an error has occurred in Magento due to some reason and refund has to be made manually
  • Return status is not "Complete" - order is not yet due for refund since the return of the item is not yet complete; this is not reflected on the pending list of Refunds Team, thus, will not be refunded
  • Order has no invoice - product return refund needs an invoice in order to create a credit memo for the refund


If you have received a refund follow-up inquiry from the customer, go to Magento and check the return status.


  • Refund status is "Refunded"

If the refund status of the item returned is already tagged as "Refunded", this means that the refund was already processed and the amount should be received by the customer within 14 business days (except when using a credit card which takes 2-3 weeks).



Please use a market-specific canned response to answer the customer's inquiry, if available.


If the refund is still not received by the customer within 14 business days after the refund was processed in Magento, escalate the ticket to Refunds Emma.


Note: You can see the date when refund was processed in Magento by clicking "Show History" in Actions Column




If a failed refund note is seen on the comments history, escalate the ticket to Refunds Emma.



  • Refund status is "Pending"


Once the product return is created, the default refund status is "Pending". The Refunds Team will process the refund for the pending product return in Magento within 3-5 business days.


If upon checking the refund status in Magento and the refund status is "Pending" for more than 5 business days, there are a few things you should check before escalating the ticket to Refunds Emma.

a. Return status is not yet "Complete"


If the return status not yet "Complete" in Magento, you should check first if the return is already completed. If yes, update the return status in Magento; if the return is not yet done, coordinate the return of the item with the carrier.



Once the return is complete, the refund will be processed in Magento within 3-5 business days and refund will be received by the customer within 14 business days.


b. Refund status is not tagged properly


Refund requests will only be received by the Refunds Team if the refund status is set to "Full Refund"/"Partial Refund" and "Pending". If this is not the case, update the refund status accordingly.


The refund request will be received by the Refunds Team once the status is updated and will be processed within 3-5 business days.




c. Check if the invoice is available


One of the possible reason why the refund is not yet processed is because the invoice is missing for the returned item. If the invoice is not available in Magento, manual creation of invoice is needed. Follow this article on the steps to create an invoice in Magento - Manual Invoice Creation - Magento.




If all of the requirements above are met, Refunds Team will process the refund in Magento within 3-5 business days and there is no need to escalate the ticket.



If the refund is still pending in Magento after 5 business days or the amount is not yet received by the customer after 14 business days (2-3 weeks for credit card orders), escalate the ticket to Refunds Emma and use the Refund Request Template.


  • Refund status is "Error"


For automated refunds in Magento, there are some instances where the refund is not processed successfully from Magento. For these cases, escalate the ticket to Refunds Emma to manually refund the order.



  • Refund status is "Without"

If a refund will be done via bank transfer. If the refund will be coursed through the original mode of payment (not bank transfer), setting the refund status to without will not reflect on the pending list of the Refunds Team, thus, will not be refunded.
