Volume Orders

Volume Orders are big quantity orders (+10 barcodes) to be delivered in the Retailer WH. These are usually arranged with the OPS Team members, so please escalate to OPS Team whenever you receive any request related to Volume Orders.

How to find Volume Orders

Sometimes we need to find Volume Orders to assign a return. In these cases, you can follow the next steps to find one and, in case you don't find it, please escalate to OPS Team.

​

Stock orders can be found in Magento -> Sales > Orders

  • Put “*” and part of the bill name available in Retailers Information | Retail;

  • Select minimum quantity as “6”;

  • Click in “Search” and “Select Visible”

  • In “Actions”, choose “Export Orders” and Submit

The stock orders must have the Shipping Address of a WH and never the Address of a Final Customer.
