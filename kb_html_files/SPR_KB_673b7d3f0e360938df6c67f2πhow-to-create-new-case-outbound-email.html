Introduction

In this article, you can find the step-by-step guide about how to create a new case or an outbound email for when you need to reach out to a customer that doesn't have a case open yet.

How to proceed?

Step 1: Go to the button on the left-side menu and click on Create an Email case:

Step 2: Insert the email addres of the customer, first name and last name.

Step 3: Then, a new page will open with more fields, please fill them accordingly:

SUBJECT: Write down the Subject of your email message.

DESCRIPTION: This is NOT the content of the email. DO NOT write the email here. Leave this option in blank. This will be for the creation of the case, it is not the email to customer yet.

IMPORTANT: You have to write the Email message on the area indicated at the Step 4 below. If you insert the Email Message under "Description", the customer will NOT receive anything.

Step 4: After that, a case will be automatically created and a profile as well (or the case will be automatically assigned to a pre-existent profile). Now is the moment to write the email, and send it to the customer from the contact box as normal.

Note: Please make sure to review and select the correct Account Email address related to your market/line of business from the option "To" address option.
