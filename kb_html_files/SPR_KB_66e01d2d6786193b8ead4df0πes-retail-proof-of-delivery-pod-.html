POD Information

A POD (Proof of Delivery) is a document provided by the carrier to proof that a certain order was delivered.

​

Orders can have more than one POD in case there's more than one Shipment associated to the order. For example:

  • The products were delivered by different carriers;

  • Some items were Out Of Stock but the remaining items were delivered, meanig we'll have more than one shipping.

​

How to download PODs

GLS ES (DBS Shipments)

1. Access the platform here using your credentials: Login (gls-spain.es)


GLS ES (DBS Shipments)

  • Username: EMMA

  • Password: 2023Emma!

​

2. Go to “Localizar Envios”.

​

3. In “Data” delete the dates and in “Referência” insert the order number.

4. Select “Pesquisar” and you will see the status.

5. To see the shipment in more detail, select “Ir”.

6. You will see all the statuses of the shipment over time and the proof of delivery that you can save.

XPO

1. Access the platform here using your credentials: XPO


2. Go to “Encomendas” / "Ordenes" and search for the Order Number.

3. On the left you can check the status of the shipment and its number in blue. On the right you can download the POD.

Note: Below the address, if it says “Real” it shows the actual delivery date; if it shows “ETA” is the estimated delivery date so that the order was not yet delivered.

Missing PODs

Sometimes, we see the order as "Delivered" but no POD is associated. This can mean that the order haven't actually been delivered.

​

In this situation, please open a Child Ticket to the respective carrier and ask for this document.
