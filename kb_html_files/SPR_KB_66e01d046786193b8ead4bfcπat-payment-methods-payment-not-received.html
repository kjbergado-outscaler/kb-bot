Step 1: Request proof of payment

Step 2: After recieving the payment receipt, it must be checked


--> Is the IBAN used by the customer correct? If no, then the customer has to ask there. (We can ask the IBAN of Payolution whether they pay back to the customer)


--> If it was transferred correctly, we look at the date, the account holder, the amount and the reference (sometimes there is also a second order)


If everything fits and the transfer was made a week or more ago, then it must be forwarded to refunds / ticket to back office through Jira
