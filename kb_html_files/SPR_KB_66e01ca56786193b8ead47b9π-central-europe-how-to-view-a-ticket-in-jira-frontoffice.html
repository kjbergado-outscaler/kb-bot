Introduction


In this article you can see step by step how to view a ticket you did not create in the "Escalation - Central Europe" Jira portal.


Step by step


First you go to the Jira portal Escalation - Central Europe - Jira Service Management (atlassian.net).


To view the tickets you go to "Requests" in the top right corner of the page and choose "Escalation - Central Europe Customer".



Now you will see "Escalation - Central Europe Customer" in the dropdown in the middle like in the picture below.

OBS. To view all statuses for tickets, you need to "un-tick" the statuses in the left dropdown called "status"

OBS. personal data have been removed from the picture due to GDPR


Click the search field in the left top corner of the page and search for the ticket number you wish to view.


Please bear in mind, that you can ONLY lookup tickets that start with ECE-




After searching the ticket number you will have visibility to he ticket as below.

OBS. personal data have been removed from the picture due to GDPR


You can view the ticket request and the comments added.
