Introduction

Divide Buy is a Credit platform that allowed its customers to buy products such as Emma products and make the payments by stages, however in 2024, Divide buy was bought by another company and as now started a liquidation process, meaning they no longer have Consumer payment handling capabilities, meaning they cannot manage transactions.

How does this impact you?

This will imply a change on the refund process for Divide Buy (DB) orders dated before April 1st. 2023.

Note: for orders after the 1st April of 2023 the process doesnt change and you will proceed with the refund process as usual.

What is the new process?

Refunds will be processed via bank Transfer.

Information needed: Account Number + Sort Code + Beneficiary Name

Requests in Magento: Should be set to pending only when the IBAN is available so our Refunds Team can proceed with the bank transfer

Requests in Fluentcommerce: Request should be made as "Return without Refund", and a Jira ticket should be opened on the "AR D2C Portal" for a refund request with the bank details of the customer.

Note: This orders will always fall outside the Trial period considering they were opened more then a year ago, so make sure to follow the correct processes when a customer requests a refund.
