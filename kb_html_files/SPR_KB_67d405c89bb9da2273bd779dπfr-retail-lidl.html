CS Responsibilities

Emma’s CS is responsible for assisting end-customers and retailers with complaints, order modifications, returns, and claims. Depending on the type of request, CS may collaborate with retailers and logistics partners to resolve issues.

Communication Flow

  1. Retailer Initiates Contact: The retailer contacts CS to report issues related to orders, claims, or logistics.

  2. CS Investigation: CS verifies the request details, checks for missing information, and assesses whether the issue is valid.

  3. Resolution and Feedback: Depending on the issue, CS processes cancellations, returns, replacements, or refunds. LIDL and customers are informed of the resolution process.

Types of Customer Requests

CS handles multiple types of customer requests, including:

Order Management

  • Where is My Order (WIMO)? CS tracks order status and updates the customer.

  • Cancellations:

    • If the order has not been processed in the warehouse, CS cancels the order in Fluent Commerce and updates the retailer's portal.

    • If the order is with the carrier, CS requests cancellation but advises customers to reject the package on delivery if it is already en route.

  • Order Edits: Changes in address or order details are handled before warehouse processing.

Return Process

  • Regular Returns: Handled by LIDL.

  • Product Claims: Initiated by the retailer with CS involvement.

    • Opened Product: CS requests a carrier pickup and informs the retailer upon return.

    • Sealed Product: CS coordinates pickup, and proof of pickup is provided to the customer.

Claims Processing

LIDL must provide the following information:

  • Reason for return.

  • Order number.

  • Customer details.

  • Photos of the product's label and of the defect.

Product Claims:

  • CS requests additional information when necessary.

  • If approved, CS arranges a replacement or refund.

    • Only customers within the 100-night trial period can request a refund.

  • If rejected, CS informs the retailer with reasons.

Logistics Claims:

  • Claims for damaged products or delivery issues require proof (e.g., photos of damaged goods).

    • CS investigates and provides resolutions such as replacements or refunds.

You can find the claim pictures requirements and examples here and how to validate the claim here.

Old Mattress Removal

LIDL manages 100% of the old mattress removal process, requiring no action from CS.

Resolution Timelines

  • Cancellation Requests: Up to 24 hours for confirmation.

  • Returns: May take up to a week to process.

  • Product Claims: Processed within 7 to 10 business days.

  • Logistics Claims: Processed as per investigation timelines.

Escalation Process

If CS cannot resolve a complaint within the standard timelines, the case is escalated to senior support teams or management. Customers and LIDL are updated throughout the process.
