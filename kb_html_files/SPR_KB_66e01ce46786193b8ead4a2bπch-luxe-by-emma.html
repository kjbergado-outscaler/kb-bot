Summary of the product


The product Luxe by Emma is a luxurious mattress that will transform the sleep experience, since it's made from the finest materials and crafted with expert craftsmanship. This new product has a comfortable ViscoPore foam topper that conforms perfectly to the contours of your body. This foam topper responds to body heat and weight and molds accordingly to provide optimal pressure relief. You can count on sleeping in an ergonomic position every night.

Principal benefits:


Innovative EvoPoreHRC foam cube springs:

Foam-spring technology combines the benefits of foam and spring structures. This means you can enjoy the comfort and support of foam while experiencing the bounce and responsiveness of a traditional innerspring mattress.


Optimal sleep climate:

EvoPoreHRC foam features a high level of breathability. The foam features open cells that effectively wick away air and moisture, ensuring a comfortable sleeping environment. The air channels promote air circulation and allow effective ventilation.

Layer specifications:


1. Emma Premium cover: The extra padded mattress cover and highly breathable fabric makes the cover feel softer than ever. Plus, the premium cover keeps you cool and comfortable all night long.

3. Premium comfort foam surface: Premium comfort foam with cube-cut technology provides perfect pressure relief.


4. innovative foam spring technology: EvoPoreHRC foam spring layer creates an unparalleled sleep experience that provides the perfect balance of comfort and support.


5. HRX Foam® minimizes back pain: It's designed to guarantee you the support you need every night for years to come. It also absorbs movement, letting you sleep through the night without worry.
