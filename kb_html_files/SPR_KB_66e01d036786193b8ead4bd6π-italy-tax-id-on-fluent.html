TAX ID FLUENT


In this article you will have a step by step guide on how to add the Tax ID (Codice Fiscale) on Fluent when the customer forgot to insert it.



How to add the Tax ID


It can happen that at the moment of check out, the customer forgets to insert the Tax ID (Codice Fiscale) and contacts the support to add it at a later date. This happens mainly because they realize they need their Tax ID on the invoice in order to benefit from the Tax deduction.


For Fluent, is still not possible to add this directly in the system as we do in Magento, however, we have an alternative solution:

By inserting in this file the order number and the Tax ID, the system will generate a new invoice and (if applicable) the Medical PDF. 


Once you added the info in the file, inform the customer that their invoice and medical PDF (where applicable) will be sent to their email in the next 24/48 hrs.
