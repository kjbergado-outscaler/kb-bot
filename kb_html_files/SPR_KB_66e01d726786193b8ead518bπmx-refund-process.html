Introduction

In this article you can find the following steps of How to Request a Refund to the EmmaRefundsTeam

Process to request a Refund D2C


1. Place the tag “Refunds Emma” when placing the first word you can verify that the correct tag will appear in the list, then you have to click on update to save the change.


2. Place private notes in English, the agent in charge should request the Refund as the following example: (at the end of this article, you can find the template) and save the changes.



3. The ticket must be assigned to the "Refund Emma" team and save.




To the Refunds with more than 60 days after the customer has made their purchase, we must request the following information to the customer(in addition to the template) and pass it on to the Refunds team:
  • Order:
  • Customer name:
  • Bank:
  • Bank account:


It`s important to mention to the customer that the refund will be made by MercadoPago in the payment method they had used in the purchase. In case it was a transfer or cash payment it will be necessary to create an account in their platform and they will be able to transfer to another account that they prefer.

Walmart / Linio / Amazon

Raised the ticket to 3rd Level, requesting the refund.



The agent must inform to the customer that their Refund will return to the payment method they used in the Marketplace, at the time of the purchase


Liverpool

If you receive a notification from the liverpool operator in Mirakl, indicating that the customer wants to cancel the purchase, the ticket can be escalated to 3rd Level, if the order has already been delivered to the customer, you must indicate that the return and refunds are managed by Liverpool and that you should contact your Customer Service Center directly.

Registration of Refunds In Request Order File


It is necessary to fill in the section of the Excel file, ReturnProductRefund-MX section.


Request order.xlsx


The Refunds section must be filled out by the CS team:


Refund Request Date: Day the refund is requested

Refund Request Full or Partial Refund

Partial Refund If it is a partial refund, indicate the amount

Date Refunded day that the Refund Team sends the proof of refund.

Refund Status: Mark as Completed once the Refunds area sends the refund proof.
