Summary of Product


The Letto Conentitore Emma Premium is available with or without a headboard


USPs

  • Excellent quality, at a more affordable price

  • An easy, space-saving design

  • Locally made and produced in Italy

  • Customizable

    • multiple color and textile options (microfiber grey and beige, or ecoleather grey and white)

    • option to come with or without a headboard



Product Specification

Overview

Bed base with upholstered headboard and 18 cm of storage space


Front opening with cushioned lift system and net included


The bed comes in 5 or 6 packages, with step-by-step assembly instructions in digital
