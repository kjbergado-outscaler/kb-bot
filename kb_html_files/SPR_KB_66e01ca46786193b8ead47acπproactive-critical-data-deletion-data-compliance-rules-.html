Introduction


On occasion, it can happen that a customer sends us information that is delicate in nature (credit card photos, credit card numbers, bank statements, identity card photos, social security card photos, etc). As this is not something we want to be held in our system, we need to take proactive action quickly to remove it.


Attention


* This article does not apply to DSR requests made by individuals asking Emma to delete all data we have on them. For those cases, read this article: here *


* These guidelines are applicable when we, Emma, want to proactively delete something we received from a customer that is of a sensitive nature in terms of data. *


How to request for critical data to be deleted?


Step 1: Go to JIRA

  • Go to JIRA and access the IT Portal and select the option "CE GDPR/Data Deletion": HERE


Step 2: Select the type of ticket

  • Once in JIRA, select Contact Center Request.


Step 3: Fill in all the required information

  • Subject: "Critical data deletion on case #XXXXXXX" (replace with the case number)

  • Contact Center: Insert the code of the Contact Center partner you work for.

  • Other Request: Select "Account Issues".

  • Which account has an issue?: Select either "Freshchat", "Freshdesk" or both, depending on where the sensitive data is located.

  • Describe your issue/request: Here you should provide a brief description of the issue and request the deletion of the data/pictures in question.
