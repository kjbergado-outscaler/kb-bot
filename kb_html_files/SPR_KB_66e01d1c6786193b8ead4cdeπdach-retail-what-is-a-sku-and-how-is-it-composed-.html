What is a SKU?SKU stands for "Stock Keeping Unit" and is a composition of numbers and letters which describe an individual article in the catalog of our magento system.
How is it composed?Let's describe the composition with the following SKU example:

​

​​

​

1) D - Dunlopillo or E - Emma

Please be aware that we no longer sell products under the Dunlopillo brand.


2) There are different abbreviations for the several product categories. Examples:

MA - mattressPW - pillowSF - slatted frame

CV - Cover

TP - Topper

3) The Name of the single product or the product group where the article belongs to. Examples: SA - (Dunlopillo) Smart AdaptFH - (Dunlopillo) Fusion HelixDD - (Dunlopillo) Diamond DegreeEL - (Dunlopillo) ElementsOR - (Emma) OriginalON - (Emma) One
4) The Width of the product
5) The Length of the product
6) These three letters/numbers give the possibility to show additional information about the product - for example the hardness of a mattress or the size of a bundle product.
