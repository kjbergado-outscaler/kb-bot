Introduction

This article shows which scenarios to transfer cases and how to do this action in Sprinklr.

When to transfer a case?

You can transfer a case in 3 scenarios:

- Case from a Line of Business that you do not support;

- Case from a Market that you do not support;

- Case from a Language that you do not support.

IMPORTANT: If you need to transfer the case, but it is related to an Emma Store topic, please follow the instructions in the article "How to: Transfer Cases to Emma Store agents".

How to transfer cases?

IMPORTANT:

We DO NOT transfer the case to another agent even if the customer is asking to speak with another colleague or if the case was previous handled by a colleague. When you receive the case, you have the onwership to handle it from now onwards. Make sure that you review the previous interactions and get a understanding of what was happen before.

DISCLAIMER: We don't have all Markets available on Sprinklr yet. This means we don't have all CS agents using this tool so in certain cases, you cannot transfer the case in Sprinklr. Please check this article to know how to proceed per market and line of business.

After confirming that it is indeed possible to transfer the case inside Sprinklr, please follow the next steps according to the channel:

​

1) For Non-Voice cases (Email, Live chat, Social media):

Step 1: Apply Macro

It is mandatory to transfer a case via the option "Apply macro", that you can find in the 3 dots button in the window from the case conversation.

Step 2: Select the macro named "Change LOB/Market/Language or unassign"

Step 3: Fill the properties accordingly to the transfer you need.

The fields "Market" and "Line of business" are MANDATORY. And the fields "Change Channel Type" and "Emma Store" are only to be used when transfering a Emma Store case (please refer to this article).

​

2) For Voice cases (Phone calls):

If you have to transfer a call to other queues, always inform the customer about it and choose the option "warm transfer" (customer is put on hold and you talk to the person before transferring the call).

​

Note: Make sure to check our Communication Guidelines: HERE

​

  • Step 1: You have to handle the customer phone call and explain to the customer that their call needs to be transfer to the correct line and for this they will be placed on hold and need to wait on the line.

    After this, click on the Transfer button on the Voice Console;

    ​

  • Step 2: Make sure the option "Internal" is selected;

    ​

  • Step 3: Go to "Select Queue" and choose the line related to the market/language of the customer.

  • Step 4: Click on "Warm transfer" option as you must inform your colleague about the transfer.

    ​

Amazon cases: how to handle?

How to understand that the case is about an Amazon Order

Look for these signs in the customer’s message:

  • Mentions “Amazon” directly.

  • Order number/invoice format does not match Emma D2C.

  • Refers to order in the Amazon app/website.

  • Email, invoice, or attachments show Amazon branding.

  • States the purchase was made via Amazon marketplace, not emma-sleep.com.

If any of these apply, classify the case as an Amazon case and send a reply using the template below, adust to your market language.

"Dear customer,

 

Thank you for contacting Emma. 

 

After looking into your contact, it looks like your order was not placed through our webstore but directly through Amazon.

 

Please note that, for Amazon orders, due to our system setup, customer support is handled directly through Amazon's app or website. As such, if your order was placed through them, we would kindly ask you to make your support request there, so that we are able to help you.

 

Thank you for your understanding on this matter. We look forward to assist you with your request through the Amazon platform, and we truly appreciate your patience and collaboration on this matter."

Agent Action After Sending Template

  • Close the case.

  • Do not trigger CSAT (as the resolution is redirected to Amazon support)
