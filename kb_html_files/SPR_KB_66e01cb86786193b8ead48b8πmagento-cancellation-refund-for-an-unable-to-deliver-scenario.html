Please be aware this is a workaround for Magento - and should not be used for scenarios in Fluent Commerce. For Fluent Commerce Unable to deliver scenarios, ensure you follow this article: Late Cancellation & Refuse at door cases - Submit a Cancellation request





When delivering orders to the customers, our delivery companies may face scenarios that unfortunately, we are unable to deliver the item to the customer.


Some companies will try to reship the items, or communicate with the customer directly to resolve the situation. But by the end, there are parcels that will ended up returned to the warehouse.



Please be aware this situation is not a return, as the order was never received by the customer and we are not triggering a return to pick up an item. This is an unable to deliver scenario.



If you need to trigger a refund to the customer on those scenarios, exceptionally, in Magento, you will trigger a return by using Other reasons and adding the relative comment as per description bellow.
