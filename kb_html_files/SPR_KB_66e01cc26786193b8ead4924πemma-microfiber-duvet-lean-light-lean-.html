Characteristics/USP



Temperature regulation and moisture release (breathable)

Thanks to the hollow fiber, the fillings, (this fiber got its name from the construction and is inspired by animals living in the cold area, such as polar bears), the duvet stays cool in hot conditions. It acts as an insulator in cold temperatures, which means that there is no more sweaty night but comfy sleep.


Lightweight with springy and lofty feelings

It feels like snuggled by a cotton candy cloud when wrapping yourself with the duvet. The spiral structure (fillings) and exclusive quilting patterns (fabric), provides optimal airflow and springy feelings, and help you plunge into the deepest of sleep.


Hypoallergenic and anti-dust mite beddings

The duvet is made of synthetic fiber, making it suitable for sensitive sleepers and allergy-prone who prefer to avoid feather or down duvets. Moreover, the duvet can be washed at 60 degrees (the ideal temperature for killing dust mites and bacteria), keeping it free from bacteria, dust mites, and dirt.


Washable at 60 degrees Celsius

Customers could maintain hygiene easily, you can put the entire duvet in the washing machine, and dry it with a low tumble. On top of that, due to the unique pattern, after washing it, your duvet will remain fluffy as brand new.


The life expectancy of this product is about 7 years. There is no guarantee from Emma.


Material


Cover fabric: Microfiber 83g/m3 - 100% Polyester

Thanks to the quilt, the cover keeps its shape longer. Basically, the hollow fiber cannot go out from where they are. This guarantees that the temperature under the cover will be everywhere the same and that the breathability will be ideal.



Filling: Hollowfibre - 100% Polyester
