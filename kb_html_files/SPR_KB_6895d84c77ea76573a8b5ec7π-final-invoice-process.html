1. Global Process and Workflow

Global Process and Workflow / Market specifics: Is your market present? Yes: Click your Market tab, ignore Global Process. No: refer to the Global Process and Workflow.

The invoice of a purchase is issued when the status of the order is changed to DELIVERED.

​

It is an automated process overseen by our finance team.

​

If the order is yet to be delivered - there is no way to issue an invoice in advance. Customer will have to wait due to financial requirements. The invoice will be sent directly to the customer' order email.

​

  1. Access the order page

  2. In Fluent Commerce scroll down to the Notifications section.

  3. You can advise the customer to check their inbox (including spam/junk folders) based on the timestamp shown in the Notifications section.

  4. You can also resend the invoice. To do this go to the 'Related' tab on the order page and click on RESEND EMAIL.

1.2 Invoice Process Related Topics

Friend referral is a loyalty campaign we have available to all of our customers.

​

What is needed from your side?

From your side, we will only need you to know some basic information about this process in case the customer reaches out and raises some questions about the program.

​

How it Works

  1. Upon finishing the purchase process, the customer will be informed about the referral program and the benefits for both parties.

  2. The customer in this case will be considered the "Advocate" and will create a personalized code for their referral code.

  3. Option 1: To use the code: By Link sharing, the Advocate can send their friends the referral link. NOTE: Not available in FR.

  4. Option 2: Advocate personalized code. Customer proceeds with order and before checkout, they select the option "Been Refered by a friend.

Q: Are there any amount restrictions?

A: Not at the moment, and we don’t expect this to change.

​

Q: The customer is experiencing issues — where should we escalate?

A: Please collect as much relevant information as possible and ask your supervisor to raise the issue with the Emma Internal Team.

​

Q: The customer's discount code isn’t working — can I generate a new one?

A: No. These codes are generated by our partner’s tool and must be tracked by the Internal Teams. Please do not create a manual code.

​

Q: Does the discount code apply to bundles?

A: This depends on the market. If this question comes up, raise it with your supervisor for further guidance.

Overview: Invoices should automatically be created in Magento once the products have been shipped; however, there are instances where the invoice is not available in Magento and the order requires to be refunded via product return (i.e. late cancellation).

​

One of the critical requirements for an order to be refunded is for the order to have an invoice. The reason for this is that a credit memo (refund) will be charged against the invoice of the order. If an order has no invoice, refund will not be successful.

​

Capture Online means that the order will also captured in the Payment Method website to trigger the charging to customers.

​

Capture Offline means that invoice will be created only in Magento. Some other PSPs do not need orders to be captured in PSP to initiate the charge to customers.

​

​

There are instances that invoice can be captured online. Invoice can be captured online if these payment methods were used by the customer: Example: Rechnungskauf (Payolution/Unzer) Klarna Pagarme Credit Card PayPal Express (AU).

Note: If capture online fails, create the invoice via capture offline.

​

Reminder: Only shipped/delivered products should be invoiced.

​

  1. Open the order in Magento. Click Invoice. You will be routed to invoice page.

  2. On the lower right side of the Invoice page on Amount field, choose Capture Offline on the dropdown.

  3. Once Capture Offline has been chosen, click Submit Invoice.

  4. You will see a notification that the invoice has been created.

  5. Invoice tab will show the invoice that you've created. It usually takes 2-3 minutes before the invoice reflect in Magento; you will need to refresh the page for the invoice to show.
