Summary of the product


The Emma Portable Topper was designed as a solution for enhanced comfort and improved sleep quality. With its low-height design and transportation grips, Portable Topper is perfect for occasional comfort on guest beds, sofas, or while traveling.

USPs (Unic Selling Points)


Affordable comfort
  • Customer gets a good quality for a fair price;
  • The product is built around providing essential comfort and fulfills the basic expectations of a customer.
Rollable: easy to pack and store
  • Elastic bands to roll and store the topper if it not used;
  • Easy to store: Customer can store the topper rolled somewhere which uses much less space compared to folding it;
  • Easy to transport: Customer can take the topper with them (for traveling or semi-permanent airbnb’s/short-term stays).

Temperature (Breathability – Cover)
  • Every night your body loses fluid – that’s why the cover of the Emma topper is particularly breathable – so your body can release excess energy in the form of heat, and you keep a pleasantly dry sleep climate;
  • Don’t wake up sweating.

FAQs

Q. Where can I use my Emma One Topper?

The Emma Portable topper is versatile and can be used on different surfaces such as beds, sofa beds, tents, RVs, and more. Its easy portability makes it an ideal option for travelers or anyone who moves frequently.


Q. Can I use the Emma One Topper for camping or outdoor activities?

In any case! The Emma Portable Topper is a great option for camping or outdoor activities as it is easy to carry and can be used on a variety of surfaces. It can provide a comfortable sleeping pad for tents, RVs, and more.


Q. How do I store an Emma One topper?

The Emma Portable Topper can be easily rolled up with the elastic straps attached and stored in its compact carry case for convenient transportation and storage when not in use.


For the double size Topper, it must be folded in half first and then rolled it.


Q. How do I clean an Emma One Topper?

To clean the topper, wash it by hand with detergent at a maximum of 30 degrees Celsius. Avoid bleaching, tumble drying or ironing as this may cause damage or color loss.


Q. How is an Emma One topper different from a traditional mattress topper?

Designed with the idea of being mobile, the Emma Portable Topper is easy to transport and use in different locations. It often comes with carrying handles and bags for added convenience. Traditional mattress toppers are meant to be used on a specific bed and are not that easy to move.


Q. Is the Emma One Topper suitable for people with allergies?

In any case! The Emma Portable Topper is a safe option for those with allergies or sensitive skin due to its hypoallergenic outer fabric. This fabric is made from a blend of polyester and viscose and has an OEKO-TEX certificate.


Q. How long does it take for the Emma One Topper to fully unfold after unpacking?

Once unpacked, it typically takes around 24 hours for the Emma One Topper to fully unfold and regain its original shape and fullness.
