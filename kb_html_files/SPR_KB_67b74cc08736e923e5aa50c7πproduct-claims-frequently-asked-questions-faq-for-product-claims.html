Mattresses or Toppers should have 3 Product Labels, one on the core, one on the foil and one on the box, they all look the same. If none of them is available, the customer should take a picture of the Cover Label. 

Beds or Slatted Frames should have a Product Label on every Packaging Box and an additional Label on the main assembly parts (Bed Base, Headboard, Slatted Frame). The customer should take a picture of one of it.

​

​

Foam Pillows should have 2 Product Labels, one on the foam product and one on the Cover, both need to be provided. If the customer cannot find them, they should take a picture of the Label on the Box. 

Microfiber Products, Bedding Sheets, Smart Sleeves and Weighted Blankets should have 1 Product Label, on the Product itself. If the customer cannot find them, they should take a picture of the Label on the Box. 
 

All information from the customer about the defect will be used by the manufacturing supplier to identify the root cause and implement preventive actions.

The picture of the label is needed to verify and hold the supplier accountable for their products. If we do not have pictures to forward to the supplier we cannot improve the quality and more importantly cannot claim back the money from our manufacturing suppliers for defective products.
