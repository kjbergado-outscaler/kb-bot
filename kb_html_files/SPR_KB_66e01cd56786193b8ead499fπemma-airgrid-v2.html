Summary of the product


The Emma AirGrid mattress has the AirGrid technology, which consists of an instantly adapting and rebounding comfort layer made from a super-stretchy material. This product is developed to give our customers a weightless sleep experience and has 2 performance features: maximum pressure relief and thermoregulation.


This V2 Mattress no longer has a foam layer around the spring layer, but instead uses the new edge-to-edge spring mattress.

USPs (Unique Selling Points)

  • Thermoregulation: Keeps you cooler than all the other mattresses. AirGrid is cool to the touch, ensuring the perfect temperature for a restful sleep.​ 

  • Breathability: Thanks to the integrated air pockets, air circulation is improved and excess heat is dissipated. 

  • Pressure Relief: Experience pressure relief that ensures free movement with optimal support and motion isolation, all while being gentle on your skin. 

  • Innovative Technology​: The AirGrid Technology is crafted from hyper-elastic polymer, this super-stretchy honeycomb structure instantly adapts and rebounds, creating a layer that feels soft and cool on your skin, ensuring superior comfort and perfect temperature all night long. 

Durability: Keeps its shape over the time. Made from an elastic material that is more sustainable than foam. Guarantee: Mattress -10 years, Accessories – 2 years.​
