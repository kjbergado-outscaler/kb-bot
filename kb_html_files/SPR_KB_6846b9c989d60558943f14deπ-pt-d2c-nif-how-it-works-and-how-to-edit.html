NIF on customer side - How it works

Customer of the PT webshop are now able to add NIF on checkout:

This information will go on the order confirmation email in the order confirmation attachment and also on the invoice attached with the shipment email.

​

How to edit the NIF on invoice

​

On occasion, the customer may forget to add the NIF on checkout, or intend to correct the NIF on the invoice provided. We can't deny this change to the customer if they make the request.

​

In Order for this to be possible, AR Team must cancel the original invoice and issue a Credit Note and new invoice.

​

To proceed:

  • Request or obtain the correct NIF from customer;

  • Open Jira to ARD2C Team - Incorrect Invoice - AR D2C Portal (formerly Order-to-Cash) - Jira Service Management.

  • Ensure to use the following information when opening the Jira:

    • [PT Market - NIF Correction] on the Summary

    • On the description, clarify if new NIF, or wrong NIF being corrected to a new one.

    • Fill up the remaining fields accordingly.


Editing or adding NIF numbers will have no impact on older orders.
