This article should be used by QAs and agents to assess if the Return Deflection process was followed correctly

Note: Only for products in trial period

Step-by-step process

1. Understand return reason

Relevant questions:

  • What expectations about the product were unmet?​

  • How long have you been testing the product? ​

  • What kind of discomfort is it about?​

  • What is the source of the discomfort? ​

  • What can we do to convince the customer to stay with and trust the product?​

Note:

If the return reason is related to a manufactoring issue, please follow the Product Claim Process.

2. After the customer clearly indicated his return reason, perform Verbal Return Deflection.

​

How to do it?

Explanations about how the product work, harmonization phase; muscular adaptation; the smell is harmfull and disappears quickly.

Find more Deflection Arguments here and here 'Making impact on Returns'

3. ONLY for mattresses in UK & IE, DE & AT & CH, FR

​

Comfort layers are a way to help customers who are unhappy with their mattresses. If a customer says their mattress is too soft, you can offer a firm comfort layer. If they find it too firm, suggest a soft comfort layer.

Remember, offering a comfort layer costs the company money, so use this option wisely to avoid returns.

How to do it?

Before you offer a comfort layer to deflect the return for the customer, please check the availability of comfort layers. Follow these steps:

1. Go to Inventory in Fluent Commerce

2. Choose Virtual Catalogue 

3. Choose your market

4. Check if the relevant firm/soft SKU is in stock

​

5. Order the right SKU, share information with the customer.
