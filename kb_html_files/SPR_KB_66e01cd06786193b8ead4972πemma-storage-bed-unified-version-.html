Materials


Bed frame and headboard

  • Fabric: 100% polyester nonwoven back fabric
  • Upholstery: 100% polyester 200gsm wadding
  • Headboard Construction: 15mm Chipboard Core, 25mm PU foam (UK: 35 kg/m^3 CMHR Foam, Euro: 23 kg/m^3 PU), 200gsm wadding
  • Frame Construction: 22mm Chipboard Core side and footboard, 40mm chipboard core backboard


Slatted Frame

  • Steel frame, machined holes for slats
  • Wooden sprung slats (7mm thickness). 34 total slats for 200cm length, 32 slats for 190cm length
  • Plastic Slat caps and corner connectors

Assembly


It requires 2 person and it takes aprox. 1h and 20 min. to be completed. Tools are included in the hardware kit of each product. Mallet is helpful but not required.


Please note:

  • Slatted frame must be assembled by the customer.
  • There are two mattress stoppers for 160 and 180 beds.
  • Slatted frame comes in one piece, however pistons and lifting mechanism must be assembled by the customer. There is only one mattress stopper per slatted frame for all bed sizes.
  • Both of the above slatted frames come with the same 1150 N pistons


IMPORTANT: the slatted frame must NOT be forcibly closed without the mattress! The mattress should be put on top of the open frame, then the bed can be closed by applying pressure on top of the mattress foot-end (not by pulling the handle)
