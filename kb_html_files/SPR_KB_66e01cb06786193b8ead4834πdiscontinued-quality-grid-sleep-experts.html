7. Returns - Solution





Deflection (O)Successful / Tried Everything / Jumped Steps / Incorrect Start / Fail / NAThe agent first tries to deflect the customer verbally and, only if this failed, moved forward with other deflection options.

Deflection order:
Verbal deflection > discount deflection > product deflection > retention.

Successful: Agent was successful in deflection, independently of the deflection order he used.
Tried Everything: Agent was unsuccessful in deflection but tried all 4 types of deflection.
Jumped Steps: Agent was unsuccessful in deflection, started with verbal deflection but then moved straight to product deflection or retention.
Incorrect Start: Agent was unsuccessful, did not start with verbal deflection and did not attempt all types of deflection.

Notes: For products with no alternative options for cross-selling or upselling, agents are only required to do verbal deflection to have a full pass (Successful) on this metric.		Agent does not try to deflect the customer's return.Not applicable for cases concerning pre-sales.

Also not applicable when from the outset of the call, the customer says 2 times clearly that there is no chance of changing his mind.
