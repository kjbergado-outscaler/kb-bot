Unsuccessful Delivery

When the carrier reports delivery issues, we need to carefully assess the case and take appropriate action. There are three main steps to address the situation:

  1. Update Customer Contact Information: If the issue is related to contact details, update the customer’s information and request that the carrier reach out before the next delivery attempt.

  2. Change Delivery Address to Parcel Shop: If updating contact information does not resolve the issue, change the delivery address to a nearby parcel shop.

  3. Request Return to Warehouse: As a last resort, if both of the previous steps have been attempted and the issue persists, request the product to be returned to our warehouse.

Here, you’ll learn how to do each of these steps correctly.

Start by clicking on the “Meine Aktionliste” option.

​

​

  • Select the Case: A list of open cases will appear. Click on the case you need to work on. The oldest are at the bottom, so we have to go from the bottom up.

​

​

  • Analyze the Issue: Review the details of the delivery issue. For example, if the carrier reports an inability to locate the correct doorbell, this could indicate a problem with the contact information or address.

​

Updating Customer Contact Information

If the issue is related to incorrect or missing contact information, follow these steps:

  • Copy the Fulfillment Number.

​

​

  • Open FluentCommerce and select Fulfillments.

​

 

  • Apply Filter: Add a filter for Ref and paste the fulfillment number.

    (Note: It may take a few seconds for the page to load.)

​

  • Locate Tracking Information: Once the page opens, search for the corresponding tracking label number.

​

​

  • Important: The tracking number in the GLS portal may sometimes be missing the last digit.

​ /

​

 

  • Verify the Order: Cross-check the tracking number to ensure it matches the order. Once confirmed, click Back to Order → Attributes.

​

​

  • Update Contact Information:

    • Look for the customer's phone number and/or email address.

​

​

  • In the GLS portal click on Zusatzinformation mittellen (Provide additional information), add the updated contact information and kindly request the carrier to reach out to the customer prior to the next delivery attempt.

​

​

 

  • Document in FluentCommerce:

    • Add a comment to the FluentCommerce case, including the following details:

      • Tracking number

      • Carrier's name

      • Explanation of the issue and the action taken.
        Example:
        “Carrier could not find the correct doorbell. Updated customer contact details and requested the carrier to reach out before the next delivery attempt.”

Important: If the order is a truck order, this procedure needs to be repeated for each item in the shipment. Additionally, the carrier may sometimes send requests via email or through Sprinklr cases, rather than through the platform.

​

Change Delivery Address to Parcel Shop

If updating the contact information doesn’t resolve the issue, you can change the delivery address to a nearby parcel shop. Follow these steps:

Find a Parcel Shop:

  • Click on this link to search for the closest parcel shop to the customer’s address.

    • Enter the customer’s address, and the map will display the closest available parcel shops. You’ll see the customer’s address signaled in the middle, and the closest shops around it.

​

Check for Weight Limitations:

Important: Some shops have weight restrictions, so before selecting a parcel shop, ensure it can accommodate the package's weight.

  • Change the Delivery Address:

    • In the GLS portal, click on Addressänderung (Change Address).

    • Enter the parcel shop's address and complete the form.

​

​

 

Add Shop Hours:

  • In the Comments section, be sure to include the parcel shop’s working hours for the carrier’s reference.

Request Return to Warehouse

If the above steps don’t resolve the issue, the final option is to request the product to be returned to the warehouse.

  1. Initiate the Return: In the GLS portal, click on Paket-Retoure (Package Return).

  2. Follow the Steps: Complete the return process as per the system instructions
