JYSK

JYSK is a chain of stores distributed throughout Europe, and the partnership is governed according to the conditions agreed upon in the contract at the European level.

  • There are no 100 test nights.

  • All orders should be created by the stores in their internal system, including POS Orders. They should consult internally; it is not Emma's responsibility to create these orders.

How to find JYSK orders in Magneto

  • Make sure the "Store" field is empty, as it will be associated with the DE website and not ES, depending on when the order was created

  • Search by e-mail JyskDS_emma_ES@example.de

  • Search for order using the number "EXXX" under "Ship Name
