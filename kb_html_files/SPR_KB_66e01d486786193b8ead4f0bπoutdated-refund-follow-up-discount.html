Overview


If a discount and/or a compensation was applied in Magento, refund will be processed in Magento within 3-5 business days; however, there are instances where a refund is still pending after 5 business days, thus, having refund follow-up tickets from the customer.


This article will help the Customer Service Agents to handle refund follow-up for Discount.

Check the Refund Status in Magento


If you have received a refund follow-up inquiry from the customer, go to Magento and check the refund status of the discount.


You can check the refund status by clicking the discount button on the upper right of order page.



You will be directed to the create discount page and it will show the status of the existing discounts applied to the order.




Another way to check the discount status is by going to Catalog > Product Returns > Discounts > All > then search for the Order Number in the Order column. The discount status can be seen on the Status column.



Handling of the refund follow-up depends on refund status of the discount.


  • Pending

If the discount refund status is "Pending" for more than 5 business days, escalate the ticket to Refunds Emma; otherwise, wait for the refund to be processed by Refunds Team within 3-5 business days.


  • Processing

If the discount refund status is "Processing", this will be refunded in Magento within the day. The customer will then receive the discount amount within 14 business days (2-3 weeks for credit card orders) depending on the payment method used. (Refer to the table below for the refund timeline.)


  • Waiting for Invoice

If the discount refund status is "Waiting for Invoice", refund will be processed once the order is shipped and the invoice is created in Magento. If the order is already shipped and the invoice is still not available, you can create the invoice manually in Magento. Follow this article on the steps to create an invoice in Magento - Manual Invoice Creation - Magento.


  • Edit requested

If the discount refund status is "Edit requested", an email was sent to the person who created the discount for the additional information or edit needed for the discount to be processed. The discount has to be edited in Magento by the person who created the discount and then the refund to go back to "Pending" status.


If editing the discount is not possible in Magento, you can create a new discount request in Magento and have the old discount request deleted by the Refunds Manager by escalating a ticket to Refunds Emma.


  • Refund Failed

If the discount refund status is "Refund Failed", escalate the ticket to Refunds Emma so the manual refund will be processed.


  • Complete

If the discount refund status is "Complete", the refund of the discount was already done and the customer should have received the refund after 14 business days (2-3 weeks for credit card orders) depending on the payment method used. (Refer to the table below for the refund timeline.)


If the customer still hasn't received the refund after 14 business days after the refund was completed in Magento, escalate the ticket to Refunds Emma.




No need to escalate all refund follow-up ticket to Refunds Emma as long as all of the requirements are met. If all the requirements are met and the refund is still pending after 5 business days, you may send a follow-up escalation ticket to Refunds Emma.
