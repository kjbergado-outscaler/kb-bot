Summary of the product


The difference between the Customizable Microfiber pillow and this new Cooling version is the thermoregulator cover, which is a layer on one side of the product.




Pictures


USPs


  • The cover has innovative Outlast cooling technology. It feels very fresh to the touch and gives long-lasting temperature regulation.

  • The pillow has a fluffier feel compared to the memory foam pillow but still offers support for the head and neck.

  • The pillow has a lightly padded cover and two removable micro elastic layers. You can decide whether you want to use one or two layers and adjust the pillow to your needs.

  • It is good for people with allergies. It is very easy to clean as you can put everything in the washing machine (vs the foam core that cannot go in the washing machine). Washing the pillow keeps it free from bacteria, dust mites and dirt.

  • Lightweight (vs Foam pillow)

  • Fits every season -> temperature regulation

  • It stays cool in hot conditions and acts as an insulator in cold temperatures.



Product Specifications


Material:


Fabric

  • Cover fabric: Microfiber 100g/m3 - 100% Polyester with Outlast cooling treatment on one side for a refreshing feeling.

  • Filling: siliconized Micro-elastic fiber - 100% Polyester

Advantages of Micro-elastic/microfiber:

  • Non-abrasive

  • Hygienic

  • Durable

  • Soft to the touch

  • Can be treated with anti-bacterial chemicals

  • Lightweight

  • Water-repellent

  • Water-absorbing

  • Long-lasting if cared for properly


It is not written on the 2 inner pillows which one has less filling than the other one. If you touch them, you can find it out easily.


Care and Label:


Brand Label: On one side a brand label is sewn in.


Care label

  • Wash at a maximum of 60 degrees Celsius.

  • Do not bleach. It could lose color or be damaged during bleaching.

  • Do not iron

  • Cleaning with Perchloroethylene. Use laundry detergent.

  • Low tumble dry

If you don´t use the dryer, make sure that the microfibers are well spread on the top and bottom of the duvet. Let the duvet dry on a plane surface. We recommend washing the cover 6 to 8 times during its whole life.


Certifications


  • EU: Öko Tex Class II 19.0.91049 (Class I for baby products) OEKO-Tex® 100 certified quality
