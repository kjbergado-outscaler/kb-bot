Introduction


As mentioned in the article WIMO - Deliver is Delay, after the Carrier has the items of the customer's order some circumstances can cause the delivery to be completed successfully. In that stage, as it's the Last Mile moment, we consider those Delay issues under Unable to Deliver. In this article, we will see what causes an order to not be delivered correctly and what actions you can take accordingly.



Why the order was not delivered successfully?


A few situations can happen upon delivery that can result in the product(s) not being received by the customers. Those are the 4 reasons of Unable to Delivery scenarios:


  • Address issue;

  • No answer from customer;

  • Lost Parcel;

  • Rejected parcel (due to delay; or advised by CS (late cancellation); or Parcel damaged).


How to solve Unable to Deliver issues?




Address Issue


This may happen due to the customer's address being incomplete or incorrect, resulting in the carrier company not being able to find the location and complete the delivery. In those cases:

  • If it's a 1MH carrier (accessories and mattresses <20kg), they will inform the customer via email that it wasn’t possible to deliver the order. If this happens, they are going to send the order to the nearest parcel shop (information is sent by email or SMS).

    • If the customer doesn't pick up in a specific period, then the item is going to be returned to sender.

  • If it's a 2MH carrier (beds and mattresses >20kg), the carrier always tries to contact the customer directly via phone number and email in order to solve the issue and finish the delivery, or re-scheduling a new date. If no feedback from the customer, proceed as follows:

    • The carrier will email the CS team to report the address issue;

    • CS agent tries to contact de customer to ask for the complete address; (Canned Responses B4.1)

    • CS agent need to send a reply maximum in the next 72h or delivery will be canceled:

      • If we have information from customer, CS agent replies to carrier email with the new address;

      • If no reply from customer, CS agents reply to the carrier informing no info and asking them to cancel the shipment and to return the items.

    • Once the Order appears with the shipment as canceled on the system, CS agent can proceed with the refund to customer.


​

No answer from customer

In this scenario we have an unresponsive customer, that is not answering the door and/or neither the phone. This can happen due to carriers not having the correct phone number of the customer; or if the customer is indeed not at the location to receive the delivery. In those cases, the carrier always tries to contact the customer directly via phone number and email first, but in case of no answer from them, please proceed as follows:

  • The carrier will email the CS team to report the customer does not respond to the carrier's attempt to deliver (no answer from the door or phone);

  • CS agent tries to contact de customer to ask for contact information/ to be attentive to the delivery (Canned Responses B4.3)

  • CS agent needs to send a reply maximum in the next 72 hours or delivery will be canceled:

    -> If we have information from the customer, CS agent replies to the carrier email with the new contact info;

    -> If no reply from the customer, CS agent replies to the carrier informing no info and asking them to cancel the shipment and to return the items.

  • Once the Order appears with the shipment as canceled on the system, CS agent can proceed with the refund to customer.



​

Lost Parcel

The Lost Parcel cases are for if the order is not delivered completed. Here we have 2 different possibilities of who informed the loss and we also have 2 different ways of handling it:


1) Carrier informed about Lost Parcel: In those cases, the carrier probably lost the item between hubs and inform us immediately.

-> How to handle:

  • The carrier will email the CS team to report the issue and to confirm in the email that the loss is their responsibility;

  • CS agent needs to create a Logistic Claim, attach the carrier's emails, and then inform customer via email about the situation (Canned Responses B5.1)

  • CS agent creates a Supplement Order and send email to the Carrier - maximum in the next 48h - informing about the new order and the item number

  • Carrier will hold the order until the supplement item arrives at the hub and then make the delivery completely to the customer;

  • Once the system indicates a new delivery date, CS agent can inform the customer via email about it (Canned Responses B5.2)


2) Customer informed about Lost Parcel: Here, it is the customer who contacted us to report their order was not delivered in full. They can have noticed during delivery but not able to resolve with the carrier; or they only noticed during assembly and decided to contact us. How to handle:

  • The first step is to email back the customer apologizing for the situation and explaining that we will investigate the case (Canned Response B4.4)

  • Then, the procedures of Logistic Claims and Lost in Transit must be followed accordingly in order to check proof of delivery from the carrier or to request binding documentation from the customer. Please refer to the article to handle the case correctly.

  • Depending on how the case evolves, you need to keep the customer informed via email. For this please use the appropriate Canned Responses from B4.5 onwards.



​

Rejected Parcel

Due to delay: This can happen when a customer is unhappy with a significant delay in the delivery date, but didn't inform us in advance and upon arrival decides to reject the delivery.

(Ticket categorization: Cancellation >> Cancellation Due to Delay)

​

-> How to handle:

The carrier will report the cancellation of the shipment via email to the CS team. The agent needs to open a ticket to Refund team to ask for the customer to be reimbursed. DO NOT open a return request.

​

Advised by CS (late cancellation): Usually, this will happen in cases in which the customer could not cancel due to the order being too far in the process, and it was instructed to reject the delivery.

(FD ticket categorization: Cancellation >> Reject Delivery)

​

-> How to handle:

The carrier will change status of the fulfillment to "return to sender complete". The agent needs to open a ticket to Refund team to ask for the customer to be reimbursed. DO NOT open a return request.

Due to parcel damaged -> Please follow the Logistic Claims procedure
