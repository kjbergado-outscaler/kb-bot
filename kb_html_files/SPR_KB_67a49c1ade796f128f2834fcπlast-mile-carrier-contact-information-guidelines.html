When to Contact a Carrier?

​

  1. Check here the conditions to BENADACH + UK.

  2. Rule of thumb, only contact carriers IF:

    1. Your question can't be solved taking a look at the tracking website or Carrier Portal (login below), Parcel Lab or at Emma's Order Status Page. Some extra cases:

      1. If you need the proof of delivery and it's not in the tracking website or in the carrier portal.

        1. If you were unable to get the proof of delivery from the carrier, please open a Jira here.

      2. It's a address correction with a 1MH carrier and the order is already with them. Understand the difference here and you can see how to troubleshoot 2MH carrier issues as well.

      3. Order is already with the carrier and status doesn't move in 5 business days (Order Stuck w/ Carrier).

If the issue falls outside of these guidelines, please direct the customer to contact the carrier directly. Find the customer contact information for each carrier here.
