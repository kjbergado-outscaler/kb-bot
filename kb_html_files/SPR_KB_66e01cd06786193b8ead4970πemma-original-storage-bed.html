Product Communication


Superior and easy access storage to optimize bedroom space
The offers massive storage space to optimize any bedroom and apartment. It can be used as an extra closet to store seasonal items like winter clothes/shoes, luggage, and sporting equipment, or for everyday items like shoes and bedding items thanks to the easy access mechanism. Using the handle you can access your storage space without having to move your mattress or any effort

Timeless and minimalistic Design to blend in every bedroom
Minimalistic upholstered design that fits in any room. Customization options for different colors and headboard designs.

Ultimate Convenience and the Emma promise

Having the Emma Bed means ultimate convenience. Ordering and delivery are just a click away. The bed is optimized for an easy and fast setup. Plus 100 nights and free return to test your new Emma bed at your home. So you can get to know your new bed without any worries and convince yourself of the quality.
