UK - Moldy Mattresses

If any new customers contact us to report that they’ve received a moldy mattress, please escalate the case number directly to your team lead and tag the Performance and Quality Managers (@Carolina Luz and @Newsha Afkha) on the Case Notes. We’ll be coordinating the collection process internally to allow for a deeper investigation into these cases.

What to do:

 

  1. Report the case to EMMA: 

     Tag @Carolina Luz and @Newsha Afkhami on the Case Notes

    * Open a JIRA ticket to us. You may use the following template:

    ​

    Hey team,
    We’ve received a Product Claim reporting Mold.

    Sprinklr Case Nr: XXX
    Claimed SKU: XXX

    Please find the pictures of the issue attached


    Thanks

     

  2. Reply to customer using the template below

  3. Offering a Resolution

    If customer insists on receiving a replacement, we are entitle as per legal advice, not to offer a replacement for these customers, then you will send the following statement:  "Please note that instead of offering a replacement or a repair, we can instead opt for issuing a refund, if we find that it would not be possible to replace a specific product due to a lack of available defect-free stock. As such, since this is what is happening in your case, we will be providing you with a full refund for this item and the previously mentioned additional compensation."

    Newsha might ask you to hold the return so we can collect the mattress for inspection, so please make sure to tag us as soon as the case arrive to receive instructions for next steps.
