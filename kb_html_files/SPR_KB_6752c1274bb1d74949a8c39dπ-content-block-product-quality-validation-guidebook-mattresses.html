Best practices for mattress claims

If a customer contacts us about a mattress not inflating or its smell, please:

  • Inform them that mattresses can take up to 72 hours to fully inflate.

  • Advise that the smell can take up to 72 hours to dissipate.

These timelines are based on quality tests. If the specified time has not yet passed, ask the customer to wait. We do not validate claims before this period to avoid unnecessary replacements. If the issue persists after 72 hours, we will support the customer further.

Scoping questions

Oftentimes, what seems to be an issue with the mattress, is actually caused by an issue with the bed. To address the root cause, we need to be sure the product issue is not caused by a bad state of the bed.

This is needed to ensure we can a) spot any deviations, and b) be sure the issue is not caused by the bed.

This will help us spot the product issue more clearly.
