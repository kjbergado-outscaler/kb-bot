Summary of the product


The Emma Essential Boxspring is a bed system consisting of at least two resilient parts lying one on top of the other, the box and the mattress. The lower box consists of a base frame in/on which a spring core is fixed or incorporated.


The sales strategy is to target this product as an entry-level BSB, that is sold along with the mattress with the option for the customer to add a topper.

USPs (Unique Selling Points)

A Box Spring Bed

  • The bed consists of a bed base, with bonell springs inside, and a mattress that is built into the design of the mattress.
  • Benefits of bonell spring bed base:
    • Increase lifespan of mattress by reducing impact on the mattress.
    • Reduces moisture retention in the mattress, through increased ventilation from underneath the mattress.
    • Bonell springs in the base coupled with 7 comfort zones in the Next Gen mattress are the perfect combination for dynamic adaptation, all night long for great support in every sleeping position and pressure relief.


A luxurious hotel-like experience


For the Emma Boxspring bed, we chose comfortable fabrics that cover the entire bed. Our headboards are ideal for resting when you read in bed or for a delicious breakfast in bed.


2 different cover colours


  • With 2 different cover colours to choose from, the Emma One Boxspring Bed will fit right in with any bedroom aesthetic
  • The covers are removable and washable too, making both cleaning and redecorating a breeze. Change your cover to change the mood, season, or simply because you want your bed to look new again.

Assembly & Booklet

  1. Align the right and left bed frames so that the sliders are on the same side.

  2. The 4 wooden feet should be attached to the visible corners of each bed base and the 2 other wooden feet to its center.

  3. Use the connector between the two-bed frames to connect them together.

  4. Slide the headboard into the bed frame sliders until the headboard touches the floor.

  5. Insert the removable covers onto the headboard and bed bases and attach them with velcro straps.

  6. Unzip the 4-sided mattress cover and place the Emma One Federkern mattress inside the cover, then zip the top washable fabric to close the mattress cover.

  7. Place the topper on the mattress.
