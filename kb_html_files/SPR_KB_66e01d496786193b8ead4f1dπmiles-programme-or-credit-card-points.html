Introduction


This articles explains what to do in case a customer asks about associating an Emma purchase with miles programme and/or credit card points.


Does Emma provide a miles programme or credit card points?


Emma has partnership with some affiliate companies that allow their customers to associate a purchase done at Emma's website in their respective miles or credit card points programmes. However, this service it is not provided by Emma, it is provided by the affiliate, so we don't have access to connect a purchase with any of those plan options.



How to handle customer request for associating purchases with miles/credit card points?


We have received some requests from customers asking to link their purchases to their miles or credit card points. It is crucial to handle these requests with empathy and clarity. Here’s how you should respond:


  1. Acknowledge the Customer's Request: Start by thanking the customer for their Emma purchase and for reaching out;

  2. Explain Our Policy: Clearly explain that associating purchases with miles or credit card points is not a service that Emma provides directly. This is managed by our affiliate company;

  3. Guide the Customer: Direct the customer to contact our affiliate company’s customer service for assistance with their miles or points. Provide any relevant contact information if available;

  4. Express Understanding and Support: Apologize for any inconvenience and assure the customer of our support for any other issues or questions they may have about the Emma product.



Example of how to reply a customer:


"Thank you for reaching out and for your recent purchase with us. I understand that you would like to associate your Emma purchase with your miles or credit card points. Unfortunately, this is not a service that we directly provide. We have a partnership with [Partner Company], and they are responsible for managing the creation and allocation of miles and points.


To ensure that your purchase is eligible for miles or points, I recommend contacting them directly. They will be able to assist you further and provide the necessary steps to ensure your purchase is appropriately credited.


If there is anything else we can assist you with or if you have any further questions about your Emma product, please do not hesitate to reach out."
