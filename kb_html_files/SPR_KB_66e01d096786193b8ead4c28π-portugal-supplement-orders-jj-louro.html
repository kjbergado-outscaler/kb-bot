Supplement Order Process for JJ Louro

​

Important update

​

We are not doing further supplement orders from JJ Louro. However, claims for JJ louro still exists and In case this happens, please process a return and refund.

​

Exception: exclusively for sommiers: In case customer needs escalation, pass to supervisor to check for an alternative (do not promisse anything, but let it be clear we're going to do our best effort in finding an alternative / solution)

​


In contrast to other warehouses, JJ Louro employs a distinct approach when dealing with Product and Logistic Claims.


ATTENTION


When it comes to products originating from JJ Louro, such as the Sommier Bed, there's no requirement to generate a supplemental order in Fluent. Simply send a message to them utilizing the provided child ticket template:



They will offer the needed assistance and re-ship orders.



Recapping the actions required

​

In the case of Product or Logistic Claims, the agent should:


  • Open a child ticket for JJ Louro.

  • Leave a comment on Fluent Commerce regarding the actions (e.g. email sent to JJ Louro requesting a new Sommier Bed).



In case the agent doesn't get a response, please contact the Supervisor.
