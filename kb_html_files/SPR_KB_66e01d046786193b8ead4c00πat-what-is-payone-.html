Check incoming payments:


Payone checks whether payments have been received on our account and thus triggers subsequent processes in the orders. Above all, the feedback from Payone sets an order to paid, which starts further processing of the order. Without this step, no barcode will be added to the order and therefore nothing will be shipped.


We can find the corresponding confirmation in Magento under comment history.


Payment not received and status pending?


If the customer says that he has already paid, but no receipt of payment is visible on Magento, you have to proceed as follows:


Step 1: Request proof of payment

Step 2: After receipt of payment receipt, it must be checked


--> Is the IBAN used by the customer correct? If no, then the customer needs to ask there. (If the IBAN belongs to Payolution, we can ask if they pay back to the customer)


--> If the payment was transferred correctly, we look at the date, the account holder, the amount and the reference (sometimes there is also a second order)


If everything fits and the transfer was made a week or more ago, then it must be forwarded to Refunds

Confirm Refunds


If we make a refund, the payment will be registered by our account, by Payone and confirmed in our system. We also see this in the comments history of the order.

Payone VS. Payolution


Payone controls our account movements and reports them to our system.


Payolution is a payment service provider that offers and manages invoice and installment purchases for us. You can find out more about invoice and installment purchases here.
