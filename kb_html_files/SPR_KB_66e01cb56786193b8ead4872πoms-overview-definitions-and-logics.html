What is FluentCommerce?


FluentCommerce is the new order management system of Emma COS.


it is a platform that tracks sales, orders, inventory, and fulfillment.

It enables the people, processes, and partnerships necessary for products to find their way to the customer who bought them.


From a CS point of view, it's the tool we use to identify order status, check customers' information, and take required actions such as handling claims, returns, and refunds.

Glossary (Terms library)

Order


Order definition


When a customer is placing an order with us, essential data about it is being generated:





While navigating to our website and placing the order, the customer selects specific options (such as product, color, size) and provides core information to ensure the purchase of the product.


Each purchase made by the customer via our webshop will become an order in our Order Management System (FluentCommerce).


The order will always contain:

  • Order number (generated after the purchase is created in our order management system)
  • Products (SKU) & quantity
  • Customer information (email / personal data)
  • Billing information
  • Shipping information
  • Payment information
  • Order Summary (Total & tax specifications)



The order management tool will be connected to other systems, ensuring the information provided by the customer while buying a product on our website, is fully delivered to his home on the expected and agreed time.

Fulfillment


Fulfillment Definition


Fulfillment is a unique ID to track the journey of a request created in a warehouse, with one or more items, at any point in time.




Every time an order is placed in our system, a logic behind will group all items according to their warehouse location, and each of these groups will be assigned a fulfillment ID.

Fulfilment Status
What does it mean?
Assigned
After payment, the order is assigned to our internal system.
Waiting for Stock
If item is not available in our Inventory - not transmitted to the fulfilment location.
Submitted to FL
The Fulfilment has been transmitted to the Fulfilment location and waiting for FL's confirmation

Picked and Packed by FL

Ready for Dispatch

Dispatched

Arrived at Sorting Centre
The Fulfilment has arrived at carrier's sorting center and on its way to the customers
Out for Delivery
The Fulfilment is loaded to a carrier vehicle and is about to be delivered to the end customer - The tracking label will be available in the OMS once the order reaches this Fulfillment Status.
Requested for Delivery Option
The customer requested another delivery option to the carrier
Complete

Canceled

Managing orders



Let's quickly review the Journey of the order to review the backend logic that runs in Fluent commerce:







The customer will place his order on our online store in accordance with his needs. The information provided while placing the order will be part of the Order details page (delivery and invoice address, payment method, items of the order, etc).









From it, orders will be created in the order management system: Fluent Commerce; and the system will communicate directly with the warehouse system (for example - Pulpo) and the related country/region carrier partners; to be finally delivered to our customers.








Cancellation


Cancellation is possible in our system only before the order is transmitted to the warehouse, which means prior to the Submitted fulfillment status.


After submitted to FL you must follow the process available in the related request article: How to: Cancellation



Return


Return an item is only possible in the order management system after fulfillment status is Shipped and the invoice is created.


You will find two ways to trigger a return in Fluent Commerce:



  • Create return with Refund an item will always be used when the item should be refunded.
  • Create return without Refund option should be used when item should not be refunded (e.g. in a product claim scenario in which a supplement will be created to replace the product).



Supplement Orders


A supplement order is an order that can be manually created in Fluent Commerce to add an item to an existing order placed by the customer.


Supplement orders are created for Replacement or Add on, and they are always created with zero value.




  • Replacement means we are creating a supplement order to replace an item; e.g. for a product claim or a logistic claim.

  • Add-on is an additional SKU (product) added as a supplement order, such as a comfort layer, or a pillow.








Composite Products


Definition



Composite products are products that consist of several components (e.g. a bed that when packed to be delivered, may contain 3 different boxes (components) with different SKU, that result in one item/product).







Products that are sold together (for example: if there is a website promotion, by a bed + pillow with a special price are considered bundle orders, not composite products - as they represent two different products).


Fulfillment Definition


If any of the components of a composed product are out of stock when being transmitted to the warehouse (when the order is placed), the order won't go through, which means the fulfillment status will be Out of stock; and the order status: pending.


After being assigned to the warehouse, each component of the order will have a specific fulfillment and a specific tracking link.


E.g. If we look at the example above: 1 bed, 3 components: this means each one of the 3 components will have its own tracking information.



Cancellation



Cancellation of the item is possible only up to Booked status.


When canceling the Composite product item, all components fulfillment will be canceled.




Product Return & pick up


Return means returning the item and triggering a refund.


A pick-up is a return that does not trigger the refund, for a scenario such as a replacement (supplement order to replace the original item returned).


The return is possible to be initiated only after all components were shipped.
