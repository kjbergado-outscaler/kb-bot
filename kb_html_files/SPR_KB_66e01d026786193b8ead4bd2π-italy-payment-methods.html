Paypal

Customer needs a PayPal Account. While on our checkout page, they can either press directly from the first page on the Paypal tab which will redirect them to the PayPal portal in which they'll pay.

NOTE: Doing this, the shipping address of the order will be the same they have linked to their PayPal account.


Or they proceed using 'Cassa', insert the delivery address, and then press PayPal in the payment methods checkout section: Customer has also the option to have their payment divided in 3 installments with PayPal.


POC for payment problems: Supervisor.

Klarna

This is our second installments payment provider. Customer can only pick 3 installments as an option, and will be able to pay only using credit card. When they select this payment method, a tab will open for them to register and have the payment accepted.

​

For any kind of problems regarding the payment, customers should refer to them. We do not have a way of communicating with Klarna CS.

​

Common Q&A:

  • I ordered using Klarna but I received no confirmation!
    We can check if we have the order on Magento with the email address used by the customer for ordering. If the order is not there, might be too early, 3 to 5 days need to pass for Klarna to accept the loan. If more than 5 days passed, escalate to Supervisor.

  • I ordered paying with Klarna and I want to return my products.
    Sorry you are not happy with one of our products. Why do you want to return them?
    If the return needs to be done -> we can create a product return on Magento, confirm that a courier will pick the stuff up (as we are used to) and advise him that for the refund/partial refund of what he already paid he needs to get in contact with Klarna customer service.

  • I returned my products but Klarna is still asking me for money/getting money out of my account!
    Let's check if we have the product return registered on Magento. If it's not there, first of all means that the customer didn't return the order, so we should register it and tell me how it will work for the pick up. Then we can tell the customer that he should contact Klarna for them to stop asking for money. If it's registered already, customer only need to get in contact with Klarna, and we need to make sure it's getting picked up.

How will the refund work?

The refunds are usually processed always on the payment method used when placed the order.

The reasons why a refund on original payment method can fail are the following:

- Card expired and bank account related closed;

- PayPal: exceeded 180 days from when the order was placed;

- HeyLight: customer has paid their debts off with them;

In all the scenarios where the refund doesn't go through, Emma Refunds team will proactively contact the Market Performance Manager who will reach the CS requesting to contact custome to have their IBAN available.
