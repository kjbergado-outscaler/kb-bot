Address/ Telephone details/ Delivery scheduled time - Up to Packing Status


Order details modification related to the address is only possible up to Packing status, as any change should be placed before the information of the order is transmitted to the warehouse.


If up to packing status:

You can proceed with modification as per Magento: what and how to article





Address/ Telephone details/ Delivery scheduled time - after Packing status


If delivery with Label


Modification may be possible directly with the carrier company:


upon tracking link availability, the customer can access it directly via the carrier company portal and try to add new address information to it.


If it does not work, when delivering failure, the carrier request will be open to us to confirm the address of the customer and we will be able to modify it.




Please note: Always leave a note with the new address or scheduled time requested by the customer as in case the Carrier company reaches us, we are able to collect information easily.



If delivery with 2 Man Handling (2MH - e.g. beds or heavy packages)


Escalate the case (Country related)

Your back office or Team lead to request contact directly with the carrier company to try modification directly.




Change Product/ Size/ SKU



Whenever there is a change of costs in the order, we are unable to edit for accountability reasons. Due to that, whenever customers reach us to change the product or size (SKU change), the process to do it will be to cancel the order and the customer place a new - corrected one.
