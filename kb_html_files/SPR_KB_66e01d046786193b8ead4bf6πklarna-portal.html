General Information


Klarna Order Status


Whenever an order is placed in our order management system, simultaneously, the Klarna order is created.


After the order is shipped and the invoice is created, the Klarna orders are supposed to get captured: the Payment to Emma will be triggered & customer will be charged by Klarna in accordance with their direct payment agreement. The status of the Klarna order will be Captured Order:


Before the order is shipped, the status in Klarna of the order will remain as Uncaptured with an expiration date for Emma, to ensure the customer the order will not be left unhandled.


If the deadline provided by Klarna to Emma ship the order expires, the status of the order will be Expired Order.

The expiration date can be extended by our teams if it can't be fulfilled under the deadline, to avoid expiring the order.




Customer service teams may need to jump in to modify manually the Klarna order status; extend the deadline or cancel one of them. This article will guide you through how to do it.

When an order is at Uncaptured status, you can at anytime capture the order following the steps below.


Attention: Ensure you investigate the Emma order status by tracking the order in the related order management system (Magento or Fluent Commerce) before capturing the order. If the order has not been shipped, the related item should not be captured.



Access the Klarna order-related page:


1. Click capture to proceed

3. Confirm by clicking Capture.

Payment Extension


After order has been captured in Klarna, payment due date will be generated which is 30 days from the day when order was captured. Customers requests to extend payment due dates to various reasons like incomplete shipment, delay deliveries or they are returning the products. If this happens, payment due can be extended in Klarna.


Access the Klarna order-related page:

1. Click Extend due date


2. Select the new due date of the order.

Note: As a general rule, we only allow extension of payment until two days or until 10 days for grave instances. Any payment extension more than 10 days should be approved by the CS Manager.


3. Extend the payment due date.

Expiry Date


As per the Klarna status explanation above, every Klarna order has an expiry date to be captured, for the purpose of ensuring the customer the order will not be left unhandled.


If an item has not been delivered yet and the expiry date is close, we should proceed with the extension of it.

Handling Expired Orders



In case the order has expired without the option to extend the order expire date and the customer is still waiting to receive the order, you will need to proceed with a new order, therefore we need to consider the following points:


  • Check the barcode status on Magento
  • If the barcode status isn't shipped yet, you can proceed with the order cancellation on Magento and request the customer to issue a new order request on our website choosing the same method of payment or any other convenient method allowed. (Cancel button will be available if the order is up to the Prepared for Export )


  • If the Barcode is already has shipped, please take the following steps:


  1. Create a new order




2. The new order will be a duplicated request of the original one placed by the customer, double check if the information is correct and "Proceed to the point of sale"


3. Inform the customer they will receive an email with the new order and that they should confirm the new order.


Canceling orders in Klarna


In case the customer wants to cancel an order we can support with this however this process, similar to a normal cancellation request will be based on the barcode status since it can only be cancelled up to the Prepared for Export status:

  • If the order status isn't shipped yet, you can proceed with the order cancellation on Magento and on Klarna this way you assure the customer won't receive the product neither will have to pay for it.


  • If the barcode status is already as shipped, you will need to create a new order in Klarna and request the customer to confirm on their side the new order and after that we should educate the customer to refuse the delivery in order for the mattress to be sent back to the warehouse, also inform the customer to contact the customer service once the the product was refused.
