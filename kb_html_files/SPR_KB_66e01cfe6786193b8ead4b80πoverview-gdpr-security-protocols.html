Introduction


Compliance guidelines are mandatory, and following them is necessary as it means abiding by rules, set guidelines or norms for the greater benefit of the business, our customers, and also our Emmies.


Remember, under the GDPR, the EU's data protection authorities can impose fines of up to €20 million, or 4% of worldwide turnover for the preceding financial year – whichever is higher.


By following them, we are assuring:

  • Honor customers' Trust
  • Protect customers' data
  • Prevent possible fraud or phishing attack
  • Protect Emma's public image
