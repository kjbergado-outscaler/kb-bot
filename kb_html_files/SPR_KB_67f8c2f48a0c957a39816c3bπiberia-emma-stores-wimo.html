Order Not Uploaded in Magento

  1. Verify Shopify to check if the order exists.

  2. If the order is missing in Magento but exists in Shopify:

    • Notify the Store Manager in Teams to escalate to Sam Smith’s team for manual upload.

    • Wait for confirmation from Sam’s team.

  3. Once uploaded, proceed with order verification and inform the customer.

Items Waiting for Stock

  • If the items are waiting for stock, please let the customer know that the stock has been delayed, and check the expected stock arrival date for the order in the backlog file and update the customer of the expected date. If they are unable to wait, please check with Offline CE as in some cases we may be able to offer an alternative.

  • If the customer would like to split the order and receive the items that are available first, please follow the process of requesting for the WFS items to be rolled back with Order Management.

Order Tracking Issues

If tracking is missing in Magento:

  • Reach out to the carrier and provide the Order Number and Barcode number to request more information on the delivery status.

    • Mobel: trinidad.loren@mobelservices.com

    • GLS: atc.schenker@gls-spain.com (always add es.sm.mad.customerservice@dbschenker.com in cc).

​

GLS Portal

  • Go to the GLS Tracking Website.

  • Click Buscar Envíos;

​

​

  • Paste the tracking number in the field Referencia and click Buscar.

​

  • You’ll be able to see the tracking status and download the POD by selecting the option Ir.

  • If the tracking is updated, download the Proof of Delivery (POD) and send it to the customer.

If you see inconsistences/delays, please forward an email to atc.schenker@gls-spain.com (with es.sm.mad.emmab2balcala@dbschenker.com in cc) to request more information about the delivery.

Mobel Tracking

  • Go to the Mobel tracking portal.

  • Go to Service Consult;

  • Enter the tracking number found in Fluent Commerce.

  • Monitor the order status for updates.

​

​

​

  • Paste the tracking number and click Filter

​

​

  • Rolling the page to your right, you can Track the order by clicking "Tracking", and download the POD by clicking in "Conforme".

​

  • If the tracking is updated, download the Proof of Delivery (POD) and send it to the customer.
