Retail Orders

Regular Flow

No order can be processed by our agents, all the orders have to be generated by retailer on their order portal. The regular orders in retail are placed via 3 different ways:

  • Retailer Portal: Available via this link by scrolling until the end of the website page. Here you can find a Manual that explains how to use the Retailer Portal; you can also share this presentation with the retailers in case they have any doubt.

All Traditional Retailers

  • EDI: Some retailers have system integration with our Order Management System. This means that the retailers place the order in their OMS and it's automatically transferred to our OMS.

Conforama & Jysk

  • Backoffice: For retailers who we don't have an EDI developed yet, and don't have conditions to create the orders on Retailer Portal. Retailers send us the orders via a specific platform and our Backoffice team is responsible to create them in our OMS.

​

Exceptions

Regular orders are created via Retailer Portal or EDI, but there are still some exceptions:

  • POS Orders: Exposition orders so retailers can have the products in store. Most of these are free of charge for the Retailer. These should always be generated by commercials.

Find more about POS Orders in this article.

  • Blocked Retailers with special agreements: Retailers with financial problems and cannot regularize the situation now. We have made special agreements with them

Find more about Blocked Retailers in this article.

POS Orders

​

Open the Retail Backoffice Portal and choose Order Creation -> Single Order Creation -> POS Iberia

​

Please give the following information to the Backoffice team:

  • Account ID

  • External Number

  • Delivery Address

  • SKUs

After creating the request, paste the Jira number as an internal comment in the Freshdesk Ticket.

​

Once the Jira is completed, confirm that the order is correct and send the Order Number and Expected Delivery Time to the retailer.

Open the Retail Backoffice Portal and choose Order Creation -> Single Order Creation -> One-time Retailers

​

Please give the following information to the Backoffice team:

  • Account ID

  • External Number

  • Delivery Address

  • SKUs

  • Leave the order in "Pending" Status

​

After creating the request, paste the Jira number as an internal comment in the Freshdesk Ticket.

​

Once the Jira is completed, confirm that the order is correct and inform the retailer that the order will be put in progress once they send the proof of payment.

​

Once they send the POP, do the POP procedure and pass the order to "Payment Received". At this time, inform the retailer about the Expected Delivery Time and send the Order Confirmation document.If any of the products appear as Out Of Stock, please inform that these SKUs may arrive later due to lack of stock.
