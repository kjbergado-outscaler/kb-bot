Where it all began


2013

The journey began with our two founders, Manuel Muller and Dennis Schmoltzi, who were friends that shared the same vision: to improve people's lives by changing the way they sleep. At that time, the mattress market was still almost entirely based offline and characterized by products that were difficult to differentiate and prices that weren't transparent. As a first step, our founders wanted to ease the shopping process by taking it online.



Team members: 7

Yearly revenue: €5.3 thousand



It starts small-literally


The first move was launching our multi-brand platform Dormando. In the beginning, we only sold other brands and the first sales were two Dunlopillo mattresses. Funnily enough, we ended up buying this brand in 2016. In these early stages. we were a team of just 7 and were working out of a tiny, 14m office which we nicknamed: "The Foxhole".






Growth begins


2014

As our business model proved successful, we extend our product portfolio and, already convinced that the long-term solution would be omnichannel, we opened a showroom in Offenbach called Bettzeit. This also became the new office space we desperately needed as we added 13 new members to our team.




Team members: 20

Yearly revenue: €538 thousand



Emma is born


2015

While busy running and scaling Dormando, we decided to branch out and create our own brand alongside. We had the background needed to develop a great product: Manuel's previous industry experience with medical mattresses and our 2-year market experience with Dormando. Therefore, three months and many late nights later, Emma was born. We'd recognized early on that the principle of "trying it out" is the key to success-an idea that still determines our daily business today. This led us to offer consumers the novelty of mattress trials that lasted 100 nights with free delivery and returns. By now, we are at 55 team members and so we moved once again- this time to a 285 m2 office space.




Team members: 55

Yearly revenue: €3 million



Expanding into new markets


2016

We've always been ambitious and had our sights set on global growth. So, after launching the Emma mattress in Germany the previous year, we then launched it in Austria, Switzerland, Poland, the Netherlands, and the UK as well. We also acquired Dunlopillo's brand rights for Germany and Austria. Setting the base for further growth by leveraging our ability in creating superior sleep products, we built up an in-house Research & Development team. With a wide variety of skills, this team pushes the boundaries of what material, technology, and neuroscience can do for sleep.



Team members: 85

Yearly revenue: €20 million



Emma breaks even


2017

This was the year where the Emma mattress won 2 awards: UK's Which? and Netherlands' Consumentenbond. This was proof of the quality of our product and that online mattress suppliers could successfully compete with traditional brands.

"Emma is a team with skin in the game" and we value the impact of testing over endless planning-this ensures our accelerated growth. As a result, we expanded into 5 more countries and launched Emma in France, Belgium, Italy, Spain, and Portugal. The decision to expand paid off and our yearly revenue reached 33 million-meaning Emma had now broken even.





Team members: 130

Yearly revenue: €33 million



Creation of Emma Sleep University


2018

Our growth continued to climb as we launched Emma in Sweden, Denmark, China, the USA, and India. And at 176 people, we had outgrown our office once again and moved to a new 2 061 m2 space. This accelerated growth is largely thanks to the "Emma way" - we're agile, execution-oriented, and move at a fast and efficient pace. We decided we needed a way to transfer this mindset to future Emmies.

The solution? We founded Emma Sleep University.



Team members: 176

Yearly revenue: €80 million



Emma is online in 20+ markets


2019

The year began with us exhibiting at various trade fairs like IMM Cologne (Germany) and Salone del Mobile (Italy). We were on an omnichannel mission to disrupt the sleep industry. As a result, we launched our new Dunlopillo Smart Select Series which introduced our patented graphite technology to the market. Emma was now online in over 20 countries after launching in Ireland, Brazil, Mexico, Australia, and Canada. Intent on becoming a true international player, we opened Emma Southeast Asia in Manila.



To stay focused, we decided to close Dormando and exclusively sell our own products.


Team members: 290

Yearly revenue: €150 million



Exponential growth


2020

Emma was going from strength to strength. In a single year, we sold 1.5 million mattresses and grew our yearly revenue by 170%. With this, we outpaced several competitors and established ourselves as the world's leading D2C sleep brand. Our monumental success didn't go up unnoticed and in April 2020 Emma merged with the Haniel Group. With Haniel as a strong partner at our side, we saw it as an opportunity to move to a whole new level of growth.



Team members: 528

Yearly revenue: €405 million



Reinventing sleep


2021

The dedicated work of Emma's Research and Development team meant sleep entered a whole new sphere as we launched Emma Motion. the world's most advanced smart mattress. Using innovative and proprietary technology, we now offer customers a product that silently adjusts to their sleeping positions throughout the night.

With the launch of this mattress, we took a huge step towards fulfilling our mission: to change the way people sleep. Soon we'll provide an individual sunrise for everyone- stay tuned.
