Discounts in Retail

Discounts are no longer available for Retailers.

Compensation in Retail

Compensations are very specifically reduced to a microfiber pillow or duvet with prior authorization from a superior. There's no defined compensation matrix created for retail, so each case must be escalated to the OPS team for further analyzation.

What to do if a retailer asks for a Compensation

  • Escalate the ticket to OPS. If you have the confirmation, please confirm the delivery address and proceed to create the supplement. Also inform the retailer about the estimated delivery time.
