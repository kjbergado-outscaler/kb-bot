FAQ´s Weighted Blanket



How to choose blanket weight?

Our small blanket weighs 7kg, it should be around 10% of your body weight, hence it fits for people weight for 60-80kg.

The bigger blanket weighs 12kg and it´s suitable for 2 people.


How to use the blanket?

Wherever and whenever the customer like. It can be used in your couch while reading or watching TV. It can be used in your bed for a nap or during the night. The blanket helps you to relax, any moment in the day is a good moment to relax.


Can I wash the blanket?

Yes the blanket has been designed to be washable. Nonetheless, as the blanket is heavy, be careful to not exceed the maximum weight that your washing machine can handle.


Can I sleep with my own duvet on top of the weighted blanket?

Yes. There is no defined way to use the blanket. You can put it under your normal duvet, on top of your duvet or without your duvet. In any case, whenever you want.


Can I use it with babies or small children?

It is not recommended as it might be too heavy for them. The weight of the blanket must be around 10% of your weight. Above 12% of the body weight, it might be uncomfortable and disturb your breathing. In case of doubt, don’t hesitate to ask your doctor.


If I have been diagnosed with a sleep disorder or other medical condition, can I use it?

Although recommended by therapists, our product is not sold as a medical device. Please consult your doctor before use.
