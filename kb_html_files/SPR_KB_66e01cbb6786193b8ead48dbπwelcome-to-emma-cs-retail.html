Whenever a customer (or a retail partner) is reaching us with an inquiry, for example, by email, it may lead to a ticket we need to handle manually. Those tickets are centralized in our Customer relationship management (CRM) tools.


It's through our CRM tools that the customer's interactions (tickets) are assigned to our Emma Customer Service team and will be handled in accordance with our workflows and solutions in place. For handling the interactions at Emma, we are using the following three tools:





Freshdesk: Customer Relationship Management (CRM) tool, which we use to record and handle inbound and outbound interaction with our customer base.

Freshchat is an integrated functionality from Freshdesk that allows us to use live chat for customers' inquiries.

Aircall & GoContact: are telephone systems that are integrated with our CRM tool (Freshdesk), which allows us to call & receive inbound calls as an available channel. (Some Contact center partners may have Aircall, some GoContact)


The channel availability is up to the country's maturity or also volume priority. Some countries may have all channels & tools available, some only ticket systems (e.g. for email requests).
