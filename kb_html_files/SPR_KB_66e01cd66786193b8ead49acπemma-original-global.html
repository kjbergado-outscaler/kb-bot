Airgocell®Motion Isolation – Low Rolling resistance


The top layer adapts to your body and provides good ventilation due to its open pores.

Benefits:

  • Moisture (sweat) will be absorbed by the top layer and then will evaporate. It doesn't affect properties on the mattress after it dissipates.

  • It is not affected by moisture -> Mattress keeps its shape during its whole life

  • Prevents you from waking up sweating (also the right temperature in the room and the right duvet is important)

  • Temperature regulation = breathability



Pressure-relieving


The middle layer adapts to your body, which helps promote optimal pressure load, both for low and high weight loads.

Benefits:

  • Adapts perfectly to your body shape.

  • Joint pain and pressure relief. People with joint pain will appreciate the conforming ability of a memory foam mattress, as it gives the feeling of hugging the pressure points of the body.

  • Provides spinal support. Memory foam conforms to your body, helping to keep your spine in neutral alignment. This ensures you won’t wake up with a sore back.

  • Limits motion transfer (Low rolling resistance) You won’t have to worry about being woken up by a partner in the middle of the night if they get out of bed. Because the mattress isn’t tied together in one consecutive coil system, if your partner gets up to use the restroom in the middle of the night, you aren’t going to feel a thing.

  • Low rolling resistance

    • What does it mean?
      If the rolling resistance is high, the body needs more strength to toss and turn, and then the person will wake up more during the night. It has much higher responsiveness than memory foam, which has a slower response. Because of this, using this foam on the top layer will provide bounciness on a mattress

  • Very durable increases comfort life. 10 to 15 years life cycle.



Supportive HRX Foam – 3 zones mattress


The bottom layer of HRX polyurethane foam, which is supportive and extremely durable, has incisions that allow the mattress to adapt well to your body.

Benefits:

  • This foam supports you in the right places, where it matters.

  • Very long durability. No sagging (Liegekuhle), even after a long time.

  • Sparwelle(material saving cutting): helps support the firmness of the mattress. Perfectly designed height and distance of this wave to create the perfect firmness/ softness of the mattress.

  • It is high point elastic. The supportive bottom layer helps the body to move effortlessly, which mean there ́s not a lot of strength required to turn over while sleeping.

  • 3 zones back support

Cover

Breathable and Climate Regulating Mattress Cover 

The Emma cover consists of a light fiber that immediately releases moisture and ensures a good sleeping climate. At the same time, the cover is stretchable and can adapt well to the body.

Characteristics 

  • Top fabric

    • 99% polyester and 1% elastane

    • Knitted white fabric going 6 cm over the sides.

    • Completely detachable top to bottom light gray zipper between border and top panel fabric.

  • Border fabric

    • 100% polyester

    • Dark grey fabric with waterfall design going 5cm into the bottom to create a mirror.

    • On one side a brand label is sewed in.

  • Bottom fabric

    • 87% polyester and 13% polypropylene

    • Anti-slip/ white woven fabric going 12 cm to the sides to create a mirror.

  • Handles

    • 100% Polyester

    • 4x Vertical Handles

Benefits

  • Breathable cover

  • Elasticity. It fits perfectly to the mattress core

  • Completely removable and washable
