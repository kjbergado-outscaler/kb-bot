Summary of the product


The Emma Motion Shift is the world's first mattress with a variable firmness level. It is ideal for all those who want to manually determine their lying comfort according to their daily needs. The Emma Motion Shift allows customers to easily adjust the firmness and support of their mattress to their individual comfort needs, without any electricity required. The technology of this product involves the Smart Valve, which controls the air that comes in and out of the mattress, letting the user adjust the pressure in the way they prefer. The Smart Valve is connected to the chamber inside the mattress, which will increase or decrease due to this manual adjustment to provide the best body support.

Smart Valve


With the Smart Valve, you can always adjust the lying feeling of your mattress to your daily comfort needs. This special luxury goes far beyond the conventional "soft", "medium" and "hard" settings because the firmness level of the Motion Shift is infinitely adjustable. This makes it probably the most adaptable mattress of all.



Shift Core


With our Smart Valve technology, you completely change the Motion Shift's comfort characteristics without electricity. This is made possible by the new Shift Core, a large-pored foam layer where air circulates freely. The individual adjustment is made via a valve with a manual regulator, through which the internal pressure in this Shift Core can only be changed by your own body weight.


The effect: the mattress becomes softer or harder in sensitive areas. At the same time, our proven HRX foam with core cuts provides continuous stabilization in the hip and shoulder area. Customized rest couldn't be more energy-efficient and simple.


Airgocell/Aquagel


The Emma Motion Shift is refined on the surface with an optimized Airgocell foam. The resilience of the new Airgocell foam ensures that the SHIFT simultaneously adapts to the body with Airgocell thus completing the intelligent comfort features of the shift without any technology.



How to use Emma Motion Shift Mattress


Step 1:

Unpack your mattress and lay it flat on your bed. If you have a double bed, make sure that the mattress side with the opening always faces the outside of the bed. 


It's recommend to wait 24h before sleep on the mattress.



Step 2:

Unpack the Smart Valve, which is in a separate outer box. There is an opening on the side of the mattress into which you must screw the Smart Valve.



Step 3:

The mattress is now set with the highest firmness level. To reduce the firmness level, first, lie down on it. Now, turn the handle of the Smart Valve to the left (“OPEN“) and then push the handle towards your mattress to reduce the internal pressure of the mattress. This will reduce the degree of firmness. Release the valve as soon as you have reached your desired degree of firmness. Then turn the handle of the Smart Valve to the right (“CLOSE“). This locks the valve and air can no longer escape.


The reduction in air pressure inside your Emma MOTION Shift mattress depends on your individual body weight. If you have a low body weight, less air escapes than if you have a higher body weight. If you like to lie even softer, we recommend that you sit on the mattress at step 3 instead of lying down.




Step 4:

If you want to increase the firmness of your mattress again, it is important that there is no weight on your mattress. So, the person has to step out from the mattress before opening the valve again to let the air in. If you now turn the Smart Valve to the left ("OPEN“) and press the valve again, air will be supplied to the mattress core. This increases the degree of firmness. Release the valve as soon as your desired lying comfort is reached and turn the handle of the Smart Valve to the right ("CLOSE“) to lock the valve again. It takes about 15 seconds until the maximum degree of firmness is restored.


Please don’t force the handle too hard when turning to open or close it as it might break.

Warning Notes


Flammability of Polyurethane Foam


The mattress core of your Emma Motion Shift is made of flammable polyurethane foams. Polyurethane foam will burn if exposed to an open flame or a large heat source.

Do not expose your Emma Motion Shift to an open flame or any other direct or indirect heat sources, such as welding or abrasive cutting. Burning cigarettes, heaters, or candles are also sources of danger. Once polyurethane foam has ignited, it burns rapidly, releases enormous heat, and consumes a large amount of oxygen. As a result of the high consumption of oxygen, there is an acute risk of asphyxiation for persons in the vicinity. The gases released when the foam burns can cause unconsciousness and serious damage to health if inhaled in sufficient quantities. Once polyurethane foam has ignited, it is very difficult to extinguish. Foam fires that appear to have been extinguished may still smolder and reignite. Make sure to have the fire department check if the fire was completely extinguished.
