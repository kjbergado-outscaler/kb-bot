Introduction - What is Test-Achats / Test-Aankoop


Test-Achats (in french) or Test-Aankoop (in dutch) is a non-profit and independent Belgian consumer platform, its goal is to inform, advocate and represent consumers.


Test-Achats/Test-Aankoop has a long experience of offering legal assistance to its members, every year more than 400,000 interactions are made.

Since 2014, consumer organizations in Belgium have developed legal tools to get collective redress via class actions. In this period, Test-Achats/Test-Aankoop launched several collective actions against the railway industry, airplane companies, resellers of concert tickets and VW (Dieselgate case). Competition, food safety, health care, the environment and the financial sector are other areas where Test-Achats/Test-Aankoop has a long-track record of activities on behalf of Belgian and European consumers.


Products or Service companies recognized through their tests and surveys have the option of requiring a license or trustmark in their labels.



How to handle Test-Achats / Test-Aankoop requests


Whenever there is a complaint from a customer via Test-Achats/Test-Aankoop we will receive a ticket from the following sender: klachten@test-aankoop.be .


An automation will assign the ticket to the Global Escalations queue, and guarantee the complaint is handled with priority by the Online Reputation Team.



Each company has their own Test-Achats/Test-Ankoop score attesting to the service and product quality.
The score is composed by:

  • Customer satisfaction - 50%
  • Reply rate - 35%
  • First Reply rate - 15%


This means that 50% of the score is completely controlled by us and that to get the maximum score on those 15% we don't even need to provide a resolution right away, just an initial reply acknowledging the complaint.


Important! From the moment the complaint is sent to us, we have only 2 working days to provide an initial reply!




Step-by-Step guide:


  1. Check the details of the complaint in the email;
  2. Handle the complaint as you would with any other customer but keep in mind that this customer already went through a lot of trouble reaching out to Test-Achats/Test-Aankoop so give the customer priority and pay extra attention to empathy;
  3. Reach out to the customer, preferably by phone and confirm the solution to the complaint by email;
  4. Once you reached out to the customer, reply to the ticket, describing how the problem will be resolved
    • Keep in mind that Test-Achats/Test-Aankoop ALWAYS validates the outcome and might contest our solution
  5. For the reply to be correctly delivered, the sender email in Freshdesk must always be contact@emma-matelas.be




Important! Complaints can be public or private, so be very careful with GDPR issues and not disclose public information. If needed, create an outbound ticket to the email used to place the order.



Complaints might be public or private


Customers can choose to make a public complaint, so make sure that no personal data is provided on such emails and that you are always GDPR compliant.


A customer might even choose to publish their complaints at any time, so this is very important to ensure from the beginning.


If needed, create an outbound ticket to the email used to place the order, don't share personal data on the initial communication.




Email addresses contact@info.testachats.be or contact@info.testaankoop.be are exceptions. Whenever the complaint comes from these 2 email addresses, it means the complaint is private and being monitored by a lawyer already so we must really provide a solution. Do not reply to the customer ticket, reply directly these 2 inboxes.




Complaints Escalation


Test-Achats/Test-Aankoop is a mediator for these complaints and might decide to escalate it, meaning they will contest our resolution and demand a different outcome.
Complaints escalations will be sent to klanten.oplossingen@emma-matras.be.




What to do if there is a GDPR Breach


If you detect a GDPR breach

  1. escalate internally immediately
  2. inform Test-Achats/Test-Aankoop immediately throgh the mediator email: moderator@test-achats.be
