Summary of Product



The Bed Linen 100% Cotton is part of the new bed linen products, and between the 3 lines launched is the most affordable one of the collection. You can compare and understand the differences in sales strategy between the products in the article Bed Linen Portfolio Overview.


In this article, you can find the details and product information regarding the 100% Cotton Fitted Sheet, Pillow Case, and Duvet Cover. This product is ideal for people looking for simple and affordable (budget-conscious) bed linen that provide an inviting sleeping environment.

USPs (Unique selling points)

  • Perfect balance between comfort and affordability: At Emma, we know the importance of a good night's sleep, and we believe it should be accessible to everyone. That's why our Essential bedding is thoughtfully designed to offer the utmost comfort without straining your budget.

  • Lightweight: We want everyone to feel weightless and be able to move freely throughout the night. Our lightweight bedding is perfect for those who appreciate the sensation of sleeping under a soft, cloud-like embrace.

  • Reversible Colors for Versatility and Variety: Our bed linens are 2 in 1 by offering two sets of bedding in one. They are reversible and give the customer the freedom to change the look of their bed effortlessly.
