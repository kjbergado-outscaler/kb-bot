Summary Product


Move without moving. Add extra comfort to your sleep experience by remotely adjusting your position of head and feet while relaxing. The motorized slatted frame includes a super-flat motor (2-times motorization) and universal compatibility with all common beds that are slatted frame compatible.

Unique Selling Points (USPs)


Change your position without effort: “Move without Moving”

Experience personalized comfort at its finest with our motorized slatted frame, boasting individual adjustments of the head and foot positions, powered by a robust, CE-certified motor. Seamlessly control your comfort with the wireless Bluetooth control anytime. Transform your bedtime routine - read in an upright position, then seamlessly transition to a restful sleep in your bed, all with the simple touch of a button.


Stable, durable, and supportive in all situations

Made from 28 resilient real wood slats, reinforced with an additional stabilizing strap, and backed by a 2-year guarantee, this MSF is the embodiment of lasting quality. With its commitment to longevity, this product is designed to last over time and elevate your sleep and relaxation in bed to an incomparable level of comfort.


Smart via Bluetooth or App

Emma’s MSF allows you to control it effortlessly and conveniently via wireless remote control or an intuitive smartphone app. This innovative technology allows you to customize your sleeping position just by a touch of a finger.
