Find the Announcement tab on the left hand side of the Supervisor Consol, and Click Create Announcement on the top right.

1.Add Title & Description

2.Media is optional (image will be shown with popup)

3.Confirmation text is what the agents will have to click (text on blue button)

4.User group is who will receive the announcement

After posting the announcement, go back to the announcement dashboard to review who has received the message (pending status) and who has clicked on the message (read status).

Note: the frequency and use of the announcement feature is up to the supervisor, but be aware not to overuse thefeature and lose the attention of the agents.
