Discounts in Retail

In retail, our client is the retailer and not the final client. This means that we sell the mattresses at a different price for the retailers so they can sell them to their clients at the recommended PVP.

For this reason, the discounts applied are not Coupon Codes or Friend Referral Discounts. The discounts are associated to the purchase price of the item through Special Campaigns or Lower Prices with Volume Orders .

We don't have discounts at the moment for B2Bte.

What to do if a retailer asks for a Discount

  • The retailer asks to apply a Coupon or Friend Referral Code: Kindly inform the retailer that these codes are only applicable on online purchases. If they have further doubts, they must contact the Commercial Team for price related aspects.

  • The retailer asks for a Discount: Kindly inform the retailer that they must contact the Commercial Team for price related aspects.

Compensation in Retail

There's no defined compensation matrix created for retail, so each case must be escalated to the OPS team for further analyzation.

What to do if a retailer asks for a Compensation

  • Escalate the ticket to OPS. If you have the confirmation, please confirm the delivery address and proceed to create the supplement. Also inform the retailer about the estimated delivery time.
