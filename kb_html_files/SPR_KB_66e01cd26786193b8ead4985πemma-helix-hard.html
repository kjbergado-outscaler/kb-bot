Point-elastic Airgocell


The top layer adapts to your body and provides good ventilation due to its open pores.

Benefits

  • Temperature regulation = Breathability

  • Moisture (sweat) will be absorbed and then will evaporate, so the customer doesn't wake up sweating

  • Mattress keeps its shape during its whole life since moisture doesn't affect properties on the mattress after it dissipates.

  • Low rolling resistance

    • What does it mean? If the rolling resistance is high, the body needs more strength to toss and turn and then the person will wake up more during the night. It has much higher responsiveness than memory foam, which has a slower response. Because of this, using this foam on the top layer will provide bounciness on a mattress.

    • How does it work? The bottom layer is supportive, it helps the body to move effortlessly, which means there is not a lot of strength required to turn over while sleeping.
