What information do customers receive about their order?

  • Order confirmation email from Shopify – Shopify order number, product names, quantities, total price and respective discounts​

  • Order being processed email - order number, email, order status portal, contacts from the respective store​

  • Retail status portal to track their orders ​

Since Retail (Emma Stores) are relying on Magento and Shopify, customers CANNOT find their order status on the e-commerce website link.

Retail Order Status Portal

In September 2024, we launched an order status portal to track store orders. ​

​

  • NL: https://winkel-bestelling.emma-sleep.nl/orderstatus/cos/ ​​

  • DE: https://shop-bestellung.emma-matratze.de/orderstatus/cos/ ​​

  • ES: https://orden-tienda.emma-colchon.es/orderstatus/cos/ ​​

  • PT: https://encomenda-loja.colchaoemma.pt/orderstatus/cos/

  • UK: https://store-order.emma-sleep.co.uk/orderstatus/​

Customers should use their Shopify order number and the email they provided during the purchase moment in order to track the order.​

Note: In case customers reach out saying that they cannot log in in the portal, check if the email & external ID are correct/is the one the customer is mentioning.

Order Status Description​

There are 5 different possible status:

​

  • Order placed – Order was placed on shopify (at the store) ​​

  • Payment received – Payment confirmation and received by Emma​​

  • Order in process – WH received the order, and it is being prepared ​​

  • Order shipped – Order left the WH to the last mile partner/carrier​ (customers won't be able to access the package tracking link using this portal. They should receive a separate communication from our last mile partner) ​

  • ​​Delivered – full order was delivered to the customer​

In case there's an out-of-stock (OOS) situation, the status that the customer will see until the barcode is unblocked is "Payment received". This might cause some confusion, and customers might reach out because they will see the status on "Payment received" for a while.
