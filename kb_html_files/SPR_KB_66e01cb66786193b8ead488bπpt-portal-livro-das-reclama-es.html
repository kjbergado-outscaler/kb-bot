Portal Livro das Reclamações

The "Livro de Reclamações" is a consumer complaints portal in Portugal. It serves as a platform for individuals to submit complaints about products or services provided by businesses. The portal is regulated by the Consumer Protection Authority.

If companies fail to address complaints submitted through the Livro de Reclamações, they may face consequences such as fines or other legal actions. Non-compliance with resolving customer complaints can harm a company's reputation, customer trust, and may lead to further regulatory actions.


Therefore, it is crucial for businesses to respond to and resolve complaints in a maximum of 14 business days to maintain a positive image and comply with consumer protection regulations in Portugal. If the company fails to respond, heavy fines can be applied.
