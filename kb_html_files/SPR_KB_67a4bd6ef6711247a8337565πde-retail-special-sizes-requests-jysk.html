Overview

Jysk has over 1200 stores in DE, and we provide customer service to all of them. We don’t perform actions on any Jysk platforms, and the contacts subjects are basically the same from the other retailers - LC, PC, WIMO, Returns and Cancellations.

You’ll notice that usually the customer doesn’t send the order number when they contact us, so your first step might be requesting this information - either the Emma number, external order number or fullfilment number.
