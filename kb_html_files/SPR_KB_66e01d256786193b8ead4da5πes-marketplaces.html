¿What is a marketplace?


It is a platform where different shops can advertise their products, thus offering a range of options to the customer.

Management of ATC for marketplace


1. Amazon- PERMANENT


Amazon customer service is managed by a specific team within Emma called Marketplaces. Whenever you receive a ticket, you should redirect the ticket to this group within Freshdesk.


Amazon usually has the same prices as on our website.


They have now launched an Emma Essentials line, so they have products that we don't have on our website.



2. Maison du Monde


1. Permanent service on your website


2. Separate orders


3. CS on her own platform.



Customer service must be done through their own platform and not via email. In other words, if the end customer writes to us, we must redirect the customer to contact us through out the marketplaces platform.


The prices are usually the same as on our website.



3. Veepee- TEMPORARLY CAMPAIGNS


1. Temporary campaign


2. Bulk order


3. CS with Business development


In this case, the Veepee team places 1 order per campaign. They will always contact the internal Business Development team at Emma and only in case they need help to manage a collection, they will pass the information to us via the Marketing team chat.



4. Westwing- TEMPORARLY CAMPAIGNS


1. Temporary campaign


2. Individual orders


3. CS with Contact Centre



Customer service must be carried out by us, but always through the shop itself, and not through the end customer. In other words, if the end customer writes to us, we must redirect the customer to contact the platform.


These three platforms, when running specific campaigns, whose purpose is to reach customers who do not know Emma, have more competitive prices and lower prices than our website.


*Returns and refunds are processed in the same way as for the retail sector- credit memo.


Email- service@westwing.es


5. Showroomprive- TEMPORARLY CAMPAIGNS


1. Temporary campaign


2. Bulk order


3. CS with Contact Center


Email- servicioclientes_es@showroomprive.com


It is important to note that the products vary according to the campaign and are shared by the marketing team. Normally they never include beds and usually include mattresses + 1 pillow + protector. However, this may change from time to time.


*Returns and refunds are processed in the same way as for the retail sector - credit memo.



We should never contact the end customer directly, but rather contact the company.
