Credit Memo Information

Credit Memos are created whenever we have a cancelation or a return. This document that discounts the total or part of the value of an invoice. It's always associated to an Order.

​

When are Credit Memos needed?

Since the customer in retail market is the retailer itself, the process for the final client's refund is combined by:

  1. Emma refunds the retailer: The refund is made via a Credit Memo, that will later deduce this amount on the payments made every 30 / 60 or 90 days by the retailer. We receive these payments, that are the sum of all invoices deducing all the Credit Memos, via POP.

  2. The retailer refunds the customer: Internally, the retailer refunds the customer.

Retailers are supposed to receive the Credit Memo automatically for FluentCommerce orders, but not for Magento orders.

How to access Credit Memos

If you can't find the credit memos on the order page, please go to Magento -> Sales -> Credit Memos. You can search by Order Number (Order #) or Credit Memo Number (Refund #).
