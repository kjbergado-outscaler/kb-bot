Introduction

Utilize this article to identify the process of creation of supplement order.


A supplement purpose is to adapt customer interaction with our products in a better way, and whit this avoid returning products or in the case of a product claim (defective) or logistic claim (lost item).


Process of creation of Supplement


1. Once the order to which a supplement will be placed is located, you must go to the 3-dot icon, located at the top, on the right side.





2. Click and a menu will be displayed, select Create Supplement Order




3. You can search the menu by product name, but the better way to avoid errors of creation is indicate the SKU to make the supplement. Select the Item and quantity, and next.




4. Fill in all the customer's address data.

Note: If a single piece of information is missing, the Next button will not be enabled.



5. Once the data is complete, you will see it again and you can check that it is correct.

Also, select the reason for the supplement: Replacement, Compensation or Add-on and then the Submit button wil be available.




6. In the order, will appear that the supplement is created



7. You can find the supplement order, searching again the principal Order, or adding the Order + (-1)



Documenting MX Supplements

After creating a supplement we should always document it on this file: Supplements_LATAM.xlsx
