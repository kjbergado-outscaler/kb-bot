Product Communication


NamingV2 Emma Original Bed

The Emma Boxbed

Objective

BBv2 is supposed to capture the mid-range market segment – prices will be affordable like our flagship mattresses

MarketsAvailable for: PH, SG, AU, NZ
ExecutionIt will be marketed both as a standalone product (e.g., via Social media ads) and as a cross-sell product (e.g., pop-ups when customers buy a mattress)
Configuration

We will adjust to country-specific bed buying behavior:

  • In some countries (especially DK, SE), customers usually buy a bed system (= bed frame + mattress + optionally a topper)

  • In these countries, we will market the entire bed system; in all other countries, we will market the bed with an optional Emma mattress.

Assembly and disassembly

Frequently Asked Questions - FAQ


Characteristics


Is it non-flammable?
Yes, it passes the required cigarette test.


Is it vegan?
Yes, the Emma Boxbed is 100 % vegan and does not contain any animal-based materials, nor is tested on animals. Unfortunately, the mattress is inedible.


Is there a fire-retardant chemical on it?
No.


Can the bed be used without headboard?
Yes. After both boxes have arrived, you can decide whether to use the headboard or not. The Emma Boxbed even offers you enough storage to put it underneath the bed, if you decide not to use it.


Can I use another (my old/my own) headboard?
Sure, you can. Just place it behind the bed frame, against a wall, to make sure it is stable.


Can I buy it without the headboard?
Unfortunately, not at the moment. We want to offer the complete Boxbed experience and for that, a headboard is a must. If you decide not to use it, you can always store it. The Emma Boxbed even offers you enough storage space to place it underneath the bed, if you decide not to use it.

Can I use an electric slatted frame?

The slats that are delivered with the bed cannot be replaced by an electric slatted frame due to the construction of the bed.


I have my own slats; can I use them?

Unfortunately, no. The Emma Boxbed has its own suspension system made of slats which are connected to the bed frame sides with holders, offering you great comfort and support.


Do I need to use the Emma mattress with it, or would another mattress be fine as well?

You can use the bed with virtually any mattress and any topper. To get the best sleep experience, we would of course recommend using an Emma mattress with your Emma Boxbed. If you want to get the Boxspring bed experience, you can add a topper to it.


Is the bed adjustable?

The Emma Boxbed frame cannot be adjusted. CS – please check the actual size offerings for your county/region


Is the bed composed of 1 or 2 beds?

The Emma Boxbed is 1 bed but, consists of different parts for easy assembly.


Do you offer different colors?

At present, we offer only grey and light grey (CS please check as per your respective countries) ; but the Emma boxbed gives you the modern boxbed look. Due to its minimalistic and chic look in timeless anthracite, the box bed fits perfectly into every bedroom - even yours.


Can I order different feet?

The Emma Boxbed has been designed to be light weight but at the same time sturdy and stable. That´s the reason we only offer a set of 6 tapered square black feet

(4 feet for small sizes) with each bed.


Durability / Quality


How was the Boxbed tested on durability?

The Emma Boxbed has been tested extensively during development, as well as in its final production stage for durability and quality.

  • The wooden slats have been tested for bending fracture load, applying up to 500 Newtons force.
  • The wooden structure has been evaluated for formaldehyde emission for Composite Wood Products, according to international regulations. We have passed.
  • The fabric used for the upholstery meets the requirements for cover fabrics, according to international furniture regulations.


Can I place the Boxbed in the middle of the room?

Yes, but we recommend placing the Emma boxbed against a wall. That will offer you the maximum amount of stability.


Does the Emma Boxbed have Oeko-text certification?

The Emma Boxbed´s quality and durability is certified under international regulation. All components, including the upholstery have been tested and certified according to international regulation.


The fabric used for upholstery meets the requirements for cover fabrics according to international furniture regulations.


What certifications does the Emma Boxbed have?

The Emma Boxbed is certified under SGS regulations.


Guarantee / COO


Where does it come from?

The Emma Boxbed was designed, created and developed in Germany by our inhouse team of experts.


Where is the Emma Boxbed manufactured?

German and Chinese engineers collaborated to develop the Emma Boxbed. We have registered different product designs with the European Union Intellectual Property Office


How long is the guarantee?

We believe in the high quality of this product. That´s why we give 100 night-trial and 5 years guarantee on the product (excluding the screws).


Measurements


Do I need a topper with the bed?

To get the Box Bed experience, we recommend buying the Emma topper/Diamond degree topper (specific to country offerings).


Which mattress height can I use?

You can use the bed with virtually any mattress and any topper. To get the best sleep experience, we would of course recommend using an Emma mattress with your Emma Boxbed. If you want to get the Boxspring Bed experience, you can add a topper to it.


How high is the bed (with and without mattress)?

If you are using an Emma Original mattress, the distance between the floor and the top of the mattress is ca.55cm. The Emma Boxbed without the mattress has a height of 36cm from the floor (including the height of feet). Distance between the floor and top of headboard is ca. 110 cm (112 cm for GC model or 110 cm for Sleemon).


Distance between slats?

The distance between each slat varies depending on the bed size - 50mm-67 mm depending on bed size


How thick are the slats?

The Emma Boxbed slats are made with strong plywood material with 7.2 cm width and 1 cm thickness.


Assemble / Disassemble


How long does it take to build-up the Emma Boxbed?

Building the Emma Boxbed shouldn’t take longer than a good nap. If you consider yourself an expert, it should take you 30 to 45 minutes. If on the other hand, it is your first time building furniture, don´t worry, the manual and tools will make it simple and it should take you 45 to 60 minutes.


Can unbuild it and rebuild it when moving out?

Yes. You can build it and dismantle it between 10 and 15 times without damaging the structure. That says a lot about its stability and durability, don´t you think?


How long does it take to dismantle it?

The Emma Boxbed can be dismantled in less than 15 minutes. Please make sure to have the manual on hand. Have you lost it? Don´t worry, you can find it on our website (This is up to the Country teams to decide how and if they want to put the manual on the website).


What do I need to assemble the Boxbed?

To assemble the Emma Boxbed, you will need to have Boxes #1 and #2 with you, the manual (included in the booklet) and the L-shape wrench, included in Box #1.


Will it be assembled by the courier?

Emma has developed a new concept for Bedding Furniture. The Emma Boxbed is so simple, easy and convenient, that you won´t need to wait for the following working day to have someone build-up the bed for you, since you can easily do it yourself. (country specific delivery and assembly/white glove)
