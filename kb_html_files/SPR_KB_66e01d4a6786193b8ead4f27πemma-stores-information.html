Introduction

Emma has multiple Stores across Europe, including locations in the Netherlands, Germany, the UK, Spain, and Portugal.

Each market has a dedicated team of specialized Retail CS agents. It is important that ALL cases from Emma Stores customers should be directed to and handled by this specialized team. ​

​

To understand how to properly transfer a case to the Stores dedicated agents, please check this article. ​

​

Store customers who reach out to CS should never be sent back to the physical stores for support. Their cases must always be forwarded to the dedicated retail agents, regardless of the communication channel through which the customers contact Emma customer support.

Important Guidelines

  • You can find our full store portfolio for all markets on AirTable. If you are a dedicated store agent and do not have access, please notify your supervisor.

  • You are can provide store addresses only for pre-sales inquiries or if the customer insists on returning to the store for post-sale cases.

  • Whenever you receive a post-sale inquiry from a store customer, make every effort to resolve the issue without directing the customer back to the store. Support them through the entire process and make sure the case is handled by a dedicated Store CS agent. For more details, please refer to this article.

  • Do not rely on Magento for Store information (address, contacts, etc) as it may not be updated.

  • Emma stores have stock for some products, depending on the location. You can check whether an order was fulfilled by a store by reviewing the order details in either Shopify or Magento.
