Receiving Purchase Orders (POs)

  1. Receive PO via email

    • Emily sends the PO list to Tiara, who forwards it to us;

    • The PO contains the list of orders for the flash sale. The spreadsheet is password protected, and the password will be shared in the email.

​

​

  1. Extract required information from the PO

    • Identify the PO number (e.g 6131628).

    • Identify the Customer Order Number (e.g., starts with 105).

    • Locate SKU, customer details (name, address, contact information), order date, and tracking information.

  2. Copy and transfer order details

    • Open the internal spreadsheet;

    • Copy data from the PO into the spreadsheet.

  3. Check data

    • Double-check that customer details and SKUs are correctly recorded.

    • Verify that the courier assigned is UPS.

Order Tracking & Follow-Up

Checking Tracking Status

  • Access the DB Schenker Portal;

  • Select Order → View Outbound Order;

  • Search for the customer through the BU Key (BU-SPRTP);

  • There, you’ll be able to identify the customer’s order status and tracking information.

  • Click Excel to download the information.

​

​

Verify Upload in Fluent Commerce

  • Navigate to Fluent Commerce.

  • Search by Sport Pursuit’s BU key to check order status.

  • Copy new orders and paste into the tracking file.

Tracking Number Allocation

  • Use XLOOKUP in Excel to cross-check the PO numbers and tracking numbers with the file you exported from DB Schenker.

Monitoring Delivery Date

  • Track delivery status daily.
