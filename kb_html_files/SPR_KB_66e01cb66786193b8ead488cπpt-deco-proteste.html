DECO Proteste


DECO Proteste is a Portuguese consumer association that focuses on advocating for consumer rights and interests. It provides information and support to consumers, conducts product testing, and addresses issues related to consumer protection.


If a company does not respond to complaints raised by DECO Proteste or fails to address consumer concerns, it may face negative consequences. This can include damage to the company's reputation, potential legal actions, and regulatory penalties. Responding to complaints from DECO Proteste is important for businesses to uphold consumer rights and maintain a positive image in the market.



How to handle it - Process in Freshdesk


DECO Proteste complaints will come directly to Freshdesk in the form of an email (reclamacao@deco.proteste.pt).


Reply to it in the same way we reply to a regular ticket, but keep in mind this is a formal complaint, no GDPR information should be disclosed.
