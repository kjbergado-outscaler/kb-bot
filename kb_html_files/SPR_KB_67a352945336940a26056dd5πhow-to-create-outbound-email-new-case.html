How to create Outbound Email from the Case Console

Step 1: At the Console, when you have a case open, you can check the top right corner for a button named "Create new case".

​

Step 2: After clicking on the button, a pop-up window will appear and there you have to proceed as the following:

​

- Customer Email Address: insert the email of to who you want to send the message;

​

- Emma Email From: Select the Emma email related to your market/Line of Business.

It's MANDATORY to select the correct email address to avoid the customer reply being sent to the incorrect queues/agents. After selecting the email, below it will appear a message indicating the Line of Business related to the email, so you can verify if the email sleected is the correct one;

​

- Assignment: This option is automatically marked and cannot be removed. This will guarantee that the new outbound email created is assigned to the agent that is creating it.

﻿
Then, click on "Next" to proceed.

﻿

﻿

​

​

Step 3: Next, a new pop-up window will appear showing the new case number created and the case will be automatically assign to you. There is no need to manually assign the case to you, as the system already does it.

You can copy the new case number just to guarantee, and then click on "Next".

​

​

​

The new case will not automatically open on your screen, but instead it goes to the left side bar on the "Assign to me" list, as indicated in red on the image below. Like this, if needed, you can navigate from one case to the other.

﻿

Step 4: . Now is the moment to write the email, and send it to the customer from the contact box as normal.

﻿

Note:

Please make sure to review and select the correct Account Email address related to your market/line of business from the option "To" address option.

How to create Outbound Email from the Home Page Console

﻿

Step 1: Go to the Agent Console Home page, and on the left side just below your name you can see the blue button indicating "Create Outbound Email":

﻿

﻿

﻿Step 2: After clicking on the button, a pop-up window will appear and there you have to proceed as the following:

​

- Customer Email Address: insert the email of to who you want to send the message;

​

- Emma Email From: Select the Emma email related to your market/Line of Business.

It's MANDATORY to select the correct email address to avoid the customer reply being sent to the incorrect queues/agents. After selecting the email, below it will appear a message indicating the Line of Business related to the email, so you can verify if the email sleected is the correct one;

​

- Assignment: This option is automatically marked and cannot be removed. This will guarantee that the new outbound email created is assigned to the agent that is creating it.

﻿
Then, click on "Next" to proceed.

﻿

﻿

Step 3: Next, a new pop-up window will appear shwing the case number created and you just have to click on "Next" to be automatically re-direct to the case page. Click and wait a couple of seconds.

​

​

﻿

Step 4: After that, the case is created and a profile as well (or the case will be automatically assigned to a pre-existent profile, as per the image below). Now is the moment to write the email, and send it to the customer from the contact box as normal.

﻿

Note:

Please make sure to review and select the correct Account Email address related to your market/line of business from the option "To" address option.

​

​

​

​

Step 5: To finalize, right down the email message to the customer and click on "Send" (example bellow).
