Product Communication


The official name of this product on Magento and Tableau is Emma Original Hybrid CE mattress; to avoid confusion internally with the Emma UK Original Hybrid Mattress, it is sometimes also being referred to as the Emma (Continental) Hybrid.

USPs


The better mattress is an affordable premium product (i.e., offering premium quality at an attractive price). The key product dimensions to be stressed are function and quality.

As springs are the key differentiator with respect to the good mattress, the (functional) focus should be on the benefits that these are bringing to the table (i.e., ideal spinal alignment + better ergonomics, immediate adjustment to all changes in sleeping position as well as directing the air flow for an ideal sleeping climate) and how this translates to better rest and relaxation. This is done by high-quality explanatory assets, e.g., pictures/videos showing the springs in action (e.g., mattress aligning perfectly after a person turns around during the night). Customers understand that they are reaching the next level of sleep with the better mattress as they are giving their body a lot back with just a small extra price to pay in comparison.


Superior comfort

Combining the best of two worlds: Our award-winning foams and premium pocket springs guarantee optimal body support, ideal spinal adjustment, and pressure relief wherever needed – for every sleeping position and body type thanks to the optimized zoning. Extra plush while surprisingly supportive.


Engineered to keep you cool

Our special Aeroflex spring technology, the breathable cover, and open-pored foams keep you cool and dry all night.


Exceptional quality

Crafted from high-quality foams and extra-resistant steel coils, this mattress will elevate your sleeping experience to the next level for years to come. To prove our point, we grant you a 10-year warranty.

Point-elastic Airgocell Foam


The top layer adapts to your body and provides good ventilation due to its open pores.

Benefits

  • Temperature regulation = Breathability

  • Moisture (sweat) will be absorbed and then will evaporate, so the customer doesn´t wake up sweating.

  • Mattress keeps its shape during its whole life since moisture doesn´t affect properties on the mattress after it dissipates.

  • Low rolling resistance

What does it mean?

If the rolling resistance is high, the body needs more strength to toss and turn, and then the person will wake up more during the night. It has much higher responsiveness than memory foam, which has a slower response. Because of this, using this foam on the top layer will provide bounciness on a mattress.   

How does it work?

The bottom layer is supportive, it helps the body to move effortlessly, which means there is not a lot of strength required to turn over while sleeping.



Adaptive Visco-elastic Foam


The middle layer adapts to your body,

which helps promote optimal pressure

load, both for low and high weight loads. 

Benefits 

  • Adapts perfectly to your body shape.

  • Joint pain and pressure relief. People with joint pain will appreciate the conforming ability of a memory foam mattress, as it gives the feeling of hugging the pressure points of the body. 

  • Provides spinal support. Memory foam conforms to your body, helping to keep your spine in neutral alignment. This ensures you won’t wake up with a sore back. 

  • Limits motion transfer (Low rolling resistance) You won’t have to worry about being woken up by a partner in the middle of the night if they get out of bed. Because the mattress isn’t tied together in one consecutive coil system, if your partner gets up to use the restroom in the middle of the night, you aren’t going to feel a thing.

Cover


Breathable and Climate Regulating Mattress Cover 

The Emma Original Hybrid CE (Continental) cover consists of a light fiber that immediately releases moisture and ensures a good sleeping climate. At the same time, the cover is stretchable and can adapt well to the body.  


Characteristics 

  • Top fabric: 99% polyester and 1% elastane, knitted white fabric going 6 cm over the sides. Completely detachable top to bottom light gray zipper between border and top panel fabric.

  • Border fabric: 100% polyester, dark grey fabric with waterfall design going 5cm into the bottom to create a mirror. On one side, a brand label is sewed in.

  • Bottom fabric: 87% polyester and 13% polypropylene, anti-slip/ white woven fabric going 12 cm to the sides to create a mirror.

  • Handles: 100% Polyester, 4x Vertical Handles

Benefits

  • Breathable cover 

  • Elasticity. It fits perfectly to the mattress core 

  • Completely removable and washable
