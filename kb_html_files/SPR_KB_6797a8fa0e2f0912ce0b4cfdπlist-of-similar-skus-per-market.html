This article provides guidelines on how to identify similar/equivalent SKUs in case the ones that customers requested is unavailable. For example:

  • A customer has a legitimate product/logistic claim but the original SKU is Out of Stock or Phased Out.

  • There's a delay, the SKU the customer ordered is Out of Stock, and the customer, wishing to cancel, is open to a suggestion of a similar item to repurchase instead.

How to find the similar item:

  1. In this link you'll find your market's file with all the current sold SKUs.

  2. In the first column you have the SKU, in the second and third columns, you'll find the equivalent items.
