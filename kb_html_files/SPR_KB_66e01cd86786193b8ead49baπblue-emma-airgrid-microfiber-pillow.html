Summary of Product


Also known as Emma Zero Gravity, the Emma AirGrid Microfiber is a pillow with the AirGrid technology. Essentially is the product that combines all the characteristics: adjustable, the cover is cooling due to breathability, and also has improved ergonomics, giving better support compared to standard microfiber pillows. Now in Blue, instead of Purple.

USPs (Unique Selling Points)


Advanced Temperature Regulation

  • Thanks to the honeycomb-patterned Grid – Air Grid®, it draws the heat away from the body and increase airflow to prevent heat retention, to help you reach a restful sleep.

  • Fits every season -> temperature regulation. It stays cool in hot conditions and acts as an insulator in cold temperatures to guarantee falling into deep sleep phase faster and having better restorative sleep.

Adjustable Height

  • There are two removable layers of hollowfiber inside, you could adjust the height and support of the pillow by taking it out or putting it back in, as well as opening each of them to adjust the level of firmness.

  • To optimize and keep correct cervical alignment as well as, a comfortable support for the head and neck:

    • Side and Back Sleepers: It is recommended to use the 2 layers

    • Stomach/Belly Sleepers: It is recommended to use only 1 layer, depending on individual lying preference

Improved Ergonomics

  • The adaptive and comfortable fiber and Air Grid® not only adjusts to your changing sleeping positions but after a good night of sleep also regenerates perfectly to its original shape.
