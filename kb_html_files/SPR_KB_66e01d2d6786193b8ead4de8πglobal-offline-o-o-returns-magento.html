Understanding Return reasons

There may be different scenarios in which customers may request to return their product. First, you should always understand if the return is:

​

  • Trial period: It means the customer would like to return for certain reasons under the risk-free trial period, the exclusive benefit offered to our Emma customers that can vary between product and country (100-nights, 50 nights, etc).

Caution: Jysk and Lidl don't have trial nights. Please only accept Claim Related Returns.

​

  • Claim related: (logistic or product claim): It means the return is related to a logistic claim (delay, out of stock, damage) or a product claim (defective product). If the return is related to a claim, please ensure you handle the request as per related claim steps, not as a Return (Trial period) request.

Triggering the Return - OMS

The data you generate while performing the return in Magento is also triggering the logistic next steps for the Emma country operations team, or it may require some Back office manual tasks.

​

That's why is crucial we provide accurate information while performing the return request in Magento.

​

Here is a step by step on how to register and effectively solve a Return Request:

​

1. The ticket is received

​

2. Return Form - Many times customers send us directly the completed form or text the return request. These are the data that we have to have. Special attention to know if the product is open or closed.

​

3. In Magento enter the External Number or the Order Number. Open the order and make the "Return Products". Remember to always put "No Refund".

​

4. Choose "Return Products" and fill in all the information in red.

  • If the Product is closed, it will go back to the respective WH. Please ask it directly through e-mail.

  • If the Product is open, it will be requested to a Charity. If we don't find any charity interested in the pick-up, we request the pick-up to XPO through Recogidas XPO-SP

​

5. The status stays as "Expected".

​

6. We go back to the ticket and create a child ticket with the association (Paco - in this case to Betel).

​

7. We send the ticket and leave it in "waiting on customer".

​

8. After the collection, the final customer must send the proof of collection to the store. The store has to send us the receipt for the Credit Memo to be processed.

​

9. In Magento, we put the order number. We have to upload the photo of the receipt to verify that the pickup was made and to process the refund.

​

​

10. Click on "upload files". Select the photo and choose the option "Confirm as received". Finally, save & upload.
