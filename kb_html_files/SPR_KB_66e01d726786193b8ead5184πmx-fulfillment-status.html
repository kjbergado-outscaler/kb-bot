What is a Fulfilment?


When an order is created in our system, every item of the order will have a related fulfillment ID.

Fulfillment Status




Fulfilment Status
What does it mean?
Pending
Payment for the order has not yet been received
Assigned
Payment for the order has been received and the Fulfilment has been assigned to a Fulfilment location, a stock has been reserved for the Fulfilment
Submitted to FL
The Fulfilment has been transmitted to the Fulfilment location and waiting for FL's confirmation
Received by FL
The Fulfilment has been received and confirmed by the FL
Picked and Packed by FL
The process of fulfillment has been started by the FL
Ready for Dispatch
The fulfillment process was done and the Fulfilment awaits the pick up from the delivery carrier
Dispatched
The Fulfilment has been picked up by the delivery carrier and on its way to the customers
Arrived at Sorting Centre
The Fulfilment has arrived at carrier's sorting center and on its way to the customers
Out for Delivery
The Fulfilment is loaded to a carrier vehicle and is about to be delivered to the end customer
Requested for Delivery Option
The customer requested another delivery option to the carrier
Complete
The Fulfilment has been delivered to the customers
Canceled
Fulfillment is canceled by an user
