Order


Order definition


Each purchase made by the customer via any of our channels becomes an order in our Order Management System (FluentCommerce).


While navigating to our website and placing the order, the customer selects specific options (such as product, color, and size) and provides core information to ensure the purchase of the product.





The order will always contain:

  • Order number (generated after the purchase is created in our order management system)
  • Products (SKU) & quantity
  • Customer information (email / personal data)
  • Billing information
  • Shipping information
  • Payment information
  • Order Summary (Total & tax specifications)




All this data will be centralized in our Order Management Tool.


The order management tool will be connected to other systems, ensuring the information provided by the customer while buying a product on our website, is fully delivered to his home on the expected and agreed time.

Order Status - Meaning




Order StatusWhat does it means?
PendingThe order is waiting for the receipt of payment (depending on the payment method)
Payment Receivedthe payment has been received by the system and is being sent to the warehouse
Processing(not always) but it means the order is in the process by the warehouse
CompletedThe system was updated by the warehouse as the package is ready to be shipped / on the way to the customer.
Cancel RequestedThe order has been canceled and waiting to be processed by the refund team
CanceledCancellation has been pushed through, and refund (if applicable) is being processed by the system
RefundedThe refund has been processed on our end and the funds have been released from our account

When an order is created in our system, every item of the order will have a related fulfillment ID.




Every item of the order will have a related Barcode ID.


The barcode (fulfillment) is a unique ID to track the journey of an Item at any point in time.

Order Changes / Cancellations requests


Once the information of the order has been transmitted to the warehouse/carrier, we can't take it back. This means modification/cancellation can only be placed in the system up to barcode Prepared for Export.





After it, we will try workarounds to stop the journey of the order, but we won't be able to do it through our system.


You can review how to proceed accessing the CS Processes (Customer Inquiries) related article.
