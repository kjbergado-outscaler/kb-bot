What you must know before opening a JIRA ticket to Learning & Knowledge Team?


1) Before opening a ticket with us, please make sure that your request belongs to one of the Types of requests below:


  • Process or Tool knowledge

  • Product clarification

  • Training request


2) Understand that each type of request has a level of Priority, and this will define what you have to fill in the Jira field of "Urgency":

Type of RequestPriority Urgency on JIRA

Process or Tool knowledge

High

Product clarification

Medium

Training request

Low



3) Only Supervisors, Team Leaders, Quality Analysts, andTrainers can open JIRA tickets to the L&K Team. If you are an agent, please reach out to your supervisor to address your issue or suggestion.



4) Please make sure that in your ticket you include the KB article as a reference and/or any Freshdesk ticket number.
