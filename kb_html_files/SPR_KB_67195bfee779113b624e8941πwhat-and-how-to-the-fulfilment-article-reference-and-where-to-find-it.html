The Fulfilment Article Reference (FAR) is the unique identifier of the item that has been sold to the customer. You can think of it as the 'barcode' of the product.

For claim cases, adding the FAR to the case is mandatory.

Format of the Fulfilment Article Reference: The FAR always has the same format, that is: 8 numbers, followed by an underscore, followed by an alphanumeric string of 7 characters. As an example: '12345678_A1B2C2'

Where to find it?

In Fluent Commerce, the FAR is called the "Article Reference". You can find the Fulfilment Article Reference via the following steps:

  1. Open the order

  2. On the order page on FluentCommerce, click on the "Fulfilments" tab:

  3. Click on the ID number:

  4. Then the Fultilment Article Reference will be under "Article Ref":

Good to Know

The FAR is mandatory when logging a Product Claim.

You must insert the FAR in the dedicated section under 'Properties' on the support case.
