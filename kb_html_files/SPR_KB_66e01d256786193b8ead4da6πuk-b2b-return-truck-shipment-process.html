1. Objective: This SOP outlines the specific procedures that customer support agents are required to follow when handling returns, whether they are regular returns or those resulting from product issues.

2. Scope: This SOP is exclusively applicable to retail customers placing truck shipment orders who wish to initiate product returns. The procedures detailed herein are designed to address the specific scenarios related to returns within this customer segment.

3. Instructions:

  • Regular Return:

  • Arrange Collection manually in the customer's warehouse: Please email Rowena with the details. Rowena will upload the collection file in Rhenus.
  • Once the collection is completed, raise the credit memo. (for John Lewis do not need to raise the credit memo)
  • Process the return via "Return Team".

  • Faulty Product Return:

  • The retailer needs to email us with Photographic evidence after retail operations will raise a credit memo against the debit.
  • Arrange Collection manually to the customer's warehouse: Please email Rowena with the details. Rowena will upload the collection file in Rhenus.
  • Once the collection is completed, raise the credit memo.
  • Process the return via "Return Team".

Important: Any Truck Shipment order should follow the terms and conditions that the retailers offer, we act as a supplier so the Retailer has the rights of it. This means that if the retailer offers a 200-night trial the agent should process the claim.

Questions related to the process:
