USPs (Unique Selling Points)

Value for Money

We use high-quality steel pocket springs in our core layer, making the mattress much more durable than a foam-based mattress. Switching to our Edge-to-Edge technology is good for your wallet and even better for your night's sleep.

Optimal Comfort

Our 7-zone mattress is designed to help you maintain good posture and keep your spine aligned. You are supported in all the right places. Whether you sleep on your back, on your side or on your stomach, our Hybrid Lite mattress provides ultimate relaxation and helps you wake up energized.

Profound and undisturbed sleep

Our improved pocket springs now run edge to edge, creating extensive motion damping. Enjoy a deep, uninterrupted sleep, no matter how often your partner tosses and turns during the night. Perfect for couples and light sleepers!

Cover specifications


Breathable and Climate Regulating Mattress Cover 
The Emma cover consists of a light fiber that immediately releases moisture and ensures a good sleeping climate
At the same time, the cover is stretchable and can adapt well to the body.
Characteristics 

Top fabric

  • 100% polyester
  • Knitted white fabric going 6 cm over the sides.
  • Completely detachable top to bottom light gray zipper between border and top panel fabric.


Border fabric

  • 100% polyester
  • Dark grey fabric with waterfall design going 5cm into the bottom to create a mirror.
  • On one side a brand label is sewed in.
  • Thicker border fabric to ensure that the edge to edge spring core won’t be detected, adding a luxurious look to the mattress.


Bottom fabric

  • 83% polyester and 17% polypropylene
  • Anti-slip/ white woven fabric going 12 cm to the sides to create a mirror.
  • Handles
  • 100% Polyester
  • 4x Vertical Handles


In France the mattress size 140x190 has 8 handles the rest of the sizes have 4 handles
