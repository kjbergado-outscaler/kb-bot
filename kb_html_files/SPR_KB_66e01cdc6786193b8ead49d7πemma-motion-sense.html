Summary of the product


The Emma Motion Sense is a smart product that uses technology to adjust the mattress during sleep and according to every single body movement of a person. Statistically, a person changes position up to 25 times while sleeping and that's why Emma developed this mattress. Our goal is to guarantee comfort and rest feeling no matter how many times the body moves while sleeping.



How Emma Sense works


The Emma Sense combines 3 technological features: Contour interaction system; Deepsleep optimization and App Control. Below we can understand exactly what are the functions of each one of them:


Contour interaction system

This function ensures a perfect individual regeneration with interactive chambers that will automatically adjust the body movements during sleep. The system works with a detection feature where the sensors identify every position change during sleep in order to have as result in optimized support and spinal alignment.


The smart control unit inflates and deflates according to the chambers and the sleeping position. For a lateral position, the chambers on head and shoulders go low; the lumber region goes high; and the pelvis region goes middle. In the case of the supine position, the chambers of the head and shoulder go middle; the lumber region goes high; and the pelvis region goes low. In the case of the abdominal position, the head and shoulder chamber go high; the lumber region goes middle; and the pelvis position goes high.


Deep sleep optimization


This feature is to help get the ideal sleep temperature. The graphite particles in the top diamond degree layer reduce body temperature by 1.5Cº faster than regular mattresses, and also the body heat gets dissipated away from the sleeper making the ideal sleeping temperature to get reached faster.


App control

The use of the Emma Motion App allows to optimize the comfort and usability of the mattress. The user can adjust the setting to their preferences via a Manual or Automatic mode.


The Manual mode let the user set the body support preference themselves. And on the Automatic mode, the user sets the sensitivity of the sensors and enters their preferred lying position. Then it's just laying down and relaxing in the knowledge that the motion sensor will automatically support the sleep based on the information included on the app. 



In the double bed, two separate cores are unified with a diamond degree topper, that allows both people to chose their individual mattress firmness, not compromising the sleep quality and no spaces in between the mattress

How to set up your Emma Sense mattress


Step 1:

For purchasing the 90x200cm mattress: Unpack the mattress and remove the plastic wrapping and place it horizontally onto your slatted frame. The side on which the power cord runs from the mattress to the outside should best be placed at the head part of the bed.


For purchasing the 180x200cm mattress: Unpack the two 90x200 cm mattresses and remove the plastic wrapping and place them horizontally onto your slatted frame. Each side on which the power cord runs from the mattress to the outside should best be placed at the head part of the bed.


Step 2:

Take the power cord and plug it into the power socket. It is recommended to use an over-voltage protection device. In the case of the 180x200cm, make sure to connect both power cords.


Step 3:

The Emma Motion Sense automatically inflates with air after the switch-on.


Step 4:

To make further settings according to your wishes, connect the mattress with the Emma Motion App (check the "How to" below).


Step 5:

Now take the topper that is in the package and place it on the mattress.




How to set up the Emma Motion App


Step 1:

Download the Emma Motion App (available for Apple or Google Play Store), and then open the app to establish the connection with your Motion Sense mattress.



Step 2:

Then go to the WIFI settings of your smartphone and select the WIFI with the name starting with “ReST…”


Step 3:

In case you want to connect two 90x200 cm mattresses with one smartphone for each, you should see two different

“ReST…” networks. You can find out which network is designated for which mattress from the respective WIFI network ID on a tag of the Smart Pump on the inside of the mattress core.



Step 4:

Now go back into the Emma Motion App and confirm that you have connected your smartphone with the WIFI network “

ReST…”. Updates are installed automatically whenever available.


Step 5:

Now choose on which side of the bed your mattress is and the connecting process is finished.


Step 6:

In conclusion, now select in the app your regular WIFI network at home and dial in to be able to simultaneously access both the bed as well as the home WIFI. Please check that you have entered the correct password for your normal WIFI network at home. You can also skip this step.


Step 7:

Now go to the WIFI settings in your smartphone and connect to your normal WIFI network at home. Then go back to the Emma Motion App and press “Done”. After this, the connecting process is completed.




Important


Before starting to use the app, people have to lie in bed in their normal sleeping position with the Smartphone in their hands. Because the weight and position affect the air pressure and feel of the bed. If they have more than one sleeping position, try the one they usually wake up in, because this is the position they usually spend the most time in at night. Make sure the pillow is fully supporting the curve of the neck. The bottom of the pillow should reach the shoulders. If they‘re a back sleeper, they can even place the pillow slightly under your shoulders.


How to control via the App


The Emma Motion App works in an Automatic and Manual mode and this means different ways of setting up the firmness of the mattress.


Manual mode:

To set the individual zones, pull the white dot up or down to the desired comfort level. You will notice that the zone bar is displayed in orange during the inflating process and in blue during the deflating process. As soon as the desired comfort level is reached, you can press “STOP” at the bottom and the zone will adjust to this setting. When setting the individual zones, your pressure map changes in real-time so you can configure the optimal pressure relief.





Automatic mode:

Pull the slider under the heading “firmness” in the right lower corner to the left to get a softer setting or to the right to get a firmer setting. All zones switch to orange (firmer) while inflating and to blue (softer) while deflating. They will move up or move down according to the movement of the body on the mattress during sleep. As soon as the firmness is steady, the bed will adjust each actuated zone slightly more firmly or softly individually, until the perfect pressure relief setting is reached. If a person should not want that the mattress adjusts optimally to each of your movements, set the slider for “sensitivity” to the left. As soon as define the ideal pressure relief and fall asleep, the bed will do the rest.

Warning Notes


Flammability of Polyurethane Foam


The mattress core of your Emma Motion Sense consists of flammable polyurethane foams. Polyurethane foam burns if it is exposed to open flames or a large source of heat. Do not expose your Emma Motion Sense to open flames or other direct or indirect heat sources such as those present during welding or abrasive cutting. Burning cigarettes, radiators or candles also represent sources of danger. As soon as polyurethane foam has ignited, it will burn fast, release tremendous heat and consume a lot of oxygen. As a consequence of the high consumption of oxygen, there is an acute danger of suffocation for people nearby. The gases released while the foam is burning can result in a loss of consciousness or cause serious health damages if significant quantities of them are inhaled. If polyurethane foam has ignited, it is very difficult to extinguish the fire. Foam fires, which have been seemingly put out already, may still glimmer and reignite. Be sure to have the firefighters check if the fire has been completely extinguished.

Data security & Remote Control

Many customers are concerned about their data security. Emma takes this issue very seriously. Therefore, the Motion app has been designed in such a way that no data transfer takes place between the customer's mobile device and Emma. This means that the app works like a remote control. In addition to the change from automatic to manual, as well as a detailed setting of the desired hardness, the sensitivity of the sensors can also be changed via the app.


If you want to be on the safe side, or if you don't have a mobile device, the Emma Motion also comes with wireless remote control.



FAQs (in progress)


Q. Does the bed have to be connected to my home wifi?


No. The Emma Motion Sense Smart Pump has its own WiFi signal, which will connect directly with your smartphone, regardless of whether or not you have a home WIFI. Should the internet be down, but you do still have electricity, your bed will retain 100% of its functions. Upon connecting to the pump, you can connect your pump to your home network, but this step is entirely optional.



Q. I'm having trouble pairing my Emma Motion Sense to my Wi-Fi. What t?


If you are having difficulties pairing your bed to your Emma MOTION Sense mattress, here are some things to consider:


ROUTER:

- Ensure that your router is not too far from the bed and that the signal is strong;

- Ensure that your router does not have MAC filtering enabled;

- WEP security is not supported (please use WPA2, WPA or None);

- 5G Wi-Fi networks are not supported;

- VPN must be disabled during the pairing process but can be enabled after pairing is successful.


PASSWORD:

- Your Wi-Fi password should be shorter than 25 characters;

- Your Wi-Fi network name must be shorter than 32 characters;

- Ensure that your Wi-Fi password is correct – otherwise your whole setup process needs to be redone.


SETTINGS:

- Captive portals (Wi-Fi networks that redirect to a webpage to proceed) are not supported;

- On some Android devices there is a Smart Network Switch or Auto Network Switch setting, this must be turned off

for the pairing to succeed.



Q. The Emma Motion Sense app does not respond or is frozen. What to do?


Most problems with the Emma Motion Sense App can be resolved easily by closing the app on your device. Follow

the instructions of your device to completely exit the app. If “clear cache” is an option for your apps, please choose

this option. The app will then restart.



Q. What happens if the bed loses the connection with the pump or the WiFi signal of my bed is no longer detectable?


The Emma MotionN Sense resorts to a mini-processor in the Smart Pump housing. As with most electronic devices, the case may occur that the bed will no longer respond to the app commands. In that case, please unplug the power cable from the power outlet, wait for 10 seconds, plug it back in, and restart the Emma MOTION App. If restarting the mattress does not solve the problem, please open the cover of the mattress to reach the Smart Pump inside the mattress, and press and hold the button on the Smart Pump which has the Wi-Fi symbol on it for 10 seconds. This should resolve the problem.



Q. My dog or cat typically sleeps with me at night. Is that okay? Will it negatively affect the functionality of the bed?


Of course, it’s okay! Your furry friends will get the best sleep of their lives as well. The size of your cat or dog will determine how much of an adjustment is made (only if you have the bed set in automatic mode) but it’s also possible to change your sensitivity level and to reduce it. If you are worried about the bed making too many adjustments because of your pet, we’d recommend leaving it in manual mode.



Q. Which mode should I be using: automatic or manual?


The mode you use and the comfort settings you settle on are entirely up to you. After receiving your bed it’s good to start with the automatic mode and to get a feeling for the different sensitivity and firmness levels. Perhaps the best attribute of the bed is that you can change the settings and modes anytime you feel like it! Afterward, we recommend you take some time to try each mode and find a comfortable setting that feels best to you.



Q. Why does the bed not inflate during installation?


To ensure that your nightly sleep is not disturbed, the inflating process is deliberately slow. The complete inflating of the mattress can take up to one hour in the initial installation. Sometimes the Smart Pump also needs a reset after it was plugged in the first time in order to inflate the bed correctly. Assure yourself that the hoses are connected properly, disconnect the Smart Pump for 10-15 seconds, reconnect it with your smartphone and select “Inflate bed.”



Q. A big bulge can occur during the inflating of the bed?


The Emma MOTION Sense includes numerous Smart Chambers, which may shift under the foam during transport. In that case, zip off the top two layers of the bed, press the Smart Chambers flatly so that they lie against each other inside of the bed in between the support rails and zip the bed closed again. It should now sit flatly.



Q. Does the bed also work during a power outage?


During a power outage, the Emma MOTION Sense temporarily loses the capacity of adjusting automatically. It will then stay in the last firmness level chosen until the electricity returns. Using overvoltage protection is strongly recommended in order to avoid damage to the electronics in the pump.



Q. Can I open my Emma Motion Sense Cover?


We do not recommend opening your cover. Tampering with the internal systems of the hub will void your warranty and refunds will not be provided. An exception is opening the cover of the mattress for resetting purposes only.
