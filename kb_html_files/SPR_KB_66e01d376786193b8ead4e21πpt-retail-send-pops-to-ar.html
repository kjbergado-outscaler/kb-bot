POP (Proof Of Payment)

Retailers don't pay the orders like a regular customer. They accumulate invoices (that needs to be payed) and credit memos (that needs to be took off of the paying value) and then pay them to Emma.
This payment can be made via SEPA - a direct debit, where no confirmation is needed on our side - or via Banking Transference - where we need to make the connection betwueen the Retailer and Accounts Receivables (the finance team).
When Retailers pay their invoices via Bank Transference, we receive 2 e-mails:

  • 1 from the Bank, with the transference proof to Emma's IBAN (that belongs to Germany)

  • 1 from the Retailer, with the invoices they are paying and the credit memos they are deducing.


These documents are the POPs (Proofs of Payment), and need to be shared with Accounts Receivables by following 2 steps:

  1. Save the received documents in this folder

  2. Fill and attach the documents to the Monday Form

How to send POPs to AR

When the customer sends debit or payment proofs, we forward them to receivables.pt@emma-sleep.com with

nadia.vasconcelos@emma-sleep.com in cc.

How to request POPs to a Retailer

Sometimes, retailers make their payments without sending any documents (POPs) to us. At these times, you should request the information to the retailer.

After receiving the Bank Transference e-mail, check with the colleagues if they have received any e-mail with this info from the retailer that payed the invoice. If not, create a child ticket and send an e-mail to the retailer requesting the POP. You can find the contacts of each of the retailers in this document .
