Refunds in Retail
Magento orders:

Since the customer in retail market is the retailer itself, the process for the final client's refund is combined by:

  1. Emma refund the retailer: The refund is made via a Credit Memo, that will later deduce this amount on the payments made every 30 / 60 or 90 days by the retailer. We receive these payments, that are the sum of all invoices deducing all the Credit Memos, via POP.

  2. The retailer refund the client: Internally, the retailer refunds the client.

​

After the creation of the Credit Memo, kindly inform the retailer that the document has been generated. Don't send this document unless the retailer asks for it.

Exception: For Miguel Barros (gerente@colchoesecompanhia.pt) always send the Credit Memos when generated.

Fluent Commerce - COS:

  • Credit Memos for Returns – each credit memo should be created by each order in Fluent Commerce (https://emmalearninghub.com/c/course?init=assignmentStart&start=true&assignmentId=1677170125331_1&objectTypeId=140&initFrom=courseCommunityLink). So no more Jiras created for Returns.
