Scope

To prevent returns of open orders that remain unresolved for extended periods by taking coordinated action between Backoffice, Order Management (OM), and Customer Service (CS). This helps reduce logistics costs and improve our OTIF (On Time In Full) performance.

Process Frequency

This procedure is carried out weekly, following the order clean-up performed by the Backoffice team.

Process Benefits

  • Prevents unnecessary returns to the warehouse.

  • Reduces logistics costs.

  • Improves customer experience, ensuring they're informed in time.

  • Boosts OTIF performance, contributing to our operational goals.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Backoffice: Identifies and compiles the list of open orders requiring action.

  • Order Management (OM): Reviews comments and categorizes the necessary next steps.

  • Customer Service (CS): Proactively contacts retailers based on OM's findings.

Step by Step Procedure

Step 1: Weekly File Review

  • Backoffice shares an Excel file every week listing open orders that need action from OM or LM.

  • The file includes: order number, current status, comments, last update date, and responsible team.

Step 2: OM Review & Filtering

  • OM reviews the comments attached to each order.
