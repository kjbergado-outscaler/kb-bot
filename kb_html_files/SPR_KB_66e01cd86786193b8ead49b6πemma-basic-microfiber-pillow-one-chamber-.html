USPs


Extremely Comfy and yet supportive

This pillow is filled with our most comfortable fiber to support you all night long! The adaptive material not only adjusts to your changing sleeping position but after a good night of sleep also regenerates perfectly to its original shape. So you can cuddle yourself every night into this comfort pillow and be comfy as on day one! Perfect for Side and Backsleepers!

100% polyester cover to maximize breathability

The 100% Polyester cover is what makes this pillow special. The down proof cover with membrane effect ensures perfect breathability but stays resistant to any bacteria or dustmites. Combined with our high-tech microfiber this pillow guarantees for a great sleeping climate. Enjoy all perks of a down pillow, and even more!


Allergy friendly and machine washable

Our Microfiber pillow is made out of our special AeroSoft filling, where dustmites and bacteria have no chance and therefore is perfect for people with allergies. The whole pillow is maschine washable so you can keep it fresh as on day one! It is OEKO Tex 100 certified.

Materials:

  • Cover fabric: 100% polyester down proof, no piping

  • Filling: 100% super soft Microfiber filling


  • Wash at maximum 40 degrees (only the fiber part, foam not to be washed)

  • Do not bleach. It could lose color or be damaged during bleaching.

  • Do not Iron

  • Cleaning with Perchloroethylene. Use laundry detergent.

  • Low tumble dry

If you don´t use the dryer, make sure that the microfibers are well spread on the corners of the pillow. Let the pillow dry on a plane surface.
