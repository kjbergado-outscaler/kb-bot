Introduction


After understanding What is WIMO and how to identify the Order Status, in this article you will learn what to do when the order of the customer is under a Happy Path and the Delivery is On Time. Additionally, we will see how to identify if the order can potentially be delayed and how to act on it.


Order is under promised delivery time


First, let's recap how to identify if the Order Status is going as expected:


-> Access the related Order Management System (Fluent Commerce or Magento) and:


  • Identify the promised delivery date

  • Check order status

  • Check all items (fulfillment) status

  • If already under the status "Shipped", check the delivery tracking link -> if no tracking link, access the delivery partner portal and check the delivery status.


Now, after checking all the information, you confirmed that the order is shipped and in the hands of the delivery company and is estimated to arrive within the promised delivery date. GOOD NEWS! This means the order is on time!


The final step is replying to the customer: Make sure to provide accurate information regarding the delivery of the order and educate the customer where to track the information by themselves in the future.


Order on time: but will be delayed


Preventing issues: it's necessary for us as well to take a look carefully at the promised delivery date and be able to identify if indeed the order will arrive, or if not, and then we can anticipate an issue and act on it to reduce customer dissatisfaction.



How to identify that the order will be delayed?


You checked the information in the Order Management system and the items ordered by the customer are showing still under the promised delivery date. But, you may face 2 situations that need to be looked into in detail because it means that the order will be delayed:


1) The item is not under "Shipped" status and it's less than 2 days from the expected delivery time:


If promised delivery date will expire in the upcoming 2 days and barcode status shows the item has not been shipped: Escalate the case.


2) The delivery date is under the expected time, however, the Barcode/Fulfillment status is showing "Out of Stock":

  • If available for your market, access the country-related Out of stock replenishment plan information that shows the prediction of item in the warehouse, and delivery expectations.
  • If not available for your market the replenishment plan, please Escalate the case following the instructions below.




-> How to escalate?


The escalation means that another team needs to work on the case to understand the problem and take actions to guarantee the order can be delivered, or at least to provide a new delivery time.


Escalation for Central Europe countries: JIRA ticket via CECE Portal



Escalation for remaining markets: JIRA ticket via the Order Management Portal




-> How to manage customer expectations?


Manage customer expectations regarding the escalation process: The teams will give you an answer in the upcoming 24 hours; and for the customer, you inform that an answer will be provided within 48 hours.
