Do you know what is NPS?


The Net Promoter Score is a customer loyalty metric that measures how likely our customers are to recommend Emma to their family and friends. The NPS score serves as a leading indicator of our fanbase and word-of-mouth!

How do we measure NPS?


We calculate NPS by subtracting the % of Detractors from the % of Promoters you will get a number from -100 to 100 and this is your Net Promoter Score.





Recapping:

  • 10-digit scale (0-6 detractors, 7-8 neutrals, 9-10 promoters)
  • Per group
  • Measure from -100 to +100


Our Global Target at Emma: 65%


What's NPS Impact on Emma?


Through NPS, Emma is able to:


  • Identify areas that need to be improved based on customer scores.

  • Understand how good we are doing in comparison to our competitor based on NPS score analysis.


About Asknicely


We use Asknicely as an integrated tool that sends automatically a survey to the customer between 30-50 days after complete purchase.

Besides that, this tool gathers all the data coming from customers and generates a dashboard containing the total number of responses divided into Promoters, Detractors, and Neutrals. In this way, Emma Customer Excellence Managers are able to analyze the data coming from the survey.
