Renewed/Refurbish mattresses

• the delivery window is 15 working days.

The delivery process is: Once the order is placed, our third-party delivery company and our refurb partner, Keen and Able delivery ltd, will contact the customer via telephone (mobile number) or email address within 5-6 working days to schedule a suitable delivery date. There will be a 2-man service, and this is an all day booking service, however, the evening before delivery (between 17:00 – 19:00) the customer will receive confirmation of 4 hour time slot.

When replying to customer asking about delivery date

  • check order on Magento then search with our internal order number (600…or 601…) delivery update on Keen&Able website (login details above).
  • If the order is close to the 15 working days, please send an email to Iwetta asking to schedule the delivery asap.
  • Double check that we have mobile phone number and/or email address of the customer as K&A may contact customer via SMS message or email.
  • If the order was placed at least 8 working days ago and the status on K&A portal is “Awaiting date” please contact Iwetta in K&A and ask her to schedule delivery. Add Emma order number to your reply. Also check if the phone number is mobile phone starting with numbers 07… If not please ask customer to provide mobile phone number. Once they provided mobile number update it on K&A portal.
  • If the status on delivery is “Allocated to route 28/7 awaiting confirmation” you can ask customer if they would like to confirm this on their behalf. (see Keen&Able Portal section) but it’s better if they do it themselves.
