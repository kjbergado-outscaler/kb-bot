The Emma Boxspring Bed = feels like a holiday every day

Our designers had one goal: that you wake up as refreshed and relaxed as if you were on holiday - every day. With the Emma Boxspring Bed, the luxurious comfort of hotels comes right into your bedroom. The padded headboard invites you to sink right into it, and our designed mattress with zoned pocket springs as well as a base of Bonell springs guarantees  outstanding ergonomics. The extra height of the base makes it easy to get into bed and…and well…also easy to stay in it!



Product Specification

The box spring bed consists of two-bed bases (right and left) that would have upholstered fabric on the sides and an anti-slip fabric on top. It is made of a pinewood frame. It has a very light set-up and at the same time, quite stable. Under suspension is a 13cm Bonnell spring with comfort foam upholstery.


  • 2 cm PU25kg Foam

  • 0.4 cm plate of needle felt

  • 13cm 5-turn Bonnell springs

  • 0.4 cm plate of needle felt

  • 9.5 cm solid wood box with passive base slats

  • Height of the core: 25 cm

Assembly and disassembly


  1. The assembly of the Boxspring bed is easy and takes only 10 minutes to do it.

  2. The assembly instructions is available on the FAQs on the website.

  • Align the right and left bed bases in such a way that the sliders are on the same side.

  • The 4 wooden feet should be added to the corners of the bed bases

  • Use the velcro between the two-bed bases to join them together.

  • Slide the headboard into the sliders of the bed bases until the headboard touches the floor.

  • Open the 4-sided zipper of the mattress cover and place the Emma helix mattress/Emma hybrid mattress inside the cover, then zip the upper washable fabric to close the cover of the mattress.

  • Place the topper on top of the mattress – either a soft side or a hard side can be chosen.

Frequently Asked Questions - FAQ


Do I have to assemble the bed myself?

  • We want to offer you the most convenient experience there is. That's why we offer you our free assembly service, which assembles the bed in your bedroom in less than 10 minutes.


How do I set up my box spring bed?

  • Align the right and left bed frames so that the sliders are on the same side.

    The 6 wooden feet should be attached to the visible corners of the bed drawers.

    The 6 non-wooden feet should be attached to the non-visible corners of the bed drawers.

    Use the connector between the two-bed frames to connect them together.

    Slide the headboard into the bed frame sliders until the headboard touches the floor.

    Unzip the 4-sided mattress cover and place the Emma Helix mattress inside the cover, then zip the top washable fabric to close the mattress cover.

    Place the topper on the mattress - you can choose either a soft or a hard side.


I would like to buy my box spring bed without a headboard, is that possible?

  • For the real box spring bed experience, the headboard, the topper and the mattress are a must for optimal lying comfort. That's why we only offer you the Emma box spring bed with a headboard.
