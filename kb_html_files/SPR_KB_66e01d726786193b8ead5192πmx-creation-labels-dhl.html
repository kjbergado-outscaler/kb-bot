Introduction


In this article you will find the process for creating DHL labels for product return, for mattresses and Box Beds, they must be closed (unpacked), open products are not returned by this option.

In the case of pillows, they can be returned open or closed.

Information prior to label creation

Important!: Before creating the label, you must have:

1. The data and address of the customer, from where the product is being returned.

2. Data of the warehouse to return the product.

3. The customer must confirm if he wishes to leave the product at a DHL Distribution Center for shipment or if he prefers that the DHL transport pick up the product at his address.

4. In the event that the client requests collection at home, we must have at least 2 days previous the collection, (date) and time (period of 4 hours where it will be collected).



Label DHL Creation Process


DHL Home Page Link: https://mydhl.express.dhl/mx/es/home.html?login=successful#/createNewShipmentTab


1. Click on Login and enter with your credentials.



2. Select the option create a new shipment and fill in fields A and B and click on next to open new tab



3. Complete with all the information from customer and the warehouse. The info should be correct in otherwise not be possible continue with the creation of labels.




4. Complete the pending spaces as below:



5. Complete information about data of packages product. You can find thi information in this article, is mentioned as Weight / Dimentions product boxes. The infomation should be in CENTIMETERS



6. Choose the account
