Our goal is for Emma One to do justice to all HEIA types. The Soft and Medium versions are also ergonomically designed so that you don't have to miss out on a good night's sleep with any version.


Durable

The excellent shelf life of Emma One was also positively noted by Stiftung Warentest. In test 10/19, the mattress in this category even received a rating of "very good (1.5)" (test 10/2019, tested in 90x200cm, hard). Supporting

Stiftung Warentest also confirms that Emma One is suitable for all body types in all positions (Test 10/2019, tested 90x200cm, hard).
