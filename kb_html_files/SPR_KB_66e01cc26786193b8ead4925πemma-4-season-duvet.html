Summary of the product


The product is composed of 2 parts, that can be used together and make it what we call the Emma 4-season duvet. Or it can be used separately since one side is thinner than the other one. The 3-in-1 duvet that will follow you all year round.



Pictures







USP (Unique Selling Points)


Comfort all Year (2 in 1)

  • Our 4-Season Duvet is a versatile choice for all seasons. With two different layers, it is possible to adapt the warmth level in order to fit different needs, using the layers individually or together.

  • When a customer buys our 4-Season Duvet, they will receive two duvets instead of just one, providing them with flexibility and comfort throughout the year

Layered Adaptability

  • Our duvet's different layers can be conveniently clipped together or apart, allowing for easy customization and adaptability to the customers’ preferences.

  • The clip-on feature ensures that the layers stay securely in place, without slipping, offering the consumer a comfortable and hassle-free sleep experience.


Green Dreams - Elevating the Sleep Experience with Eco-Friendly Materials

  • Benefits of Recycled Hollowfiber:

    • Insulating and hypoallergenic properties.

    • It's easy maintenance allows for simple and quick cleaning in a washing machine and dryer, without losing quality or comfort.

    • The durability of hollowfiber is another important advantage, ensuring that the bedding product lasts for many years.

    • By choosing products with recycled hollowfiber filling, it is possible to enjoy restful and peaceful nights while contributing to a more sustainable world.

Breathability:

  • Fiber and fabric highly breathable, which allows air to circulate freely throughout the duvet, preventing overheating and ensuring that the consumer won't wake up feeling too hot or too cold during the night.

FOR UK ONLY: TOG information


TOG is a measurement of the warmness of the entire duvet and something valid only for UK.



The entire 4 season Emma Duvet has a 10,5 TOG: the thinner duvet is 3,5 tog and the thicker duvet is 7 tog.



Product Communication


  • Benefits of Cotton Fabric:

    • One of the main advantages of cotton is its breathability, which allows air to circulate and wicks moister away, keeping the body cool and dry.

    • It is also soft and comfortable to the touch, making it a great option for sensitive skin.

    • Additionally, cotton is a durable and long-lasting material that can withstand frequent washing and wearing without losing its quality or shape.

    • Furthermore, since it is a natural material, it is also allergy-free.

    • More and more people are appreciating cotton fabric as a material of choice these days.
