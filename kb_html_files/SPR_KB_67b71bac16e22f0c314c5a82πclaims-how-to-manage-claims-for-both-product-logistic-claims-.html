General Information

At Emma, we support customers with two types of claims:

  • Logistic Claims: Complaints related to delivery issues such as delays or product damaged during transport or delivery.

    For more info, check the Logistic Claims folder.

  • Product Claims: Complaints related to the quality or production defects of EMMA products. These are guarantee claims.

    For more info, check the Product Claims folder.

How to Manage the Claim

Correctly logging and storing data regarding claims is critical for Emma. Via correctly documenting the information about claims, we can improve our production and delivery processes to reduce related issues, reduce costs, and maximize the customer experience.

Managing Claims in Sprinklr using the Guided Workflow
