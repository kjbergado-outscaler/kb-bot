Faulty mattress cover replacement

Information needed to start replacement process:

  • Photos with fault visible
  • Customer shipping details including phone number and email address
  • Mattress type and size

In Magento select relevant order number (please see top of page 5), click on Product Claim:

  • Select correct size
  • Select relevant reason, upload picture/s
  • Select Supplement order in bottom of page and press Save

Create “Supplement order” for mattress cover.

  • Remove all existing orders!
  • Select correct cover type and size, add customer’s details to shipping address including customer phone number.
  • Replace John Lewis email address with customer email address otherwise customer will not receive tracking delivery updates.

Advise John Lewis customer service that new cover has been send and it will take approximately one week to deliver it.

Advise that debit and faulty mattress exchange are deal with internally.
