POS Orders

POS Orders are exposition orders for retailers to have the products in store. Most of these are free of charge for the Retailer. big quantity orders (+10 barcodes) to be delivered in the Retailer WH. These might be:

  • Sent to Sales Team: Sales Team will forward the orders to CS to create and follow-up on them. Always create Jira as the process in Create Order | Retail.

  • Sent to CS: In this case create and follow-up on the orders.Always create Jira as the process in Create Order | Retail.

​

How to find POS Orders

Sometimes we need to find POS Orders because of Product Claim or for Collection when Offboardings happen.

​

POS orders can be found in Magento -> Sales > Orders

  • Put “*” and part of the bill name available in Retailers Information | Retail;

  • Select "Group" as Tax Free

  • Select "Type" as Marketing

  • Click in “Search” and “Select Visible”

​

The stock orders must have the Shipping Address of a Store or WH and never the Address of a Final Customer.
