Introduction


In this article, you will find step-by-step how to access and how to navigate the Emma Customer Service Learning Hub, Emma's new training platform. There you will be able to find several E-learning courses to support you to successfully onboard at Emma or refresh your knowledge. Find more information below.


Welcome to the ELH by Luma Moreira


How to access it?



Step 1: Access the following link - Start - Emma Learning Hub


Step 2: Click Emma SSO to log in (This step will require you to already have your Emma email in place).




Step 3: Once you are in the platform, pin the Emma CS Learning Hub tab. In this way, you can guarantee quick access.

All settled!


Issues Accessing
