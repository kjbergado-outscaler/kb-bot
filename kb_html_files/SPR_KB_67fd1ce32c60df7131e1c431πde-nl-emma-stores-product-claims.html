This SOP outlines the step-by-step process for handling product claims. It includes the conditions under which claims are valid, the required documentation, and the necessary actions in the order they should be completed. This procedure applies to all product claims received by the company through direct customer contact or store reports.

We treat the concern as a Product Claim when the customer has a product that is already beyond its trial period.

​

Receive & Identify Product Claims

Claims can be received through:

  • Customer directly via Sprinklr (email inquiry).

  • Store via the communication channel (store manager reports on behalf of the customer).

Gather Information & Troubleshoot

  • Ask the customer the following:

    • When did they start using the mattress?

    • When was the package opened? (If the mattress remained packed for more than 4 weeks, inflation could be affected.)

  • Provide troubleshooting steps:

    • Advise the customer to wait up to 14 days for full inflation.

    • Advise the customer to let the mattress “breathe” for 24 hours and use loose sheets in the first 4 weeks.

    • If the issue is related to smell: advise the customer that this slight smell will wear off after the first few hours. Make sure the mattress is aired out in a well ventilated room.

  • Defect Acceptance Range: Claims can only be accepted if the mattress has been opened in over 72 hours.

Request Supporting Evidence

  • If the issue persists after troubleshooting:

    • Request photos of the mattress showing the defect.

    • Request a photo of the product label with the barcode.

You can find the claim pictures requirements and examples here and how to validate the claim here.

The pictures should be taken without the mattress cover.

Evaluate the Claim

  • Review the submitted photos.

  • If the defect is confirmed, proceed with a replacement order.

  • If more information is needed, follow up with the customer.

Should the customer be unable to provide a mattress label since it is already tarnished or accidentally removed, the agent should seek approval from a supervisor to proceed.

Confirm Customer’s Preference

  • Ask the customer if they prefer:

    • Receiving the replacement before returning the faulty product.

    • Returning the faulty product before receiving a replacement.

Create Supplement Order in Magento

  • Generate a supplement order in Magento with the same delivery address.

    • Refer to this article for instructions on how to create supplement orders.

  • Inform the customer of the expected delivery timeframe.

Arrange Product Collection

  • Request the collection.

  • Inform the customer they'll be contacted to schedule the pickup.

No need for daily monitoring; customers will be contacted directly by the carrier.

Update Magento & Close Case

  • Update the order notes in Magento with:

    • Details of the replacement.

    • Collection confirmation (if applicable).

  • Once completed, close the case in Sprinklr.
