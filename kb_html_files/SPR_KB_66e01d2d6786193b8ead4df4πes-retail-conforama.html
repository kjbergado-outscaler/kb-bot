Conforama Orders

Conforama orders enter via EDI, so no orders should be created manually. The orders can take up to 24h to pass from Conforama's System to Emma's system. If after this time the order is not available in Magento, the issue should be escalated to Emma.

​

If you check that the order is in "Packing" for a long time, then the SKU is probably wrong, and we'll not be able to deliver the item. You can check the correct SKUs in ES B2B - SKUs & Prices.xlsx.

​

Orders in "Packing" with wrong SKUs can also have other particularities like a random delivery date and "Out of Stock Dummy Warehouse" assigned:

Delivery Times:

Acessories

11 Business days

Mattresses

11 business days

Canapés

15 business days


Conforama have specific delivery times agreed in the contract. This way, we need to deliver the orders in a maximum of 11 business days for Accessories, 11business days for Mattresses and 15 days for Canapés. These days start to count when the order enters in our OMS.

​

When sharing the delivery times with Conforama, as well as for other retailers, we should not use the Delivery Times in Magento as these ones might be wrong.

​

Instead:

  • If the order is stuck in "Packing", escalate to Emma and inform the retailer that we're checking;

  • If the order is "Submitted to WH" or after, we should share the tracking link with the retailer. If it's still not available, please inform that the order is ready to be shipped from the WH and we will share the tracking link once it's available. Add a snooze of one day and check the day after, until it's available.

  • If the order is "Waiting for Stock", please go to Retail Lead Times.xlsx and check if the order can be delivered in the agreed times. If yes, inform that we expect to deliver the order in the agreed time and that we'll share the tracking link once it's available. If not, we should inform that we're Out of Stock for this item and share the estimated delivery time.

to Emma.
