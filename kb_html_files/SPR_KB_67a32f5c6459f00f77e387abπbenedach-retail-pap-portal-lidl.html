What is it for?

The PAP Portal is a tool used for verifying whether orders have been correctly cancelled or returned. Is is a research only tool, so we are not allowed to perform any actions in this platform.

When you log into the PAP Portal, you will be presented with the main page. Here, you can search for and check the status of an order.

​

How to Check Your Order Status

  1. Copy your Lidl Order Number (490XXXXXXX);

  2. In the PAP Portal, go to Bestellungen (Orders) → Erledigte Bestellungen (Completed Orders);

  3. Click on Bestellnr. (Order Number);

  4. Paste your order number into the search bar and press Enter;

  5. Select the order;

  6. Check the order status:

  • Cancellation: If the order was cancelled, the field Stornierte Menge (Cancelled Quantity) will be marked as 1.

  • Return: If a return request was made, the field Returnierte Menge (Returned Quantity) will be marked as 1.

The goal is to have the PAP Portal automatically linked with Freshdesk so that cancellations and returns are automatically updated in the portal. However, due to occasional inconsistencies, our internal team must be notified about any cancellations and return requests, so they can check if the portal is updated.

You may read more about this process on the Cancellations and Returns articles.
