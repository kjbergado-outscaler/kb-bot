New supply chain set up in NL

  • We will have deliveries every 10 days starting from now on. 

    • A truck will leave our WH every 10 days, on a specific and previously agreed date, and take care of our pending deliveries until that date (all deliveries should happen between 0-3 days after the truck leaves the WH).

    • You can find the calendar for the deliveries below.

  • All status in Magento, except Waiting for stock/reject or deleted, mean that the order will be delivered within that 10-13 day window (always check the calendar shared above).

  • Please be careful with supplements. If the order is within the 10 days before the previous pickup, it will be delivered to the customer and there's no need to create a supplement order.

This should help you to better understand the lead times and better manage customer expectations.

If you have any questions about the deliveries, reach out to Alena Lauter.
