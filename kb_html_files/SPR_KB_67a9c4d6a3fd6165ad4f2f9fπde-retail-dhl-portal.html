Booking Pick-up

The pick-up must be booked with DHL when the mattress is unpacked.

Log in to the DHL Portal

  • Use your credentials to access the DHL Portal.

  • Select Aufträge from the menu.

  • Click on Auftragerfassung to begin the order entry process.

​

​

Enter Order Information

  • Internal Order Number: Enter the order number in the following format:
    W-0001-XXXXX-E-CS.

​

​

Select Pick-Up Type

  • In the dropdown, choose the appropriate pick-up option:

    • One-Man Handling: For items up to 120x200 cm.

    • Two-Man Handling: For items larger than 120x200 cm.

  • After selecting the relevant option, click Continue.

​

​

Customer Information

  • Fill out the customer’s details in the following fields:

    • Name

    • Address

    • Telephone Number

    • Email

Important: DHL can't organize the pick-up without the customer's telephone number, so please make sure this information is correct.  

​

​

  • In the Empfänger Kontaktadresse field, input the Emma Warehouse number: 245.

  • Once all information is filled out, click Weiter (Continue).

​

​

Fill Out Product Details

  • Complete the following fields:

    • Menge (Quantity): Enter the quantity of items being picked up.

    • Produkt-Nr. / Qualitat (Product Number / Quality): Enter Emma.

    • Produktbez (Product Reference): This information can be found in FluentCommerce, under the "Order Items" section. Look for the field labeled "Product/Bundle Name."

    • Bruttogewicht (Gross Weight): Enter the total weight of the items.

    • Volumen (Volume): Enter the total volume of the items.

    • Anz. Packstücke (Number of Packages): This should match the product quantity.

    • Warengrouppe (Product Group): Select Matratze / Lattenrost [MATLAT] from the dropdown menu.

Check Pick-up status

  • Open the Aufträge menu.

  • Select Auftragsübersicht from the options.

​

​

  • Paste the tracking number into the Search field.

  • Press Enter to search.

  • Click on the corresponding Order.

​

​

Now, you can view the pick-up status.
