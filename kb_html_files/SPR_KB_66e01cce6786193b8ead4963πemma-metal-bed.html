Product Communication


NamingEmma Metal Bed
Objective

The Metal bed is supposed to capture the lower to mid-range market segment – prices will be affordable like our flagship mattresses (e.g. in DE, the 160 cm x 200 cm size without Headboard would cost 349€; FR with headboard 399€). It should capture a different type of customer, to further broaden our reach into the market.

ChannelsPrimary sales channels will be our Emma website and possibly Marketplaces.
Target positioningThe Metal bed is designed for a specific customer (18-35 years, both genders, lower to middle income) as well as to speak to the typical Emma customer.
MarketsThe bed is designed to be tested globally, starting in Nov 2021 with our large markets DE, UK, FR, NL for the start.
ExecutionIt will be marketed both as a standalone product (e.g., via Social media ads) and as a cross-sell product (e.g., pop-ups when customers buy a mattress).
Configuration

The product comes as it is, customer can choose only the size. It will be offered in a bundle with the mattress.

We have two versions of the Metal Bed. One with Headboard “Metal Bed” for UK and FR. And the “Metal Base” the base without headboard for DE and NL.

Bed quality - tests


  • 100% Vegan - the Emma Metal Bed is 100 % vegan and does not contain any animal-based materials, nor is tested on animals.


  • The Emma Metal Bed has been tested extensively during development, as well as in its final production stage for durability and quality.
    • The wooden slats have been tested for bending fracture load, applying up to 500 Newtons force.
    • The wooden slats have been evaluated for formaldehyde emission for Composite Wood Products, according to international regulations. We have passed.


  • Cleaning Tips: Use lightly damp cloth to clean the bed. Use just water. Use of soap and chemicals is not recommended.



Assembly and disassembly


The assembly of the Metal bed is easy and can be done by the customer. It takes about 35 minutes to assemble the complete bed.


In order to assemble the Metal Bed, the customer would need to have received:

  • Bed base part 1 box and Bed base part 2 box



The bed base box 1 contains an assembly booklet and the foil cutter to unpack all the bed elements.


The booklet and manual for assembly is available as part of Bed base part 1 box. The L-shaped wrench is available as part of Bed base box2.


The booklet and manual for assembly will also be available on the website, along with an assembly instruction video.


The Metal bed can be built and dismantled up to 5 times without damaging the structure. The bed can be dismantled in less than 15 minutes.
