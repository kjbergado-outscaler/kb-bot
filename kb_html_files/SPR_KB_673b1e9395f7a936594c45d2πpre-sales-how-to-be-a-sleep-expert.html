Pre-sales Process (Product Information category)

Step 0 - Know Emma products and service offering; sleep science

Use Emma products - how to offer? folder to deepen your knowledge, make sure to be updated with the FAQs section (Help Center) on the website.

Step 1- Welcome the customer: build rapport

​

Use Emma's tone of voice with a small tweak: Sleep Expert Tone of Voice

Step 2 - Analyse the customer & inquiry: what is your concern, how can I help?

​

You have to ask:

What is your preferred sleeping position (side, back, stomach, or combination)?

Do you often feel too warm when you sleep?

Optional:

What features are most important to you in a mattress?

When sleeping, do you prefer a soft or firm mattress?

In terms of comfort, do you give more importance to support and pressure relief, or the slight feeling of sinking?
