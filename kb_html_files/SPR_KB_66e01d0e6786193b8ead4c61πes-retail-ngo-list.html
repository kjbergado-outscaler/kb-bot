NGO pick up of returns

At Emma we work with NGOs as the first option for the return of our open mattresses or mattress + other products. Why?

​

It is a way to reduce the expenses of the returns + to give a second life to products that otherwise would be destroyed.

How do we contact them?


Every association must have a case created on Sprinklr to contact them. We will need to disclose a list of information with them.

Please, only use the e-mail address to contact charity. Phone should be used to unblock cases, not to request collection.

​

What to expect from them?

Charity should answer the collection request within 3 days. Then, they will have 6 additional days to pick up the products. If charity does not meet expectation, please request the collection to Mobel.
