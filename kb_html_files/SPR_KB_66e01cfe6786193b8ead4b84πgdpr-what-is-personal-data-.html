What is Personal Data?


When customers make a purchase at Emma Websites or contact our customer service regarding their orders, they are required to share some personal information with Emma, for example, name, address, email, etc. This information is called Personal Data.


Although personal data can be divided into 2 types:


  • Personal Data: Name, identification number, telephone number, order related information, for example.

  • Sensitive Personal Data:

    • personal data revealing racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs;
    • trade-union membership;
    • genetic data, biometric data processed solely to identify a human being;
    • health-related data;
    • data concerning a personâ€™s sex life or sexual orientation.



How GDPR impacts our work as CS in Emma?


As an agent, you are responsible for all the information shared by the customer while handling an interaction, in other words, while handling a ticket (phone call, email, or even chat).


A agent should only deal with the Personal Data of the customer which are relevant and requested in our procedures.



A agent should NEVER make comments or leave notes with Sensitive Personal Data from our customers.
