Certain cases require our Customer Service team to communicate with different teams, such as Order Management, Payment providers, Carriers, etc.


Whenever this happens, a case will be escalated to the back office team.


Escalations are done via Jira Portal.

This is the article to explain to you how to use the Jira portal to reply to customer service team requests.

Be aware: When handling Jira tasks, "customer" refers to the reporter (the agent who escalated the ticket).





Understanding note/reply functionalities:


Add an internal note: Just add notes and do not notify the customer (Jira reporter). It can be used to loop other users/teams.

Escalating to another team


If you need to assign the ticket to another team (such as the Refunds team, Order management, etc), you can do it by the screenshot and the steps below:


  1. Click on the item to change the queue
  2. Select the required queue (case is automatically transferred).



When you need to move an escalation and escalate the ticket to an Emma internal team.


Carrier issues / delays (Product has been shipped) =move to "OM & OPS: Last mile"


Product has not been shipped yet = move to "OM & OPS:OPS escalations"


If there is no tracking update for 5 working days a logistic claim and supplement can be created.

If there is no tracking a logistic claim and supplement can be created and moved to "OM & OPS:OPS escalations" in Jira where the backoffice agent informs that the supplement was done already.



OBS. If there is no tracking link and the barcode is customer shipped. You need to escalate this to "OM & OPS: OPS escalations"


If the warehouse is PS, make sure to check this article first, to search for the tracking link in the excel sheet provided.

Important notes:


  • Leave an internal note before escalating the task to another team to clarify needs.

  • After assigning the task to the related queue, if a page pops up to provide details of the task, do not add any information.

  • Ensure you put the Task status as Waiting for 3rd party so agents know it has been escalated and the case will be handled as soon as possible.
