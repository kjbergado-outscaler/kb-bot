Slatted Frame


In addition to the mattress, the slatted frame is also decisive for the quality of lying. The slatted frame supports the mattress and makes a significant contribution to pressure relief. It has an influence on the durability of the sleep system, since an old or worn-out slatted frame, for example, cannot fully support a new mattress.


Did you know that the slatted frame makes up approx. 30-40% of the lying comfort?


Even with very high mattresses, you can feel the influence of the slat base. For example, if you place a folded folding rule lengthwise between the mattress and the slatted frame, you can immediately notice a change in the way you lie down.






Pillow

The pillow is responsible for holding and supporting the cervical spine. The purpose of the pillow is to fill the space between the head and the mattress at the ideal height. For this reason, neck support pillows are often the right solution.


If the customer prefers a more cuddly pillow, solutions made of synthetic or even natural materials are also possible.

In most markets, we offer customers solutions with synthetic fillings. In very few markets products made of feathers and down are also available.


A special role in the pillow takes stomach sleepers. Very flat pillows are recommended for stomach sleepers. A common solution from the practice is e.g. down bedding for infants from the stroller. Due to their low filling weight, they are ideally suited as stomach sleeper pillows.

Bed Linen & Fitted Sheets


Bed linen plays a major role in moisture removal, as well as heat retention. Decisive for the quality and price here is also material, weave, finishes, and printing methods.

The main types of bed linen are:



  • Linon/ Renforce: A simple plain weave fabric, usually made of cotton which has neutral climate properties. The cotton provides good moisture absorption

  • Satin: A high-quality bed linen made of cotton woven in a satin weave. Very smooth fabric with good and cuddly properties. Can be offered at a very high price due to refinement and higher quality materials, as well as elaborate prints.

  • Flannel: A twill-bonded product that gets a very soft surface by roughening. Very warm bedding. In simple qualities, however, the bedding tends to pill.


Since the bed linen should absorb and retain moisture of the sleeper, the qualities are usually made of cotton.

The color design, as well as the motif bed linen significantly shape the look of the bedroom.


In addition to the bedding, the fitted sheet also has a direct point of contact with the skin. Fitted sheets are usually jersey fabric, which is canvas bound. The decisive factor for the quality and durability of the sheet is the cotton used. The finer the cotton, the tighter the weave and the higher the quality. With increasing quality, fitted sheets also become more and more opaque.
