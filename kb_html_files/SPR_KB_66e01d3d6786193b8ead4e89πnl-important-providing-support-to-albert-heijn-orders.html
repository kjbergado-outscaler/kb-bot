Unless you are part of Marketplaces team, you are not supposed to handle customers requests from Albert Heijn.

[If you are part of Marketplaces team, please check this article instead.]


What to do if you receive a ticket/call related to an order posted with Albert Heijn?


Albert Heijn, (AH) is the largest supermarket chain in the Netherlands with a market share of 34.8% in 2020.

They sell some of our products through a flash sales page and Emma provides customer support for those products.


We have a specific team of agents handling marketplaces orders and they are also providing support for AH's and should automatically receive those tickets as customers should use a specific email for such requests (AH@emma-sleep.com).


However, sometimes Customers might call or use a different channel to contact us, such as a different email address, contact form, Facebook, Instagram or other.


In such cases, tickets will go to the regular queue and won't be assigned to marketplaces.


If such, you need to :

  1. set the Group field to "Marketplaces"
  2. Fill in the Marketplace Order Number with the information provided
  3. set the field Marketplace Name to "Albert Heijn"
