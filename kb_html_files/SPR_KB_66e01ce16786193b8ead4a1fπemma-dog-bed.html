Summary of the Product


In this article, you will learn more about the Emma Dog Bed that is bringing the same "human" restful sleep comfort for our customer's pets. The Dog Bed is for those who value the comfort and well-being of their dogs, having them as true members of their families.

USPs (Unic Selling Points)

  • Comfort -> Created to provide the Emma comfort of sleep to pets (illustrated by the Emma mattress look and by the product coming roll-packed), using memory foam to potentiate comfort and provide orthopedic capabilities. Memory foam pad and bolsters provide optimal support and comfort.

  • Minimalist design -> The focus was to engineer the best sleeping comfort and streamline the design, having no edges to facilitate transportation if needed and provide flexibility (standalone bed, mattress for a dog crate or for transportation in a car).

  • Lying surface -> That has a plush fabric to potentiate a warm feeling. Since the product has no sides, the plush surface should be leveraged as a warmth and comfy night feeling.

  • 2 different sizes to accommodate all dogs -> A product that is suitable for dogs of almost all sizes. Test will be run with 2 relatively large sizes (L and XL â€“ see below for measurements).

  • Removable and washable covers: Washable at max. of 30 degrees.

  • Non-slip bottom: The product was designed to not be slipping and guarantees stability for your dog's sleep.

FAQs


Q. Can I cut the mattress, the fam part to fit in my current dog bed?

Emma doesn't recommend cutting the product, as it was designed and developed as an entire piece to give comfort to dogs. Additionally, the warranty expires when the goods are cut.


Q. Can I also buy it for my cat?

Emma doesn't recommend it for cats. The product is optimized to resist damage done by dogs, but it cannot be completely ruled out depending on the condition of the claws of cats.


Q. The cover was damaged, can I just have a new cover?

At the moment is not possible to replace only the cover. If the product is still on the night trial, it's possible to make a product replacement for the customer.
