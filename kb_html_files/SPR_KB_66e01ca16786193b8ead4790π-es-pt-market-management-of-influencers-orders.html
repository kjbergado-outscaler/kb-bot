What is Influencer Management Orders?

The management of Order issue from our influencers, handled by our agency via email. We need to manage these issues and provide the appropriate solutions.

How to Manage It?

  1. We will receive an email from the following address: incidencias@nomahub.com.

  2. The following details must be provided by the agency:

    1. Order Number

    2. Influencer Name

    3. Influencer email

  3. Review the request and manage it as a regular case directly with the influencer to whom the order refers to. Reply to the agency stating the customer was reached for the relevant assistance.

  4. For logistics and products, the supplement should be created without waiting for the product to be returned first.

  5. We do not make exchanges of products and the agency must reach the Emma internal POC for a new order to be created if an exchange is requested.
