We have spare parts available for beds

If a customer reports a problem with a bed, always check if we can send a spare part instead of the full item or box. See the instructions on the page 'Product Claims: Spare Parts'.

In Belgium, The Netherlands, Germany, Austria, Switzerland and France, se have spare parts for:

  • Box Bed v5

  • Storage bed

  • Slatted frame
