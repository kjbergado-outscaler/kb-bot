Summary of the product


In this article, you find all the details related to the Emma Temp. Topper. The product has a system that can actively cool down or heat up the mattress in all seasons, so no more cold and hot nights with uninterrupted sleep. It is a 2-sided topper: cooling (cooling fabric) and warmer (microfiber fabric) side to reinforce the cooling and heating effect.

-> Target Audience: People or couples who struggle to have the best sleeping temperature. (No more sweating and freezing nights or fighting to have the right sleeping temperature all year long)

USPs (Unic Selling Points)


  1. Sleep better, whatever the weather.

    Set your bed to your ideal temperature for superior sleep every night.


  2. Stay cool on hot nights; warm up on cool nights.

    The Emma Temp Topper is a cozy mattress topper with a built-in water circulation system of tiny tubes that can cool or transfer heat. The tubes are sturdy and discreetly woven into the liner, which is made of 100% soft, breathable cotton. So the only thing you feel is blissful comfort.

Better still, do this 30 minutes before bedtime, so when you climb into bed, you're ready for a big night of catching quality sleep.

    At 70x190cm, the Emma Temp Pad is ideal for one person. So, if you share a bed and your partner has one too, there's no need to compromise: both of you can sleep at your perfect temperature.

  6. Ready, set, sleep.

    You decide how long you need your temp Pad to warm you up or cool you down, for 1 to 12 hours. This means your bed is exactly how you like it for as long as you like.

  7. Snooze control.

    With the hand remote control, adjusting the temperature is one click away. No need to install an app, and no blue light to disturb your sleep.

Product performance


  • Cooling feature: the temperature dropped from ~3°C to 3.5°C within the first 30 minutes with ambient temperature at 30°C. (For example: if the environment temperature is 18C they will gradually feel 15C on the topper. How do we operate the product?


Step 1: Assemble the temp pad, put the pad on the mattress, and cover the mattress with a duvet/blanket. Locate the control unit at the same height as the mattress.


Step 2: Input room-temperature water into the machine


Step 3: Choose the function (Cooling or heating) on the machine panel


Step 4: Set up the water temperature that you prefer


Step 5: Start your restful night

Product use recommendations


  • Customers have to use Filtered Water inside the box. Tap water can contain sediment that can damage the interior of the box.

  • It's highly recommended to start using the Temp Topper around 30-60 min before going to bed, like this it will be at the ideal temperature for the sleeping moment. Also, Emma's team suggests setting the timer to shut down the machine after a couple of hours.

  • It's recommended to use the topper between the mattress (mattress protector included) and the bed linen. Having the bed linen on top can protect the topper to get dirty easily and also avoid the topper to slip.

  • The topper can be folded, but we don't recommend to be rolled it due to the water tubes inside.

  • For a couple with different temperature preferences, the recommendation is to have 2 separate toppers (single size), in order to be possible to adjust each one of the products according to the customer's needs.

FAQs


Q. What exactly comes with the Temp Topper?


The Temp Topper is a temperature-regulated mattress topper that includes a hydropower water system, and is managed by a connected control unit containing a reservoir of water. If you are a first-time user, you will be provided with a double-sided, water-powered cool mesh mattress topper, a control unit, and a remote control to conveniently regulate the temperature of the topper.


Q. What is a hydropower water system topper?


A hydropower water system topper is a type of mattress topper that utilizes a water-based system to either cool or heat up the bed. It is typically connected to a hydropower unit, which circulates the water to maintain the desired temperature.


Q. What are the benefits of using a hydropower water system topper?


The main benefit of using a hydropower water system topper is that it allows you to regulate the temperature of your bed according to your preferences. This can help you get a better night’s sleep and improve your overall comfort level. Additionally, it can be beneficial for individuals who experience hot flashes, night sweats, or other temperature-related sleep disturbances.


Q. What is the difference between a normal electronic blanket and our temp topper?


The Temp Topper surpasses electronic blankets in terms of safety and versatility, as it utilizes water instead of electronic components. Unlike electronic blankets which only offer heating capabilities, our Temp Topper provides both cooling and heating functionalities, making it an ideal and practical solution for year-round comfort.


Q. What is the difference between a normal topper and our temp topper?


A regular topper is designed solely for providing comfort. However, our Temp Topper goes beyond this by not only enhancing comfort but also offering a unique feature to cool down or heat up the user, thus creating the optimal sleeping temperature.


Q. Is the temp topper loud at night?


The noise level of the temp topper is 41.7dB when you put the machine beside your feet. To better understand, it is just like you are in a quiet library (40dB) or your refrigerator (50dB) is working. If you are sensitive to the noise while sleeping, set up the temp topper 30 minutes before you sleep and set up a timer! There’s always a solution!


Note: Normal breathing=10dB, Quiet library sounds=40dB, Refrigerator=50dB, Electric toothbrush=60dB, Washing machine=70dB.


Q. How much will I save on my energy cost?


Temp topper is definitely a cost-efficient choice for energy cost saving. The power consumption is 0.164kWh, and with this low power consumption, it can keep you warm on the cool freezing nights and also cool you down during the hot sweaty nights!


Markets should compare the AC and heating consumption for energy cost calculation.


Q. What is the temperature range that we can set?


The panel or remote control allows you to adjust the water temperature between 12°C and 50°C. When cooling, the temperature decreases from around 3°C to 3.5°C within the first half hour when the surrounding temperature is 30°C. It is also efficient at heating and provides immediate warmth. Once you set your desired sleeping temperature, the temp top enhances your sleeping experience.


Q. Can I sleep cool, and can my partner sleep warm?


Absolutely! If you and your partner have different temperature preferences, you can opt for two single Temp Topper units. This will allow each of you to set your desired temperature and sleep comfortably at your own individual levels of warmth or coolness.


(DE - If you have a double-sized bed, unfortunately, it won't be possible for one side of the Temp Topper to be cooler while the other side is warmer. The entire Temp Topper will remain at the same temperature.)


Q. Will the topper size fits my bed?


Everyone wins at 70x190cm, the Emma Temp Topper is ideal for one person, allowing each user to individually customize their sleeping climate. So, if you share a bed and your partner has one too, there's no need to compromise: both of you can sleep at your perfect temperature.


Q. How often do I need to refill the water?


You only need to refill the Temp Topper's water tank once a year, and it's important to use filtered water for the best results.


Q. How do I clean the topper and the control?


To clean the topper, hand wash with laundry detergent at a maximum of 30 degrees Celsius. Avoid bleaching, tumble drying, or ironing as they may cause damage or color loss. Use Perchloroethylene for cleaning. The control can be wiped clean with a damp cloth.


Q. Can the topper be used with a mattress protector?


Yes, the topper can be used with a mattress protector. However, it is important to ensure that the protector is breathable and does not interfere with the circulation of water through the topper.


Q. Is the topper safe for individuals with allergies or sensitivities?


Yes, the topper is safe for individuals with allergies or sensitivities. This is because the topper is made with a hypoallergenic top fabric consisting of 40% Polyethylene and 60% polyester.


Q. Can the topper be used with pets?


It is generally safe to use the topper with pets. However, it is important to ensure that the hydropower unit and hose are kept out of reach of pets to avoid accidents. Furthermore, it is recommended to use a cover to protect the topper from scratches or other damage.
