Introduction

This article is related to the ES market ONLY and provides information about comfort layers that customers can offer as Return Deflection.

​

When to apply this process?

We can offer Comfort layers to customers who are unhappy with their mattresses, in order to decrease returns due to softness and unstable feelings. For example, if a customer says their mattress is too soft, you can offer a firm comfort layer.

At this moment, we have some mattresses as main drivers for returns in the ES D2C market, and for those the comfort layer can be offered.

Firm Comfort layer SKU: ECLCF_AAA 

Note: Remember: Offering a comfort layer costs the company money, so use this option wisely to avoid returns.

Offering Firm Comfort Layers

Agents should only offer the comfort layer firm as a return deflection tool in the case of the claims listed above and related to the following models and sizes:

- Emma Hybrid Premium: EMAHE_AAC and EMAHE_ATC (90x190, 135x190, 150x190 and 150x200);

- Emma Hybrid Termoluxe: EMAHC_AAC (90x190, 135x190, 150x190 and 150x200);

- Emma Hybrid Elite: EMAGG_AAC (90x190, 135x190, 150x190 and 150x200).

Steps to Use Comfort Layers

​

- Unbox product (rolled in boxes similar to mattresses).

- Unzip the mattress cover and place the comfort layer on top of the foam layers.

- Zip back the mattress cover.

Notes on Comfort Layers

​

- Comfort layers may come in our previous (blue) boxes, but the product and the quality remain the same.

- Comfort layers do not have an expiration date.
