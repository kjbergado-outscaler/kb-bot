Product Specification

Overview

The Wooden Bed, also called ‘Emma Original Wooden Bed is composed of a chipboard core and natural oak veneer. There are

  • 4 short legs (if headboard, then 4 + 2 headboard legs)

  • 2 sideboards, 1 foot- and backboard (footboard and backboard are not the same!!)

  • Sturdy flat slats made of birch plywood (9mm) (4 belt-connected sets)

  • Metal telescope middle beam with height regulation middle feet.

  • Headboard panel (for customers who choose this option.) – 2 headboard legs will follow this piece.

Customers that purchase a bed with a headboard will receive 4 legs small legs but only use 2 in the assembly. The headboard comes with two long legs. The remaining legs can be used in case he/she wants to assemble them without the headboard.
