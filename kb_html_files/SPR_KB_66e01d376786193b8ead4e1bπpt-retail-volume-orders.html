Volume Orders

Volume Orders are big quantity orders (+10 barcodes) to be delivered in the Retailer WH. These might be:

  • Sent to CS: Backoffice is the responsible for creating this type of orders. Always escalate to them via Jira, following the process in PT Retail - Create Order .

  • Placed via Retailer Portal: Some retailers have specific logins to create volume orders, so they are automatically assigned to the respective WH & Carrier, as well as benefit from lower prices.

How to find Volume Orders

Sometimes we need to find Volume Orders to assign a return. In these cases, you can follow the next steps to find one and, in case you don't find it, please escalate to OPS Team.

Stock orders can be found in Magento -> Sales > Orders

  • Put “*” and part of the bill name available in PT Retail - Retailers Information (Billing Name);

  • Select minimum quantity as “6”;

  • Click in “Search”

After this, you can either:

  1. Open orders one by one until you find an order with the desired SKU;

  2. Click on “Select Visible” -> “Actions” -> “Export Orders” -> Submit

The stock orders must have the Shipping Address of a WH and never the Address of a Final Customer.

Follow-up File

All issues requiring actions or changes in Pallet Orders (identified as such in Fluent) should be escalated directly to Maigerlis.

This document has been created to help with the communication flow and to keep track of pallet orders: Outbound Pallets.xlsx

In this document, you can:

  1. Place questions/changes related to pallet orders.

  1. See the communications to be sent to retailers related to pallet orders (e.g., orders with transmission issues).

  1. See the tracking links for pallet orders to better inform retailers about their status.

Please note the difference between these two types of orders:

  • Parcel Orders: These are integrated with the warehouse and follow the normal process.

  • Pallet Orders: These are not integrated with the warehouse, so the status in the system may not be accurate, and tracking links are not available. The source of truth is the document shared in this message.

 

Missing Items

In case of missing items, check with the warehouse and escalate to Maigerlis if the response is not accurate. If the retailer requests a credit note for items not delivered, agents can call to inform them that we can arrange the shipment of the missing items if they still want them.
