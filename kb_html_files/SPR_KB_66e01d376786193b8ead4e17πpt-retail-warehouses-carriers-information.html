Delivery Characteristics

GLS

​

Appointment

GLS send e-mails with updates on the delivery:

1. When the tracking is available. This e-mail also shows the estimated delivery date.


2. When the item is out for delivery.


SMS

GLS sends SMS with updates on the delivery in the following moments:

  1. When the shipment is created


​

  1. When the item arrives to the delivery agency

​

  1. In case of absence incidence when trying to deliver

​

  1. When the item is out for distribution (only sent once)

​

  1. When a collection is requested

​

In the end of each SMS, GLS share the tracking link where customers can easily track and manage the delivery.

Reschedule

Once the client accesses the tracking link, he can suggest a new hour and time or add additional delivery information.

Contact in Delivery Day

The drivers might call on same day of delivery to confirm the delivery, although it's not mandatory. An SMS is sent when the item is out for delivery.

​

Storage Time

Free storage is for 15 days (calendar days) from arrival at the destination warehouse, after the 15th day it is returned to the Consignee (DBS Alcala).

​

Missing Information

If there’s missing information (eg: door / floor number) the carrier should contact the client via phone. This information should also be shared with Emma.

​

XPO

​

Appointment

The appointment is done via WhatsApp message. It's sent when the first cross-dock is done. (Ie; Pick up Madrid, destination Lisbon, Cross-dock Valladolid. When leaving Valladolid, WhatsApp message is sent).

Reschedule

The WhatsApp link leads to a platform that confirms the service and date. The client can modify the proposed date easily:


Contact in Delivery Day

The drivers might call on same day of delivery to confirm the delivery, although it’s not mandatory.

Missing Information

If there’s missing information (eg: door / floor number) the carrier should contact the client via phone. This information should also be shared with Emma.

Appointment

Phone Call & Email

The appointment is made once the order has arrived at the distribution center at destination, always a phone call and then the carrier will send an email with the confirmation of the date.

Delivery time slot

The carrier informs the customer of a time slot in which the delivery will be made.

Contact in Delivery Day

The carrier contacts you approximately 1 hour before delivery although it’s not mandatory.

Reschedule

If after being quoted the customer cannot receive the order on the agreed day, he has to contact Emma to notify and Emma has to notify Mobel so that they can notify the delivery center and they can take the order out of the route. Once the order is taken out of the route, Mobel contact the customer to re-quote.

IMPORTANT: If the customer or Emma does not notify Mobel of the cancellation of the appointment and they come to the address it is considered a no-show and Mobel would need authorization for a 2nd delivery.

Storage Time

Storage is not contemplated if the holding period is less than 15 days from the time the goods arrive at the destination branch or warehouse.

Missing Information

If there’s missing information (eg: door / floor number) the carrier should contact the client via phone. This information should also be shared with Emma.

GLS

Whenever we make requests to GLS, a child ticket must be created for each order. This also applies to cases where two orders are from the same customer. Before calling GLS, we should send an email.

 

JIRA - Warehouse

Offline WH Communication 2 - Jira Service Management (atlassian.net)

Estimated response time: 48 hours.

If the fulfillment or barcode is in packing, waiting for stock, or prepared for export, it is not in the warehouse, and we should not create a JIRA ticket because they will not be able to help. When the status is marked as “Order submitted to warehouse,” means it's already with the warehouse.
