Schultz Schlafkultur is our supplier / manufacturer for the Click & Sleep fiberglass slatted frames.

Please pay attention to this when ordering:

Keep first and last name as short as possible, maximum 30 characters.


  • Click & Sleep:

  • NV = non-adjustable = X1

  • KF = head & foot adjustable = X2

  • EL = electric = X3


  • Click & Sleep:
    No custom manufacturing possible possible, as requested in the example below. The customer can order additional slats and change them themselves.



The slats cost €2.90 ec/net per piece (standard size), plus packaging costs + postage:

  • Up to 3 slats 4,90€ (for overlength 120/140 width bulky goods + 20€)

  • From 3 to 10 slats 9,90€ (for overlength 120/140 width bulky goods + 20€)

  • From 10 -15 slats 14,90€ (for overlength 120/140 width bulky goods + 20€)
