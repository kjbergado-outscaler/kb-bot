Instructions

  • Login to MyDynalogicShipments

  • Select New Order

  • Enter a unique reference in the field for [ordernumber], it does not matter what it is named, as long as it has not been used by another order before.

  • Select Pickup in the orderkind.

This will automatically select the orderkind "2XL Service Only"

Do not worry, this is still just a service only shipment, nothing will be picked up. Service only orders fall under the same ‘category’ as Pickups as our systems are fundamentally based on packages, where services were added in later years and had to be made to fit the existing systems’ parameters.

  • Select any additional services that are required to be executed at the customer’s location.

    • For assembly services, choose BEDINS

    • For just moving the boxes in the house to the right location (without assembly services) choose KAMERK

    • If it concerns a Motion Sense Matrass, choose the EMMAMS service.

    Note: You can choose multiple additional services at the same time if needed.

  • Enter the contact information in the corresponding boxes.

  • Select the country of the customer’s address.

Note: New boxes to fill out should appear after choosing a country!

  • If the address is known to the Dyna systems, it will fill automatically based on the combination of zipcode and house number. If the address is not known to our systems you will be asked to fill it in manually.

  • After filling in this information we can select a preferred date to execute the installation services. The choice is between the standard settings [Dynalogic choice] or setting one yourself through the [own choice] menu:

Be aware that the preferred date here does not always automatically correspond to an actual planned transport date (it usually does, but there are some exceptions so it’s best to check just in case). For the actual planned transport dates for any shipment/order, please refer to the "History" screen on the Track page.

As mentioned before, the system thinks in package above all else, meaning that even though this is a "service only" order, we will still need to work with the colli (package) information part at the very end. This -again- will not mean that the drivers will come by to pick something up, it is simply a technical requirement that has to be dealt with.

The "description" field here is usually used to reiterate what is expected of the drivers in a particular case (e.g. "Boxspring assembly services"). The LxWxH can all just be entered with a [1], same goes for the weight. If anything crucial is still missing, the page will highlight the issue in red and will inform you of what (else) is required.

Once everything is filled, press the Add new collo to list button, the screen should look something like:

That should mark the end of entering a "Service Only" order through the online MDS page, all that is left is to press the green button on the right-hand side of the screen to Save the order and make it reality.
