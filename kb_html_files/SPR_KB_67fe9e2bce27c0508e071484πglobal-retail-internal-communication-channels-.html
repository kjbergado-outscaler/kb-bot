This article provides instructions on how to create posts in the newly established channels in Teams. The goal is to keep everything centralized in the channels, reducing the use of multiple chats, where information can easily get lost.

Instructions

  • Communication should be in English;

  • Always tag the people you need assistance from;

    • Add a  at the beginning of a post representing an ongoing issue/question

    • When the issue/question gets solved, edit the post and replace the red square by .

    • Title: should be short and easy to search (include order number if applicable); e.g:

      • WIMO - [Order Number], Stock Validation, Refurbishment Orders/Reshuffles, Stock Depletion, Warehouse, Last Mile, Forecast, Reshuffles, Onboarding, Offboarding, BO Tasks, CS Communication (…)

    • Always search for the order number/topic/issue you have before posting to avoid duplication. 

    • Avoid to the maximum creating new chats with people in the channels to tackle topics. If other chats are created about topics that can go in a channel, kindly redirect them to the appropriate one.

  • Meeting chat topics and discussions must also be documented in the relevant channel.

How to create a post

  • Select the channel.​

  • Click "Start a post."​

  • Follow the guidelines provided in the previous slide and write your inquiry.​

  • Select "Post."​

Done! Your post has been created, and you'll receive a response as soon as possible.
