Guide for agents – DHL Request for Information (Before Item Pick-up)


Procedure Overview

When DHL requests confirmation from our customers regarding whether they are subject to any applicable sanctions (for instance with expected money laundering, or other criminal activities*), customer service agents are required to send a specific canned email response. This response will ensure that we address DHL’s request promptly and accurately while maintaining our standard communication protocol.


Due to data protection law restrictions, Emma cannot collect, access, or send this information to DHL on the customer’s behalf. This means that we (the customer service agent) must ask the customer to directly provide this information to DHL by themselves. 


*Please note that this does not imply the customer is involved in any criminal activities. It simply indicates that a suspicious data point prompted the authorities to request additional information from the customer.


Steps to Follow

  1. Receive and Review the Request: Check for an incoming request from DHL that requires customer confirmation of sanctions status. Ensure that the request is clear and that you have all necessary information (e.g., customer details, DHL request specifics).

  2. Send the Canned Email Response: 

    1. Locate the canned email template designated for DHL’s request for sanctions confirmation (the email template is written at the end of this guide). 

    2. Verify that the template is up-to-date and includes any necessary placeholders for customer-specific information.

    3. Attached the relevant files that customer will be sending to DHL for processing. 

    4. Review the personalized email for accuracy and completeness before sending this Canned Email Response to the customer.

    5. Immediately send the request for information to the customer so that they do not miss the deadline to send the information to DHL.

    6. Monitor for any responses from the customer after this. If the customer provides additional information or requires further assistance, address their needs promptly.


Key Points to Remember

  • Accuracy: Always ensure the information in the email is accurate and matches the request from DHL.

  • Professionalism: Maintain a professional tone and provide clear instructions.

  • Documentation: Log all requests and responses to keep a record of the communication.

  • Follow-Up: Be attentive to any follow-up actions required if the customer does not respond promptly.


Canned response

Email to customers (data for DHL screening) You should use this email template alongside the email request from DHL.

English: 

 

SUBJECT: URGENT - Requirements from DHL to pick up your product
Dear [customer name], 
We have just received the enclosed information request relating to you, from DHL, which is the logistics partner in charge of picking up your product. Specifically, in order to comply with legal obligations, DHL needs to confirm and declare that you are not subject to any applicable sanction list with regard to applicable regulations (such as export control regulations).
Specifically, DHL requires you to send a copy of your Proof of Identity (e.g. in the form of a copy of your identity card or passport), in order to carry out the screening. 
Alternatively, you may send them a data point capture (containing the recipient's name, date of birth, and place of birth) for sanctioned party list matching.
We kindly request that you send this information to DHL through dhl-2mh-klaerfallservice@dhl.com within 5 working days upon receipt of this email.
Due to data protection law restrictions, Emma cannot collect, access, or send this information to DHL on your behalf. Therefore, please reply to DHL directly, within the established timeframe stated above.
Please note that in the absence of your timely and complete response to this request for information, your pickup will be cancelled by DHL. Please also be aware that Emma may only proceed with your refund when we receive confirmation from DHL that the product was collected. 
Thank you for your understanding. 



German: 

 

Dringend - Anforderungen von DHL zur Abholung Ihres Produkts
 
Sehr geehrte/r [Kundenname],
 
wir haben soeben die beiliegende Informationsanfrage von DHL erhalten, die als Logistikpartner für die Abholung Ihres Produkts verantwortlich ist. Insbesondere zur Erfüllung rechtlicher Verpflichtungen muss DHL bestätigen und erklären, dass Sie nicht auf einer relevanten Sanktionsliste in Bezug auf geltende Vorschriften (wie z. B. Exportkontrollvorschriften) stehen.
 
DHL benötigt daher eine Kopie Ihres Identitätsnachweises (z. B. in Form einer Kopie Ihres Personalausweises oder Reisepasses), um die Überprüfung durchzuführen.
 
Alternativ können Sie auch eine Datenerfassung (mit Namen des Empfängers, Geburtsdatum und Geburtsort) für den Abgleich mit der Sanktionsliste einreichen.
 
Wir bitten Sie, diese Informationen innerhalb von 5 Werktagen nach Erhalt dieser E-Mail direkt an DHL über die E-Mail-Adresse dhl-2mh-klaerfallservice@dhl.com zu senden.
 
Aufgrund von Datenschutzbestimmungen ist es Emma nicht gestattet, diese Informationen in Ihrem Namen zu sammeln, zu verwalten oder an DHL weiterzuleiten. Bitte senden Sie die Informationen daher direkt an DHL innerhalb des genannten Zeitrahmens.
 
Bitte beachten Sie, dass bei ausbleibender oder unvollständiger Antwort auf diese Informationsanfrage Ihre Abholung durch DHL storniert wird. Ebenso möchten wir darauf hinweisen, dass Emma Ihre Rückerstattung erst nach Erhalt der Bestätigung von DHL, dass das Produkt abgeholt wurde, bearbeiten kann.
 
Vielen Dank für Ihr Verständnis.

When can you resolve this case?

After you have sent this email to DHL, there is no further action to take for this ticket, and you can resolve this ticket. 


Note: It may still happen that the customer contacts us again, regarding other details about the order. If that is the case, please handle it as a normal ticket.
