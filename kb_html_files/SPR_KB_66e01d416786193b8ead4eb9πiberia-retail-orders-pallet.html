Scope

This is the Ops process for Emma Stores in Portugal and Spain.

Order Management

  • Order creation:

    • Shopify is used as a tool to make the sales at the shop floor.

    • We have an automatic connection that uploads orders from Shopify in Magento twice a day (in the morning and in the evening). Only orders marked as paid and with all the customer information will be uploaded in Magento. ​

    • The order is transmitted to the warehouse (if we have stock).

    • Warehouse prepares the order.

    • Carrier picks up the order.

    • Carrier contacts customer SMS (GLS) / Call (Mobel) for delivery.

    • Order delivered to end customer.

Refund Order Magento & Shopify

The steps to make the return and refund are as follows:

  1. The Customer/Store contacts CS to return the product.

  2. CS manages the order pickup and creates the "Return products" in Magento.

  3. Once the pickup is completed and the store sends the proof of pick-up.

    1. Criteria:

      Return Status - Complete | Customer Refund Type - Full refund/pending | Customer refund status - pending.

  4. Cs inform the Store that the order will be refunded through the same payment method the customer used for the purchase.

    1. Billing and Refunds Administrator monitors the customer refund pending list on a daily basis. Parcel or order in the pending list is refunded via Shopify portal.

    2. Billing and Refunds Administrator put a comment in Magento Customer inquiries field e.g. Refunded xxx EUR via Shopify.

      Customer will receive the refund on the original payment method used and timeline as to when the refund will receive differs on a per payment method basis.

    3. Billing and Refunds Administrator creates a credit memo in Magento.

    4. Billing and Refunds Administrator set the customer refund status to REFUNDED to remove the request from the list.
