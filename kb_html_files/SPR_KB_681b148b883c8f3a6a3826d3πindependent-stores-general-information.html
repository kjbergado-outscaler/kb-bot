Independent Stores are franchises - meaning they follow the same structure of our O&O stores, but are owned by a third party.

For Independent Stores, the support setup is currently similar to Wholesale, with cases primarily coming in via email and phone. The focus is on standard B2B support, but there is an expectation that the scope of support will expand to include end-to-end customer service in the future.

Current agent roles in this LOB are generalists, with no specific key account management in place.

​

Key Highlights

  • Current support model mirrors Wholesale processes.

  • Generalist agents handle a wide range of inquiries.

  • Supports business owners via email and phone.

  • Potential future shift to include end-customer support.
