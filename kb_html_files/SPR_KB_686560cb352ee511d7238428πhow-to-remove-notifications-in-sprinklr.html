If you are receiving email notifications from Sprinklr and wish to stop them, please follow the instructions below.

​

⚠️ Important: This configuration must be adjusted individually by each user. It cannot be changed centrally or by an administrator.


Unfortunately, we cannot change this configuration on our side, because it needs to be changed on a User level. This means that you need to open Sprinklr with your account and manage the notifications to block those emails for you. 

I t's very simple, just check the steps below (and the images): 


1) Log in to Sprinklr

Access the platform using your personal user credentials.

​

2) Go to Notification Settings

  • Click on your profile icon (usually located in the top-right corner).

  • Select “Settings” from the dropdown menu.

  • Navigate to the “Notifications” section.

​

3) Adjust Email Preferences

​

Locate the email notification options.

Uncheck or disable the types of notifications you no longer wish to receive.

Save your changes.

​

4) Confirmation

You should stop receiving the selected email notifications shortly after saving your preferences.
