How to assign tickets for assessment?

In order to assign tickets for assessment you will need to go to the Cases tab and apply the filters you want to use for the sampling process. After this the process will split, depending on the type of assessment you want to conduct.

-> For Quality audits

Filters to be used:

- Custom date filter (to sort the tickets from the time period you wish to review);

(Usually this will be the previous week, Monday to Sunday, unless asked otherwise by your manager.)
