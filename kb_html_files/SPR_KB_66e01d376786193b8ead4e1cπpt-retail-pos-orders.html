How to find POS Orders

Sometimes we need to find POS orders for Product Claims or for Collections when offboarding happens.

​

POS orders can be found in Magento -> Sales > Orders

  • Put “*” and part of the bill name available in Retailers Information (Billing Name);

  • Select "Group" as Tax Free

  • Select "Type" as Marketing

  • Click in “Search” and “Select Visible”

The stock orders must have the Shipping Address of a Store or WH and never the Address of a Final Customer.
