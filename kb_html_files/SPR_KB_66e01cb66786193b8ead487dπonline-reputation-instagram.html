Introduction

This article is meant for the agents working in the Online Reputation team. It summarizes why we use Instagram, how we answer the customer queries via this platform and how to log it in Freshdesk.

What is Instagram and why is it important?

Instagram is a hugely popular photo and video-sharing social media platform. Users can share photo or video posts; temporary Stories that live on your profile for 24 hours; Reels, which are 15-second-max short-form videos; IGTV videos, and can even shop directly from e-commerce brands through the app. The app can be downloaded for free and is available on iPhone and Android.

Using Instagram for business can drive brand awareness, boost sales, and build and track audience engagement. It can be distinguished from other social media platforms by its focus on visual communication. 

Instagram is an effective way to advertise a product, as a picture speaks a thousand words.

Communication guidelines

For Instagram communication guidelines, please see this article.

! If the chat case is out of SLA, only apologise after 5 minutes.

! Remember that a social media platform has high visibility, everything sent via Instagram can be easily shared, trendable and go viral.

! Whenever needed, try and transfer the conversation to a more traditional channel such as email.

Tip: After 2 minutes of no answer from the customer, you can move to the next case. Do NOT ask the customer if they are still there, as this is not a regular chat. When the customer comes back, the chat will open again.

How to handle Instagram cases

​

If a Comment:

  1. Assess Tone and Frustration Level:

    • Evaluate the level of frustration expressed in the comment. Use the appropriate tone and language based on the guidelines.

  2. Reply to the Comment:

    • Acknowledge the issue and inform the customer that you’ve sent a direct message to gather more details and investigate further

      Example message, personalize first: Hey @instagram_handle. We're sorry to hear about the trouble with your order. We've sent you a direct message to sort this out. If you could share your order number there, we’d be happy to help you resolve the issue. Thanks!

  3. Send a Direct Message:

    • Reach out to the customer via direct message to request additional information (e.g., order details, contact info).

  4. Investigate the Issue:

    • Gather all necessary information, check the customer’s history, and review any previous interactions to understand the context (if available).

  5. Resolve and Confirm:

    • Resolve the issue and confirm with the customer that it has been addressed.

    • Apply ACW: categorize the case & mark the case as closed in the system.

​

If a Direct Message:

  • Treat the direct message as a standard case and process it accordingly.

​

If a Story/Post/Reel Mention:

Review the Content:

  • If Negative:

    • Reach out to the customer to request more details. Process the case as a standard issue.

  • If Positive:

    • Show appreciation by thanking the customer for their mention in a friendly and professional manner.

If Related to a Store Order:

  • Process the case as a standard store order issue.

  • If you’re unsure about the next steps, escalate the case internally with all relevant details.
