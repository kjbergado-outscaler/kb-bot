Context

Lidl orders involve truck shipments, and we have no access to individual order details. A designated contact, Alexandre Vieira, manages Lidl inquiries, except Product Claims.

Instructions

Customer Inquiries

Verify Inquiry Type

  • If the inquiry is related to a warranty claim for a product purchased at Lidl, assess whether it falls within the agreed 3-year warranty period (starting from the purchase date).

  • If the inquiry concerns a return and refund request outside of the 100-nights trial period, direct the customer to Lidl.

Handling Warranty Claims

  • Process all customer claims within the agreed 3-year warranty period from the purchase date.

  • Follow the established warranty claim guidelines to determine if the defect is covered.

  • If the claim is approved, accept it, and Lidl will refund the customer. Afterward, we will refund Lidl for the purchasing price of the item.
