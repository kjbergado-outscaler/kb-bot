Shipping Note Information

A Shipping Note is a document that contains all the products that are going to be delivered in a certain Shipment.

​

Usually, each order only has 1 Shipping Note, but they can also have more than one in case there's more than one Shipment associated to the order. For example:

  • The products were delivered by different carriers;

  • Some items were Out Of Stock but the remaining items were delivred.

When are Shipping Notes needed?

Shipping Notes are not needed for all deliveries. However, there are some exceptions that need to have this document with them in order to see the order delivered:

  • Warehouse Deliveries (ECI, Auchan, Sonae and Grupo Feira)

  • Islands Deliveries (Jomare, Móveis Abel, Electro Mendes and Dicocina)

  • Jysk Orders

Some retailers also need this document to close the order in their system. They will request this document in order to close the order in the system and pay Emma the respective Invoice.

Missing Shipping Notes

Sometimes the Warehouse / Carrier don't have the Shipping Note needed to deliver the products to the retailer. When that happens, we'll receive an inquire to send the missing Shipping Note. In these cases:


1. Open the respective order in Magento and go to Shipments.

2. Open the Shipment and click in "Print" to open the PDF document

3. Save the document and send it to the respective e-mail

Note: The delivery note (guia de remessa) is the order confirmation document and can be found in WinSCP for Fluent orders.
