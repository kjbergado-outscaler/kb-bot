What is Klachtenkompas


Klachtenkompas.nl is an initiative of the Consumers' Association, where consumers can expose their experience with a company and formalize a public complaint.
That complaint is handled by the relevant company directly in order to come to a solution together.


All complaints are public and as well as the outcome: if there was a solution for it and if the company replied or not.
This way anyone who goes to the Klachtenkompas page can see if there are complaints about companies and how they deal with them.
Website: Klacht oplossen? Meld je klacht op Klachtenkompas van de Consumentenbond


This is very relevant for the Dutch market and for Emma since Klachtenkompas is a very high visible consumer protection platform that customers rely on.


The company who receives a complain has 10 days to reply to it until it is blocked.
Once the 10 days end, Emma can no longer reply to it and Klachtenkompas will publish it mentioning the lack of reply, which harms our reputation even more.

How does the platform looks like

There are 3 main sections in the landing page:

  1. Menu with the the overview of open requests
    1. Open and waiting an initial reply
    2. Waiting for response for follow up
    3. Assessed - complaints received, not replied to and blocked from further action
  2. List of complaints
    1. you can see all the complaints in each folder and when it was created
  3. Complaint details
    1. initial complaint
    2. further comments made by the customer
    3. order details
    4. existing attachments
    5. text box for our reply
    6. tool for us to uploadattachments
    7. field for us to select if the reply is an Update, Response or Solution


How to reply to reviews


We have only 10 days to reply to Klachtenkompas complaints before they are blocked Complaints should have an initial reply a maximum of 5 days after being created.


Step-by-step guide

  1. Go through the complaint and further customer comments
  2. Check the order details and understand what happened with the order by checking OMS and existing tickets on Freshdesk and possible escalations
  3. Provide a reply asap on the Answer text box
  4. Attach documentation if needed
  5. Mark it accordingly to what we are relayng to the customer:
    1. Status update: if there is no internal update yet and, for instance, if we are still waiting for the outcome of an escalation
    2. Question or solution if we need to have further information from the customer (ex: order data missing or we are proposing to replace one item by a different one)
    3. Conclusion when we are able to provide a solution or if the situation has been handled and communicated to the customer already
  6. If we the message contains personal details and information specific to the order select Private message
