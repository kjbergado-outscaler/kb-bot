Process of Product Claim


The customer can contact us in case their product has a defect such as:


  • Very soft or firm foam.
  • Mattress sinking (regularly these cases occur after months of having purchased the product).
  • holes in the mattress
  • Foam defects: (broken, dirty, different color, uneven foam)
  • Mattress not inflated properly
  • Cover: (broken, dirty, torn, broken closure, etc.)


Validation Criteria


Always validate the claim photo shared by the customer


Always double-check what kind of photo is being provided. Ask yourself these questions:


  • Is it an actual Emma product?
  • Does the photo provided match the product the customer ordered? Or are they both not related?
  • Does this photo look like something taken off the internet?



If the case proceed, it is necessary to register the Product Claim in the Request order.xlsx file
