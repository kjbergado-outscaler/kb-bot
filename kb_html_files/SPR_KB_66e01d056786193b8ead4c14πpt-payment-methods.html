What is it?

We make available to the MULTIBANCO (ATM) network holders the possibility of paying invoices from thousands of suppliers, anytime and anywhere, through the MULTIBANCO (ATM) references. There are several companies that offer this service to their clients, all they have to do is request it from their bank.

​

How to use?

This payment method is widely used by the Portuguese, due to the convenience and security that this operation offers. Since 1989, you can make your payments for electricity, water, telephone, insurance, among others, remotely from a ATM.

​

  • To make a payment by MULTIBANCO (ATM) Reference, just go to a machine and select "Payments and Other Services" > "Payment of Services" and then enter the Entity, Reference and Amount. We advise you to keep the receipt, since it serves as proof of your payment.

​

  • On an Automatic Payment Terminal you can also perform this service by selecting "Payment of Services" > "MB Card?" > Entity > Reference > Amount > PIN

​

  • Through your Homebanking you can also make a payment by Multibanco reference without having to leave the comfort of your home. Check your bank's website and see if you have this option available.

What is it?

​

PayPal is an intermediary service for buying and selling products and services over the Internet. In addition to these commercial exchanges, it also allows you to receive or send money using your account on this platform. It is possible to use PayPal in different contexts. It is therefore important to understand how payments are made through this service.

​

How to use?

​

To use PayPal you must first create an account on the PayPal site. This process has no cost associated and can be done in a few steps:

​

  1. Go to the PayPal website;

  2. In the upper right corner of the page, choose the "Create Account" option;

  3. You will have to choose between a personal or business account;

  4. Enter all the information that is asked of you (such as your bank account number, email address, among others);

  5. Once you have confirmed your details, you will be sent an email to confirm your account;

  6. In the email, click on the link provided to confirm your account.

  7. The PayPal account will be associated with your bank account or credit card, as well as the email you entered. There is no account number, it is through the email address that it will be identified when making payments.


If you do not want to associate your card or bank account directly, you can always create a virtual card through the MB Net system.

Just access your PayPal account, then click on "my profile", choose the option "my money" and on "update", then enter the card data.


After creating your PayPal account, you will be able to make and receive payments through PayPal. All you have to do is log on to the platform with your access data.

​

Making payments

​

If you are shopping online at a website and want to use PayPal to pay, you only need to be logged in, and in transactions is never communicated to the seller your bank account or card details, or their security codes.

​

This is because this service takes care of the automatic debit of the amount in question in the user's PayPal account and sending that amount to the seller.

​

Take note:

In case a payment is not claimed, you can cancel it by accessing your account through the PayPal website or app.

​

Once the transaction is complete, you will receive confirmation by email and the money you received will go into your PayPal account. You can then use that amount for purchases or transfer it to your personal bank account.
