When to use it

  • Invoice corrections (VAT, wrong quantities, wrong prices, wrong address)

  • Credit Notes Creation (if not available in WinSCP)

  • Credit Notes Creation - Logistics Discounts

It is also necessary to include the Sprinklr case number in the Jira ticket.

After completing the return process in Fluent, we should wait 10 days to open a JIRA AR B2B to request the credit note if it is still not available in WinSCP. If you do not receive a response within 48 hours, add the JIRA information to the “JIRA” file: JIRAS.xlsx (sharepoint.com)

Regarding the process of invoice correction due to an unapplied discount, please create an AR B2B tickets and tag Joana Peixe (Sales Manager).
