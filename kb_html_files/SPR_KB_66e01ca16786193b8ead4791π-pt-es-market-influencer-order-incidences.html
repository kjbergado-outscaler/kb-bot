Incidences with Influencer Orders

We have many collaborations with influencers to whom we send our products for promotion. Sometimes, this process can create incidences during delivery.

We work with an agency that manages these collaborations. Whenever they receive communication reporting an issue, they forward the cases to us.

How Does the Email Look?



What Should We Do?

  • Manage the escalation through an outbound email to the influencer as if it was a normal customer case.

  • Reply to the agency with the action taken. (example: "We have reached the customer and requested pictures to assist with the resolution of the claim, thank you.")

  • This is considered a high-level escalation.

Note: The agency might not have the order number, but they will provide as much information as possible.
