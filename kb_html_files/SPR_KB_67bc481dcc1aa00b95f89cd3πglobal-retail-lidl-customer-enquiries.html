Warranty Claims

Introduction

This article serves as a guideline for handling customer enquiries in CH and ES, as well as in all markets where we have previously operated campaigns with Lidl.

We have aligned with Lidl to handle all customer enquiries within the agreed 3-year warranty period, starting from the purchase date in a Lidl store. All tickets will be resolved in accordance with our warranty claim guidelines.

Instructions

  • Check the purchase date;

  • If a warranty-covered defect is identified, the claim will be accepted;

  • Lidl will refund the customer;

  • We will then reimburse Lidl for the purchase price of the item.

Exception

For the ES and CH markets, where a logistic return process is not in place, Lidl will arrange pickup and disposal of mattresses directly from customers.
