Summary of the product

The Emma Flip Topper is a great way to extend the life of your mattress. As the name suggests, you can flip the Flip Topper to either side and choose between a firmer or softer feeling.

The blue layer is made of Airgocell foam and it's the softer one; and the grey layer is made of HRX supportive foam, which makes this side firmer.


Product Pictures





USPs (Unic Selling Points)


  1. Reversible/ Flippable: The topper is double-sided, which means that the customer can choose between soft and hard feeling, just by flipping the topper over.

  2. Breathability: The Airgocell® foam -which is also used in the Emma mattresses -is very porous and thus ensures an ideal exchange of air. Open cell foams provide thermo-regulating benefits. You don’t wake up sweating (also right temperature in the room and right duvet is important).

Product Information


Product Communication


1) One fits-all concept

  • We recommend the topper to people between 50kg and 130kg. Nevertheless, people under and over that weight can also sleep on the topper without problems.

  • NOT designed for babies -> because of hardness. A hard surface to sleep on is recommended for babies, which allows the baby to grow during asleep.

  • Reasons why babies shouldn't sleep on an adult topper:

  • It may affect baby's growth.

  • Hard side of the topper is not hard enough for babies. The head is the heaviest part of the baby ́s body and need to be supported.


2) Topper won't be affected

  • Under all extreme climate conditions, as long as the topper is kept inside and protected, this will always get back to normal once returned to a regular climatic conditions.

  • The topper may be kept rolled packed up to 6 months.


3) Mattress properties might change when using Toppers or comfort layers

  • Cut-outs will be further away from the person, affecting the zoning;

  • Even with a soft topper, the mattress might be harder;

  • Psychological effect;

  • Mattress will be higher.

Product Core

Point-elastic Airgocell: The top layer adapts to pressure and responds to the movement for ideal comfort. This is the softer side of the Topper.


Benefits

  • Moisture (sweat) will be absorbed by the top layer and then will evaporate. It doesn´t affect the properties of the topper after it dissipates. *right temperature in the room and the right duvet is important

  • It is not affected by moisture -> Topper keeps its shape during its whole life

  • Temperature regulation = breathability


Supportive HRX (Cold) Foam: The bottom layer of HRX polyurethane foam, which is supportive and extremely durable. This is the harder side of Topper.


Benefits

  • This foam supports you in the right places, where it matters.

  • Very long durability. No sagging (Liegekuhle), even after a long time.

  • It is high-point elastic. The supportive bottom layer helps the body to move effortlessly, which means there´s not a lot of strength required to turn over while sleeping.
