This article provides the process steps to be followed when a partnership proposal arrives in the CS UK queue.

​

Identify the Case

When a case related to a partnership proposal is received, confirm that the request is regarding a potential partnership and not a general inquiry.

Forward the Case Internally

  • To: sahil.mehra@emma-sleep.com (Head of Wholesale UK)

  • CC: andrea.perez@emma-sleep.com (Customer Excellence Manager)

  • Email Subject: Partnership Proposal – Forwarded from CS

​

Email Body:

​

Dear Sahil,

I hope you're doing well.

We received this partnership proposal via the Customer Service email and wanted to share it with you for review. Since this falls outside our scope, we’ll proceed to close the case on our side. Please let us know if you need any further details.

Best Regards,

[Your Name]

Respond to the Sender

Send the following template:

Subject: Partnership Proposal Inquiry

Dear [Sender's Name],

Thank you for reaching out. We have shared your request with the appropriate internal team, and they will review it. If this is a partnership opportunity that we would like to explore, someone from our team will get back to you.

Please note that this email is not the correct contact for partnership proposals, so we kindly ask you to refrain from reaching out to this address for such matters in the future.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

Close the Case

  • Mark the case as closed on Sprinklr.
