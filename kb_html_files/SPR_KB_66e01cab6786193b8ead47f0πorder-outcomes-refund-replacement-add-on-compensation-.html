Introduction


When managing reverse logistic orders, we may need to trigger specific actions for the order based on the customer outcome. Magento Refund, replacement orders, add-ons, or compensation requests.


Let's review the possible orders outcomes in this article.




Overview of outcomes






IMPORTANT:

Compensation is NEVER a reason to create a Supplement Order. The creation of Supplements is ONLY to be done for "Add-on" and "Replacement".




Return (Trial Period request)






Return + Full refund
The customer returns the product and gets his paid money fully refunded

  • Order Management tool: Create a return and trigger a full refund


No return + Discount

Return is deflected based on return defense strategy (Compensation table discount offered)

  • Order Management tool: Create a discount and provide details of the amount to be refunded.


No return + Add on

a Return is deflected with an add-on product ( supplement order ) such as comfort layer, pillow, or other free item we may send depending on the market strategy.

  • Order Management tool: Create a supplement order adding related 0 value SKU (product).




Product Claim





Product Claim: Return + Full refund
The customer returns the product and gets his paid money fully refunded

  • Order Management tool: Mark the product claim with the return outcome and trigger a full refund



Product Claim: Return + Replacement

Customer returns the defective product and we replace the item with a supplement order.

  • Order Management tool: Mark product claims with the return outcome with NO REFUND.
  • Create a supplement order with zero value


Product Claim: Compensation (return deflected)

Customer agree to keep the claimed product, and we offer monetary compensation based on our compensation table.

  • Order Management tool: Mark product claims with the compensation outcome with NO REFUND.
  • Add a value to the compensation based on the compensation table.



Product Claim: Add-on (return deflected)

Customer agree to keep the claimed product, and we offer product compensation based on country strategy (as a pillow).

  • Order Management tool: Mark the product claim with the Supplement order as the outcome with NO REFUND.
  • Create a supplement order with zero value with the offered item.



Logistic Claim





Logistic Claim: Return + Full refund
The customer returns the product and gets his paid money fully refunded OR the parcel is considered lost, and the customer wants a full refund.

  • Order Management tool: Mark the product claim with the return outcome and trigger a full refund



Logistic Claim: Return + Replacement

Customer returns the defective product and we replace the item with a supplement order OR the parcel is considered lost, and we replace it with a supplement order.

  • Order Management tool: Mark product claims with the return outcome with NO REFUND.
  • Create a supplement order with zero value


Logistic Claim: Compensation (return deflected)

Customer agrees to keep the claimed product, and we offer monetary compensation based on our compensation table OR the parcel is considered lost, and we replace it with a supplement order and offer monetary compensation based on our compensation table.

  • Order Management tool: Mark product claims with the compensation outcome with NO REFUND.
  • Add a value to the compensation based on compensation table.



Logistic Claim: Add-on (return deflected)

Customer agree to keep the claimed product, and we offer product compensation based on country strategy (as a pillow).

  • Order Management tool: the Mark product claim with the Supplement order as the outcome with NO REFUND.
  • Create a supplement order with zero value with the offered item.
