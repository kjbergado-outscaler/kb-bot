Here is the information for the Retailer customers in case they contact Customer Service.

Marketplaces


AMAZON

  • AMAZON is a very important partner of ours, the requests may only be processed by AMAZON agents
  • We usually receive inquiries via the “Seller Portal”
  • We have an SLA (Service Level Agreement) – we must (always) reply within 24 hours (except weekends)
  • Different rules apply to AMAZON customers (compensation, replacement items)
  • Some other processes too
  • Tickets should go to 2nd level or to the AMAZON group


Otto, Lidl, Baur, Jysk

  • delivery

Emma is responsible for delivery (with the exception of Concord mattresses). We can see the shipment tracking and give the delivery status. The order numbers are not always found in Magento. It is best for us to search for the order using the last name, first name and the e-mail address of the respective retailer:


Otto2@example.de


Lidl@example.de


Baur2@example.de


If the goods are delayed or lost, we are not allowed to create a supplement. The customer must always contact the retailer's customer service. An additional discount or other type of compensation is also not possible.


Return/Exchange/Complaint:


The customer must also do this directly with the retailer. The test phase also applies, but the retailer is responsible for processing the exchange or return. The customer must contact the contract partner as payment and reimbursement are made via Retailer. We cannot order a new cover either.


Comfort layer:


We cannot provide customers with a free comfort pad (putting on S1). The customer should also deal with this directly with the retailer.

What do we do if an Emma product is cheaper at a retailer?


The customer does not have an Emma product yet - wants to buy one now:


We don't offer the discount, the customer should buy the product directly from the retailer. We politely refer to the retailer.


The customer has already bought/ordered an Emma product and the test phase is not over yet.

  • The customer should send us the offer
  • We check the offer online
  • We offer the price without retailer newsletter/new customer discount first
  • If the customer does not agree with this, we can give the full discount
  • A maximum discount of 45% discount can be given

Example:


Otto offers Emma One medium 90x200 for €159 and an additional 10% for the newsletter subscription - in total, the customer can get the Emma for €143.10.


The customer bought an Emma from us for €179 and is within 100 days. Would like to get the Otto price, otherwise the Emma will be returned and a new one bought by Otto.


Step - we offer the discount 20€ (179 – 159).


Step – if the customer does not agree to this – then grant a maximum of €35.9 (179-143.10).


What do we do if the campaign is already over?


The customer will contact you within the promotion but we will only process the ticket after the promotion (e.g. the customer will contact you on Saturday and the retailer offer is valid until Sunday)


The customer should expect that we will not reply during weekends


We ask for the invoice from the retailer, only then can we offer/grant the discount


If the customer has not yet created the order and the promotion is over, we cannot grant a discount
