What is a KPI?


Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are the critical (key) indicators of progress toward an intended result. KPIs provide a focus for strategic and operational improvement, create an analytical basis for decision making and help focus attention on what matters most.


“What gets measured gets done.” - Peter Drucker


Managing with the use of KPIs includes setting targets (the desired level of performance) and tracking progress against that target. Managing KPIs often means working to improve leading indicators. Those indicators are precursors of future success.



Customer Service KPIs


  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Net Promoter Score
  • Productivity
  • First Response Time
  • First Contact Resolution
  • Resolution Time
