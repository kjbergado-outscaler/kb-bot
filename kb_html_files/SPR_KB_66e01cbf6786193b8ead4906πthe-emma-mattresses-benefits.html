Emma One



Benefits:


  • The Airgocell and Visco layers give the mattress a point-elastic surface. This means for the customer that the mattress adapts perfectly to the body.

  • The HRX layer as a base supports the sleeper, thus preventing the customer from sinking too deeply into the mattress and having the feeling of sagging on the mattress.

  • Due to the symmetrical structure, the customer can turn the mattress at any time, but not flip it.

  • Due to the better cutting technique in the shoulder zone, the shoulder sinks deeper into the mattress to improve the sleepers' ergonomics. Thus, despite the slightly firmer lying properties, the mattress is adaptable in the shoulder.

  • The cutting techniques of the mattress result in excellent ventilation, which reduces sweating.

  • The polyester functional fabric in the cover very quickly wicks moisture away to the outside and ensures a dry sleeping climate. The functioning of the cover is similar to high-performance sportswear.


Emma One Pocket Spring



Benefits:


  • The Airgocell and Visco layers give the mattress a point-elastic surface. This means for the customer that the mattress adapts perfectly to the body.

  • The spring core consists of 250 springs per square meter. It ensures better ventilation of the mattress compared to the foam variant and is more suitable for people who tend to sweat more.

  • By using different springs in the shoulder and pelvic area, the sleeper sinks deeper in the necessary places to improve the ergonomics of the sleeper.

  • The pocket spring core makes it easier to turn on the mattress as it gives the necessary support by springing back.

  • Due to the symmetrical structure, the customer can turn the mattress at any time, but not flip it.

  • The polyester functional fabric in the cover very quickly wicks moisture away to the outside and ensures a dry sleeping climate. The functioning of the cover is similar to high-performance sportswear.

Emma Original



Benefits:


  • The Emma Orignal is only available in one hardness.

  • The Airgocell and Visco layers give the mattress a point elastic surface. This means for the customer that the mattress here adapts to the body. Since the Emma Original is equipped with 8cm of pressure-relieving foam instead of 3cm, the mattress is significantly more elastic and adaptable than the Emma One models.

  • The HRX layer as a base supports the sleeper and thus prevents the customer from sinking too deeply into the mattress and having the feeling of sagging on the mattress.

  • The symmetrical design allows the customer to turn the mattress at any time, but not flip it.

  • The cutting technique in the shoulder area allows the shoulder to sink deeper into the mattress, improving ergonomics for the sleeper. Thus, the cutting technique further supports the pressure-relieving surface and provides excellent ergonomics on the mattress.

  • The mattress' cutting techniques result in excellent ventilation, which reduces sweating.

  • The polyester functional fabric in the cover very quickly wicks moisture to the outside and provides a dry sleeping climate. The functioning of the cover is similar to high-performance sportswear. Compared to the Emma One, this fabric is thicker and feels more sympathetic to the skin.
