Portal da Queixa


Portal da Queixa is a consumer complaints platform in Portugal. It serves as an online space for individuals to file and share complaints about products and services provided by companies. The platform is widely used, and companies are expected to respond to and address complaints posted on Portal da Queixa.


If companies neglect or fail to respond to complaints on this platform, they may face negative repercussions. Potential consequences include damage to the company's reputation, loss of customer trust, and regulatory actions. It is crucial for businesses to engage with and resolve complaints on Portal da Queixa to maintain a positive public image and comply with consumer protection expectations in Portugal.

How to handle it - Process in Portal da Queixa


1. Enter into the platform. Once you are logged in, click in:

  1. Reclamações

  2. Caixa de entrada

  3. Select the case


2. Once you press on the case, you will read the information and reply.

Once replied, never forget to mark it as "resolver". 



Do not forget reply is public, so please do not disclose any GDPR information.
