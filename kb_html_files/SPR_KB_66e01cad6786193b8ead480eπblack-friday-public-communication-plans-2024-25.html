Pricing/ Discount Errors


Why wasn’t the discount applied to my purchase?


We are sorry for the inconvenience. It seems there was an issue with applying the discount during your purchase. Please double-check that the promo code was entered correctly and that it applies to the items in your cart. Also, don’t forget that discount codes during Black Friday last for a short period of time. Can you please provide your order number so we can resolve this for you?


Why did the price change at checkout?


We apologize for the misunderstanding. Prices during Black Friday can change quickly due to limited-time promotions or inventory levels. If you saw a different price before checkout, it might have been during a flash sale or special offer. However, we understand how frustrating this can be, and we’d like to help. If you can provide more details about the price you saw, we’ll do our best to investigate and see if we can honor that price.



I saw a lower price earlier, can you honor that?


We’re sorry to hear about the price discrepancy. Our prices can fluctuate due to ongoing promotions and limited-time offers. If the price you saw was recent and within the same day, we’ll be happy to review your order and see if we can make an adjustment. Please share more information, like the time and product details, so we can assist you further.



Can I get a refund of adjustment for the incorrect pricing?


Thank you for bringing this to our attention. If there was an error in the pricing at the time of your purchase, we’d be more than happy to issue a refund for the difference or make the necessary adjustment. Please provide your order number, and we’ll process this for you right away to ensure you’re charged the correct amount.
