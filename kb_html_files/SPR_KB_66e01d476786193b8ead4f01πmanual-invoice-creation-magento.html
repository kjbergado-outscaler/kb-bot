Overview

Invoices should automatically be created in Magento once the products have been shipped; however, there are instances where the invoice is not available in Magento and the order requires to be refunded via product return (i.e. late cancellation). One of the critical requirements for an order to be refunded is for the order to have an invoice. The reason for this is that a credit memo (refund) will be charged against the invoice of the order. If an order has no invoice, refund will not be successful.


Here are some of the reasons why invoice is not available:

  • item on the order was not yet shipped
  • failed automatic invoice creation in Magento after the product was shipped


This article will show the instruction on how to create an invoice for orders that required to be refunded.

Capture Online vs Offline

Capture Online means that the order will also captured in the Payment Method website to trigger the charging to customers.

Capture Offline means that invoice will be created only in Magento. Some other PSPs do not need orders to be captured in PSP to initiate the charge to customers.


Invoice for Capture Online

There are instances that invoice can be captured online. Invoice can be captured online if these payment methods were used by the customer:


Rechnungskauf (Payolution/Unzer) Klarna Pagarme Credit Card PayPal Express (AU)


*Note: If capture online fails, create the invoice via capture offline.

Steps in Invoice Creation


Reminder: Only shipped/delivered products should be invoiced.


1. Open the order in Magento. Click Invoice.


You will be routed to invoice page.



2. On the lower right side of the Invoice page on Amount field, choose Capture Offline on the dropdown.

Note: In case Capture Online/Capture Offline dropdown is not available, just click Submit Invoice.


3. Once Capture Offline has been chosen, click Submit Invoice.



4. You will see a notification that the invoice has been created.


5. Invoice tab will show the invoice that you've created. It usually takes 2-3 minutes before the invoice reflect in Magento; you will need to refresh the page for the invoice to show.
