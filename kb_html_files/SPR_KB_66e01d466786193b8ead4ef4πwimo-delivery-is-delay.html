Introduction


In this article, you will learn what to do when items are out of the promised delivery date, and consequently, the order is delayed. Whenever you face a delayed order, you are responsible for providing clear information to the customer about the status of the order and what will happen next In order to do that, you must follow investigation steps to identify what is the root cause of the delay and manage the customer expectations regarding the solution and delivery time.


Review:
Make sure to also read all the related WIMO articles, such as WIMO - What is it? and WIMO - Delivery is On Time.




The journey of an item: Unhappy Path


As explained in the article What is WIMO, we have different phases that the item goes through after it's purchased and before arriving to the customer. During this journey, unexpected issues can happen that could cause a delayed delivery.


Delivery is delay


We will always try our best to minimize the negative impact of the delay and appreciate the customer for choosing Emma. If information is available about the delay, give an explanation, and be transparent with the customer. Always show a willingness to help and to ensure the customer receives their item at the earliest convenience.


The image below shows with blue dots the phases in which we can face problems and the arrows indicate which can be the issue:



  • Order Stuck: Problems with the transmission of the order like address errors, payment issues, or system bugs.

  • Order Out of Stock: The item is out of stock and the order has yet not be fulfilled. Or is delayed, but already fulfilled.

  • Warehouse delays: Order dispatched with delay by the warehouse.

  • Last mile delays: These happen with unable-to-delivery scenarios or due to Logistic Claims (parcel lost, partial delivery, etc).




Why the order is delayed?

Step 1: Investigate the order in detail


After being contacted by a customer asking about their order, you will have to open the order in the case and look at the details of it to understand what exactly is happening. It's possible that only one item was not delivered, or that we are having an issue at the warehouse. So, before determining the reason and taking further action, please check carefully the order in the system.


  1. Check the "Promised delivery date": expire within 2 days or it is already in the past?

  2. Check the "Fulfilment status" of all items of the order

  3. Item by item identification: which status is each item at?

Step 2: Identifying the reason for the delay

Whenever you face a delayed order, you are responsible for providing clear information to the customer about this order. In order to do that, you must first investigate the case to clarify what is the reason for the delay and manage customer expectations about the outcome and the expected arrival of their item.


Identifying the reason is necessary as based on that you will know exactly how to handle to support the customer. We have 4 different scenarios that can cause a delivery to be delayed and each one of them has distinct resolutions. Let's check in detail each one of them:




Order Stuck

The Order is Stuck when is not being transmitted in the system, normally due to a Payment Issue, Address typo, or System bug. Those cases happen between the phases "Purchase" and "Order Confirmed" and when the Order Status is at Unknown or the status is for more than 05 days stuck in one of the following:

For Magento: Packing; Prepared for Export.

For FluentCommerce: Assigned;

How to work on it?

  1. Check OMS (Order Management System);

  2. Identify that the order status is unknown;

  3. Open a ticket to Back-Office team (for Central Europe countries) or to OM team (for all other countries) informing them the status is stuck and asking them to review the order;

  4. When the team responsible gets back to you, you check the delivery date update and send it via email to the customer.

​

Order Out of Stock

We may face situations where the product purchased by a customer is facing an out-of-stock situation. For those cases, the order will not go to "Order Confirmed" and will neither be "Submitted to Warehouse".

The Fulfillment or Barcode status will show as "Waiting on Stock".

​

How to work on it?

  1. Check OMS (Order Management System)

  2. Identify that the status is "Waiting on Stock";

  3. Check if there's an Updated Delivery Date and follow the instructions .

Warehouse and Last Mile Delays

​

After the Order is Confirmed it will be "Submitted to Warehouse" and later will be "Dispatched", since the carrier will pick-up the item to make a delivery. Those are the Fulfillment/Barcode status that you may see:

For Magento:

Order submitted to warehouse

Order received by warehouse

Waiting for Delivery date

Parcel data submitted / ready for shipping

Arrived at sorting center

Out for deliver

For FluentCommerce:

Submitted to FL

Received by FL

Picked & Packed by FL

Ready to Dispatch

Dispatched

Arrived at the sorting center

Out for delivery

​

How to work on it?

  1. Check OMS (Order Management System);

  2. Identify which is the status (if order with Warehouse or with order with carrier);

  3. Check the Last Mile delivery times article and the Excel sheet there;

  4. The file gives an estimation time of how long an order stays with the warehouse and how long stays with the carrier based on the SKU of the item;

  5. After checking those dates, please use the Canned Responses of WIMO (number XX) to inform the customer accordingly about the delivery date.
