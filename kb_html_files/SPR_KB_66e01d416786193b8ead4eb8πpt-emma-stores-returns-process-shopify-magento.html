Reimbursement process for Portugal

Cancellation order in Magento

  • If the order is in Waiting for stock, Packing you can cancel the order in Magento.

  • If the order status is "Order submited to Wh, Order Received to Wh":

    • You must contact the warehouse and cancel the order.

    • Ask the Backoffice to cancel the barcodes.

  • Agent CS triggered the cancellation via Magento

  • The store team refunded via Shopify portal.

    • Customer will receive the refund on the original payment method used and timeline as to when the refund will receive differs on a per payment method basis.

  • The OTC team put a comment in Magento Customer inquiries field e.g. Refunded xxx EUR via Shopify.

  • The OTC team confirms the order in the manual cancellation to remove the request from the list.

  • After Packing status in Magento – you need to create a ticket to WH to cancel the barcode and ask for a refund. Please add the order number and inform if it's a Cancellation or a Return: Refund Requests - AR D2C Portal (formerly Order-to-Cash).

Product Return

The steps to make the return and refund are as follows:

  • The Store contacts CS to return the end customer's product.

  • CS manages the order pickup and creates the "Return products" in Magento.

  • Once the pickup is completed and the store sends the proof of pick-up.



CS informs the Store team that order was returned (return with the carrier, pick-up at the customer's address).

After we receive the collection declaration, and our return process is completed in Magento, we just need to inform the store that they can proceed with the refund to the end customer (if the order was paid via GetNet).

​

  • If paid via Shopify, raise a ticket with the AR team to process the refund Refund Requests - AR D2C Portal (formerly Order-to-Cash).

    • Customer will receive the refund on the original payment method used and timeline as to when the refund will receive differs on a per payment method basis.

  • If the order was paid via GetNet, the Store Team will process the refund for this in the store via the payment terminal.

    ​

  • The OTC team put a comment in Magento Customer inquiries field e.g. Refunded xxx EUR via Shopify.

  • The OTC team creates a credit memo in Magento.

  • The OTC team set the customer refund status to REFUNDED to remove the request from the list.

If the order does not have barcodes created and you need to do a return, please open a Jira to the OTC team.

​

Discounts and Compensation

  • Agent triggered the discount and compensation request in Magento.

  • The store team refunded via Shopify portal.

    • Customer will receive the refund on the original payment method used and timeline as to when the refund will receive differs on a per payment method basis.

  • The OTC team put a comment in Magento Customer inquiries field e.g. Refunded xxx EUR via Shopify.

  • The OTC team creates a credit memo in Magento.

  • The OTC team confirms the order in the manual cancellation to remove the request from the list.
