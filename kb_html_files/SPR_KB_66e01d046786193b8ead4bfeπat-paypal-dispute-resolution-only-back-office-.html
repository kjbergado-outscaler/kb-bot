1. Sign up for PayPal using LastPass.
  2. Then you go under "Tools" to conflict resolution.



We only process the conflicts for DE, AT, CH. We simply leave the rest in the PayPal portal.


It is not always clear from the outside whether it is an order that we need to process or not, in which case you always have to go into the "EUR" case and take a look at it.



Under "due" on we can then search for the date on which processing has not yet taken place. We always process 10 days to 2 weeks in advance so we are under no pressure. E.g. As in the picture, we have processed PP until 03/06/2021 and are therefore now filtering 03/07/2021.



Under "Answers" you will then come to the PP conflict.


This is a PP message and not a conflict, but we're processing it.


Here the customer writes us a message via PayPal, if we don't reply to the message within the time limit, a case will be opened automatically.


Under "show more" you can see the name and email address of the customer, we are looking for the customer in Magento. If we cannot find the customer, we ask the customer for his order data.



Please then compare the invoice number in Magento with Paypal so that you can be sure that you are in the correct order.

Depending on the situation, we can act like that:

  • We leave a comment in Magento with example "PayPal message / SN specified".
  • If the goods have been delivered to the customer in the meantime, we send the customer a message that the "mattress, etc." has already arrived and enter the tracking data from Magento under "Add shipment data".
  • If the goods are not with the customer, we check in Magento whether a replacement delivery has been created and, if necessary, can write that one has been created and that it has been delivered or is being processed. Depending on the situation.
  • If there is no replacement product, we can let the customer know when the product will arrive or ask if the customer would like a refund or a new delivery. Depending on the current delivery situation and how this is currently being regulated
  • If the customer wants a cancellation or if the goods have been returned to the sender and the customer wants a refund, we must enter the full amount (a product or an entire order) under "Send refund". If an invoice has already been created in Magento, we must also make an invoice correction. We go into the invoice and press "Invoice correction" (next to Print) and then click Refund (offline) at the bottom.

Magento also knows that the refund has been made.


We will also refund the amount in PayPal. In the case of messages, we have the option of selecting an action “Send refund” at the bottom, then the window appears: Here I check again whether the amount really matches that in Magento.


After that we can press Send Refund and the customer should be refunded the same day. In rare cases this doesn't work, then it has to be forwarded to Tolga.


In addition to the messages, there are also PayPal conflicts, here you have no opportunity to communicate with the customer:


If a cancellation is still possible in Magento, or you know it will still be a long time before the goods arrive, it makes sense to cancel because in this situation we cannot prove to PayPal that we have delivered the goods and, in the worst case, the customer has delivered one Refund through Paypal and the goods received.


After canceling in Magento, we respond in PayPal with "I will send a refund to my customer and close the case." Thus, the customer receives the refund from PayPal.


Here, too, we leave a comment in Magento with example "PP conflict / reimbursed via PP".


Most of the time, however, there is a tracking number that we can provide. At DPD we always indicate DPD DELIStrack.


We indicate this when the shipment is with the customer or will be with the customer soon.
