Introduction

This article is meant for the agents working in the Online Reputation team. It summarizes the use of Idealo, how we answer the customer queries via this platform and how to log it in Sprinklr.

​

What is Idealo and why is it important?

Idealo is a price comparison platform that allows consumers to compare prices for millions of products across various online retailers. The platform aggregates product listings from multiple online stores, displaying prices, shipping costs, customer ratings, and retailer information to help users make informed purchasing decisions.

For businesses, Idealo increases visibility, improves conversion rates, and offers competitive insights, allowing retailers to adjust their pricing strategies effectively.

What the customer sees

When users search for our shop on Idealo, they have an overview of:

  1. The number of reviews, the current Score and historical data of reviews.

  2. The option to write a review.

  3. The different reviews, sorted by experience.

  4. Reviews, written by customers.

How to handle an Idealo review

So, what should you always consider when you are replying to a review?

  • Always keep the Emma tone of voice - please refresh your memory on Emma's tone of voice via this article ​. For more guidelines, see this article .

  • Reply according to GDPR procedures - check this article for further information regarding GDPR.

​

How to reply to positive reviews (4-5 stars reviews):

  1. Engage with the customer, appreciate the fact that we are also pleased by providing a good experience

  2. Check if there is anything something you can help with

  3. Try to not always use the same reply, customers value being acknowledged, so don't always use the same sentence.
    Since "happy" reviews don't need any particular intervention they will be faster to reply, create a file with a sample of possible replies and use it to be more efficient and still portray a customized approach.

    1. Examples:

      • "Hello (customer name), thank you very much for taking the time to leave us a review, your opinion helps us to improve our services, thank you for trusting Emma!"

      • "Thank you (customer name) for your message. We are glad to hear that the quality of your sleep has improved with our products and we wish you sweet dreams!"

      • "Hi (customer name), thank you very much for sharing your experience with Emma. We are glad you are enjoying our products, and we hope you continue to do so for many years to come. Thank you for your trust in Emma!"

      • "Hello (customer name), thank you very much for your positive feedback, we hope you enjoy your products Emma!"

How to reply to negative reviews (1-3 stars reviews):

​​More GUIDELINES​

  1. How to reply

    1. These reviews are harder to handle as they will require more time and effort on your side

    2. It is like a regular CS ticket but with higher visibility on a public platform

    3. You should provide a reply as soon as possible even if just to acknowledge we will reach out and solve the problem

    4. Replies can be edited so once you have a definitive solution just update it

      ​

      2. What should always be included:

      Start by greeting the customer & sympathize with him and acknowledge the problem

    5. "Thank you for bringing the issue to our attention..."

    6. apologize and explain that the issue is not a standard at Emma

      • "We are sorry to hear that..."

      • "What happened is not in accordance with Emma's standards..."

    7. identify the next steps: either collect more customer information for follow up or specific actions that will improve the situation

      • "I have requested your information here on Trustpilot, could you send it to me so I can track your order?"

Step-by-Step Process
1. Assess the level of frustration and use the appropriate language. (Check guidelines)​  

2. Did a customer provide valid order information? (Try to search for the order number in the order management system FluentCommerce/Magento)

- YES (valid order information provided):

  1. Investigate the issue 

Store orders: If the case is related to the store order, process it as a usual case. If you are not sure about the required actions - escalate internally.

2. As there's no integration with the Idealo platform and Sprinklr, only send an outbound email to the cst. Choose the right sender email.

 

3. Leave a response in Idealo.

 (e.g. Hi John! 

I’m so sorry you’ve had to deal with this. I understand that [wondering when your mattress will be delivered] is terribly [frustrating and time consuming]. 

I see that [the delivery company missed you on Monday, but they will make another attempt to deliver on Thursdsay. I’ve sent you an email with the details and next steps of your order. Please kindly check your spam folder in case you haven’t received it.) 

4. After you completed the email and your Idealo reply, don't forget to note any actions you took in Magento/Fluent Commerce.

5. When you are able to provide a good solution for a customer's problem, send one more email:

"Dear (customer name), 
We are so glad we were able to solve your issue and that everything worked out with your order. We hope you are glad about our resolution too. We hope you enjoy your Emma products and have wonderful nights with them. 
Best Wishes, 
Emma Team"

6. Close the outbound case. Consider them resolved.

​

- NO valid order information provided:

  1. Leave a reply on Idealo acknowledging the issue and asking for a valid order number.

  2. If after 3 days, the customer does not provide the information - consider this review-case completed.

  3. After customer provided information, investigate the case and send an outbound email to the customer. Choose the right sender email

 

6. Edit your response in Idealo: indicate that you have sent an email to the customer.  

7. After you completed the email & your Idealo reply, don't forget to note any actions you took in Magento/Fluent Commerce.

8. When you are able to provide a good solution for a customer's problem, send one more email:

"Dear (customer name), 
We are so glad we were able to solve your issue and that everything worked out with your order. We hope you are glad about our resolution too.  
We hope you enjoy your Emma products and have wonderful nights with them. 
Best Wishes, 
Emma Team"

9. Close the outbound cases. Consider them resolved.

What to do after a reply

After you received customer infomation and left a reply on Idealo, please use "Set Email" on the ticket fields in Sprinklr and add customer's email there. This would allow us to have a 360* customer profile.


After you completed the outbound email, don't forget to note any actions you took in Magento/Fluent (if you have the customer information).

2. Do we flag reviews on Idealo if the customer does not reply within 3 days?

- No, because Idealo does not have this option. Rather than flagging, ask the customer for the order number & focus on personalization if they reply past the 3 days.

3. Do we respond to a review that is posted on the wrong market?

-Yes, if you noticed that the review belongs to another market, aknowledge customer's issue by replying to the review.

4. Should we suggest a customer to contact CS support?

- No, customers are likely leaving a review because they are not receiving sufficient support from CS.
