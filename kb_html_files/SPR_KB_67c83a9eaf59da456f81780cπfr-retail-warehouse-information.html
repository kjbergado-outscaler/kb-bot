The Supply Chain for France Offline: Warehouse operations with Rhenus (REN1_FR).

​

Location: Located in Gretz-Armainvilliers, Île-de-France is reachable within an hour and is connected to the rest of the country by: 

  • ROAD: Connection to the A4 highway Paris- Strasbourg 

  • AIR: Paris CDG airport (42 km) – Paris Orly airport (38 km) 

  • TRAIN: Gretz-Armainvilliers station (3 km), RER E 

​

  • Warehouse: Storage and handling solutions for ~23% of France Emma Volume with ~7,000 pallet positions available for varying SKU requirements – accessories, mattress, to beds. 

  • Systems: Integrated WMS for data visibility, efficiency, and scalable fulfillment operations. 

  • Certifications: ISO, ICPE, AEO, MASE Accredited warehouse for safety and quality standards.
