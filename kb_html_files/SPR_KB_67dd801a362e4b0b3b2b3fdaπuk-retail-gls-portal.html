Scope

To track and manage non-refurbished orders through the GLS portal, ensuring accurate updates in the LivingSocial Tracker.

Instructions

Accessing the GLS Portal

  • Portal URL: GLS Tracking Portal

  • No login required.

Tracking Non-Refurbished Orders

  1. Search for Orders:

    • Use the tracking number (usually starts with 2, 1, or 5).

​

​

  1. Verify Delivery Status:

    • If the order is marked as Delivered, update the LivingSocial Tracker.

    • If tracking information is missing, escalate to GLS support via email.

  2. Locate Tracking Information in Fluent Commerce:

    • Navigate to the Fulfillment Section under each ticket in Fluent Commerce to find the GLS tracking number.

​

​

Handling Missing Tracking Information

  1. Escalate to GLS Support:

    • Send an email with the Order Reference, Customer Information, and Description of the Issue.
