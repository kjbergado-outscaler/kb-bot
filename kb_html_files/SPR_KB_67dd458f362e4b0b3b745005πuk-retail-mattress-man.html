Introduction

Mattress Man orders and returns are managed via Fluent Commerce and email communication. The process includes receiving inquiries, verifying order details, assessing faulty issues, approving replacements or refunds, and coordinating warehouse collections. It does not cover order placement and logistics, which are managed separately by Inna. For any additional clarifications, Inna or senior management should be consulted.

Receiving Inquiries and Identifying Issues

Receiving the Inquiry

  • Mattress Man issues are communicated through Sprinkler (primary method) or email (secondary method due to migration issues).

  • The email subject typically includes 'MM' (Mattress Man) followed by the customer's order reference number.

  • The responsible person (e.g., Inna) receives and uploads orders into Fluent Commerce.

Requesting Order Information

  • Always ask for the Purchase Order (PO) number or invoice for order verification.

    • You can find the Internal and External Order numbers in the Invoice:

  • Locate the order in Fluent Commerce using the provided details.

  • Confirm if the issue relates to a faulty product, return request, or credit memo.

Handling Faulty Issues

Requesting Details

  • Ask the retailer to provide:

    • Type of mattress

    • Delivery date

    • Photographs of the issue

  • Verify if the mattress is still under the 200-night trial period.

    • If within trial, offer a replacement or refund.

    • If outside trial, only a replacement is available.

Evaluating Faulty Claims

  • Review the photos to determine if the issue is valid.

  • If further evidence is needed, request additional photographs.

Note: Education Before Replacement: If a mattress is misused rather than faulty, provide proper guidance instead of approving a replacement.

You can find the claim pictures requirements and examples here and how to validate the claim here.

We are not authorized to process product claims related to the mattress cover being cut during the unboxing process using the Emma Cutter, or defects resulting from sleeping on the incorrect side of the mattress.

Approving Replacements

  • If the issue is confirmed, instruct Mattress Man to:

    • Send a replacement mattress from their stock.

    • Collect the faulty mattress and store it in their warehouse.

  • The faulty mattress will be collected monthly.

Do not ask for a debit note, as the credit memo is processed automatically in Fluent Commerce.

Processing Returns & Credit Memos

Create Return Process in Fluent Commerce

  • Click the three dots in the top-right corner → Select "Create Return with Refund".

Fill Out the Return Form

  • In the return form, you may fill out blank fields with a dash (“-”). Since it’s a retailer order, the form will auto-fill those fields.

    • The fields Company Name, Street, City, Postcode, and Email are automatically filled and you can leave them as they are.

Returned Item Details

  • Quantity of Items: Select the quantity of items to be returned by clicking the “+” icon.

  • Item Condition: Choose the appropriate condition:

    • Packed - Never used

    • Packed - Used/Re-packed

    • Unpacked

  • Return Reason: Select “Product Claim”.

  • Comments: Add the customer’s reference number (MM19XXXX) in the “Comments” field.

  • Click Submit on the top right corner.

  • Flag “No pickup disposal by retailer” → Click Submit on the top right corner. 

  • Notify Mattress Man that the credit memo will be sent to their Accounts Team.

Monthly Warehouse Collections

Receiving Collection Requests

  • Mark Hall from Mattress Man contacts us ate the end of the month to arrange the collection of the mattresses they have on their Warehouse. He should provide:

    • PO numbers of faulty/returned mattresses.

    • Quantity of mattresses to be collected.

Note: Mattress collection is based on the quantity provided by Mark Hall, not individual cases.
