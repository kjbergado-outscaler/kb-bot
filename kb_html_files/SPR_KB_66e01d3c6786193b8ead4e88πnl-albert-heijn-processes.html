How do customers purchase Emma products in AH?


AH website will have a page per product and it will work just like our sales page in our website with an overview of all the products and the included discounts in the deal.

  • Customers go to slaapdeals.ah.nl or AH app and click on the Emma Slaapdeal banner that will lead to the flash sales page OR customers pay in an AH store and buy a voucher
  • Customers redeem the voucher online and place their order via the AH portal
  • Order appears in Magento
  • Delivery process as per usual


How will customers reach us for support?

When buying from AH, customers receive a confirmation email with a specific email address to contact for support.


This way:

  • Customers will never have contact with AH directly once their order is placed
  • Customers will send an email to AH@emma-sleep.com
  • However, if customer calls our support number or uses a different channel to request support, the Front Office team or CS team will assign the ticket to your team by setting
    • Group = Marketplaces
    • Marketplaces Name = Albert Heijn
  • Regardless of how the ticket entered Freshdesk, you can find them by just filtering your view for
    • "Marketplaces Name = Albert Heijn"


Regarding support, and as mentioned before, only two processes from AH orders are different from a regular purchase, namely Discounts and Refunds.
These 2 processes are only different because the order was posted directly with AH and we, at Emma:

  • don't have any bank details (needed for discounts that weren't properly attributed)
  • did not process payment and will not be the ones effectively paying the customers refund, we will start process, but AH will be the ones compensating the customer.

Check the next sections to see how to handle each process.

How to handle DISCOUNTS


Customers might complain regarding discounts that were not attributed and requiring compensation.

When such situations happen you will need to check what is the current discount for the product and cross-check with the order.


Discounts are processed and paid by Emma and normally processed via Magento and through the Central Europe Jira escalation channel.

The only difference is that we need to request for the customer's bank details:

  1. Since we don't have payment details from the order (as it was placed with AH directly) we must ask the customer's IBAN
  2. Once we have such IBAN we must update Magento and add it to the comment section (since IBAN field is not editable) - see the image below
  3. Last step is to create a Jira ticket (JIRA Escalation Portal) for follow up, adding information about customer IBAN and asking Backoffice colleagues to process the compensation/discount to the customer bank account.


Link for the Central Europe Jira Escalation Portal: JIRA Escalation Portal


How to handle REFUNDS

This is a recurrent request from customers and the only difference is that you will need to fill in a form for AH to effectuate the compensation.


We are the ones to initiate and document the request, but Refunds are processed directly by AH (as they received the order and the money for it).


Here are the steps:
  1. Agents must fill the following form for AH to initiate refund:
    1. REFUNDS FORM - Samenwerking Emma GmbH - Albert Heijn | week 23 Slaapdeals (office.com)
  2. And need to do the refund process in Magento, and leave a note informing that the form was filled (this information is needed to understand who failed - us or AH - if the customer is not properly refunded)
  3. Partial refunds should be done as they were a refund
