One-Time Retailers

One-time Retailers orders are pontual orders made by companies that don't have a partnership with us, so that can they benefit of 0% VAT. These orders need to be created by the backoffice and arrive to the CS through the Sales Team.

Order Creation

Open the Retail Backoffice Portal and choose Order Creation -> Single Order Creation -> One-time Retailers

​

Please give the following information to the Backoffice team:

  • Account ID (If it's the first purchase and there's no Account ID, please inform in the ticket)

  • Client's name

  • Billing Address

  • Client’s E-mail

  • Delivery Address

  • SKUs

​

After creating the request, paste the Jira number as an internal comment in the Freshdesk Ticket.

​

Once the Jira is completed, confirm that the order is correct and send the Expected Delivery Time and Order Confirmation document to the one-time retailer. If any of the products appear as Out Of Stock, please inform that these SKUs may arrive later due to lack of stock.
