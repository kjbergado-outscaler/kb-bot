Introduction

We have been facing quite a few issues regarding supplements of Phased-Out Products. We end up in a loop of having to recommunicate with the customers that we are once again cancelling a product after they contact for WIMO and agent supplements the same exact product without confirming Stock Availability or Phased-Out files.

Product Claim; Claims; supplement; Stock; Phased out

As you know we shared this KB regarding on how to confirm the stock of a product: 

How to check for available stock of an SKU.

 

To complement the process above, we also have available a list of Phased out SKU's for the BENEDACH markets, please make sure to check the files bellow. Always check fist the Phased Out SKU's if applicable to your market, regardless of stock in Fluent if an SKU is in the file then don't supplement.

For the remaining markets, please use the stock information available in Fluent.

 

With the collaboration of our team, we are sharing a file (Replacement Matrix) where we can provide a direct replacement for certain out-of-stock or phased-out products without having to escalate it internally. 

 

When customer contacts and we observe that a product is out-of-stock/Phased-out, if that SKU is part of this list, you can supplement it immediately with the suggestion shared on the 1st or 2nd option columns. Some of the products are not a direct replacement but an upgrade, so there's some suggestions on what to tell customer in case a change in product is needed.
