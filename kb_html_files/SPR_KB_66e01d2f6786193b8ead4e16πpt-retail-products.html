Out of Stock

Steps to take If the retailer can see that the order is out of stock on their side :

  • Open the order in the system and check the status of the barcode. If it is in “Ready For Dispatch,” it means the order was ready to be picked up by the carrier from the warehouse.

  • If the barcode is in “waiting for stock,” open the file Retail Stock File IBE.xlsx (sharepoint.com) and check the “Stock Alcala” tab to see when we will have stock. Inform the retailer of the stock receipt date and that the order will be prepared from that date. If we do not have a date, we can ask Maigerlis to check internally.

  • If the order is prepared, check the carrier’s platform to see if we already have a label, i.e., a shipment. We can then send the tracking link and the delivery forecast. If the order does not yet appear on the carrier’s platform, inform the forecast according to Magento and send the tracking as soon as it is available.

  • We can make a supplement for out-of-stock items if we are talking about original covers or comfort layers. For other types of items, we cannot.

  • If the barcode was rejected by the warehouse, escalate to Maigerlis.

  • If you see an order “waiting for stock” in Fluent, but we have the product available, escalate to Maigerlis. Do not open a ticket to the warehouse.
