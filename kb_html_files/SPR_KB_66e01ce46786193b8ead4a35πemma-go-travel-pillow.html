Summary of the product


The Emma Go Travel Pillow gives you a comfortable journey no matter where is your destination and your transportation option. Our travel pillow is designed to provide maximum cervical support in any position. The product is easy to store and transport, and you can take it anywhere with our portable carrying case. It will help you to sleep more comfortably during the travel and to arrive pain-free and feeling refreshed and rested.

USP's


Product featureCustomer benefit
4 in 1
The travel pillow can be used as a neck-supporting travel pillow, camping pillow, breastfeeding pillow, and lumbar pillow, as you can unzip it to turn it into a flat pillow as well.
Portable carrying case

Easy to roll up and put into the pillow case that comes with it. That keeps the pillow clean and helps with easy transportation. The pillow case has two features: It has an elastic band for you to slide it onto your luggage handle and it has a sling that you can use to hang it onto your backpack.


360 degrees support for your neck
The special cut adaptive material (memory foam) and the individual adjustable pillow support the neck 360 degrees.
Washable cover Completely removable and washable to stay hygienic.
2 colors option It's possible to choose between the orange or the blue travel pillow.

The "Wash & Care" label is included and placed on the inside of the cover.


  • Handwash only

  • Dry horizontally, avoid direct sunlight
