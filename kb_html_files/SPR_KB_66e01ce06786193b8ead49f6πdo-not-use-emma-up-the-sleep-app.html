Summary of product


The Emma Up app is for everyone who wants to understand what impacts their sleep and get better sleep in the long term. Different from other digital tools aiming at improving sleep, our product combines scientific insights and habit-building into a personalized program.


We understand that sleep is individual and cannot be improved with a standard solution. So, the app allows the user to have an individualized step-by-step program of interesting content and important habits that lead to better sleep.


The Emma Up is about progress, not perfection: the app's tools are designed to help the user achieve their goals and give the freedom to determine how much progress they want to make. Good sleep increases your energy level and concentration during the day, as well as boosts your mood and productivity.



USP's / Features


Educational content: The app teaches about good sleep. You learn new things about sleep every day and understand how good sleep works better every day.


Build habits: Emma up helps you understand what your body and mind need for great sleep. Based on neuroscience, will create an individual and customized program with daily habits to help you sleep better in a way that fits your lifestyle.


Sleep check-in: You will be able to track your sleep and get to know your sleep habits, and also check your progress.


Experts support: Tackle your sleep goal together with your own human sleep coach. Our certified sleep experts are there to support you. Simply reach out to them via chat – whether you’re sharing concerns and struggles or getting advice on how to nudge yourself to better sleep.


Created by specialists: All of our suggestions are of course science-backed. The app was created in collaboration with experts from Oxford University: Prof. Russell Foster, director of Sleep Institute; and Prof. Jason Ellis, director of Sleep Research.

How the App works


Questionnaire

In order to define the sleep chronotype of our users, they have to finish the survey, with questions like their sleep goal, how rest or tired they feel when waking up, and what time of the day they feel their best.



Educational content

After the questionnaire, the user will have access to the Homepage and redirect to the first educational content. The user will be able to read the daily piece of information related to their sleep program.



Habits

On this feature, the user will learn about what is sleep habits, and what kind of healthy habits they can start to pay attention and to track. They will be able to mark them on the app.



Sleep check-in

Once the reading part is done, the app will ask the user to report about sleep, so we can start gathering data. On Sleep check-in the user will score his/her sleep using emojis that represent how bad/good was the sleep. After completing this step they will get a celebration screen.



The daily loop

Every day when opening the Emma Up app, the user will receive a notification to start with Sleep check-in. After this, it will redirect to the Educational Content of the day. The user can read just that piece or, if prefer, read also new chapters.

And finally, the user will see the Habits page, to update the most recent routine information.
