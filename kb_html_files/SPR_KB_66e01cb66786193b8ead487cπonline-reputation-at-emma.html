What is Online Reputation and why is it important to us?

Online Reputation Management

Online reputation management is how we describe the actions we take to monitor, address, analyze, and shape the perception customers have of our brand.

One of the crucial elements of online reputation management is tracking and handling Emma's mentions across different digital touchpoints, which may include social media platforms; marketplaces, product review sites; complaint websites, among many others.

Now that consumers shape brands as much as companies do, it’s critical to understand how positive online reviews attract potential customers, how negative ones drive them away, and what you can do to turn satisfied customers into online advocates.

What is a good online reputation?

A good online reputation is a positive perception of a person or business based on the information that appears about them online.

A good online reputation means more customers will be open to buying our products and trusting our company. It also means that we proactively reach out to our customers, establish a relationship with them, and have them become our promoters.
