Introduction


Also known as Emma Simple Pillow, the Emma Basic Foam, it's a simple version of the Emma Original Foam: it has only one entire block of Visco foam, which is not adjustable. It has the same benefits as a standard Visco foam and it's suitable only for side or back sleepers, since it's not possible to adjust layers in order to be thin enough for a stomach sleeper. USPs


Visco Elastic Foam
Memory foam pillows are firmer but contour to the shape of your body, and even adjust as you shift or move during sleep.
Ergonomic pillow

Perfectly adapts to the shape of your body. It distributes optimally the pressure and supports the head and shoulders, relieving pressure efficiently.

Breathability cover

Every night your body loses fluid. That's why the cover of the Emma cushion is particularly breathable. So, your body can release excess energy in the form of heat, and you keep a pleasantly dry sleep climate. And another benefit is to don’t wake up sweating.

Allergic-friendly and machine-washable pillow cover

People with allergies can use this pillow freely. Users can wash the pillow cover to stay hygienic.
