Previously, refunds were done in Shopify, but they are now handled in JIRA.

Request Refund via JIRA

  • Navigate to the AR D2C Portal > Refunds > Refund Requests.

  • Select the appropriate refund option based on the scenario.

Ensure the correct refund type is selected to avoid rejection.

​

​

Confirm Refund Details

  • Verify the customer’s original payment method.

  • Ensure the correct Shopify Payment ID is used (as different line items may have different IDs).

Make sure to provide complete information in the JIRA ticket and process the necessary requests in Magento (e.g. Product Return) to avoid any delay in the processing of the refund.

For partial refunds (of bundles for example), please attached confirmation of the price breakdown shared by the sales team.

If a customer is requesting a price match, check this with the store sales team to verify before agreeing or processing.

​

​

Submit JIRA Ticket & Notify Customer

  • Fill in the JIRA ticket correctly (ensuring it is not declined due to incorrect details).

  • Add relevant notes in Sprinklr and Magento.

  • Notify the customer of the estimated refund processing time (7-14 business days).

Always copy the Jira ticket number and paste it in the comments in Magento.

 

Exceptions

Sometimes, we have highly aggravated customers in the stores (e.g. threatening with legal actions, calling the police, etc.). In these extreme situations, our store managers are authorized to do a refund on the store and activate it themselves via Shopify. When this happens, our internal refunds team needs to be informed about it asap. ​

High-level view of the process​

1. Store team can directly trigger the refund on Shopify;

2. Customer will receive an email saying the refund was processed​.

  • Refund will happen on the same payment method the customer used at the time of purchase​;

  • It might take up to 14 days for the money to be received, depending on the payment method (paypal, aplazame, klarna, etc. might take longer since we are depending on a third party)​.

3. Store team should open an email ticket with CS immediately and share all the details about the order and refund (within 5 hours after the refund)​

4. CS team will open a Jira ticket to the refunds team informing them about it.​

5. Refunds team will update Magento & Shopify with all the additional and necessary information about the refund.​
