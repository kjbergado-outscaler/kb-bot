USPs


  • The cover has innovative Peach touch technology. It feels very soft (a bit like velvet).

  • The pillow has a more fluffy feel compared to the memory foam pillow but still offers support for the head and neck.

  • The pillow has a lightly padded cover and two removable micro elastic layers. You can decide whether you want to use one or two layers and adjust the pillow to your needs.

  • Fits every season -> temperature regulation

  • It stays cool in hot conditions and acts as an insulator in cold temperatures.

Product Information


Materials:

  • Fabric

    o Cover fabric: Microfiber 100g/m3 - 100% Polyester down proof weaving cover
    Benefits: it attracts sweat and wicks it away from your body. That means the fabric will not retain the moisture if you sweat during the night, on the contrary, it will travel alongside the fibers until they reach the surface where they can evaporate.

    o Filling: siliconized Micro-elastic fiber (not siliconized for UK) - 100% Polyester

    Advantages of Micro-elastic/microfiber

    • Non-abrasive

    • Hygienic

    • Long-lasting if cared for properly

    • Soft to the touch

    • Lightweight

    • Water-repellent

    • More breathable than foam

    • Can be treated with anti-bacterial chemicals

It is not written on the 2 inner pillows which one has less filling than the other one. If you touch them, you can find it out easily.


Layers:

  1. Microfiber cover: The breathable cover is padded at the top and bottom with a layer of micro-elastic material. For extra comfort, the surface is finished with a velvety-soft peach touch.
  2. Thicker microfiber layer: The larger inner cushion is filled with more micro-elastic fibers. So your head is gently bedded every night.
  3. Thinner microfiber layer: The smaller inner cushion is lighter-filled and therefore feels wonderfully soft and fluffy.

Label

Brand Label and Care label included.

Brand Label: On one side a brand label is sewn in.

o Why do you need care and washing symbols?

They tell you how your items should be washed and dried for they to not get damaged and last for a long time.

Care label

  • Wash at a maximum of 40 degrees Celsius.

  • Do not bleach. It could lose color or be damaged during bleaching.

  • Do not iron

  • Cleaning with Perchloroethylene. Use laundry detergent.

  • Low tumble dry

If you don´t use the dryer, make sure that the microfibers are well spread on the top and bottom of the duvet. Let the duvet dry on a plane surface. We recommend washing the cover 6 to 8 times during its whole life.
