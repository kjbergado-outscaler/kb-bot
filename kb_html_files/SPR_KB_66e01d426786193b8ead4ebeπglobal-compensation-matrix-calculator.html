Global Compensation Matrix

Amazon agents only: No compensation matrix for Amazon in case of order delay. After 10 days of order stuck (=no updates on tracking), a supplement has to be created. See the respective article (Amazon) How to: Supplement Order.


The Global Compensation Matrix was created to set a standard and manage the compensation that is being given out to the customers.



IMPORTANT: The Global Matrix is for any country that is not on the European continent. For any market located in Europe, please refer to the article Compensation Matrix - European markets

Compensation Calculator


When escalating a refund you may need to define the discount or compensation amount to a customer, you will access the compensation matrix by using the Compensation Calculator.



When using the Calculator, you will provide all necessary conditions and a percentage of compensation will be displayed with the Maximum total to be offered to the customer in the provided scenario.


This amount is calculated based on the row total amount of the SKU (product) related to the country, automating and simplifying the information and ensuring we are not overcompensating the customer.
