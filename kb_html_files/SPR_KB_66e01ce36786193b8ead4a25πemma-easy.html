USPs


The Easy is an innovative mattress, designed to be wallet-friendly while giving good comfort and the right support. The wave structure provides excellent ventilation and better regulation of body temperature. Feel the innovation and sleep better than ever before.


Support

The Easy has 3 ergonomic zones! The different zones provide excellent pressure distribution, so you can sleep comfortably in any position.

Maximum adaptability

The secret to why the Easy can adapt to all body types is the new generation Airgocell® Comfort Foam.

Ventilation

Easy ensures an optimal sleeping climate every evening so that your body stays cool on hot summer evenings and you don't get cold in winter.

Layers

Airgocell®Motion Isolation


The top layer adapts to your body and provides good ventilation due to its open pores.


Benefits:

  • Moisture (sweat) will be absorbed by the top layer and then will evaporate. It doesn't affect the properties of the mattress after it dissipates.

  • It is not affected by moisture -> Mattress keeps its shape during its whole life

  • Prevents you from waking up sweating (also the right temperature in the room and the right duvet is important)

  • Temperature regulation = breathability


Supportive HRX Foam


The bottom layer of HRX polyurethane foam, which is supportive and extremely durable, has incisions that allow the mattress to adapt well to your body.


Benefits:

  • This foam supports you in the right places, where it matters.

  • Very long durability. No sagging (Liegekuhle), even after a long time.

  • Sparwelle (material saving cutting): helps support the firmness of the mattress. The perfectly designed height and distance of this wave create the perfect firmness/ softness of the mattress.

  • It is high-point elastic. The supportive bottom layer helps the body to move effortlessly, which means there ́s not a lot of strength required to turn over while sleeping.

  • 3 zones back support
