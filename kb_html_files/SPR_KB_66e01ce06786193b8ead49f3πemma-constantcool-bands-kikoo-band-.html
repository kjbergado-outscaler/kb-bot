What are Emma ConstantCOOL Bands?


Emma ConstantCOOL Bands are thin and flexible Graphite bands that are integrated inside of a mattress core for efficient heat removal.





All Night Cooling Effect


Emma ConstantCOOL bands can continuously pull excessive heat away from the body to help maintain the ideal sleep temperature all night.



No Noise and No Electricity


Compared to electric ventilation systems, such as AC or cooling mats, Emma ConstantCOOL bands provide the best sleep temperature without using any electricity and they are noise-free.



A Natural Cooling Technology that keeps comfortably cool for 12+ hours


ConstantCOOL bands only remove the heat you don’t want from your mattress by using simple physics, so you can sleep and relax at just the right temperature, hour after hour.



Why do people need ConstantCOOL Bands?



Ideally designed for hot sleepers who suffer from overheating during sleep.



Foam mattresses are made from insulating materials, meaning that the heat our body releases into them accumulates over time.




Talking about hot sleepers:


Their bodies can easily release heat and cause heat accumulation in a short time, which leads to a super hot and even sweaty night of sleep.


With Emma ConstantCOOL Bands, the extra body heat will be pulled away, so they can sleep in a cooler temperature, and extend their deep sleep cycle, which further improves their sleep quality overall.

Potential FAQs on ConstantCOOL Bands



Pre-sale:


How long will it keep me cool? (Pre-sale)
ConstantCOOL bands offer 12hrs+ of natural mattress cooling, with 2°C lowered in the foam. This is different to other technologies, which only provide an instant and short-lived cooling effect.


Will I feel cold with the ConstantCOOL bands, especially in winter?
No, the cooling effect from ConstantCOOL bands is independent of summer or winter, because it is about body heat, and it only takes the extra heat away while maintaining a good temperature balance, so you will feel comfortable, but never cold.


Are the cooling bands safe？ (Pre-sale)
Yes. ConstantCOOL's heat-releasing graphite strips are completely safe. Graphite is a natural mineral, and we use the best sustainably sourced and long-lasting materials.


Do the cooling bands need electricity to work? (Pre-sale)
No. ConstantCOOL bands use natural cooling to release body heat. Our perfectly-positioned ConstantCOOL bands remove excess body heat to achieve a naturally comfortable temperature. There’s nothing electric to switch on, charge up or run down.


How do I purchase ConstantCOOL bands? (Pre-sale)
To order ConstantCOOL bands, simply choose “+ ConstantCOOL” on the Hybrid Premium Mattress (NL), Hybrid (ES) page, then you will have everything set. Our manufacturer will integrate the bands into your mattress, no operation is needed from your end. Just enjoy the comfort of a mattress removing body heat.
