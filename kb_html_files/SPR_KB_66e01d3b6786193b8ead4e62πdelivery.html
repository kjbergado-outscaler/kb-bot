2 different types of delivery:

  1. Delivered by us (Usually we use UPS, Rhenus, DPD)

Delivery done by UPS: If you need to contact UPS (eg address update, request to send order back) call UPS phone number and say “Agent” so you will get connected.

Delivery done Rhenus. To check the tracking update for Rhenus see Rhenus website. If you need to contact Rhenus please contact their customer service. (If needed see further information on Fresh Desk in Solutions-UK-Delivery-Rhenus)

Delivery done by DPD. Check providing tracking number and if needed contact them using email address. A one-hour time window will be sent to the customer via email and text on the morning of delivery. (If needed see further information on Fresh Desk in Solutions-UK-Delivery-DPD)

  1. Delivered by Amazon (Fulfilled by Amazon)

This is how tracking information is displayed in Magento if order is fulfilled by Amazon.

We are unable to track the order and need to advise customer to contact Amazon directly for tracking information and delivery updates.

Sometimes when payment is pending and order is fulfilled by Amazon, the order is not visible in Magento. Please advise customer to contact Amazon customer service directly.
