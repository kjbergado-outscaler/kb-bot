Credit Memo
Definition

When a cancellation is done in Fluent commerce, automatically a credit memo is created in the system. You can immediately find it by accessing the Credit memo section in the Menu bar, as per How to: Navigating Fluent Commerce article.

A credit memo is a financial term for a monetary transaction, e.g. a refund to the customer.

Each credit memo has a unique reference number:



For a return WITH REFUND, the credit memo is created only after the status of the return is updated to Complete, as per related article: How to Return an item


Credit Memo Status

The credit memo status will reflect the status of the transaction:

Created

The credit Memo has been created

Awaiting for Submission

Credit Memo is waiting for its submission

Requested

Credit Memo has been acknowledged

PSP Pending

Credit Memo is processing

Completed

Credit Memo is completed

Failed

Credit Memo has failed


View of Credit Memo Status on Fluent Commerce

You may find it under Returned Orders WITH REFUND.

Steps to Send a Credit Memo to the Customer

​

  1. Locate the order the customer is contacting us about.

  2. Go to the "Related" tab, you’ll find the "Credit Memo" section there, at the end of the page.

    1. If a refund has been issued, a “Resend Email” button will be available.

      ​

  3. Check the status of the credit memo:

    1. If the status is "Awaiting Submission", "Requested", or "PSP Pending", it means the refund has not yet been issued.

      1. → In this case, please open a Jira to ADRDC for further investigation.

    2. If the status is "Complete", the refund has already been issued.

    3. If the status is "SENT_TO_CUSTOMER, ask the customer to check their junk/spam folder, as the email may have been filtered there.

  4. If the customer still hasn’t received the proof of refund, you can click “Resend Email.”

    1. Inform the customer that the email can take up to 8 business hours to arrive.

    2. If they still haven’t received it after that time, ask them to contact us again for follow-up.

​

​

Important Note: In some cases, the "Credit Memo" or "Resend Email" button may not be available. This usually happens when a supplement was offered instead of a return, meaning no refund was triggered.

​


Refunds for Compensation & Discount follow-up requests

Currently, whenever you need a refund of a specific amount for compensation, you are yet not able to perform this on Fluent Commerce.

For partial refunds (compensation / discount follow-up or composable products cancellation refunds: you should proceed with the manual request via our Order-2-Cash Portalto our Emma Refunds team.



ARD2C JIRA Portal



Attention: starting from 13/05/2025

Every time a refund is applicable and the agent needs to request it through the ARD2C Jira (manual refund), agent needs to:

1. Confirm if the email that the customer is contacting is the same as in Fluent Commerce. If not, confirm with the customer is the email in there is accurate and change it if it's not.

2. Send the following macro:

​

Hi [Customer's first name],

Thank you for your patience.

We have now initiated your refund request which is currently being processed by our internal team. If the account details and personal information we have on file are complete and correct, the refund will be processed accordingly. In this case, you can expect to receive it within 14 working days.

However, if there is any missing or incomplete personal information in our system, due to GDPR rules, our Refund support will contact you directly via email and ask you to fill a form providing the new IBAN and all the necessary details in order to process your refund.

Once again, we do appreciate your patience and understanding throughout this process. If you have any questions in the meantime, please feel free to contact us.

Kind regards,
[Agent's first name]
