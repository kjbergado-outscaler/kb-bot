What is the KB Smart Assist Widget?

​

This widget is where you can access the knowledge base without having to leave your case!

​

Besides allowing you to search the knowledge base, for all the interactions in English the Artificial Intelligence suggests Knowledge Base Articles accordingly to the conversation between agents and customers. It gets key-words from the conversation and search for articles with the same words, and recommends the articles for you during the case handling.

​

The suggested articles will appear on Live-chat, Voice and Social Medias, but not on Emails. And on this first version, it works for English conversations only.
