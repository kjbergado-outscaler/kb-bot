1. Introduction

In this article we will discuss the different payment methods we have in the German market. The customer sees the options when they check out:

2. Which payment methods do we have in Germany?

How does it work?

PayPal works as an intermediary between the customer and a bank. A user adds a bank account or credit/debit card to the PayPal system and whenever they are making an online payment, they can select which account to debit. All transactions are processed through PayPal, instead of the user's bank. If the customer chooses this payment method, they will automatically be redirected to Paypal.

​

Useful Links

​

  • Link to access payment portal from customers side: https://www.paypal.com/signin/

How does it work?

Put simply, a credit card is a type of loan: the bank or credit card issuer extends the customer a line of credit — money that they otherwise would not have. In exchange, the customer pays them back by the monthly payment due date.

​

Customers can pay conveniently and securely by credit card. Their credit card details are encrypted and transmitted using the secure SSL method. We accept Visa and MasterCard.

​

Useful links

​

  • Link to access payment portal from customers side: https://www.mastercard.com/global/en.html

What is it?

Alma is a payment method that offers the possibility of paying in 3x or 4x without paying interest.

​

Conditions

For values between the minimum limit of 100€ and the maximum limit of 6000€

​

How to use?

Customer will be redirected to Alma's server to pay the order's amount.

​

How does it work?
Klarna is a ‘Buy Now, Pay Later’ (BNPL) service that allows online shoppers to pay for products later in the month or in instalments at no extra cost. It means, in effect, that Klarna buys the items for the customer. The customer then pays Klarna back at a later date.

​

Useful Links

​Link to access payment portal from customers side: https://app.klarna.com/login

What is it?

This option allows the customer to use their Google or Apple wallet directly to proceed with the payment.

​

How to use it?

The wallet is an app for smartphones that has a digital version of a credit card and facilitates payments. Select it and further instructions will appear.

​

NOTE: Apple Pay will only appear if you use an Apple device and if on a Mac, only if you are using the Safari browser.

PayPal Express

​

The express method will fetch directly customer information from PayPal and provide it to Emma.

 

This means that this might lead to incorrect address, email or other personal information issues. 

In this scenario provide support and update necessary information.

​

  1. Explain to the customer you are seeing the issue in the system

  2. Review the following KB here.​

  3. Based on what the status of the order is at :
    A. Inform Customer that you will be updating the info
    B.Inform the customer that the info cannot be updated at this point due the order having progressed and we will need to wait for a tracking link.
