Payment Methods in MX D2C

Mercado Pago is the online payment platform that we use at Emma Mexico. The tool allows charging through different channels.

Through Mercado Pago, customers can pay with credit cards, bank transfer and even cash.


How does it work?

Once the customer has filled in the shipping and billing information for the delivery of their purchase, they must select the option on our website to pay and they will be redirected to the Mercado Pago portal, where they can select the payment method they prefer.


The payment methods that Mercado Pago offers us are:


  • Credit card
  • Debit or reloadable cards
  • Credit Market (It is a line of credit offered by this same platform)
  • In cash with transfer or bank deposit (Only for banks: Bancomer, Banamex or Santander)
  • In cash with a ticket: When the buyer completes his purchase, Mercado Pago will give him all the information and instructions to pay. And they will also send an e-mail with that information. The places of payment for this option are: 7-Eleven, Circle K, Soriana, Extra, Calimax, Santander, Citibanamex, Chedraui, Telecomm, BBVA Bancomer, Farmacias del Ahorro and Oxxo stores.
  • Payment will be credited within 1-2 business days.
  • With balance recharged in your Mercado Pago account

FAQs

Do I have to have a Mercado Pago account to buy with Emma?

No, it is not necessary to have a Mercado Pago account, the Portal will provide the various payment methods that the buyer can choose to make their purchase.


How can I choose the monthly installments for my purchase?

When selecting the credit card payment option, you must fill in your data and then select the monthly installments.

Although Mercado Pago offers up to 18 months without interest, certain credit cards do not offer the same, an example is American Express, which only offers 12 months without interest.


Can I pay with paypal?

Yes, Mercado Pago has a section to pay with Paypal within the same platform, the customer only has to select that option and it will be sent to the PayPal page to log in and approve the payment.


The payment method is rejected

These problems can be caused by:

The card-issuing bank declined the payment.

Mercado Pago rejects the payment because it did not pass the internal security controls of its platform.

In these cases, the platform requests that the customer use another payment method or card.


I have not received my refund money

The case must be escalated directly to Mercado Pago, on its portal Main menu > Help > Contact us > Send Message

Explain the client's situation and wait for a response from them, this afternoon from 24 to 48 hours to be attended by the portal.

The reference to make the transfer does not work:

Unfortunately Mercado Pago only issues the reference once, and if it is not used, the customer will have to make the purchase again, the first purchase will be cancelled.

Mercado Pago Purchase Flow in D2C

After the customer has filled in their purchase data, a pop-up window on the Mercado Pago platform will open.


The customer can choose between the different payment methods, either, Enter their Mercado Pago account or among the guest options, if they have a Credit or Debit Card, Electronic Transfer, Cash or PayPal.


If you choose the Credit or Debit card option, the following option will appear and the customer must fill in their data.



Cash Option: The customer must choose between the following store options and later it will indicate their email, so that they can send the respective information for their payment.

Transfer Option: As in the previous option, the client must choose between the following bank options and later they will indicate their email, so that they can send them the respective information for their payment.


PayPal: The window will open for the customer to log in and will be redirected to the PayPal website.
