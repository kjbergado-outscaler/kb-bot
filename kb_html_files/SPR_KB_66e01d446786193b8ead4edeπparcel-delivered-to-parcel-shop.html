Delivery to Parcel shop


This article is to clarify what a parcel shop is, when is the parcels delivered to a parcel shop, and how the customer is informed.


What is a Parcel shop?


A parcel shop is a store, gas station, or similar where the carrier can deliver parcels for the customers to pick up whenever it fits them.


It is always marked on the store which carriers they collaborate with as in the picture below for a GLS parcel shop.





Why and when is a parcel delivery to a Parcel shop?

Normally a parcel is delivered to a parcel shop when the carrier has tried to deliver the parcel to the customer's address and the customer was unavailable or not home.


The carrier does not wait outside the customer's house and we cannot expect the carrier to call the customer when arriving to the customer's address.



If the customer was unavailable for the delivery the carrier will sometimes deliver the parcel to a parcel shop near the customer's address.


We cannot guaranty the customer that the parcel will be delivered to a parcel shop if the customer is not home when the carrier tries to deliver the parcel.



If the tracking says it was delivered in a parcel shop then that is the case

If the tracking does not say delivered in parcel shop and the tracking does not say returned to sender, then the carrier will try and deliver the parcel to the customer's address again one of the next working days,




The customer can go to the parcel shop in it's opening hours, which they can find on google, to pick up the parcel.

How is the customer informed?

The customer will receive a notification either via SMS or email directly from the carrier.


In this notification, the customer will find the tracking number, address of the parcel shop and other necessary information the customer needs to pickup the parcel.


Deliver parcel from parcel shop to customer's address

It is not possible for the carrier to pick up the parcel again once it has been delivered in the parcel shop.


This is because the parcel is already marked as delivered in our and the carrier's system.


It is therefore not possible for us or the carrier to redeliver the parcel.

Parcel delivered in Parcel station / Parcel locker

When a parcel is delivered to a parcel station or parcel locker, it means the same as when delivered to a parcel shop.


The difference between these locations is:

Parcel shop = store

Parcel station / parcel locker = no human service provided


Notification differences:

Parcel shop = notification via SMS or email

Parcel station/parcel locker = notification via SMS, email, or psychical note in customer's mailbox.


No matter the type of notification, the customer will always receive the notification from the carrier directly and it will contain all the necessary information to pick up the parcel.

Parcel shop. The customer gets the parcel with the help of a human.



Parcel station / parcel locker. The customer gets a code in the notification from the carrier, goes to the station/locker, types the provided code, and the "door" will open and the customer can collect their parcel.
