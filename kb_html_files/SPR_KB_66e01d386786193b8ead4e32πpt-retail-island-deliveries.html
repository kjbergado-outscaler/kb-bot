Island Deliveries

In Portugal we work with 5 retailers on the islands:

  • Azores: Jomare; Euromotas

  • Madeira: Móveis Abel; Eletromendes; Dicocina

​

These retailers work with freight forwarders, located in mainland Portugal, who send the merchandise by boat to the island. Emma is only responsible for the delivery to the freight forwarder and shipping to the island from there is in the responsibility of the client.

​

This way:

  • The shipping address for all orders from these customers will be the forwarder's address, with each retailer having its own address.

  • The Shipping Note must be sent to the assigned carrier (DBS Land) with the final address, so that the delivery is delivered to the recipient by the island carriers. This management is done by the Backoffice Team.

Deliveries to the Islands should always go by Pallet.​

If other retailers want to send products to the Islands, these go directly by the carrier. The client is in charge of paying shipment costs on those orders. Do not send mattresses in drop shipping to islands, even if it is a replacement.

​

If you need to send DBS a shipping note regarding a delivery for these retailers, please open a ticket to the Backoffice:

  • Order Management

  • Prepare Documentation

  • PT Islands

​

Please ask the team to send you the Shipping Label to send to the carrier, as we have received that incidence. Then, forward the Shipping Label to the respective carrier.
