​

​

​

​

1.7- Insert the product SKU, you can locate it in Fluentcommerce, refer to Step 2, sub step 1.2.

Note: Are you seeing an SKU that starts with 'XEM'? For instance:
XEMBDWDB180200_00150:EBDOW180200SIA? The part before the ':' mark is only to indicate this SKU is part of a bundle. To review the stock for this item, you must only search for the last part of the string. In this example, that is 'EBDOW180200SIA'.

1.1- Select “View Details”

1.9- Check if the SKU has:

  1. Location Ref: This will indicate the
    warehouse that has the stock.

  2. ATS (Available Stock to Sell). The thresholds are:

    1. ATS for Mattresses needs to be more then 15

    2. ATS for Beds and Accessories needs to be more then 20

    2.- The component/part or product has enough stock

    • If YES→ Proceed to Step 6 (Outcome
      will be Supplement).

    • If NO → Proceed to Step 6 (Outcome
      will be Refund)
