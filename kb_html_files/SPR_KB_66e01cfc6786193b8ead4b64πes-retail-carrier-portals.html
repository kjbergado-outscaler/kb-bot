How to use the carrier portals for Spain?

1. GLS

1. Press on "Tracking Shipment Status"

​

2. Clean the section "date", add on the reference number the "order number" and unmarked all the options that do appear below. Then press the buttom to "search".

​

3. Once you will search you will be able to check the status of the order + the POD of delivery if the order has been already. delivered:

​

MOST COMMON STATUS THE ORDERS WILL GO THROUGH


1. Grabado/ label created- GLS has received the pre-registration of the shipment but have not yet physically received it at our facilities.

2. Shipment picked up by GLS- the order is already recieved by GLS and they are beginning the process of shipping it to its destination.

3. En tránsito a destino- The package has left its origin and is being transported to the agency in charge of delivery.

4. En Delegación de Destino/ en plataforma de destino- The order is already in the final delegation of the transporter, which is the closest one from which the customer lives.

5. En Reparto-The shipment is in the hands of a delivery person from our network and will be delivered according to the conditions of service and delivery route. If the FlexDeliveryService is activated, you can select the delivery time slot or another delivery option in the e-mail you will receive, which will be respected as far as possible, taking into account the delivery route.

6. Entregado- delivered already to the customer. You will be able to download the POD that will serve as a justification of delivery with the customer in case of any issues.

​

OTHER STATUS YOU CAN FIND

​

ALMACENADO- the order has some problems, and that is why it cannot be sent
ANULADA- it has been cancelled
CERRADO DEFINITIVO- the sent has been closed without possibility to be reopened
CERRADO POR SINIESTRO- the order was damaged and hence we need
DEVUELTA- returned
DEVUELTA AL CLIENTE- returned to the client
DEVUELTA ALMACEN- returned to the warehouse
EN DEVOLUCION- in the process to be returned to the warehouse
EN TRANSITO MARITIMO- de camino a destino vía marítima (Ej: Islas Baleares)
ENTREGA PARCIAL- la entrega se ha entregado parcialmente y falta algún producto por entregar.
ENTREGADO EN PARCESHOP GLS- the delivery has taken place in a GLS parcelshop. This only applies to pillows, bed linen and accesories.
PENDIENTE PREPARACION- waiting to be prepared
RECEPCIONADO EN PS GLS- recieved on the PS of GLS
SOLICITUD DE DEVOLUCION- request to be returned

2. XPO


1. Press on "Orders"- "Every order"

​

2. Add on the "search" area the order number and press enter.

​

STEPS OF THE ORDER

All the orders will follow the next steps:

​

​

1. Recogido- the order has been already picked from the warehouse.

2. En tránsito- the order has been already picked from the warehouse and it is being transported.

3. En Depot- The order is already in the final delegation of the transporter, which is the closest one from which the customer lives.

4. En Reparto- the transporter is already prepared to delivered the order, which will most probably be delivered in the next 24-48 hours.

5. Entregado- delivered already to the customer. You will be able to download the POD that will serve as a justification of delivery with the customer in case of any issues.

​

You can also check the estimated delivery time as you can see in the image. FYI that it is not 100% accurate. Also, every customer with XPO will be able to select the delivery date and time via a link that XPO will send to their Whatsapp.

​

How to understand which one is the carrier?

Orders with Base Tapizada or Canapé will be handled by XPO as it requires "2 man handling", while orders that do not have those products will be managed by GLS.
