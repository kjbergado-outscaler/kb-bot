What is Parcel Perform?


Parcel Perform is a platform that combines delivery information from all carriers in a single platform improving customer experience. It collects parcel statuses from all our carriers and then triggers the correspondent transactional email, like shipment confirmation & out for delivery. The best part is that for the customer it will look like an Emma page.

The Tracking Link will map the delivery across its different moments, enabling us to help customers follow their order and monitor the different statuses of delivery, seeing what they are seeing.




Where can I access the Tracking Links


You can find them in two different places in Magento's Order page:




  1. In the Shipping & Handling Information block, which includes both the Tracking Link and the link to the carrier’s website. They both show the same information, but with different formats: the Parcel Perform link looks like an Emma page and the carriers website depends on each carrier.





  2. In the Purchase Orders section you can see the current status for all the Barcodes (including date and time) next to each product and click on the link.



Both options will lead you to the web page with the Parcel Perform widget, where you can see the tracking information:


How can this be useful?


Now that you know how to reach the Tracking Link page it's time to understand how can this be useful for you.

Once you access the Tracking Link you will be able to find information about the order status allowing you to have a better overview of the shipping evolution besides the status description, you will also find time and location stamps.



Therefore you should use this:

  • When you need Proof of Shipment regarding Logistic claims, if this is the case access the Tracking Link web page, take a screenshot and attach it to the logistic claim. 


For Logistic Claim it only applies to Lost & Stolen products and Damaged Products
