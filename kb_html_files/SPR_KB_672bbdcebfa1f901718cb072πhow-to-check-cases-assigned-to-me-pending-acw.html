Introduction

In this article you have instructions about how to find on Sprinklr all your cases: assigned to you; pending; closed, etc.

How to find "all my cases" on Sprinklr

Step 1: From the homepage of the Agent Care Console, click on the button that indicates Console

​

​

Step 2: Click on the small arrow:

Step 3: Click on the second "My work" and a drop-down menu will appear and then you click on "All my cases".

Step 4: Here you will be able to see the numbers of your cases according to the Case Status.

How to check cases with "Pending ACW"

ACW means "after case working" and is related to the actions and information you must input after handling a case. Especially after handling Phone calls, a pop-up window will appear named "Execute ACW" where you have to add the case status, case category and other information.

If you don't fill it in time, the case will count as pending and you will have to finish it later. For you to find those "pending ACW" cases, please follow the next steps.

Step 1: From the homepage of the Agent Care Console, click on the button that indicates Console

Step 3: Click on the first "My work" and a drop-down menu will appear and then you click on "Pending ACW".
