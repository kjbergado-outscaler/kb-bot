Article Summary

In this article, you can check what the Supervisor Console looks like, what to see after you log in and what are the icons on the left-hand side and right top corner menu at your Supervisor Console.

Supervisor Console Overview

Supervisor Care Persona page

The image below shows the Supervisor Care Persona page, which contains all the necessary features to help you get started with your work day.

Icons on the left-hand side menu

As per the image above, you have 9 options on the left-hand side menu however only the following will be relevant for you:

  • Home: It takes you to the Supervisor Persona homepage, same as you see above.

  • Team / Agent Monitoring: Dashboard that allows you to monitor the activity of your agents

  • Queue Monitoring: Dashboard to monitor the load of the queues you are responsible for

  • Callbacks: List of callbacks schedule by agents

  • Announcements: feature to create announcements that can be launched to all or specific users on the platform

  • Reports: Comprehensive dashboard with widgets (graphics) to understand queue performances and agent activities

Additionally you should have an icon for Knowledge base after all to icons mentioned above.
