1. How to initiate a return

Returns for accessories for Magento orders in BENEDACH and France are handled by DHL.

Step by step guide (read carefully):

  1. Ask the customer to enter his/her data and to also add the order nr. in the reference field (this is important to track the return afterwards)

  2. If the customer has more than one parcel, ask him/her to create as many return labels as there are parcels using the link. (or do it for the customer)

  3. (If you want to take the time and do the customer a favour, you may do steps 2./3. and 4 for the customer and send the customer the finished return label)

  4. Ask the customer to print this label and to paste it on the box

  5. Some parcel shops also provide a printing service. The customer can review their parcel shop on this page, to see if it offers a printing service for the return label.

  6. Inform the customer that product(s) must be returned inside a square-shaped cardboard box or plastic bag with following maximum dimensions, otherwise it might be rejected at the drop-off point.

  7. Ask the customer to keep the return receipt until the refund is issued.

  8. The customer needs to send us a proof of return. Once we receive the proof of return, you need to manually update the status of the return to 'Complete'. Once the status is set to 'Complete', this will trigger the refund process.

2. How the refund is triggered

For Magento

The customer needs to send us a proof of return. Once we receive the proof of return, you need to manually update the status of the return to 'Complete'. Once the status is set to 'Complete', this will trigger the refund process.

3. When can this case be considered resolved?

Once the customer has his/her return label, and they have no further questions, you can close the ticket.
